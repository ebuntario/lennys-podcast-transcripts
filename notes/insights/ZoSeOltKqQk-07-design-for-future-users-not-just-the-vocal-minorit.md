When making product changes, design for the larger number of future users rather than over-indexing on the vocal minority who may be unhappy. Be transparent, authentic, and listen to feedback, but make strategic decisions that improve the experience for the broader user base. This approach is key to effective [[concepts/change-management|change management]].
