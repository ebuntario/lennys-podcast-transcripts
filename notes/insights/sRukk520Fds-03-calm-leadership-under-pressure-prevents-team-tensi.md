When a leader reflects stress onto their team, it causes everyone to tense up and ironically produces worse results. Maintaining a calm, even-keeled demeanor allows for clearer thinking during high-pressure situations. Developing this [[concepts/leadership-composure]] often comes from experiencing stressful cycles and learning that challenges eventually pass.
