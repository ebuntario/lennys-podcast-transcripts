Some product improvements that show neutral or negative short-term conversion metrics can significantly increase long-term user success. For example, better default templates might not immediately convert more signups but can help users create higher-quality stores that generate early sales momentum. This demonstrates why [[concepts/long-term-planning|long-term evaluation]] is essential for understanding true product impact.
