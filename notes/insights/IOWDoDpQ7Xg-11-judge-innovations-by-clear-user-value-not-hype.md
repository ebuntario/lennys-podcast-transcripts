When evaluating new technologies, cut through the hype by asking a simple question: "What clear, significant value does this provide to end users?" If you cannot articulate a compelling answer that makes users' lives easier, the innovation likely lacks substance. Sustainable adoption is always rooted in [[concepts/tangible-user-benefits|tangible user benefits]], not technical novelty alone.
