When Farhan hedged between React Native and native mobile development at Shopify, it cost 18 months and 100 engineers' time. Toby's feedback was clear: you'll never get in trouble for taking a bold risk that fails, but you will for not taking enough risk. [[concepts/bold-decisions|Bold choices]], even when they fail, provide clearer learning and direction than cautious compromises.
