Identify potential podcast topics by noticing what subjects make people lean in during casual conversations. The topics that generate follow-up questions and sustained interest in social settings indicate genuine [[concepts/personal-passion|passion]] and audience demand. This organic validation ensures you'll enjoy creating content about subjects that naturally engage others.
