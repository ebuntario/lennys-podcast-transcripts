Effective philanthropy should address both fundamental needs and soul-nourishing experiences. Invest in base-level solutions for issues like homelessness and mental health, while also funding public art and cultural projects that uplift communities. This [[concepts/philanthropy-strategy|barbell strategy]] ensures support for practical survival and the inspirational elements that improve quality of life.
