Making a significant impact often involves pursuing new opportunities despite uncertainty, such as restarting a tenure clock or founding a company based on a vision. The key is to focus on [[concepts/passion-driven-work|alignment with mission and people]] rather than over-analyzing every potential downside. This [[concepts/intellectual-fearlessness|intellectual fearlessness]] is essential for creating something novel and meaningful.
