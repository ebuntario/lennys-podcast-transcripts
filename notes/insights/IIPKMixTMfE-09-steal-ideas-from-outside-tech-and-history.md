Look beyond the tech industry's short-term cycles to history and other domains for patterns and solutions. Understanding how complex systems emerge from simple parts or how empires bundle and unbundle provides powerful mental models. This [[concepts/idea-synthesis|idea synthesis]] can make the problems in front of you more interesting and solvable.
