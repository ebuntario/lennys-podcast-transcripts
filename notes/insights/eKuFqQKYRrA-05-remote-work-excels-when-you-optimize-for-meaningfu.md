Instead of trying to replicate office dynamics remotely, successful distributed companies create intentional gatherings for high-velocity creative work and relationship building. These "bursts" or offsites, when made frictionless through proper infrastructure, provide the [[concepts/team-collaboration|collaboration]] and social connection that sustain teams between meetings.
