In planning or brainstorming sessions, use a "$100 voting" ritual where participants allocate virtual dollars to problems or solutions. This quickly reveals alignment (or misalignment) and forces prioritization. It's an effective way to get the "elephant in the room" out and focus discussion on the most important [[concepts/idea-prioritization|ideas]].
