Many failed products begin with [[concepts/solution-first-thinking|solution-first thinking]] where teams develop technology then search for customer problems. The Fire Phone exemplified this approach with its 3D effects technology lacking meaningful customer problems to solve. Successful innovation starts with identifying significant customer problems before considering potential solutions.
