Executives at banks, insurers, and pharmaceutical companies turn to SAFe because it provides a prescriptive, seemingly complete operating model for software development. They often lack internal leaders who have scaled [[concepts/digital-transformation|digital product]] organizations, making a detailed framework appealing despite its shortcomings.
