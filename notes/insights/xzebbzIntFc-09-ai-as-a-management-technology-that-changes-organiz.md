AI is fundamentally a [[concepts/ai-management|management technology]] for coordinating intelligence. It will change individual spans of control by excelling at summarization, potentially reducing layers of hierarchy. The critical issue is that AI systems will reflect the [[concepts/organizational-values|organizational values]] of the companies that build and deploy them, making corporate governance and ethical alignment more important than ever.
