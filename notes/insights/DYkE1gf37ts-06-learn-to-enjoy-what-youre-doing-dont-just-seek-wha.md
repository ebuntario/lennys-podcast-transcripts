Arranging your external life to be enjoyable often fails, as evidenced by wealthy individuals who remain unhappy. A more powerful approach is to ask, "How can I enjoy this current activity 10% more?" This internal shift naturally leads you toward activities you genuinely enjoy and builds sustainable satisfaction.
