True [[concepts/proactive-ownership|ownership]] involves stepping beyond your formal responsibilities to address problems and seize opportunities. This mindset, encapsulated in "an owner never says that's not my job," drives individuals to contribute to overall success rather than staying within narrow role boundaries.
