The need for individuals to stay at the cutting edge of their craft aligns perfectly with an organization's need to be agile and adaptive. This [[concepts/incentive-alignment|incentive alignment]] provides a powerful tailwind for change. Individuals should view developing AI fluency and a full-stack mindset as critical for their own [[concepts/career-resilience|career resilience]], not just a corporate mandate.
