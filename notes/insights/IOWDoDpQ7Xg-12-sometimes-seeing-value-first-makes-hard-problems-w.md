The team behind Google Docs saw the "what if" value of collaborative editing before understanding how hard the technical implementation would be. If they had understood the difficulty first, they might have abandoned the idea. Let a compelling [[concepts/end-vision|vision of the end value]] motivate you through challenging implementation phases, not the other way around.
