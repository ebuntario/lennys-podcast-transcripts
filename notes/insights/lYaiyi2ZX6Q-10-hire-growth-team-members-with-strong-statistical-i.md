The first growth hires should possess strong statistical intuition to avoid flawed decision-making. Look for candidates who understand sampling, selection bias, and can design a proper [[concepts/experimentation|experiment]]. Assess this through case studies that require them to reason from first principles and design a measured test, rather than just proposing to build a feature.
