Understanding your own personality traits, such as where you fall on spectrums like politeness, directness, or openness to abstract ideas, is foundational for working with others. Using a framework like the Big Five helps you recognize that others don't think like you, which prevents you from getting "lost in the broader spectrum." This [[concepts/self-awareness|self-awareness]] allows you to make intentional choices about your behavior to improve team outcomes.
