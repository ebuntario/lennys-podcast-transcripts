The biggest barrier to executing a good strategy is often internal [[concepts/organizational-politics|organizational politics]] and conflicting interests. Different factions have their own agendas, and there is a natural hesitancy to make hard choices that reallocate power or resources. Effective [[concepts/team-leadership|leadership]] requires being a decisive "decider" who can forge consensus or mandate a focused [[concepts/action-agenda|action agenda]] despite these dynamics.
