During [[concepts/onboarding|onboarding]], presenting limited or single recommendations (like one doctor or appointment time) reduces [[concepts/choice-overload|choice overload]] and accelerates decision-making. This approach decreases cognitive barriers and increases the likelihood of users completing a key initial action, such as booking a first appointment.
