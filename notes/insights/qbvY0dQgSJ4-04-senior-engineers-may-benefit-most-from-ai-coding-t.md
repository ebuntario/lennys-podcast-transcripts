A randomized trial within an engineering team suggested that the highest-performing [[concepts/engineering-organization|senior engineers]] received the biggest productivity boost from AI coding assistants. This contrasts with the perception that junior engineers would benefit most, though results can vary by team culture and individual resistance to new tools.
