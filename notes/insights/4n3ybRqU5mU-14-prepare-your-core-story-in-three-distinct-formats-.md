Have your key story ready in three formats: spoken, written, and visual (like a whiteboard drawing). Also, prepare three lengths: a 75-second elevator pitch, a 6-minute version for planning contexts, and an extended version (like an 80-minute TED Talk length) for deep dives. This [[concepts/preparation|preparation]] ensures you can [[concepts/audience-adaptation|adapt your communication]] effectively for any situation.
