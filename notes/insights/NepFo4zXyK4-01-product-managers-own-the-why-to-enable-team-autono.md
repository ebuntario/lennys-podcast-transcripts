A product manager does not need to be the sole source of ideas, but they are uniquely responsible for owning the "why" behind a product or feature. This clarity enables designers and engineers to make great local decisions autonomously, which is essential for [[concepts/scaling|scaling]] effectively. The "why" provides the guiding principle that aligns distributed decision-making across the team.
