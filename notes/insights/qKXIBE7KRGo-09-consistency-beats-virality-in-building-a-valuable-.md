Focus on consistent, valuable content rather than chasing one-off viral hits. When launching a channel, prepare a pipeline of content to build momentum and establish a regular cadence. [[concepts/audience-building|Building a true audience]] is about deepening relationships with the right people who find lasting value in what you do, not just accumulating low-engagement followers from viral bait.
