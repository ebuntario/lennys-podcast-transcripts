Fundamental human functions like sleep, diet, exercise, playfulness, and socialization serve as core wellbeing indicators. When these are consistently disrupted, it is a flashing red alarm that something detrimental is happening and change is necessary. The body keeps the score, and ignoring these signals means ignoring a direct message that your current path is unsustainable.
