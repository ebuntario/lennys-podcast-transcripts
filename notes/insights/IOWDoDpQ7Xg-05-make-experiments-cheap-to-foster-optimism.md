Optimism and innovation thrive when the cost of trying new ideas is low. Sharpen your tools and processes so that initial experiments can be built in days, not months. When [[concepts/prototyping|rapid experimentation]] is cheap, teams are more willing to take creative leaps and discover unexpected value, as seen in the early days of Google Docs.
