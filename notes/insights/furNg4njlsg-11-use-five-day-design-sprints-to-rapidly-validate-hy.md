For zero-to-one initiatives, Miro employs five-day design sprints to mock up concepts, create prototypes, and gather user validation. This compressed timeline forces teams to quickly test core assumptions, as seen with Miro Talktrack where early feedback revealed users wanted [[concepts/asynchronous-collaboration|interactive collaboration]] over simple screen recording.
