Product teams should view sales and go-to-market teams as a major asset for building [[concepts/customer-empathy|customer empathy]], not as a distraction. Salespeople interact with far more customers than any single PM can, providing invaluable insights into customer needs and pain points. Leaning on this field intelligence helps product managers make better-informed decisions about what to build and how to build it.
