People leave jobs for one of four core quests: to "get out" of a draining situation, to "take the next step" in skill-building, to "regain control" over time and work, or to "realign" with their core strengths. Understanding which [[concepts/career-quests|quest you are on]] provides direction for what to seek in your next role, moving beyond vague dissatisfaction.
