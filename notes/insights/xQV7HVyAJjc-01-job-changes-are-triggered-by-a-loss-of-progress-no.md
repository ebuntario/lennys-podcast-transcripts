The moment you stop making progress in your career is the moment you start looking for another job. People often attribute job changes to luck, but luck is simply when opportunity meets preparedness. This framework applies the [[concepts/jobs-to-be-done|Jobs-to-be-Done]] theory to careers, viewing a job as a "product" hired to make specific progress in one's life.
