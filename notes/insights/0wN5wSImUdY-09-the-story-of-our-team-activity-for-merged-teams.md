When teams merge, facilitate a "Story of Our Team" activity where each group creates a timeline of key milestones, achievements, and member join/leave dates. Sharing these stories builds a shared sense of history and mutual respect before planning a joint future. This exercise accelerates [[concepts/team-building|team building]] and cultural integration during a merger.
