For temporary or functional leadership roles, consider using an election where candidates give a short speech. This engages the team, surfaces who is genuinely interested, and creates buy-in for the selected leader. It's a democratic [[concepts/decision-making|ritual]] that works well in corporate settings for roles like coordinating cross-company standards or culture initiatives.
