Entrepreneurial culture often socializes people to bullshit about everything being "up and to the right." Creating spaces for shared, authentic experiences—like circles where people can tell the truth without fixing—counteracts this. This [[concepts/authenticity|authenticity]] builds trust and is a critical component of the leadership equation.
