A core promise for beloved B2B tools is to avoid features that make individual contributors' (ICs) workflows worse to satisfy middle-manager reporting needs. These customization requests for tracking lead to bloated software and disengaged users. The long-term health of the product depends on aligning incentives by making the [[concepts/user-centric-design|IC experience]] joyful and frictionless.
