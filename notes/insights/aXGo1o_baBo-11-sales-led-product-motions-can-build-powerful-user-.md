Contrary to some product orthodoxy, a [[concepts/sales-led-growth|sales-led motion]] does not preclude building a great, user-centric product. Many powerhouse companies are built this way, and PMs in these environments must deeply understand the commercial model. The key is balancing market feedback with product craft to drive sustainable growth.
