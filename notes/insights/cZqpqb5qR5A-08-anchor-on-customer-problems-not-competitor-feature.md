Reacting to every competitor feature leads to [[concepts/competitive-myopia|competitive myopia]] and losing your unique value. Competitors' shipped features are the tip of an iceberg built on their own customer research. Instead, regularly revisit your own user interviews and problem statements to stay anchored on solving for your specific customers, playing the long game rather than engaging in feature parity battles.
