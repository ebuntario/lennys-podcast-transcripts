Signs of a valuable community include learning new things regularly, having opportunities to contribute and share your knowledge, and enjoying the company of kind, interesting people. Engagement alone is not the best success metric; signal-to-noise ratio and mutual trust are more important. A healthy [[concepts/community|community]] facilitates both [[concepts/networking|networking]] and practical [[concepts/learning|learning]].
