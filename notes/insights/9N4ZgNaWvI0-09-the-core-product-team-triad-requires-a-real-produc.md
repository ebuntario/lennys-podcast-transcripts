The empowered product team model rests on four key competencies: a real product manager, a real product designer, a real tech lead, and a real [[concepts/product-leadership|product leader]]. The product leader is essential for coaching the team, developing product strategy, and ensuring the organization supports empowered work. Without this leadership competency, teams cannot function effectively.
