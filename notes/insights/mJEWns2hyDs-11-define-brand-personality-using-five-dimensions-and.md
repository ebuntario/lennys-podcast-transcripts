Strong brands spike in two of five personality dimensions: sincerity, excitement, competence, sophistication, and ruggedness. To add nuance, define five brand attributes as "we are X, but not Y" statements (e.g., "playful but not silly"). This creates [[concepts/brand-personality|brand personality]] tension that makes the brand more interesting and guides visual design and copywriting.
