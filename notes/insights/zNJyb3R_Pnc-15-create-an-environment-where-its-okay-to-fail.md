When testing new platforms or strategies, don't be too hard on yourself if it doesn't work immediately. Each platform has unique user behavior and functionality to learn. Foster a culture where the team knows it's okay to fail because you're either winning or [[concepts/learning-from-failure|learning]], which reduces pressure and encourages innovation.
