Large language models sitting idle overnight represent wasted potential. The future involves [[concepts/ai-agents|AI agents]] working continuously to build in anticipation of human needs. At Block, an engineer's agent watches his screen, listens to conversations, and proactively opens pull requests for features discussed hours earlier, showcasing a shift towards [[concepts/proactive-automation|proactive automation]].
