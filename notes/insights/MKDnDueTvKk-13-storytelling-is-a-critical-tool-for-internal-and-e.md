Driving change requires repetitive, compelling storytelling about the vision, the bets being made, and the "why" behind them. This is essential both internally to build team belief and externally to manage user and stakeholder expectations. Consistent [[concepts/strategic-narrative|strategic narrative]] helps align efforts and provides a framework for receiving and incorporating feedback.
