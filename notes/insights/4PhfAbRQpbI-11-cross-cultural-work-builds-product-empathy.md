Working in different countries and cultures is profoundly formative for a product manager. It forces you out of your comfort zone and builds deep [[concepts/empathy|empathy]], a critical skill for understanding diverse users and problems. Experiences like managing teams where you don't have all the answers teach you how to collaboratively find solutions and share the product ownership load.
