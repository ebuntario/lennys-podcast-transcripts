Engage with journalists on social media by commenting on their posts or sharing their work before you ever send a pitch. This makes your name familiar, so when your email arrives, they're more likely to open it. This pre-pitch [[concepts/relationship-building|relationship building]] demonstrates genuine interest and increases the perceived relevance of your outreach.
