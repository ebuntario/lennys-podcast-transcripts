When evaluating candidates, look for curiosity and range rather than specific domain expertise. People with diverse experiences and learning agility often outperform narrow specialists in dynamic environments. Their [[concepts/learning-ability|ability to adapt]] and connect disparate ideas makes them valuable in fast-changing organizations where today's solutions may not solve tomorrow's problems.
