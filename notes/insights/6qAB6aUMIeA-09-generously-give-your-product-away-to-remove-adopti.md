For new AI categories, aggressively giving your product away for free—beyond a standard [[concepts/freemium-model|freemium model]]—is a non-negotiable growth strategy. Treat LLM costs for free users and giveaways as marketing expenses, empowering users to become distribution agents through hackathons and events.
