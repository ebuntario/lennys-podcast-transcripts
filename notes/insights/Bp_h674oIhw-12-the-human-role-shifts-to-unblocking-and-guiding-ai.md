As AI agents handle more initial creation, the enduring human skill becomes the ability to unblock them when they encounter limits or errors. This involves [[concepts/debugging|debugging]] complex issues, understanding system architecture to guide improvements, and providing the creative direction that seeds the agent's work. Effective [[concepts/human-ai-collaboration|human-AI collaboration]] hinges on this facilitative role.
