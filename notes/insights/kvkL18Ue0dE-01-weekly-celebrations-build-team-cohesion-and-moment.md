Weekly celebrations where teams share their most awesome accomplishments create a sense of belonging and excitement. This simple ritual makes people feel part of something special and can improve team dynamics even before formal [[concepts/goal-setting|goal-setting]] processes are implemented. Regular recognition reinforces positive behaviors and builds momentum for achieving larger objectives.
