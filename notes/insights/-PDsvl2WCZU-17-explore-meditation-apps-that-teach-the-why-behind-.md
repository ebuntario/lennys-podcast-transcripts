For those skeptical of meditation, try apps like Waking Up that explain the underlying principles and neuroscience, making the practice more accessible to analytical minds. Understanding the "why" behind [[concepts/meditation|meditation]]—such as how it affects focus or emotional regulation—can increase commitment and effectiveness. Alternatively, tools like Aura offer on-demand, coach-led sessions tailored to specific moods or needs, fitting meditation into a busy schedule without requiring daily discipline.
