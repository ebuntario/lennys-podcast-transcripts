Pure communication tools may rely on direct network effects, but social networks and marketplaces must evolve to incorporate cross-side and [[concepts/data-network-effects|data network effects]] to grow and defend their position. For example, to monetize with ads or improve personalization, a social network must develop cross-side dynamics between creators/consumers and leverage user data.
