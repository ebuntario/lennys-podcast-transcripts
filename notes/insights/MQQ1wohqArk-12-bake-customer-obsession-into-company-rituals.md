Institutionalize customer connection through simple, regular rituals like a company-wide weekly email with raw customer feedback quotes and ratings. Combine this with easy access to research share-outs, in-product feedback tools, and community spaces for deeper dialogue. These rituals ensure [[concepts/customer-centricity|customer-centricity]] remains a daily touchstone for everyone, not just product teams.
