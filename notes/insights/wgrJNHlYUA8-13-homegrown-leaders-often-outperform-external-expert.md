Promoting internally cultivates leaders who understand the company's unique culture and communication style. While they may not be the "world's best" in a narrow skill, their deep [[concepts/organizational-alignment|alignment]] and ability to collaborate within the system often lead to greater collective impact. External hires must first listen and learn the existing culture before attempting to change it.
