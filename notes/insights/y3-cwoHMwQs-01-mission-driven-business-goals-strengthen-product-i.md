At mission-driven organizations like The New York Times, business goals serve the mission rather than the other way around. This means growing a [[concepts/subscription-business|subscription business]] exists to strengthen an informed democracy, not just to drive revenue. Product managers in this environment measure impact both through metrics like subscriber growth and through real-world outcomes like policy changes triggered by deeply reported stories.
