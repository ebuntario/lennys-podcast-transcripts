As you become more senior, the problems that reach you are fundamentally unsolvable in a perfect way. Your role is to choose the best suboptimal option, clearly communicate the principles behind the choice, and build the [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]] framework for the organization. Accepting this reality is key to effective [[concepts/leadership|leadership]].
