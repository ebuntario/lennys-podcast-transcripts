When a team has a shared, unambiguous understanding of what the product is for, it makes countless daily decisions instant and obvious. For example, Slack's early team held the line that it was "a tool for work," which settled debates about social features like blocking. This clarity in [[concepts/product-vision|product vision]] prevents scope creep and keeps the team aligned.
