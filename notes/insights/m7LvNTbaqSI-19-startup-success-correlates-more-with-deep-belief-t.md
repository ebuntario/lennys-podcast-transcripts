Successful founders come in diverse personality types—from quiet introverts to charismatic extroverts—with no single optimal profile. What unites them is an internal gravitational force of belief that they will succeed despite evidence to the contrary. This [[concepts/founder-conviction|founder conviction]] creates a reality-distortion field that convinces teams, investors, and eventually markets of their eventual success.
