Adopt a mindset where the goal is continuous growth and learning—"becoming"—rather than reaching a fixed state of "being." In product management, the moment you think you've mastered it is the moment you risk becoming obsolete. Embracing a [[concepts/growth-mindset|growth mindset]] ensures you continue to evolve and adapt alongside changing technologies and markets.
