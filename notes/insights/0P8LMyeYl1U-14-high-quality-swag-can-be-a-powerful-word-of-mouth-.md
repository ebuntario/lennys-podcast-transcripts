Investing in exceptional, desirable swag (like branded AirPods) for your champions can drive significant word-of-mouth. When people are excited to show off high-quality items, it sparks conversations and provides natural opportunities for them to evangelize your product. This tactic amplifies [[concepts/brand-advocacy|brand advocacy]] among your most valuable users.
