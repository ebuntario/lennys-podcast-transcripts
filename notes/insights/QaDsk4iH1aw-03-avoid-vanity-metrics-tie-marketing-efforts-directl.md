Clicks, views, and impressions are often "bullshit numbers" that don't reflect true impact. Effective [[concepts/marketing-metrics|marketing metrics]] should be tied to core business outcomes: signups for self-serve products, or sales-qualified leads, pipeline, and revenue for B2B companies. This focus ensures marketing channels are litigated based on their real contribution to growth.
