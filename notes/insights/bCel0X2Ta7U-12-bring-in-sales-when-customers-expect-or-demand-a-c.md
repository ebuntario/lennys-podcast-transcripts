Hire your first dedicated salesperson not just when you're maxed out, but when moving upmarket to customers who want to talk to someone before buying. Some customer segments have a strong preference for a sales interaction, and meeting that expectation is a key part of an effective [[concepts/go-to-market|go-to-market]] strategy for scaling product-led companies.
