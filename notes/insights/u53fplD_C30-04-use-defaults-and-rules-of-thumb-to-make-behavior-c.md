Defaults (like automatic enrollment) are the first line of defense for reducing [[concepts/decision-fatigue|cognitive effort]] in behavior change. When defaults aren't feasible, [[concepts/heuristics|rules of thumb]] (e.g., "I only take Lyft on weekends") simplify decisions by replacing complex cost-benefit analysis with clear, pre-committed guidelines.
