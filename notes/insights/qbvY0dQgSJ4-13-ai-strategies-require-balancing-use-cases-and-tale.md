A successful [[concepts/ai-strategy|AI strategy]] has two key pillars: identifying valuable use cases and securing the [[concepts/talent-development|talent]] to execute them. Companies often start by upskilling employees and providing tool access to build AI literacy, but must eventually measure concrete outcomes to justify continued investment.
