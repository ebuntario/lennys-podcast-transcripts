Long-term business planning is a fantasy because the further out you plan, the less you know about the future decisions you're making. Staying close to the present and figuring things out as you go allows for [[concepts/adaptability|adaptability]] and prevents being locked into obligations that may lead to unhappiness. This approach values current intuition over outdated plans.
