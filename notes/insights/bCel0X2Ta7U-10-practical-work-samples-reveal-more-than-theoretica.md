Asking candidates to complete a few hours of practical work related to the role reveals their problem-solving approach, communication style, and understanding of technical constraints. For a [[concepts/product-management|product management]] role, this shows how they structure a narrative, evaluate solutions, and think about measurement far better than abstract or theoretical questions.
