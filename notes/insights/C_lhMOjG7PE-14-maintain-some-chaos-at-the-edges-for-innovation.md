If you don't have 2-3 projects "on fire," you're probably not pushing hard enough or taking sufficient risks. Controlled chaos at the edges of an organization indicates healthy [[concepts/innovation-culture|innovation]] and stretching beyond comfort zones. This discomfort creates resilience and learning that stability cannot provide, preparing the organization for unexpected challenges.
