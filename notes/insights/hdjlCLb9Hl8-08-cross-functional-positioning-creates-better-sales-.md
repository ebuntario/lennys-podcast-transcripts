Involving product, marketing, and sales teams in [[concepts/positioning|positioning]] work yields stronger differentiated value and more effective pitches. Product teams understand technical capabilities, sales teams know buyer objections, and marketing teams can translate this into compelling messaging. This [[concepts/cross-functional-collaboration|cross-functional collaboration]] prevents sales from ignoring marketing-created materials.
