Avoid over-reliance on metrics; cultivate a strong [[concepts/product-sense|product intuition]] by deeply understanding your customers and their motivations. Ask questions, maintain a beginner's mind, and earn the right to place features prominently. A good product manager balances data with an intuitive feel for what feels right for the user.
