The first step to being an effective coach is having a solid, explicit definition of what a good product person looks like in your specific context. This compass should separate hard-to-develop personality traits from learnable skills and know-how. Many leaders have this definition implicitly but fail to reflect on and document it, which hinders structured [[concepts/coaching|coaching]] and development.
