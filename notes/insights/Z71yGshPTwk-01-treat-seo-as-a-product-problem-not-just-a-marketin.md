Effective [[concepts/seo-strategy|SEO strategy]] requires product thinking, not just marketing execution. Product managers should lead SEO efforts by understanding the user's self-discovery journey and creating assets that solve their problems mid-funnel. If you can't identify what a user would search for to find your solution, SEO likely isn't the right channel for your business.
