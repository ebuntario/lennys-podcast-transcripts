The most valuable part of weekly OKR updates isn't reporting progress but documenting what prevented achievement. Tracking "I tried this but failed because..." creates organizational learning about systemic obstacles. This [[concepts/transparency|transparency]] about challenges enables better problem-solving than simply reporting completion percentages without context.
