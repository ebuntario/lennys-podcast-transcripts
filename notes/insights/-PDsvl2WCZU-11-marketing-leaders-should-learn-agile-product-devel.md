Traditional marketing leaders can evolve into Growth CMOs by studying agile product development methodologies, even if they don't become product managers themselves. Resources like the book "Hacking Marketing" or courses from Reforge and Maven teach how to apply sprints, backlogs, and iterative cycles to marketing activities. This knowledge not only improves internal workflows but also enhances [[concepts/cross-functional-collaboration|collaboration with product teams]], breaking down silos between marketing and product.
