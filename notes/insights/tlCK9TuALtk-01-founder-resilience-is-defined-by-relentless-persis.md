Every successful business has been rejected by multiple venture capitalists. The defining trait of a true [[concepts/founder-mindset|founder]] is the drive to keep going despite hearing "no" from everyone, even when facing a weeks-of-runway situation. It only takes one "yes," but you must persist without question.
