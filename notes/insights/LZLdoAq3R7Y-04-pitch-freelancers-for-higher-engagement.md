Freelance writers are often hungrier for stories than staff journalists because they rely on pitching to get paid. They receive fewer pitches and are more likely to read and seriously consider a relevant story. Targeting [[concepts/freelancer-pitching|freelancer pitching]] can increase your chances of engagement, as they are incentivized to find and develop compelling narratives.
