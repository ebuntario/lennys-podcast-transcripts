Breakthrough innovation requires a culture that encourages big, ambitious bets (10X thinking) rather than just incremental improvements. Leaders must create environments where teams feel safe to propose and try [[concepts/innovation|innovative ideas]] that might fail. While portfolio balance is needed, consistently thinking too small stifles potential and mirrors historical failures like Kodak's missed digital camera opportunity.
