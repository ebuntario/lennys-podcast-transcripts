You can begin developing [[concepts/product-sense|product sense]] and PM skills immediately by critically analyzing your favorite apps and identifying their top problems. Practice designing solutions in your head or on paper to train your intuition. When a formal opportunity arises, you will be prepared because you've already done the foundational work.
