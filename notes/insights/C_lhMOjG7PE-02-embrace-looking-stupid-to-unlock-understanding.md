Asking "stupid" questions publicly can be a superpower because it accelerates collective understanding. Many people have the same question but are afraid to ask, so voicing it breaks the ice and clarifies confusion for everyone. The goal is not to annoy but to deeply [[concepts/psychological-safety|understand the content]], even if it means facing occasional blunt feedback.
