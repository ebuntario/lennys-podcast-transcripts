The Chief Product Officer is responsible for facilitating the development of products that deliver value for customers, which in turn translates into value for the business. This involves defining and improving the [[concepts/organizational-product-strategy|strategy]] and execution processes, removing organizational bottlenecks, and ensuring the rest of the company understands and can participate in the product development cycle. Success is measured by improving the conversion rate and magnitude of impact from the product portfolio over time.
