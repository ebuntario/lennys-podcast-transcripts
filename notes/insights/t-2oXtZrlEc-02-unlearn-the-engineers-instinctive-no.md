Engineers often develop a reflex to say "no" to new ideas due to the maintenance burden of past half-baked projects. To cultivate a [[concepts/product-mindset|product mindset]], sincerely try to make an idea work for 10 minutes before rejecting it. Documenting the attempt to make "yes" work leads to better evaluation and preserves potentially high-impact directions.
