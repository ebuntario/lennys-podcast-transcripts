[[concepts/product-onboarding|Product onboarding]] is frequently an under-optimized "no man's land" because ownership is unclear between marketing, product, and customer success teams. For product-led or freemium models, marketing should often own onboarding as a key tool for driving activation and value realization, requiring clear [[concepts/cross-functional-alignment|cross-functional alignment]].
