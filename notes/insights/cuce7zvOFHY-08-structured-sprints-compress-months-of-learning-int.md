Running back-to-back [[concepts/design-sprint|Design Sprints]] after a Foundation Sprint allows teams to build and test multiple prototypes with real customers in just 3-4 weeks. Founders report this process condenses what would typically take 3-4 months of scattered building, launching, and iterating. The structured, focused approach creates an intense [[concepts/iterative-learning|learning velocity]] that dramatically accelerates the path to validation or pivot.
