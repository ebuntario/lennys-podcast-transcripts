Create content that deeply resonates with a specific audience rather than trying to appeal broadly with mediocre appeal. This [[concepts/niche-positioning|niche positioning]] builds passionate advocates who become your most effective promoters. When you focus on being someone's favorite show, you cultivate the "1000 true fans" who drive sustainable growth through loyalty and sharing.
