Research indicates that bringing distributed teams together intentionally, even just a few times a year, can boost both connection and productivity by about 30%. These gatherings should combine focused work sessions, collaborative workshops, and social activities to rebuild relationships and momentum. This structured approach to [[concepts/remote-work|remote work]] recognizes that periodic, high-quality in-person interaction sustains team cohesion for months.
