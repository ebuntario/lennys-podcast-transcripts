Your "how to win" must be supported by capabilities that competitors either cannot or will not replicate. A true [[concepts/competitive-moat|competitive advantage]] often comes from complex, multifaceted systems—like unique hiring practices or deep cumulative expertise—that make imitation prohibitively difficult or unattractive.
