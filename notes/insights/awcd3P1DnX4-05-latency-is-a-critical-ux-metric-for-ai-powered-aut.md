For AI-powered in-editor suggestions, latency directly impacts the developer's ability to stay in flow. Experiments showed the optimal response time is around 200 milliseconds. Exceeding this threshold makes the tool feel interruptive, breaking concentration and reducing its utility as a [[concepts/flow-state-enabler|flow state enabler]].
