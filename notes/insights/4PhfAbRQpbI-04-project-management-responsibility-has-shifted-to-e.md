A significant shift in the last decade is that the responsibility for day-to-day [[concepts/project-management|project management]] (e.g., ensuring sprint goals are met) has largely moved from product managers and scrum masters to engineering managers. This evolution requires explicit discussion in the roles and responsibilities exercise to ensure accountability is clear and the team's operational cadence is maintained.
