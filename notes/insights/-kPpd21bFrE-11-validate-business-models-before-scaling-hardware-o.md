Early in his career, Dmitry learned to [[concepts/validate-before-scaling|validate business models before scaling]] after building hardware for a cinema ticketing system that failed due to premature investment. The lesson: stay lean, prove customer adoption first, and avoid capital-intensive solutions until the model is validated, even when building products you personally love.
