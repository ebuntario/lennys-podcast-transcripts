Teams often disagree on what [[concepts/product-metrics|engagement]] means, leading to misaligned product efforts. To solve this, define engagement as an uncomfortably specific user action (e.g., "booking a doctor's appointment within 24 hours of signup") that correlates with long-term retention and provides immediate user value.
