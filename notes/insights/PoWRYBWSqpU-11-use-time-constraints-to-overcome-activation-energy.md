Paradoxically, giving yourself less time to complete a small task can make it easier by increasing focus and overcoming [[concepts/activation-energy|activation energy]]. Structure your day with a series of quick, completable tasks to build momentum through small bursts of accomplishment energy. This [[concepts/productivity-hacks|hack]] leverages the psychology of starting and finishing rather than battling procrastination.
