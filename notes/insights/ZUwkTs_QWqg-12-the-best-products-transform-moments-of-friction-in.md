Exceptional products go beyond merely solving a pain point; they re-frame a necessary but annoying task into a positive experience. An example is a children's toothbrush that turns two minutes of brushing into an interactive game. This principle of [[concepts/user-delight|creating delight]] is a hallmark of great product design, moving users from tolerance to active enjoyment.
