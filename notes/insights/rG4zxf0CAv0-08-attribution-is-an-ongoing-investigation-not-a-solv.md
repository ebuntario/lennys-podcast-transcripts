No single attribution model is a universal truth; it requires continuous validation through multiple lenses. Combine cookie-based tracking with statistical modeling, surveys, and [[concepts/attribution-modeling|media mix modeling]] to triangulate performance. Treat attribution as an evolving investigation rather than a one-time setup.
