[[concepts/community-led-growth|Community-led growth]] creates ubiquity and name recognition, which de-risks a product for larger companies considering adoption. When people see a tool discussed on social media, podcasts, and by friends, it builds legitimacy and reduces perceived risk, paving the way for moving upmarket into the enterprise.
