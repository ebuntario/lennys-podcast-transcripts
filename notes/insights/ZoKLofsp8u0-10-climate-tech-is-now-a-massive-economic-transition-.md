Governments and corporations have committed to decarbonizing, moving trillions of dollars from old systems to new ones. This represents a larger economic transition than the software boom. Startups in [[concepts/climate-tech|climate tech]] are now driven by strong customer demand from corporations needing to meet decarbonization pledges, making it a tremendous business opportunity, not just a philanthropic one.
