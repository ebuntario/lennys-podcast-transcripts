People can typically only give to others what has been given to them or what they have experienced. Many leaders provide poor [[concepts/coaching|coaching]] because they have never been effectively coached themselves and lack alternative models. To become a good coach, you must first observe good coaching in action and practice the techniques in low-risk environments.
