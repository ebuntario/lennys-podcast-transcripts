The most valuable advice you can give is often what people don't want to hear. As a leader or investor, prioritize being respectfully truthful over being immediately liked. While telling hard truths may cause short-term discomfort, it builds long-term [[concepts/leadership|respect]] and can save the company, whereas pleasing people in the moment often leads to worse outcomes.
