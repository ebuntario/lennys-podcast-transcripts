A product manager's role is akin to a conductor leading an orchestra, ensuring each function (engineering, design, legal, etc.) plays its part in harmony. The PM's job is to unify the team toward a shared goal and maintain the right tempo, not to be the star of the show. This [[concepts/team-leadership|team leadership]] approach emphasizes elevating experts and coordinating their efforts seamlessly.
