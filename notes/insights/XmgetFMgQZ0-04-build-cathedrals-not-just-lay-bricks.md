Teams perform better when they feel they are building a cathedral, not just laying bricks. It's crucial for leaders to help teams see the broader vision and how their work contributes. Use multiple formats—write-ups, metrics, directional mocks—to show different facets of the [[concepts/vision-communication|cathedral]] so everyone can see their part in it.
