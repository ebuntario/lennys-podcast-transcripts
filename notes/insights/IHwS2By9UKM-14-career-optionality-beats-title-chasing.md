Prioritize [[concepts/career-optionality|career optionality]] over specific titles by evaluating opportunities based on whether they expand your future choices. [[concepts/non-linear-careers|Non-linear careers]] (advising, contracting, content creation) often provide greater fulfillment than traditional corporate ladders. Build depth through full-time roles first, then diversify your monetization models.
