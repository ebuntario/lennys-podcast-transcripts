For a SaaS business, a basic [[concepts/growth-model|growth model]] consists of three components: acquisition channels (traffic sources and conversion), retention (activation and survival rates), and monetization (revenue per customer). Modeling these forces an understanding of how the business works and reveals the non-linear interactions between them, such as how retained customers fuel virality or fund reinvestment in paid marketing.
