You cannot improve at speaking without speaking, just as you can't learn to cook only from books. Games like Conductor or Triple Step provide short reps with immediate feedback, creating intentional "turbulence" to highlight areas for growth. This [[concepts/deliberate-practice|deliberate practice]] in a low-stakes environment builds the ability to recover gracefully from mistakes, which is the mark of a pro.
