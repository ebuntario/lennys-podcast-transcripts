During a crisis like COVID-19, planning cycles must shrink to react to rapidly changing data—Airbnb moved to two-week planning sprints. Dissolve sub-teams and operate as one unified company, moving talent to the most critical problems. Leadership must provide calm, honest communication about uncertainty while pivoting the entire organization toward survival.
