While most early-stage startups hire engineers and PMs first, a multi-method researcher can provide significant leverage. During critical phases like a [[concepts/product-pivot|product pivot]], a researcher gathers evidence that reduces guesswork and moral luck, helping founders make more [[concepts/evidence-based-decisions|evidence-based decisions]]. A single researcher in the first 10-20 employees can accelerate learning and de-risk major strategic bets.
