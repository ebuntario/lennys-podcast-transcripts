An early monetization model (e.g., taking a percentage of all transactions) can create future problems like disintermediation, where matched parties transact off-platform. As a platform's value shifts from initial trust-building to ongoing matchmaking, the fee structure may need to evolve. Founders should avoid overcommitting to early models that limit future [[concepts/platform-strategy|strategic flexibility]].
