Individual pieces of feedback, like tweets, should be treated as signals rather than direct mandates. They act as a "canary in the coal mine" indicating a potential larger issue. A product manager must balance this vocal minority with a broader portfolio of [[concepts/customer-feedback|customer feedback]] sources to avoid blind spots and make informed decisions.
