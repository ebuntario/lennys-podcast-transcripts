In a [[concepts/marketplace-dynamics|two-sided marketplace]], prioritizing the supply side is often the key to unlocking growth. If users open an app and find no available suppliers—like drivers or creators—the demand side will not return, regardless of marketing or user experience. This makes solving real pain points for suppliers a foundational step for the entire business to operate.
