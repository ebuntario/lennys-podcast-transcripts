Early career setbacks (job losses, visa issues, failed experiments) feel catastrophic but become minor footnotes in a longer trajectory. Most people underestimate how much time they have for multiple career chapters and transformations. This perspective helps maintain [[concepts/resilience|resilience]] during difficult periods and encourages [[concepts/long-term-thinking|long-term thinking]] about professional development.
