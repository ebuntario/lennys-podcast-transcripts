You cannot spec your way to a great product; building requires endless [[concepts/product-innovation|iteration]] based on customer feedback. A technical co-founder deeply understands this process and makes better technical decisions. Non-technical founders must learn to value engineering as the core hard work, not just an execution service for their idea.
