When evaluating a recruiter, provide your role requirements and ask them to recite back their understanding and suggest a few candidate ideas on the spot. This tests their listening skills, market knowledge, and ability to calibrate quickly. A good recruiter should demonstrate immediate, thoughtful [[concepts/talent-calibration|talent calibration]].
