The simplest way to create value is to solve a problem, which is why founders should [[concepts/problem-focus|fall in love with the problem]] first. This problem serves as your North Star, guiding decisions and reducing deviations from the course. When you lead with the problem, your story becomes more compelling because customers care about the value you create for them, not just your solution.
