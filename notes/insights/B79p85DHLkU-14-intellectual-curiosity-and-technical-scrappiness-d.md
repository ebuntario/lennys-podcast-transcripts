Top marketing technologists demonstrate both intellectual curiosity about new tools and technical scrappiness to solve problems independently. They don't need formal engineering degrees but should understand JavaScript, Python, and API documentation well enough to prototype solutions. This combination of [[concepts/learning]] and practical technical skills enables them to navigate the constantly evolving marketing technology landscape effectively.
