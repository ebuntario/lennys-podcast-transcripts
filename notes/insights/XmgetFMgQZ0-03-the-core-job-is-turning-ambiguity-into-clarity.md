The unifying thesis for product people is that their core job is to turn ambiguity into clarity. Everything from defining the problem and target customer to prototyping solutions starts with ambiguity. Great product managers excel at spotting this ambiguity and systematically creating [[concepts/decision-making|clarity]] for their teams.
