When assessing talent, the best predictor of future performance is past performance, specifically looking for repeated [[concepts/signs-of-excellence|signs of excellence]]. Top performers typically have a history of multiple outstanding achievements, whether professional or personal, that demonstrate grit, drive, and the ability to succeed. A strong signal is when a former boss leaves a company and later returns specifically to recruit that person, putting their own reputation on the line.
