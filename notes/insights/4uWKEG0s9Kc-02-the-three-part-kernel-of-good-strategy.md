A robust strategy consists of three core elements. First, a [[concepts/diagnosis|diagnosis]] that explains the nature of the challenge and what makes it hard. Second, a [[concepts/guiding-policy|guiding policy]] that outlines the overall approach for dealing with the challenge. Third, a set of [[concepts/coherent-actions|coherent actions]] designed to implement the policy, which must not be self-contradictory.
