Founders must objectively analyze the effort required to close their first 10-20 customers. If those customers need hand-holding, security answers, or custom onboarding, that indicates a sales-led motion is necessary. Ignoring this reality because you dislike sales can lead to failure, as the initial customer cohort often defines the DNA of the business.
