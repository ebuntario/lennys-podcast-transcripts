Product operations is not a solution for product managers who lack core skills; that requires training and coaching. Instead, it addresses organizational and systemic challenges that hinder skilled PMs, such as disjointed data access, inconsistent processes, or poor cross-functional communication. It applies [[concepts/systems-thinking|systems thinking]] to remove these barriers so talented teams can perform at their best.
