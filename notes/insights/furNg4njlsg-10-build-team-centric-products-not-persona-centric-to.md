Miro's core differentiation is architecting for cross-functional teams rather than individual personas. Since innovation happens at the intersection of diverse roles, products designed for [[concepts/team-centric-design|team workflows]] create unique value that single-persona tools struggle to replicate. This shapes everything from feature prioritization to platform capabilities.
