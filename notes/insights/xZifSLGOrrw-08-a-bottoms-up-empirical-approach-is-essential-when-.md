When building in a rapidly evolving field like AI, where future capabilities and user behaviors are uncertain, a truly bottoms-up, empirical approach is critical. Instead of extensive upfront planning, teams should "aim fuzzily" at long-term visions but prioritize learning through rapid experimentation and iteration on shorter time horizons. This requires an [[concepts/organizational-structure|organizational structure]] that grants high individual autonomy to try things quickly.
