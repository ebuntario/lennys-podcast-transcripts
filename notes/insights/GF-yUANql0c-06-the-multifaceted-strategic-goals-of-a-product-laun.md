A product launch should be evaluated against specific strategic goals beyond just customer acquisition, such as recruiting, fundraising, gathering feedback, or securing partnerships. An often-overlooked benefit is [[concepts/team-morale|boosting team morale]], as a public launch allows the team to celebrate their work and share their accomplishment, creating a unifying moment.
