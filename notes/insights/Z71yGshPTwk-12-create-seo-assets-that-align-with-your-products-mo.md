SEO content should directly support your [[concepts/product-led-growth|product's monetization path]], not just attract traffic. Zapier's integration pages and Canva's templates work because they lead users toward product usage and conversion. Don't copy programmatic SEO tactics without ensuring they drive users toward your specific business model.
