Hire your first marketer when you have some semblance of [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] and need to accelerate one-to-many growth. For product-led growth companies, this hire may come earlier than for top-down sales models, as marketing replaces the initial sales function. The key test is whether a marketer would have impactful work to do and whether the product can handle the influx of users they would drive.
