[[concepts/ai-evaluation|Evals]] are not just for unit testing in CI/CD. Their highest value comes from running them on sampled production traces regularly (e.g., daily or weekly). This provides a continuous, specific measure of application quality in the real world and creates a flywheel for [[concepts/continuous-improvement|continuous improvement]].
