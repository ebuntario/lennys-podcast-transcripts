When assessing whether to stay in or leave a role, evaluate three key dimensions: the steepness of your learning curve, the health of your environment (including advocates and resources), and how interesting you find the core problem. Focusing on acquiring skills and experiences, rather than specific titles or companies, provides a more flexible and fulfilling framework for long-term [[concepts/professional-growth|career development]].
