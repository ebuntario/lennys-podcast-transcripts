Great products start with a clear intentionality—a vision of the world you want to create for customers. At Instagram, Kevin Systrom insisted Stories be real-time, ignoring data suggesting users wanted to share old photos. Use A/B testing to navigate toward your intended destination, not to determine the destination itself. This [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] avoids designing by committee and builds conviction.
