Safeguard focused work time by aggressively [[concepts/time-management|time-blocking]] your calendar. Implement simple team communication protocols, like tagging messages in Slack with "no rush" or "EOD," to signal urgency without forcing constant context-switching. Regularly audit time tracking data to adjust schedules and improve [[concepts/productivity|productivity]].
