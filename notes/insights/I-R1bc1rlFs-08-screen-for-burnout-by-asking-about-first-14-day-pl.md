A key interview question for any sales or product leadership role is: "What do you want to do in your first 14 days?" If the answer isn't centered on meeting customers or joining sales calls, it's a major red flag. Many experienced candidates are burnt out and prefer to focus on process over customers, which is detrimental for a growing company.
