Traits often seen as weaknesses, like being "too quiet" or having a [[concepts/neurodiversity|neurodivergent]] thinking style, can be powerful strengths when understood and applied correctly. For example, deep listening is a leadership asset, and spatial thinking common in dyslexia fuels visionary leadership.
