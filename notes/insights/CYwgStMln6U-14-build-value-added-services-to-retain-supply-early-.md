An effective early-stage tactic for [[concepts/supply-retention|retaining supply]] is to build a compelling basket of value-added services that make your platform indispensable. OpenTable did this successfully with restaurant management tools. By providing unique services beyond mere matchmaking, you create a stronger [[concepts/customer-value-proposition|value proposition]] that locks in supply and differentiates your marketplace.
