Combat [[concepts/procrastination|productive procrastination]] by identifying the one critical task for the day and committing to just five minutes of work on it first. This small start often builds momentum, turning minutes into significant progress. The technique breaks the mental barrier of beginning a daunting task and ensures priority items get attention.
