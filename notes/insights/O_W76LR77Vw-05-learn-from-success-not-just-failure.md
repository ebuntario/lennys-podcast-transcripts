The common trope of "learning from your mistakes" has little substance compared to learning from your successes. Seeing how things are done right is far more informative than analyzing failures. Therefore, a key [[concepts/career-advice|career heuristic]] is to join winning teams where you can observe and internalize the patterns of success.
