True [[concepts/product-led-growth|product-led growth]] often works by empowering passionate internal users to become champions within their companies. The role of sales and product teams is to equip these champions with data, stories, and connections to help them make a stronger case for adoption. Growth is fueled by enabling human connections and advocacy, not just top-down selling.
