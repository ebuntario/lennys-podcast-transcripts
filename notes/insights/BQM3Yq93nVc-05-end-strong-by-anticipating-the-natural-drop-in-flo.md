As you near the end of a speech, you naturally regain self-awareness and uncertainty can creep in. Use summary prompts like "So to wrap up..." or "My point here is..." to cue your brain to finish strongly. Landing the plane confidently ensures your audience remembers the entire message positively, not a weak ending.
