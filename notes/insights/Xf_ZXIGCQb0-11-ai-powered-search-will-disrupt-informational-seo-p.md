The integration of AI answers directly into search results (like Google's SGE or ChatGPT) represents the biggest shift in [[concepts/seo-strategy|SEO]] in over a decade. Informational queries are most at risk, as answers will be provided in the search result, reducing click-throughs to websites. Growth teams must start planning for this change, which may shift traffic from organic to paid or require new optimization strategies for AI platforms.
