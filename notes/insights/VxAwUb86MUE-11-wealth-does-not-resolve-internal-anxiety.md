Achieving financial success does not eliminate underlying anxiety or dissatisfaction; it often just changes the peer group you compare yourself to. The "anxiety loop" is a chemical reaction based on your psychology and past, not your external circumstances. Addressing internal mental health through professional help or medication can be more impactful for [[concepts/happiness|happiness]] than pursuing ever-larger financial goals.
