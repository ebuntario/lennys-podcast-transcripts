For platform teams, balance is dynamic: one sprint may focus on high-priority features, while the next addresses optimizations and bug fixes. Critical production incidents always take precedence. Effective [[concepts/prioritization|prioritization]] in this context relies on intuitive decision-making informed by clear goals and an understanding of user impact.
