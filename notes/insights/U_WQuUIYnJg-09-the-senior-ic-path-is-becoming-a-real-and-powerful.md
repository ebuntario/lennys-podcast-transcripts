The industry is correcting a long-standing bug where product managers felt compelled to move into management. The senior [[concepts/individual-contributor|individual contributor (IC)]] path is becoming a solidified and respected track, similar to engineering and design. For builders who love deep craft, domain expertise, or solving specific ambiguities, this path offers a way to advance without managing people and is increasingly valued by companies needing deep specialists.
