When using generative AI tools like ChatGPT, avoid starting with a blank slate and accepting the first output as truth. This leads to poor direction and wasted optimization. Instead, think through your problem and draft your answer first, then use AI to challenge, improve, and tighten your argument. This approach leverages AI as a [[concepts/ai-productivity|thought partner]] while preserving critical human judgment.
