Marketing technology teams should automate permissioning systems based on roles, experience, and departments rather than manually approving individual requests. This prevents security incidents like accidental mass emails while scaling administration efficiently. Automated [[concepts/access-control]] systems consider tenure, department, and role to determine appropriate tool access, reducing administrative overhead and improving security posture.
