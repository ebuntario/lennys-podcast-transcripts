AI's ultimate effect on society is not predetermined; it is shaped by human choices and actions. While technology generally serves as a net positive for civilization, it remains a [[concepts/technology-ethics|double-edged sword]] that requires careful stewardship. The responsibility lies with individuals and communities to guide its development and application toward beneficial outcomes.
