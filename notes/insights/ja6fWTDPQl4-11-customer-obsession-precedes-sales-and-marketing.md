For over a year, Mercor had no sales or marketing team, focusing 100% of resources on building exceptional product experiences. This intense [[concepts/customer-obsession|customer obsession]] created word-of-mouth growth where customers from one business wanted to work with them at their next. Founders should prioritize creating six-star customer experiences before investing heavily in go-to-market functions.
