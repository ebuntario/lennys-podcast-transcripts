It is crucial to become known for a particular area of excellence, such as shepherding complex launches or solving technically difficult problems. When you establish this [[concepts/reputation-building|reputation]], you can crush the projects you are given, which in turn leads to more responsibility. People naturally give more responsibility to those known for being excellent at something.
