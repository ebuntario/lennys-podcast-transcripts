To achieve ambitious outcomes, the individual responsible for execution must go deep enough to understand all details and constraints firsthand. They cannot stop at second-hand explanations like "because X person said"; they must uncover the root cause, such as a contractual obligation or a specific regulatory consequence. This [[concepts/ownership|deep ownership]] and [[concepts/first-principles-thinking|first-principles thinking]] is essential for breaking through assumptions and creating better user experiences.
