The parable of the fisherman highlights the importance of aligning your long-term goals with your current actions. It questions the logic of spending years building a business solely to eventually afford the lifestyle you could have now. This framework encourages evaluating whether a [[concepts/career-planning|career move]] is genuinely a step toward your desired life or an unnecessary detour driven by societal expectations.
