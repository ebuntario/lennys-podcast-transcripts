There is no substitute for actively using a wide variety of products to develop strong [[concepts/product-intuition|product intuition]]. By being a curious and hungry consumer, you build muscle memory for what feels good, what solves problems elegantly, and what patterns work. This practice helps you discern between superficial trends and meaningful innovations.
