Effective product strategy requires a portfolio approach that balances refining the core for reliable growth with investing in speculative bets that add new capabilities. While optimizing existing features (like improving onboarding or recommendations) drives steady metrics, you must also allocate resources to projects that might hurt short-term metrics but enable future [[concepts/product-evolution|product evolution]] and new forms of engagement.
