Rapid technological change makes traditional multi-layered, multi-functional product development insufficient. The [[concepts/full-stack-builder]]—a polymath who can handle strategy, rewards design, UI, and system architecture—enables faster iteration cycles. This role focuses on the complete product loop rather than staying in a single functional lane, increasing team velocity and metabolic rate.
