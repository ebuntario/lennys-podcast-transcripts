As PMs become more senior, they often shift focus from controllable elements (their craft, outputs, learning) to uncontrollable factors (organizational decisions, stakeholder behaviors, project assignments). Maintain focus on improving skills within your control—data analysis, communication, strategy development—rather than obsessing over external factors. This [[concepts/mindset-shift|mindset shift]] prevents career stagnation and keeps you on a growth trajectory.
