Product leaders aiming for senior roles should prioritize breadth—working on platform, core product, and specialized areas like growth or monetization. This [[concepts/skill-diversification|skill diversification]] provides a holistic understanding and makes you adaptable. Variety in experience across different company stages also builds a more robust skill set.
