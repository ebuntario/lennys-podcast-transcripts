The most important and most frequently overlooked risk in product development is [[concepts/customer-value-creation|value]]. Teams often assume value because they are given a roadmap of features to deliver, which negates the need for a product manager's core question: "Should we build this?" Simply because users can use a product does not mean they will buy, choose, or actually use it.
