Technical correctness or being right is secondary to building trust and demonstrating genuine care for others' outcomes and incentives. Effective [[concepts/influence|influence]] and partnership are built on this foundation of trust. Once people believe you care about their success, they become open to your ideas and collaboration, enabling real progress more than any logical argument made in isolation.
