Be flexible with your announcement date to secure coverage from your first-choice reporter. Getting the right story in the right publication drives more value than hitting an arbitrary internal deadline. Start working with an agency or on your [[concepts/media-outreach|outreach]] about six weeks before a planned announcement to allow time for pitching and coordination.
