A [[concepts/growth-team|growth team]] cannot fix a slowing business caused by core product or go-to-market issues. Growth amplifies existing momentum but cannot reverse fundamental decline; hiring a growth head during a downturn often leads to failure. First, stabilize the business trajectory through product and marketing fixes before layering on growth optimization.
