Co-founders should agree on a [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]] protocol for disagreements before they arise. Options include deferring to the person who cares most, the one with the most expertise, or taking turns. Having this pre-established [[concepts/conflict-management|conflict management]] system transforms inevitable disagreements from emotional battles into structured, productive discussions, preserving the working relationship.
