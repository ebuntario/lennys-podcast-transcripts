High [[concepts/talent-density|talent density]] is the foundational prerequisite for other cultural elements like [[concepts/radical-candor|candor]] and freedom. Without a team of exceptional individuals, it becomes dangerous to operate with minimal process and high levels of direct feedback. This density allows for a culture where people can thrive on autonomy and continuous improvement.
