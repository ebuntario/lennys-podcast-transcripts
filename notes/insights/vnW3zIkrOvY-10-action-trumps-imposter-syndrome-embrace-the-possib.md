Instead of trying to eliminate [[concepts/imposter-syndrome|imposter syndrome]], accept it as a common feeling and proceed anyway. The mindset of "I might fail, but let them be the ones to reject me" liberates you to take career risks. This approach prioritizes opportunity and learning over the paralysis of perceived inadequacy.
