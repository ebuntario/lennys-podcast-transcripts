When pushing back on ideas or navigating difficult conversations, effective product managers transition discussions from emotional territory to logical frameworks. This creates space for more equitable dialogue and better decision-making. While occasional emotional intensity can be useful for urgency, most product discussions benefit from this [[concepts/conflict-resolution|conflict resolution]] approach that maintains relationships while advancing the work.
