The most effective product descriptions and launch copy use the language customers would use to describe the product to a friend. Avoiding buzzwords and [[concepts/authenticity|PR-speak]] in favor of simple, human language makes the value proposition clearer and more relatable, which is critical for capturing attention in a crowded space.
