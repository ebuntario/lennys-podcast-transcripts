The maturity of OKR implementation evolves through phases: starting with no clear OKRs, moving to siloed department OKRs, and finally achieving tightly integrated company-wide OKRs. The most effective state involves clear company-level objectives with department OKRs supporting them, enabling dependency mapping and ensuring all organizational levers are pulled to drive the most important objectives.
