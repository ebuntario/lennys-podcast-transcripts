Frameworks like SAFe often separate tactical product owners from strategic product managers, reporting into different chains. This prevents product owners from gaining strategic experience and blocks a natural [[concepts/professional-growth|career progression]] into product leadership. Companies should unify titles and create a single competency ladder for product roles.
