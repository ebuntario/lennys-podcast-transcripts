Being perceived as "kooky" or unserious can be a strength, as it allows you to operate outside rigid organizational norms and entertain unconventional ideas. This posture fosters [[concepts/open-mindedness|open-mindedness]] and makes you more receptive to [[concepts/disconfirming-evidence|disconfirming evidence]], which is essential for strength and adaptation. Managing your [[concepts/ego-management|ego]] to welcome challenging feedback is a key component of [[concepts/cognitive-flexibility|cognitive flexibility]].
