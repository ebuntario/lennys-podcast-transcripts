In the volatile [[concepts/messy-middle|messy middle]] of a venture, teams need to see progress to stay motivated. Leaders must act as stewards of the narrative, merchandising small wins and milestones even if they seem minor in the grand scheme. Setting micro-goals and celebrating them short-circuits the brain's need for constant reward, helping the team endure periods of anonymity, uncertainty, and anxiety.
