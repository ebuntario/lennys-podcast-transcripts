To quickly improve a skill like strategy or communication, find and study exemplary artifacts (e.g., strategy docs, executive emails) from respected colleagues. Reverse-engineer them by identifying the core questions they answer, their structure, and what makes them effective. This [[concepts/learning-frameworks|active deconstruction]] is faster than passive reading and reveals patterns of excellence.
