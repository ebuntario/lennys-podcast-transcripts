You will receive more feedback than you can possibly address. To [[concepts/prioritization|prioritize]] effectively, anchor on what the company needs most at that moment. Categorize feedback into "must-haves" critical for business success and "nice-to-haves." Focus your energy on the must-haves that enable your team to deliver on core [[concepts/strategic-alignment|strategic objectives]], even if other valid feedback must wait.
