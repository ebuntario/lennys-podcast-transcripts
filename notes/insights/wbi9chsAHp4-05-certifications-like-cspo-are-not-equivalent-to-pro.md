Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) credentials often come from two-day classes that teach process mechanics but not product discovery or strategy. Many consulting firms profit from selling these certifications as part of an "agile industrial complex." Top tech companies prioritize demonstrated experience and outcome-based skills over [[concepts/certifications|certifications]] when hiring product talent.
