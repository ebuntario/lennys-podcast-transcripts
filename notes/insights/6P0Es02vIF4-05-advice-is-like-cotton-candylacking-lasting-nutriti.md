Giving advice often provides a temporary sugar high but lacks lasting impact because it may not address the real problem or be relevant to the recipient's context. True growth comes from helping individuals figure out their own path through [[concepts/coaching|coaching]], which fosters internal discovery and sustainable change. What worked at one company may not apply elsewhere, making self-derived solutions more powerful.
