The goal of effective speaking is to enter and maintain a [[concepts/flow-state|flow state]], where you are focused on conveying ideas rather than the mechanics of speaking. This mindset shift means addressing root causes like discomfort with pausing or lack of trust in your emotions, rather than symptoms like filler words. Solving these deeper issues frees up mental space and allows for more natural communication.
