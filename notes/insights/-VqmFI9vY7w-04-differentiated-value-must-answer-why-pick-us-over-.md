Your [[concepts/differentiated-value|differentiated value]] should clearly articulate benefits that only your solution can deliver, derived from unique capabilities. Start by identifying what customers would do if you didn't exist, then map your distinctive features to the specific value they create. This value proposition becomes the core of your sales pitch after establishing market context.
