Instead of asking open-ended questions to understand someone's thinking, use "magic questions": state your assumption as a fact and end with "Is that right?" or "Do you agree?" This technique, like saying "If we did X, would that be okay?" helps you [[concepts/communication-skills|calibrate your understanding]] by seeing what they affirm or reject, revealing their mental model faster than direct explanation.
