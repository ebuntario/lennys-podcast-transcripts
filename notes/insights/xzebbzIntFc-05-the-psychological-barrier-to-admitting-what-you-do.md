The most common reason founders struggle to define an MVP is an upstream reluctance to admit what they don't know. [[concepts/founder-mindset|Founder psychology]] often resists stating, "We want to test to find out what customers want," because it feels like admitting uncertainty. However, embracing this uncertainty is the prerequisite for effective [[concepts/testing|experimentation]] and learning.
