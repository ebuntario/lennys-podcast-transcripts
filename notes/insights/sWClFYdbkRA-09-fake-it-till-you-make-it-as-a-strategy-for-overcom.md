"Fake it till you make it" doesn't mean lying, but rather saying yes to opportunities slightly beyond your current capabilities and figuring them out along the way. This approach helps overcome [[concepts/imposter-syndrome|imposter syndrome]] by creating room for growth and learning through experience. It's particularly valuable early in careers when formal qualifications may be lacking.
