The most effective way to create a [[concepts/word-of-mouth|word-of-mouth]] loop is to build a product that "blows their socks off" and creates empowering stories users want to share. This involves prioritizing "love marks" and unique interactions in the product to build a feeling that the software is speaking to the user personally.
