A simple but effective tactic for maintaining focus is to use technical blocks, like editing your computer's host file, to physically prevent access to distracting websites. The initial resistance is overcome after a few minutes, leading to a state of [[concepts/deep-work|deep work]]. This method is more effective than willpower alone because it removes the choice entirely.
