An acquisition should be approached like a fundraising round, with a deliberate funnel of potential buyers. Starting conversations early under the context of partnership or vision-sharing, rather than an outright sale, builds [[concepts/networking|critical relationships]]. This preparation makes it more likely that an acquirer will think of you when they have a strategic need.
