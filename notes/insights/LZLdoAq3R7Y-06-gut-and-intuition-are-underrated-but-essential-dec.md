Every business decision is ultimately a judgment call, where [[concepts/gut-instinct|gut and intuition]] absorb thousands of known and unknown factors. While data can be an input, it's rarely the sole decider; experienced judgment is what companies look for in executives. Giving your intuition credit and asking "how does this feel?" can lead to more resonant and effective outcomes than purely analytical approaches.
