High-growth companies typically start their [[concepts/product-operations|product operations]] journey with the business data and insights pillar to monitor performance and get strategic inputs quickly. In contrast, larger enterprises undergoing digital transformation often prioritize the process and governance pillar to establish a coherent [[concepts/product-operating-model|product operating model]] and create transparency across many teams.
