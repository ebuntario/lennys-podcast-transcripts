Effective [[concepts/leadership|leadership]] involves [[concepts/seed-planting|planting many cheap seeds]] (small projects or ideas) and investing further only in those that show signs of organic growth. Look for ideas that have inherent [[concepts/compounding-loops|compounding loops]], like network effects, where success accelerates further success. This "farming for miracles" is a systemic approach that doesn't require predicting winners upfront.
