Effective organizations set broad annual directions and outcomes through investment plans, then empower teams to figure out the path through iterative, chaotic execution. This approach acknowledges that detailed yearly roadmaps are unrealistic in fast-changing environments while still providing alignment through [[concepts/strategic-thinking|strategic direction]].
