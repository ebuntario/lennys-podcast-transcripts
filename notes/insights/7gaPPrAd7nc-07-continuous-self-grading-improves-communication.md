Develop the habit of grading yourself after every communication—whether it’s a meeting, email, or elevator pitch. Ask, “How could I have answered that question better?” This [[concepts/continuous-improvement|continuous improvement]] mindset, combined with seeking feedback from managers, sharpens your ability to communicate clearly and directly, a skill critical at every career stage.
