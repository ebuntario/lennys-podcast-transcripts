When staffing a new high-growth venture, prioritize hiring people with early-stage company experience who thrive in ambiguity. Be upfront about the chaotic pace, long hours, and high expectations. Compensate them with ownership and outcome-based incentives tied to the new business's success, separate from the parent company's structure, to foster an [[concepts/entrepreneurship|owner mentality]].
