[[concepts/strategic-focus|Strategic focus]] acts like a magnifying glass, concentrating your organization's energy on a critical, addressable challenge. Trying to pursue too many priorities diffuses effort and turns a potential good strategy into a bad one. Each new "yes" to an additional goal risks diluting the [[concepts/power-leverage|leverage]] needed to overcome the core problem.
