Marketplaces possess unique aggregate data that individual sellers cannot access. Platforms can use these insights to guide sellers toward behaviors that increase their sales, such as optimizing listing photos or timing promotions. This [[concepts/data-driven-decisions|data-driven guidance]] helps sellers succeed while improving the overall health of the marketplace, as the platform has a holistic view of buyer trends and preferences.
