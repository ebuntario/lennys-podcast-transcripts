Build a culture where the default is to make decisions immediately, not schedule a follow-up. This involves modeling from leadership, embedding tempo into processes like quarterly planning, and holding firm to deadlines. When everyone expects and values rapid [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]], it becomes a self-reinforcing cultural norm that drives overall execution velocity.
