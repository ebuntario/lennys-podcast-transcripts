A newsletter or creator business tied closely to an individual's output lacks a traditional exit path. Its value is often a multiple of annual revenue, but it's difficult to sell because the asset is intrinsically linked to the creator's continued involvement. This contrasts with building a company that can operate independently, highlighting a key [[concepts/scalability|scalability]] consideration for solo entrepreneurs.
