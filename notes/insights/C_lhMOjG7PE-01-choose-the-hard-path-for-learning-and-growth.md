When faced with multiple options, choosing the harder path often leads to greater long-term benefits even if it fails. You gain valuable experience, work with smarter people, and build skills that remain useful regardless of the outcome. This approach prioritizes [[concepts/learning-mindset|continuous learning]] over short-term comfort, ensuring you win by growing regardless of immediate success.
