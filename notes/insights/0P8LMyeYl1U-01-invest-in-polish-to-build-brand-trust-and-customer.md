Even when moving fast, invest in small details like proofread emails, quality visuals, and thoughtful sample content. This signals to customers that you built the product with them in mind, which builds [[concepts/brand-building|brand trust]] and makes people want to root for your success. When you're small, you need as many advocates as possible.
