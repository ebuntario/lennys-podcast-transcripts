Effective [[concepts/onboarding|onboarding]] guides users through trivial first steps that quickly culminate in a meaningful accomplishment. This approach overcomes the "fear of the blank page" by providing immediate, surprising wins. The goal is to make users feel capable and surprised by what they can achieve in minutes.
