Startups building on AI have durable opportunities in domains requiring deep industry knowledge, specific compliance understanding, or differentiated go-to-market relationships. Foundational model companies are unlikely to build specialized solutions for legal, biotech, or lab equipment integration. The defensibility comes from [[concepts/domain-expertise|understanding specific workflows]] and the people who use them, not just technical implementation.
