Founders often possess exceptional drive and the ability to "hold onto a rock" and push projects forward. However, they tend to have low tolerance for organizational waste and will directly call it out. Be prepared for a [[concepts/team-culture|founder-dense team]] to be differentially higher output but also to experience higher attrition as ambitious individuals move on to lead their own ventures.
