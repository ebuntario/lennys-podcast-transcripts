Reluctance to implement a [[concepts/price-increases|price increase]] is often an internal, emotional hurdle for founders, not an external, logical barrier from the market. As Warren Buffett noted, pricing power defines a great business. Founders should strategically plan price increases as a value exchange and not be paralyzed by the fear of customer pushback.
