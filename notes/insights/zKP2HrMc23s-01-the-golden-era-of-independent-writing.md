The internet is entering a golden era for writers, moving beyond the historically poor economic models for supporting great writing online. The [[concepts/subscription-models|subscription model]] enables creators to be directly valued by their audience, allowing them to make a living or even a fortune from a relatively small number of dedicated supporters, without needing massive scale or playing attention games on ad-driven networks.
