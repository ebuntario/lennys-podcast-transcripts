Do not set pricing and forget it. Companies often labor over product features but treat pricing as an afterthought. Instead, treat your [[concepts/pricing-strategy|pricing strategy]] like your product roadmap, revisiting it every 6-12 months alongside major product launches to ensure you are being compensated appropriately for the value delivered.
