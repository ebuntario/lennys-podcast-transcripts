The product operations function was created to solve the bidirectional feedback gap between a centralized product team and a globally distributed operations team. This role, often embedded with product but accountable to operations, ensures new features are effectively launched globally and that ground-level insights from local markets inform product development. It is a key mechanism for achieving [[concepts/global-scalability]] in operationally complex businesses.
