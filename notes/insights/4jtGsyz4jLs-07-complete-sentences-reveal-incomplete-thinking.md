Overusing bullet points and sentence fragments can mask unclear ideas. If you struggle to turn a fragment into a complete sentence, it often means the thought isn't fully formed. Forcing yourself to write in [[concepts/complete-sentences|complete sentences]] is a litmus test for the rigor of your thinking.
