Kevin Kelly's "1000 True Fans" concept is a powerful proof point for the viability of the [[concepts/subscription-models|subscription model]], showing that a creator can make a living from a dedicated niche. However, on a networked platform, that initial thousand is often just the beginning; network effects can then help scale that audience to several thousand or more, turning a livelihood into a significant business.
