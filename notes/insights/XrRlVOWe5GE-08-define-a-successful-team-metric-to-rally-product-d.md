For collaborative B2B products, identify a leading indicator of long-term monetization that occurs earlier in the user journey. For Slack, this was "successful teams"—getting five people to use the product for the majority of the work week. Teams that hit this were 400% more likely to upgrade. Rallying the entire product org around improving this metric, rather than just top-of-funnel or revenue metrics, aligns efforts on driving real, sustainable [[concepts/user-adoption|adoption]].
