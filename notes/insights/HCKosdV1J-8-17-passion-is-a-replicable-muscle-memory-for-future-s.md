In a future where AI may replace many jobs, fostering [[concepts/passion|passion]] in any domain is critical. Passion drives the initiative to constantly learn and improve, creating a "muscle memory" for taking action. This replicable behavior is more important than the specific skill itself, as passionate individuals will always find a path to create value and adapt, regardless of how the world changes.
