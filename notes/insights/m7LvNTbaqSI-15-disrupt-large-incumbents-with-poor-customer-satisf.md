A promising strategy involves identifying publicly traded companies or private equity-owned businesses that are widely disliked by their customers. Researching industries with established, large incumbents delivering poor software experiences can reveal substantial [[concepts/market-opportunity|market opportunities]]. This approach helped Zip identify procurement software as a target area ripe for disruption.
