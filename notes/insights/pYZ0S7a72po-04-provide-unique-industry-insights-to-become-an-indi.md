As a vendor, you have a cross-section of data on your customer segment. Transform this into unique insights and benchmarks that help your customers navigate the market. Shift product marketing toward research to create exclusive content, moving from a tool provider to a [[concepts/valued-advisor|valued advisor]] that customers cannot easily replace.
