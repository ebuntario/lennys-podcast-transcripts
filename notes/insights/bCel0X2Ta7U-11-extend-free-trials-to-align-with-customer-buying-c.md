A user's decision to convert from a trial is tied to their own schedule, not yours. Longer trial periods capture users who need time to evaluate, fit the purchase into their budget cycle, or start a new project. This user-centric approach to [[concepts/conversion-optimization|conversion optimization]] often yields higher overall conversion rates than arbitrarily short deadlines.
