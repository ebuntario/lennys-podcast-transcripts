Experienced product managers and software engineers possess critical skills for climate tech startups, such as [[concepts/product-management|product management]], execution, and understanding what "good" looks like. These are often more valuable than specific domain expertise, which can be learned or partnered with. The transition to a greener economy needs operational talent to scale solutions.
