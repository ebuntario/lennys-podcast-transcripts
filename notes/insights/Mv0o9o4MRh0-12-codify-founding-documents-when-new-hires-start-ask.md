The signal to document your mission, long-term goals, and operating principles is when new hires repeatedly ask foundational questions about priorities and ways of working. These documents crystallize your [[concepts/mission-vision|mission and vision]] and become essential tools for [[concepts/onboarding|onboarding]] and alignment, ensuring everyone understands what the company is trying to accomplish and why certain investments are made.
