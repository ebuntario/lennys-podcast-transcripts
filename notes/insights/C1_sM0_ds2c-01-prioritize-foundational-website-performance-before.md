Early-stage growth requires building a solid foundation before investing in paid channels. Start by ensuring your website is fast, functional, and discoverable by search engines. Only after these basics are covered should you consider [[concepts/paid-acquisition|paid advertising]], as a slow or poorly optimized site will waste ad spend and fail to convert visitors.
