Beauty and functionality are not opposites; beauty enhances functionality by making products easier to use, more approachable, and more compelling. Furthermore, beautiful design increases user [[concepts/psychological-safety|trust]], as it signals painstaking attention to detail and care for aspects both seen and unseen.
