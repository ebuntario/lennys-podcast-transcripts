In a landscape where new AI capabilities can emerge overnight, rigid [[concepts/roadmap-planning|roadmaps]] become a liability. Companies must cultivate extreme [[concepts/strategic-adaptability|strategic adaptability]], willing to slaughter existing plans to pivot quickly when a breakthrough affects their domain. This requires a fluid, non-siloed culture and a commitment to [[concepts/agile-development|agile development]] principles at a strategic level.
