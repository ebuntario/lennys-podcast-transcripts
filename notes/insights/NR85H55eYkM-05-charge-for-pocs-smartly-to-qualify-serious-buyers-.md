Implementing a fee for your [[concepts/proof-of-concept|POC]] acts as a lead qualification mechanism, filtering out curious buyers from serious ones. Clearly state that the POC fee is for building the business case and is separate from the final commercial price, which should be based on the co-created ROI model.
