A successful public writing career is often built on years of unnoticed, consistent effort. Writing regularly, even for a small audience, builds a repository of work, refines your voice, and establishes [[concepts/career-capital|expertise capital]]. When you later launch a monetized project, you have a back catalog that demonstrates credibility and attracts an initial audience who already values your perspective.
