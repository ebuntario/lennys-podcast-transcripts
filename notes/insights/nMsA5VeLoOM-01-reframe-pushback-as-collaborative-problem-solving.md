The term "pushback" frames disagreement as a physical opposition, putting people in a negative mindset of needing to say no. Instead, reframe the goal as shifting direction or helping the business succeed, which is a collaborative mindset. Effective [[concepts/communication|communication]] starts by understanding the underlying goal and aligning on a shared vision for success.
