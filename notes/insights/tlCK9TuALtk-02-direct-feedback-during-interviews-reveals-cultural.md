Providing candid, tough feedback after an interview, especially regarding cultural concerns, is a powerful way to assess a candidate's character and how they handle critique. If someone is genuinely not a fit, they will likely react poorly; if they are receptive, it demonstrates humility and a desire to improve, which are key for [[concepts/company-culture|cultural alignment]].
