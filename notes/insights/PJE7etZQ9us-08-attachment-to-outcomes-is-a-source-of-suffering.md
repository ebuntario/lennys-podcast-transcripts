When your sense of self-worth becomes attached to external success (like podcast growth or income), it fuels anxiety and suffering. The Buddhist principle states that what we do to push away suffering often increases it. Enjoy the puzzle of creation as an art project, but detach your [[concepts/self-worth|self-worth]] from the outcome.
