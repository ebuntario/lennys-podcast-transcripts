PMs should build something simple on the side, even if non-technical, to understand the full product lifecycle from sales to support. This [[concepts/entrepreneurial-mindset|entrepreneurial experience]] builds humility, breaks down technical barriers, and provides perspective beyond feature-specific responsibilities.
