Simulate high-pressure scenarios with scripted [[concepts/dress-rehearsals|dress rehearsals]] to stress-test your platform and gather rapid feedback. This practice reveals friction points under realistic conditions and validates that the system can handle mission-critical workflows. The intense, short duration of these simulations yields a high density of actionable insights.
