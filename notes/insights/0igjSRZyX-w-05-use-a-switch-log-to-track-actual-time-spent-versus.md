Your calendar shows intentions, but your trail of work reveals reality. The [[concepts/switch-log|Switch Log]] technique involves messaging a note (e.g., in Slack) every time you change tasks. This creates a record of where your time actually goes, enabling data-driven adjustments to your schedule and ensuring you spend time on high-leverage activities.
