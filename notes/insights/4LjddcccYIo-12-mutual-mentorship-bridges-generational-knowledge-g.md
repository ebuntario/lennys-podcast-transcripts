Effective [[concepts/mentorship|mentorship]] flows both ways: older professionals can teach leadership and emotional intelligence while learning technical skills from younger colleagues. This reciprocal approach creates value for both parties and fosters genuine [[concepts/age-diversity|intergenerational collaboration]] rather than one-way knowledge transfer.
