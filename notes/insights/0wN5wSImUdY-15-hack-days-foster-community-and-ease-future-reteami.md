Regular hack days where people form cross-functional teams to build anything they choose strengthen organizational community and relationships. These events create a network of connections that make future reteaming smoother, as people are not strangers when they need to collaborate. This practice builds a more adaptable and innovative [[concepts/organizational-culture|organizational culture]].
