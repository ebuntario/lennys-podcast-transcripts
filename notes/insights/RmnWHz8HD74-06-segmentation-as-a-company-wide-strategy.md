Effective [[concepts/customer-segmentation|customer segmentation]] is not just a go-to-market exercise but a company-wide strategy. It should be based on attributes that correlate with revenue potential and buying behavior, such as company size, growth trajectory, and specific workload types. This framework should be understood by every function, especially product managers, so they build features targeted at winning specific segments.
