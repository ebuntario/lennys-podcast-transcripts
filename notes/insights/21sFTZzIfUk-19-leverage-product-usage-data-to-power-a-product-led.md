A strong PLG foundation that includes a [[concepts/product-led-growth|product-led sales motion]] puts you in a powerful position by generating a significant volume of highly qualified leads from the product itself. Insights from user and team behavior within the product should be made available to GTM teams in smart ways, allowing them to focus on the most promising opportunities. This model, powered by an evolving understanding of usage and adoption patterns, informs where and how sales teams should engage.
