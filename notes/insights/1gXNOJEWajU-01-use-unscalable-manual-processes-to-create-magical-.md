When building a new product, don't be afraid to use manual, unscalable processes to create a magical [[concepts/customer-experience|user experience]] that feels automated. At Anchor, they hired college interns to manually submit hundreds of thousands of podcasts to Apple Podcasts, making distribution feel like a one-click process. This focus on [[concepts/friction-reduction|reducing friction]] was a key driver in rapidly capturing market share from more established but commoditized platforms.
