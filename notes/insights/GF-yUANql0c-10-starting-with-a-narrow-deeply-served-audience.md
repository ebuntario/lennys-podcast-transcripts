Focusing initially on a very narrow audience makes it easier to build a great product and craft resonant messaging. This [[concepts/niche-targeting|laser focus]] allows for deeper understanding and better service, and expansion opportunities often reveal themselves later, whereas starting too broad dilutes effort and clarity.
