[[concepts/Product-led-sales]] converts self-serve usage into sales opportunities by escalating individual problems to company-wide solutions. While self-serve monetization typically caps around $10,000 due to credit card limits, sales can close much larger contracts by articulating the [[concepts/enterprise-value-proposition]]. Products are good at showing user capabilities but poor at communicating organizational benefits, which is where sales bridges the gap.
