Life and software are largely about maintenance; acquiring new features or products creates an ongoing obligation to sustain them. Companies should periodically return to basics, ruthlessly edit outdated features, and improve neglected nooks of the user experience. A focus on [[concepts/product-maintenance|continuous upkeep]] is crucial for managing technical debt and ensuring long-term product health.
