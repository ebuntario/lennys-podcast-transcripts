Engineering has agile, design has design thinking, but product management lacks a named, cohesive abstraction for organizing work at the product and business layer. The concept of a "product system" or operating system aims to fill this gap, providing a checklist of the systems needed to build a cohesive R&D organization that effectively melds customer delight with business ambition.
