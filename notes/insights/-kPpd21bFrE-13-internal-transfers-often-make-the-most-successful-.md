A substantial portion of Revolut's successful product owners come from [[concepts/internal-transfers|internal transfers]] from operations, engineering, or data roles. These individuals already understand the domain, culture, and customers, making their transition to product leadership more effective than external hires with more experience but less contextual knowledge.
