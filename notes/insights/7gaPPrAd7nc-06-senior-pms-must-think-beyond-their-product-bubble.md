As PMs grow, they must expand their view beyond product and tech to consider anything that influences their product’s success—marketing, operations, finance. Adopt an [[concepts/ownership-mindset|ownership mindset]]: you own a problem until you find someone else to own it. This wide-angle perspective helps identify and knock down barriers to success that others might overlook.
