Most founders believe they have a bottom-of-funnel problem, but it's almost always a top-of-funnel [[concepts/sales-qualification|qualification]] issue. This stems from reaching out to the wrong people, using ineffective messaging, or not solving a widely felt problem. Spending time on unqualified leads equates to zero pipeline progress.
