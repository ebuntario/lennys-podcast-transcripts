A powerful interview question is "teach me something" unrelated to the job. It reveals a candidate's ability to explain complex topics clearly, their passion for learning, and their [[concepts/communication-skills|communication skills]]. An inability to break down and teach a familiar concept can indicate struggles with clarity and knowledge sharing in a team setting.
