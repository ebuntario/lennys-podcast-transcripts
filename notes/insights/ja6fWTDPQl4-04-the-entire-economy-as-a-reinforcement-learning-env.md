A compelling perspective is that the entire economy will become a [[concepts/reinforcement-learning|reinforcement learning]] environment machine, where human-created contexts and rubrics train AI models. This shifts the narrative from job displacement to the creation of a massive new category of jobs focused on defining success criteria and verifiers for AI systems across every domain.
