Job features like salary and title are static attributes, while job experiences are how those attributes work through time and space to create energy or drain. For example, money is often a surrogate for respect, paying bills, or feeling valued. Focusing on the [[concepts/job-features-vs-experiences|experiences you want]] helps design a role that provides lasting satisfaction, not just initial attraction.
