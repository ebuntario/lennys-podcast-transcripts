The need for [[concepts/product-operations|product operations]] often emerges during periods of rapid company growth, particularly with the rise of [[concepts/product-led-growth|product-led growth]] models. As product teams and their responsibilities expand, the natural evolution is to create dedicated support structures to manage increased complexity. This role helps calm the "perfect storm" of scaling by improving internal alignment and transparency across growing organizations.
