For [[concepts/b2b-marketing|B2B companies]], press coverage often doesn't directly drive signups but provides crucial [[concepts/credibility-signaling|third-party validation]]. This validation helps with sales outreach, recruiting, and signaling legitimacy to potential customers who may be skeptical of newer companies. The logos of reputable publications on your website and in outbound emails serve as powerful social proof.
