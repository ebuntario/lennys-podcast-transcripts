Great product managers often excel in one of three areas: creative vision (like a filmmaker), data science/analytical rigor, or general management/leadership. Instead of trying to be average in all three, aspiring PMs should identify their natural strength on this triangle and [[concepts/career-advice|gravitate toward roles that leverage it]], as exceptional PMs typically dominate one vertex while being competent in the others.
