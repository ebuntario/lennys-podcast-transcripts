Effective leadership combines deep care for individuals with direct, results-oriented feedback. You can create a culture where success is measurable and expectations are clear while also investing in people's career development. The key is to frame feedback with clear intention—stating you believe in the person's potential and are offering support—which builds trust and drives performance. This embodies the [[concepts/radical-candor|radical candor]] principle of "care personally, challenge directly."
