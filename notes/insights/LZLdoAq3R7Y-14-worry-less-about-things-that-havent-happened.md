Most things you worry about never actually happen, so a powerful [[concepts/mindset|mindset]] is to consciously worry less. Anticipating future problems creates unnecessary stress and consumes mental energy that could be used for creation. In business and life, adopting a "you'll find out" attitude towards potential outcomes reduces anxiety and keeps focus on the present.
