Focusing on [[concepts/customer-research|talking to users]] and analyzing their feedback yields far more significant improvements for AI applications than trying to stay current with every AI news development. Many teams waste time debating new technologies that offer marginal performance gains, while ignoring the substantial impact of direct user insights.
