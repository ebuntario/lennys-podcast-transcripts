When evaluating a new channel, analyze how it plans to monetize, as aligning with its monetization strategy can provide a competitive edge. For instance, if a channel is launching mobile ads and you have deep experience in that area, you can position yourself as a valuable alpha tester and case study. This [[concepts/channel-partnerships|strategic alignment]] increases the likelihood of custom collaborations and ensures your solutions remain relevant as the channel evolves.
