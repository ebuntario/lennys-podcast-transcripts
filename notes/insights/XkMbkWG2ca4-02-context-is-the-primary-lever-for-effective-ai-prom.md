The most critical factor in getting valuable outputs from large language models is providing sufficient context. Treat the model like a highly intelligent person who has never met you; without detailed background, it can only give generic answers. Effective [[concepts/prompt-engineering|prompt engineering]] is essentially the art of clear, contextual communication with the AI.
