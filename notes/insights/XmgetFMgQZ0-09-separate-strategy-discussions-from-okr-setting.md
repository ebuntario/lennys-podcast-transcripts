A common mistake is conflating strategy discussions with OKR setting. Have distinct rituals for each: one to debate strategic direction and another to set measurable goals. This separation ensures the [[concepts/scenario-planning|strategy]] is thoughtfully developed before being translated into specific, trackable objectives.
