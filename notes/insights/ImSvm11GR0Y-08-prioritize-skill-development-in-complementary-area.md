When transitioning into product management or leveling up, focus on developing skills that complement your existing background. If you come from design/research, prioritize data or technical skills; if from data/tech, focus on design/research capabilities. This complementary approach provides maximum leverage for career advancement and helps you develop the well-rounded [[concepts/product-management-skills|skill set]] needed for product leadership.
