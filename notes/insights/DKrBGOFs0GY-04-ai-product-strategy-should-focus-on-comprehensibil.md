A core role for product teams in the AI era is making powerful capabilities comprehensible and opening users' eyes to what's possible. There's a massive "overhang" between what models can do and how they're used daily. Product must bridge this gap through intuitive design and education, helping users develop the new skill of effectively [[concepts/ai-adoption|collaborating with AI]].
