In a highly competitive context where table-stakes features are missing, extreme clarity and focus enable speed. Directly connect engineers to customer churn reasons, group them by feature gaps, and prioritize by potential ARR impact. This [[concepts/execution-speed|execution speed]]-first approach is for putting out fires, not for long-term holistic product development.
