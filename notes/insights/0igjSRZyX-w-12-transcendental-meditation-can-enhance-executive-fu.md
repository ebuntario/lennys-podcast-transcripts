Regular practice of [[concepts/transcendental-meditation|transcendental meditation]], taught one-on-one by a qualified coach, can significantly improve focus, creativity, and emotional regulation. The physical rest component is crucial. Making this a non-negotiable part of your schedule creates a foundation for sustained [[concepts/peak-performance|peak performance]] in high-stress environments.
