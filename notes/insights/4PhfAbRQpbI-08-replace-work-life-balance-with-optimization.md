The concept of "balance" can be unrealistic; a more practical framework is optimization. Ask, "What are you optimizing for in this period (day, quarter, year)?" This acknowledges that you can't have everything equally all the time and allows for intentional sprints of intense work followed by periods of recharge, leading to a more sustainable [[concepts/work-life-balance|work-life integration]].
