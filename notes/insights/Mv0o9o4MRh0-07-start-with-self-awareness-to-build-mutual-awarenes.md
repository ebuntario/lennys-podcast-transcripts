Effective [[concepts/management|management]] and company building begin with the individual, not the team. The most fundamental skill is cultivating deep [[concepts/self-awareness|self-awareness]] of your motivators, strengths, and blind spots. By exposing this to others and actively seeking [[concepts/feedback|feedback]], you create a foundation of mutual understanding that enables high-performance collaboration.
