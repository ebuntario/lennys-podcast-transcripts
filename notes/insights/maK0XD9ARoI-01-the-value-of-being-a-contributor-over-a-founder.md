Not everyone needs to be a founder to achieve success or impact. Many talented people would be better off joining an existing team where their skills are needed, working on a problem with a viable [[concepts/business-model-math|business model math]]. You can achieve acclaim, financial reward, and cultural impact through collaboration without the burden of raising a seed round.
