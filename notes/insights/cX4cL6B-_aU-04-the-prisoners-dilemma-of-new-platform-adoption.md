Companies face a [[concepts/competitive-dynamics|prisoner's dilemma]] when new platforms emerge: if you don't participate, your competitors will, and customer expectations will shift accordingly. There is no opting out of the game, so the strategic choice is to engage early and figure out your exit strategy as the platform evolves.
