If your company is hard to feature directly, position yourself as part of a larger trend or issue. For example, share unique data about your industry or highlight a common problem your business encounters. This [[concepts/trend-pitching|trend pitching]] strategy can get you included in broader stories, providing exposure and establishing your authority without requiring a full feature.
