View your career as an interesting map to explore rather than a ladder to climb. Prioritize learning, growth, and meaningful experiences over titles and linear progression. This [[concepts/mindset|mindset]] encourages calculated [[concepts/risk-taking|risk-taking]] and leads to a more fulfilling professional journey focused on the work you'll remember, not just the resume you build.
