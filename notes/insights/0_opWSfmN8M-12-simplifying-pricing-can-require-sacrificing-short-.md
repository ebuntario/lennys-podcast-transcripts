Moving from complex, revenue-optimizing pricing to simple, fair pricing often means giving away significant short-term ARR. However, this sacrifice rebuilds customer trust, promotes longer-term relationships, and creates internal alignment around [[concepts/customer-obsession|customer obsession]]. The long-term health and growth of the business depend on this foundational fairness.
