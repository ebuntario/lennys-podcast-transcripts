A powerful tactic to build trust and influence quickly is to ask the most influential person in the organization to teach you or let you intern for them. This extends their trust to you and makes them accountable for your growth, as their reputation is tied to your success. By immersing yourself in learning from the best, you accelerate your own [[concepts/competence|competence]] and relationship-building.
