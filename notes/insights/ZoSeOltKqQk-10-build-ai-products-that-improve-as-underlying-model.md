When building with large language models (LLMs), ensure your product's core value improves as the foundational models get smarter. Avoid building too many compensatory features for current LLM weaknesses, as that work will become obsolete. Your long-term [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] should focus on a differentiator that endures and enhances with model advancements.
