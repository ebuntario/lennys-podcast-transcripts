A key signal to hire your first salesperson is when you, as a founder, are spending more than 20% of your time on sales activities. At this point, you need leverage to prevent your calendar from being consumed. The goal is to free up your time for other critical functions like recruiting, which should also command about 20% of a CEO's focus.
