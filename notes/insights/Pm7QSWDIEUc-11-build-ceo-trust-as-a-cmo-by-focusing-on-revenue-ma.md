CMOs often lose CEO trust by not being sufficiently focused on revenue or by failing to articulate how marketing spend drives business outcomes. To build trust, a CMO must have a solid handle on metrics, provide reliable growth predictions, and think strategically about new markets and growth areas. This combination of financial accountability and forward-looking [[concepts/growth-strategy|strategy]] allows the CMO to be a true partner to the CEO.
