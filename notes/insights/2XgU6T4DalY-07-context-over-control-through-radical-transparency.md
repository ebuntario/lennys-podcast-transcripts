A principle of "context, not control" is operationalized through [[concepts/radical-transparency|radical transparency]]. Leaders share notes from leadership meetings broadly with the organization, providing context on top priorities and challenges. This empowers teams with the information they need to exercise good judgment within a [[concepts/freedom-and-responsibility|freedom and responsibility]] model.
