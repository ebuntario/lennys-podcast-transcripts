For products handling sensitive data or critical workflows, every interaction—from ads to support—signals whether you are a trustworthy partner. Early-stage companies must consciously craft these touchpoints to project competence and care, enabling customers to feel secure in adopting your solution. This deliberate [[concepts/brand-building|brand-building]] is essential for word-of-mouth growth in trust-sensitive categories.
