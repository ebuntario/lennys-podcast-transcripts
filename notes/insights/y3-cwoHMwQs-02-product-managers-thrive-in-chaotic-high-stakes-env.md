Chaotic problems create ideal conditions for product managers to excel by forcing structured thinking amid uncertainty. Successful product leaders in these environments focus on diagnosing what's true, establishing conviction, and rallying teams around shared context and single goals. This requires [[concepts/prioritization|prioritization]] skills and the ability to let less important things slide when necessary.
