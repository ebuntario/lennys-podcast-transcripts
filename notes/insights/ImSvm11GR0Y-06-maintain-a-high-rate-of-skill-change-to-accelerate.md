Your career growth correlates with your rate of skill acquisition and change. Early-career PMs experience rapid growth because they're constantly learning new skills, but this often slows at mid-senior levels. Intentionally seek opportunities to develop new capabilities, benchmark yourself against industry leaders, and embrace challenging assignments to maintain momentum. This commitment to [[concepts/learning|continuous learning]] drives sustained career advancement.
