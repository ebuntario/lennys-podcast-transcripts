With 120 direct reports, Farhan eliminated scheduled one-on-ones in favor of being constantly available for unscheduled support. This approach prioritizes solving real-time problems over ritualized check-ins. By creating systems (like pair programming and clear backlogs) that address common [[concepts/management|management]] needs, you reduce dependency on managers for routine unblocking.
