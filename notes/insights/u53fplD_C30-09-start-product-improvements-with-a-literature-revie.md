Before designing experiments, conduct a [[concepts/customer-research|literature review]] using resources like Google Scholar to see what behavioral interventions have worked in similar contexts. This grounds your [[concepts/hypothesis-generation|hypothesis generation]] in existing evidence and helps avoid reinventing solutions, increasing the likelihood of effective product changes.
