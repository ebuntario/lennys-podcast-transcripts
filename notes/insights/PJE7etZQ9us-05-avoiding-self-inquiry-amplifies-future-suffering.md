The fear of opening "Pandora's box" often leads to bypassing techniques like overwork or spiritual escapism. However, unsorted childhood baggage inevitably demands payment, often through depression or self-sabotage later. Engaging in [[concepts/radical-self-inquiry|radical self-inquiry]] now prevents greater emotional tolls in the future.
