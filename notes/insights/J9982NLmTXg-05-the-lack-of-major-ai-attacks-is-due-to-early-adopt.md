There hasn't been a catastrophic AI security breach yet primarily because AI agents with significant real-world power (like database write access or robotic control) are not yet widely adopted. The risk escalates dramatically as [[concepts/ai-agents|agentic AI]] systems that can take autonomous actions become more common.
