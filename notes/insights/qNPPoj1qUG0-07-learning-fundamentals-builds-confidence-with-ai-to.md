Gaining basic coding or model-training skills, even through no-code tools, builds confidence and a better mindset for working with AI. Understanding the fundamentals helps you comprehend how the tools that assist you were created, rather than using them "blindfolded." This [[concepts/technical-fluency|technical fluency]] is empowering.
