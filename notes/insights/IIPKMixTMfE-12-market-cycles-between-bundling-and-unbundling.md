Markets naturally oscillate between phases of fragmentation (unbundling) and consolidation (bundling). Recognizing which phase the market is in helps decide whether to build a vertical point solution or a horizontal platform. We are currently at the tail end of the SaaS unbundling wave, shifting back towards [[concepts/product-bundling|bundling]].
