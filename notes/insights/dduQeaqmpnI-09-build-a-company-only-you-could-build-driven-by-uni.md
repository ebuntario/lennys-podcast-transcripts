Instead of constantly pivoting to chase trends, founders should focus on building the one company that wouldn't exist without their unique insight, expertise, and mission. This [[concepts/mission-driven|mission-driven]] approach, which involves saying no to distractions and avoiding the "blitz scale" playbook, is the path to creating something that genuinely matters and can change the world.
