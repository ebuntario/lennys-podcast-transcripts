Working at a top company provides access to excellent artifacts, processes, and people. Proactively learn by observing high-performers in meetings, studying internal strategy documents, and asking to see the iterative process behind final outputs. This environment serves as a powerful [[concepts/learning-frameworks|accelerator]] for [[concepts/professional-growth|professional development]].
