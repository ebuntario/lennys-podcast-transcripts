The reason [[concepts/bootstrapping|bootstrapping]] is great for entrepreneurs is that it forces them to practice making money, which is the core skill needed to run a successful business. In contrast, venture-backed companies often practice spending money and lack the scrappy discipline required for [[concepts/profitability|profitability]]. This foundational practice is crucial because you cannot suddenly become good at making money if you've never had to do it from the start.
