The most powerful communities align the company's goals with the personal or professional goals of its members. For example, supporting ambassadors who want to build consulting businesses or sell templates creates a [[concepts/ecosystem-development|powerful flywheel]] where their success directly fuels product evangelism and ecosystem growth.
