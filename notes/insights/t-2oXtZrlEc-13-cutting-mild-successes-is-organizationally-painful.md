Discontinuing a product that has some traction but isn't a core winner is often 10x more painful than expected. Teams have built roadmaps and emotional investment, making [[concepts/strategic-decision-making|strategic decision-making]] to cut them a difficult organizational challenge. This pain should be a key consideration before greenlighting new product ventures.
