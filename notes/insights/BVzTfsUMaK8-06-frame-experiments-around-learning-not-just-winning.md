Cultures that only reward data scientists for "winning" experiments encourage incremental, risk-averse tests. Instead, frame experimentation around hypothesis testing and learning. An experiment that fails but provides a strong signal about user behavior or market dynamics is valuable because it updates the organization's [[concepts/mental-models|mental model]] and informs future strategy.
