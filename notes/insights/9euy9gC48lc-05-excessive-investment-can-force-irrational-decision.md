Having too much capital can be as damaging as having too little, as it can lead to an unwinnable arms race with competitors. Companies may feel pressured to spend irrationally on promotions like national television advertising simply because the money is available. This highlights that [[concepts/business-strategy|business strategy]] must be sound regardless of funding levels, and that timing and execution often matter more than the sheer amount of investment.
