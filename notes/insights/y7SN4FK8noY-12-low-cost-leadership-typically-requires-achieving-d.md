Being the low-cost provider is a legitimate strategy, but it usually demands a race to achieve dominant scale in your chosen arena. It is difficult to be a sustainable niche cost leader, as scale is often the primary driver of cost advantages in areas like procurement, distribution, and R&D amortization.
