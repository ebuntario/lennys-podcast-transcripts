A fully distributed company can implement a global salary parity model, paying the same rate for the same role regardless of the employee's location. This approach supports a truly equitable [[concepts/remote-work|distributed culture]] and attracts top talent worldwide by valuing contribution over geography. It requires a commitment to fairness and can be a significant differentiator in hiring.
