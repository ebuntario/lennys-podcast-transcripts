AI model training has evolved from simple preference ranking tasks (e.g., comparing short stories) to complex expert-driven tasks that take hours and require PhD-level professionals. Current examples include having world-class web developers build entire websites or doctors explaining nuanced medical topics to models. This shift represents moving from models "knowing things" to models "doing things," which requires digitizing deep human [[concepts/human-judgment|human judgment]] and subject matter expertise.
