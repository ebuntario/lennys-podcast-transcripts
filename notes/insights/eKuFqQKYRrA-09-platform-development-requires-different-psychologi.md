Platform PMs must adjust to much longer development cycles (often 2+ years from infrastructure to end-user impact) compared to direct product work. They need to find ways to celebrate intermediate milestones and maintain team motivation throughout these extended [[concepts/product-development-cycles|development cycles]] without immediate user feedback.
