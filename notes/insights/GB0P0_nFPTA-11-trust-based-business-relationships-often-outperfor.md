Operating on handshake agreements and mutual trust, while occasionally risky, reduces transaction costs and builds stronger partnerships. This [[concepts/trust|trust-first approach]] assumes most people will honor their commitments, creating more efficient and human-centered business interactions than constantly relying on legal contracts.
