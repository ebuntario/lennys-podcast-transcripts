While AI may automate administrative tasks like backlog management, it elevates the importance of high-level product skills. A product manager's focus on [[concepts/business-viability|business viability]]—navigating legal, ethical, and business model constraints—becomes even more critical, especially when dealing with probabilistic AI outputs. Upleveling skills is key to staying relevant.
