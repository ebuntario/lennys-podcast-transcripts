A marketplace business rarely starts as a scaled marketplace. Founders should first identify and solve a specific, painful friction for one side (e.g., payment, trust verification) without requiring scaled liquidity. This initial [[concepts/value-proposition|value proposition]] provides a wedge to attract users before evolving into a platform that facilitates two-sided matching.
