When seeking guest appearances on other podcasts, position yourself as an expert on a specific topic that aligns with their audience's interests. This targeted [[concepts/pitching|pitching]] demonstrates you understand their show and provides clear value. Generic "I'd love to talk about business" pitches get ignored, while "I can teach your audience how to optimize X" offers concrete benefits.
