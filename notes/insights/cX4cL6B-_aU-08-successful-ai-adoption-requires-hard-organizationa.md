Companies that successfully drive [[concepts/ai-adoption|AI adoption]] implement hard constraints, such as limiting headcount growth until teams prove they cannot accomplish tasks with AI. These [[concepts/constraints|constraints]] force behavioral change more effectively than voluntary guidelines or training programs, creating necessary pressure for transformation.
