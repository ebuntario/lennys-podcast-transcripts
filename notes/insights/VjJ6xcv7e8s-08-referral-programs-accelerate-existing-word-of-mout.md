Incentivized [[concepts/referral-programs|referral programs]] (like Dropbox's) are powerful accelerants for growth. However, they work best when built on a foundation of strong, organic [[concepts/word-of-mouth|word-of-mouth]]. If people don't naturally want to talk about your product, adding incentives is unlikely to fix the core issue, which is a lack of must-have [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]].
