Effective listening involves three levels: Level 1 (internal/focused on self), Level 2 (focused completely on the other person), and Level 3 (global/environmental awareness of context and body language). Skilled listeners consciously shift to Level 2 to build connection and notice non-verbal cues, which is a foundational [[concepts/coaching|coaching]] and leadership skill.
