To make someone feel truly heard, first, have them share their thoughts verbally. Second, reflect back what they said until they confirm your understanding is correct. Third, for deeper feedback, reflect the unspoken thoughts or stronger emotions you imagine they might be holding back. This process of [[concepts/active-listening|active listening]] validates their experience and builds psychological safety.
