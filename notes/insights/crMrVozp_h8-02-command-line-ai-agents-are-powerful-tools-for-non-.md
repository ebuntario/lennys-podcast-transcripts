Tools like Claude Code, which are command-line [[concepts/ai-agents|AI agents]] with access to your local file system, are underrated for non-programmers. They can autonomously process large volumes of text files—like meeting notes or books—over extended periods, performing complex analysis and summarization tasks that would be cumbersome in a standard chat interface. The initial hurdle of using a terminal is outweighed by the ability to delegate long-running, data-intensive [[concepts/knowledge-work|knowledge work]].
