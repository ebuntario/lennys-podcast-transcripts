Real work over 30-90 days reveals far more about a candidate's fit and capabilities than interviews ever can. Shopify's massive internship program (1,000+ interns) serves as an extended [[concepts/job-trial|job trial]] that benefits both the company and candidates. This approach surfaces cultural fit, actual skills, and working style more accurately than any interview process.
