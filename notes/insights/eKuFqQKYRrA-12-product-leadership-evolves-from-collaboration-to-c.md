Junior PMs should emphasize balanced decision-making across functions, while senior leaders must develop the conviction to make strategic calls and take accountability. As you advance, your role shifts from facilitating collaboration to providing clear [[concepts/strategic-direction|strategic direction]] with your "ass on the line" for outcomes.
