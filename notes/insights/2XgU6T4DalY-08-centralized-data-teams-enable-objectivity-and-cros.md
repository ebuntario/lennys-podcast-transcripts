Keeping data science, engineering, and consumer insights in a centralized team (rather than embedded in business units) fosters [[concepts/objectivity|objectivity]] and cross-pollination of ideas. This structure allows the team to act as truth-tellers, pursue their own curiosity, and maintain world-class [[concepts/functional-expertise|functional expertise]], though it requires exceptional partnership with other departments.
