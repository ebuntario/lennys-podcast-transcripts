During pitches, demonstrate expertise by reframing the conversation around what the client truly needs. Instead of mechanically answering an RFP, propose a tailored solution based on your experience. This [[concepts/consultative-selling|consultative approach]] builds trust and differentiates you from competitors who simply follow instructions.
