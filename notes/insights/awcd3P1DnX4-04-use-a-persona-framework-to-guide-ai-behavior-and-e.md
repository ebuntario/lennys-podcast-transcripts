Framing an AI tool as a "pair programmer" creates a useful persona to guide its expected behavior. This helps answer ethical questions: a good pair programmer wouldn't distract with offensive or irrelevant content. This [[concepts/persona-framework|persona framework]] informs principles for filtering outputs and designing interactions, moving beyond simplistic blocklists to more nuanced, context-aware models.
