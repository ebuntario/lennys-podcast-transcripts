Founders cannot outsource early sales because they possess deep subject matter expertise that third-party sellers lack. This direct interaction creates a critical feedback loop for refining the product, messaging, and [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]]. Skipping this step often leads to failure when hiring a sales leader prematurely.
