Product teams can operate with some ambiguity, but marketing demands crystal-clear, black-and-white messaging to scale effectively. Fuzzy or gray value propositions get lost in translation across a growing organization. [[concepts/product-marketing|Product marketing]] bridges the gap between technical product depth and what the market and sales teams can effectively communicate.
