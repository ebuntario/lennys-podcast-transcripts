A manager cannot convincingly rally a team around a purpose or project they don't personally believe in. If you lack conviction, engage in dialogue with leadership to understand the rationale, break down the assumptions, and find aspects you can align with. Authentic [[concepts/leadership-conviction|belief and conviction]] are prerequisites for inspiring and leading a team to success.
