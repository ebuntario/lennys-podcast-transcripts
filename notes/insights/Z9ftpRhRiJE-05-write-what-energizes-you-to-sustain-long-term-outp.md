The key to consistent [[concepts/writing|writing]] is to focus on topics that are personally energizing and aligned with your current work. Writing under external deadlines or for financial gain often leads to burnout. By publishing what you're genuinely curious about, you create a sustainable practice that also refines your professional thinking.
