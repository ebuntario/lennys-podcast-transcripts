For global teams, short, casual video or audio recordings can effectively replace some synchronous meetings and lengthy written documents. A quick video explaining a document's context or providing feedback feels more personal, conveys tone, and can be consumed on the listener's schedule. This practice is a key enabler for effective [[concepts/asynchronous-communication|asynchronous collaboration]].
