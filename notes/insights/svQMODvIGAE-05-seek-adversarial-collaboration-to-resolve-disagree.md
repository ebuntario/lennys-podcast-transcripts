A powerful method for [[concepts/truth-seeking|truth-seeking]] is to find someone who strongly disagrees with you and write a paper together, designing a study to resolve the issue. This approach, exemplified by Daniel Kahneman, embodies [[concepts/open-mindedness|open-mindedness]] and focuses on discovering what's true rather than winning an argument.
