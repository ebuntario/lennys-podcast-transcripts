True [[concepts/leadership|leadership]] and strong teams are built on love, defined as the choice to extend yourself for someone's growth, not on being universally liked. When you genuinely care for the person behind the role, you can give raw, direct feedback knowing it's in their best interest. This foundation of care fosters psychological safety and powerful collaboration.
