Accelerate your growth by consciously seeking out three types of learning: experience (doing the work), exposure (observing others in action), and education (formal training). Many early-career professionals over-index on education, but being a learner in exposure contexts—like being present in high-stakes meetings you're not leading—is equally powerful. This framework ensures a more holistic and purposeful approach to [[concepts/career-development|career development]].
