The "founder mode" concept can be dangerous when it gives founders permission to avoid learning essential [[concepts/ceo-role|CEO skills]]. The skills needed to launch a company are different from those required to grow, scale, and exit it. Great CEOs learn to calibrate between the visionary founder mindset and the disciplined operator mindset as needed.
