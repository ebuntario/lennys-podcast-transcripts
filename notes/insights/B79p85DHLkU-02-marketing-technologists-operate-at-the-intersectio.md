Effective marketing technologists combine deep technical expertise in first-party and third-party systems with strong persuasion skills. They must convince stakeholders across product, engineering, and marketing to prioritize platform changes that enable future growth. This role requires [[concepts/cross-functional-collaboration]] to quarterback initiatives that span multiple departments while managing upward, lateral, and downward relationships.
