For any company, there are typically only two to three potential buyers who would find your product extremely strategic. To get noticed, identify the area where you bring a [[concepts/competitive-advantage|competitive advantage]] and inflict pain on that specific buyer in a way they cannot ignore. This approach makes your company impossible to overlook, prompting serious acquisition interest.
