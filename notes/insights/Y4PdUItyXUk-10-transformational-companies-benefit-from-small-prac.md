Large organizations undergoing [[concepts/digital-transformation|digital transformation]] should create small, protected teams that can complete full practice cycles independently. These "pods" allow teams to develop new ways of working without being overwhelmed by organizational inertia. The reps gained in these environments build capability that can later scale more broadly.
