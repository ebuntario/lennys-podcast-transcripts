A comprehensive creative brief should include your purpose, positioning, personality, visual inspiration, and counter-examples. This document serves as a [[concepts/brand-strategy|brand strategy]] bible for onboarding employees, briefing agencies, and ensuring consistency across all marketing efforts. It transforms abstract brand concepts into actionable guidelines.
