To succeed, an isolated innovation team should be physically separated, granted process freedom, and report directly to a senior leader with decision-making authority. Leaders must explicitly tell the rest of the organization not to disturb the team, shielding them from bureaucratic processes. This structure provides the clear communication and protection needed for [[concepts/innovation|innovation]] to flourish.
