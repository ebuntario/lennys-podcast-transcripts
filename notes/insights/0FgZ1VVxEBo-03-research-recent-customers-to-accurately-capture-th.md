When conducting [[concepts/customer-research|customer research]], prioritize interviewing customers who signed up 3-6 months ago, as they accurately remember their life and frustrations before your product. Long-term customers may reconstruct their past narrative, but recent customers provide the authentic [[concepts/customer-context|context]] needed to refine positioning and the product experience.
