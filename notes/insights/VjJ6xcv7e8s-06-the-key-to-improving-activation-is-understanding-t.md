Improving [[concepts/activation|activation]] is less about trying generic tactics and more about deeply understanding the specific problem preventing usage. A powerful method is to directly ask users who dropped off *why* they didn't proceed. This qualitative insight, combined with quantitative data, leads to high-impact tests that address the real friction point in the [[concepts/user-experience|user experience]].
