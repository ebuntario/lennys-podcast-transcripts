AI founders must tackle [[concepts/monetization-strategy|monetization]] from the seed stage due to cost dynamics and the risk of under-monetizing immense value. AI products often tap into labor budgets, which are 10x larger than software budgets. Starting with a low price anchors customer expectations and makes it difficult to capture the full value later.
