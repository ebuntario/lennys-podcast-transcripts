When staffing a project, it is better to deliberately [[concepts/project-staffing|understaff]] than to overstaff. Overstaffing leads to politics, wasted effort on low-priority work, and creates organizational "cruft" that slows progress. The key leadership wisdom is knowing how to understaff without critically under-resourcing the project.
