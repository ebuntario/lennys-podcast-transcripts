To launch a new product vertical quickly, assemble a small, founder-led team (e.g., one entrepreneurial engineer plus a designer) and give them a monomaniacal focus. This team should first immerse themselves in the existing platform and culture, then build with direct, high-touch feedback from senior leadership. This model minimizes bureaucracy and maximizes [[concepts/velocity|velocity]].
