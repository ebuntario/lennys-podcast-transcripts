It is a huge lift to try to change someone's worldview or passions. Instead, take the thing you want to talk about and shape it to fit into their existing worldview. This approach involves finding your audience's [[concepts/cultural-erogenous-zones|cultural erogenous zones]]—the topics they already care deeply about—and building a bridge from that to your message.
