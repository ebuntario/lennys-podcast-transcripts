When incubating a new product, shield the small team from the processes and scalability requirements of the core business. Give them the freedom to move fast, prove product-market fit, and solve problems differently. Holding them to a different standard prevents unnecessary slowdown and can generate innovative approaches that later benefit the larger organization. This is essential for fostering a true [[concepts/startup-mindset|startup mindset]].
