Simply granting AI access to a company's entire knowledge base leads to poor performance and hallucinations. Success depends on cleaning and curating [[concepts/golden-examples|golden examples]]—high-quality, representative data—for the AI to learn from. This [[concepts/data-curation|data curation]] work is a critical initial investment that dictates the agent's eventual effectiveness.
