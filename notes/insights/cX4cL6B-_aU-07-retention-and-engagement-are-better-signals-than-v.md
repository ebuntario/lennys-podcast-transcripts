When evaluating new platforms, [[concepts/user-engagement|retention and depth of engagement]] are more important indicators than vanity metrics like MAU or signups. Historical winners like Facebook and Google succeeded not because they had the most users initially, but because they had superior engagement and retention curves that indicated escape velocity.
