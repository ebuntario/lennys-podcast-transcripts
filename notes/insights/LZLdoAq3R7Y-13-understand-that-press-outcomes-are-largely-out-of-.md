You cannot guarantee coverage, control the final story, or predict its impact. Therefore, press should be an add-on strategy, not a primary growth driver. Smart [[concepts/expectation-management|expectation management]] involves recognizing this uncertainty and focusing on elements you can influence, like a strong pitch and a compelling narrative.
