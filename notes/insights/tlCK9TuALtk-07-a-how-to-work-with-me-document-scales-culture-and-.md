A written document outlining your working style, expectations for success, personal growth areas, and commitments to your team is a powerful tool for scaling culture during rapid growth. It creates transparency, helps new hires acclimate quickly, and can be a decisive factor for top talent who value autonomy and clear [[concepts/communication|communication]].
