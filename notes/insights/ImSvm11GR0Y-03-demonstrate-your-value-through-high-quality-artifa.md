Your career advancement depends on showing how you contributed to impact through tangible work products. Maintain excellent PRDs, product strategy documents, experiment analyses, and stakeholder communications that demonstrate your thinking and skills. These [[concepts/product-artifacts|artifacts]] serve as concrete evidence of your capabilities and differentiate you from PMs who may have worked on successful products but can't show their specific contributions.
