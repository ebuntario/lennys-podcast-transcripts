Investments in performance, stability, or developer velocity are hard to justify with immediate metrics but are critical for protecting existing value. The argument shifts from driving new growth to preventing the erosion of hard-won gains in conversion or engagement. Securing buy-in for this work starts by aligning your immediate cross-functional team on the long-term risk of ignoring these [[concepts/technical-debt|foundational elements]].
