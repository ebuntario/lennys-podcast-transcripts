A classic execution failure is having separate internal teams building competing versions of the same core capability (e.g., two different live video products). This creates wasted resources, political friction, and a confusing user experience. Successful integration requires unified leadership and a single, cohesive [[concepts/technical-vision|technical and product vision]] from the start.
