Expect to hear "no" about 99 times for every "yes" when fundraising from VCs, as partners typically invest in only 1-2 of the 100-200 companies they see annually. This [[concepts/fundraising|fundraising reality]] requires resilience and viewing each rejection as a step closer to a yes, rather than taking the feedback personally.
