Product-led growth and enterprise sales must operate as complementary channels, not competitors. Establish clear handoff points where self-serve adoption triggers sales engagement, supported by product marketers who translate enterprise needs into product requirements. This [[concepts/channel-orchestration|orchestrated approach]] treats both motions as parts of one unified customer journey.
