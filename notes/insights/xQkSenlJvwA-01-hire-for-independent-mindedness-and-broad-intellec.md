Palantir screened for very independent-minded people who weren't afraid to push back and question everything. They also sought individuals with broad intellectual interests beyond just technology, which created a distinctive and high-performing [[concepts/company-culture|company culture]]. This combination attracted talent that was undervalued in the broader tech ecosystem at the time.
