Adopt a mindset of "measuring in hundreds" when starting new endeavors. Early failures are insignificant if you view success as the result of hundreds of attempts; quitting after a few tries guarantees failure. This perspective embraces [[concepts/compounding|compounding]] effort and resilience as the true path to achieving meaningful outcomes.
