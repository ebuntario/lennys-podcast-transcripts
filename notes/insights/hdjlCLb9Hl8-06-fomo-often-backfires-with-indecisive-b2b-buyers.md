Research shows that using FOMO (fear of missing out) with indecisive B2B buyers actually decreases close rates. Adding pressure to already anxious buyers creates paralysis rather than action. Instead, focus on reducing [[concepts/risk-aversion|risk aversion]] through guarantees, phased implementations, and helping them make confident decisions.
