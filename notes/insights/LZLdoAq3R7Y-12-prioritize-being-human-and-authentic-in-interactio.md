During interviews, avoid corporate talking points and be open about challenges and failures. Journalists respond better to genuine, vulnerable conversations than to polished, controlled narratives. This [[concepts/authenticity|authenticity]] leads to more favorable coverage because it creates a human connection and provides the relatable insights audiences crave.
