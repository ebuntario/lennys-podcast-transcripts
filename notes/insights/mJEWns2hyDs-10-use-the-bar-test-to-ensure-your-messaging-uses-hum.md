To avoid jargon-filled messaging, use the "bar test": imagine a member of your target audience describing your product to a peer in a casual setting. The description should be a simple sentence like "It turns your iPad into a point of sale." If it sounds like something a real person would say out loud, your [[concepts/messaging|messaging]] is on the right track.
