Miro maintains a rolling six-month roadmap updated quarterly. The first three months have 80% confidence, the next three have 50%. This structure satisfies enterprise customers' need for visibility while preserving the [[concepts/strategic-agility|agility]] to pivot based on new customer insights, competitive moves, or technological breakthroughs.
