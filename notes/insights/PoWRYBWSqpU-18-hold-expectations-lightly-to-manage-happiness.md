[[concepts/happiness|Happiness]] can be framed as reality minus expectations, making [[concepts/expectation-management|expectation management]] a powerful lever. Holding goals and outcomes lightly allows you to adapt to reality without disappointment and see humor in setbacks. This builds [[concepts/resilience|resilience]] and prevents the pursuit of perfection from undermining meaningful progress and pride.
