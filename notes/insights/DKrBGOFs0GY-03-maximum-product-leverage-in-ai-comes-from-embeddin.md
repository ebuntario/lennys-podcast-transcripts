The highest-impact work happens at the intersection of model capabilities and product experience, not by using models off-the-shelf. Product teams should be deeply embedded in the [[concepts/ai-research-integration|post-training and fine-tuning process]] to create unique value. This collaboration ensures product features are deeply integrated with model advancements, creating defensible differentiation.
