Effective [[concepts/growth-strategy|growth strategy]] starts by understanding the fixed "physics" of your business: the market, product, business model, and channels. This analysis reveals underutilized levers, such as using drivers as real-world marketers. Change only one variable at a time within this framework to ensure bets are rooted in reality and not dependent on multiple unknowns changing.
