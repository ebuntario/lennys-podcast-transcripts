When building [[concepts/zero-to-one|zero-to-one]] products within large companies, maintain a [[concepts/portfolio-strategy|portfolio strategy]] with the expectation that 50% may fail. These projects require patience, minimal early scrutiny, and freedom to iterate quickly. Successful innovation teams operate best with limited resources but maximum autonomy to pivot or prune ideas.
