The core challenge in hearing "no" is managing the feelings of rejection, disappointment, or fear it triggers. Once you can regulate those emotions, a "no" simply becomes valuable data for your next iteration. Practicing [[concepts/emotional-regulation|non-attachment to outcomes]] allows you to learn from rejection without being derailed by it.
