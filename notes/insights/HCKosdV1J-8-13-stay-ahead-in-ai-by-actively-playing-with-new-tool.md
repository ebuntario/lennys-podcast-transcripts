The risk for experienced professionals is becoming stuck in their ways. To stay relevant in the age of [[concepts/artificial-intelligence|AI]], you must actively play with new tools and technologies. Experiment with AI drafts for emails, generate poems, or try various generative tools to understand their capabilities and implications. This hands-on exploration is essential for developing intuition about how these technologies will transform your work.
