Early-stage companies often sign vendor contracts without considering long-term implications, but scaling businesses must manage contracts strategically. Marketing technologists should evaluate pricing structures for scalability, assess liability exposure, and negotiate terms that support future growth. This [[concepts/contract-strategy]] prevents cost surprises and ensures tools can support the business at scale without requiring painful migrations.
