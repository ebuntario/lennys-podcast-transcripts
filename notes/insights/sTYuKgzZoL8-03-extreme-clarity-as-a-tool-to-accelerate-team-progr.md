Extreme clarity means ensuring everyone shares the same understanding of facts, options, and trade-offs, even if they disagree. This is achieved through tactics like canonical nomenclature, real-time visual editing in meetings, and numbered lists for precise reference. The goal is to eliminate misunderstandings and focus conversations on substantive [[concepts/decision-making|decision making]].
