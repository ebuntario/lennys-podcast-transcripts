Creating a culture where it's safe to say "I don't understand" is crucial for [[concepts/strategic-clarity|strategic clarity]]. Often, confusion is shared but unspoken; voicing it can expose misalignment and pivot the entire company. This requires leaders to value vulnerability and impact over perceived seniority or expertise.
