When interviewing for marketing technology roles, candidates should demonstrate how they prepared systematically for the interview. This reveals their approach to learning new domains and solving complex problems. Interviewers value candidates who research the company, understand its technology stack, and can articulate how they would assess and improve systems within their first week.
