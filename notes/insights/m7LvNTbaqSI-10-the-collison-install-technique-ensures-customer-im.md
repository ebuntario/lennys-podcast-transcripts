When customers agree to purchase but don't implement the product, founders should personally ensure completion through hands-on assistance. The Stripe founders would visit customers' offices, take control of their keyboards, and implement the integration themselves. This [[concepts/sales-tactics|sales tactic]] turns theoretical agreements into actual usage, preventing churn from incomplete implementations.
