Mentorship is a multi-directional flow where learning comes from mentors, mentees, hires, community members, and even interns. Maintaining a ready mindset to absorb new information from anyone is crucial for scaling as a leader. Often, advice you once gave can be reflected back to you later, or new founders can provide completely novel [[concepts/continuous-learning|frameworks]] you hadn't considered.
