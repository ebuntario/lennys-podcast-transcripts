As you scale, organize teams into functional groups (e.g., paid ads, content) and regional teams. Centralized functional teams maintain expertise and best practices, while regional teams provide local market knowledge and execution. This structure balances [[concepts/operational-efficiency|operational efficiency]] with market-specific adaptation, avoiding the siloing of specialists that can hinder skill development.
