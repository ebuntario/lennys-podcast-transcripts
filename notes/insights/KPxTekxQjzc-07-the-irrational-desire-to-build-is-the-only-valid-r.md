Starting a company for financial gain alone is insufficient motivation to withstand the inevitable difficulties. You need an irrational desire to create something larger than yourself that improves the world in some way. This deeper [[concepts/founder-motivation|purpose]] provides the psychological fuel necessary to persevere through the many bad things that happen along the journey.
