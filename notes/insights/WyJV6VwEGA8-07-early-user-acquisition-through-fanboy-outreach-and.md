An effective early strategy is to identify and reach out to central, inspiring figures in your target community as a fanboy seeking [[concepts/customer-feedback|feedback]], not just as a growth hack. This approach works particularly well with designers, who are skilled at giving constructive criticism. These conversations often organically convert influential [[concepts/early-adopters|early adopters]] who become evangelists, providing invaluable insights and validation.
