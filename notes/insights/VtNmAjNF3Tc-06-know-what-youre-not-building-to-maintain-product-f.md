A critical component of [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] is being crisp about what you are *not* building. It is easy to get swayed by individual customer requests, but a product that tries to be everything to everyone often fails. Clear prioritization and focus are essential to avoid dilution and ensure the product delivers on its core value proposition.
