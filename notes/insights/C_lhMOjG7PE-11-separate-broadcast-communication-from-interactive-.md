Moving announcements and status updates from Slack to a feed-based platform (like Workplace) reduces distractions while preserving important information flow. This separation allows people to consume broadcast content on their own schedule without interrupting [[concepts/focused-work|deep work]]. Interactive chat remains for conversations requiring immediate back-and-forth discussion.
