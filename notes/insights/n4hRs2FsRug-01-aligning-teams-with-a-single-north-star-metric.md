Rallying an entire organization around a single, clear [[concepts/north-star-metric|north star metric]] creates powerful alignment and simplifies prioritization. At Etsy, making Gross Merchandise Sales (GMS) the central drumbeat for every meeting and launch decision provided a universal measuring stick for success. This clarity helps teams at all levels understand how their work contributes to the company's primary goal, even if their contribution is indirect.
