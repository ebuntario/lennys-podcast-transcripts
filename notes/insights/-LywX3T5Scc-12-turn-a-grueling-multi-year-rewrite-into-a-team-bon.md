When facing a critical but demoralizing project like a full codebase rewrite, create a visual game to track progress and maintain morale. Using physical metaphors (like moving bath toys across a board) in regular stand-ups makes the long, dark tunnel feel more collaborative and fun. This [[concepts/internal-communication|creative communication]] helps a team bond and persist through a period with no visible customer-facing output.
