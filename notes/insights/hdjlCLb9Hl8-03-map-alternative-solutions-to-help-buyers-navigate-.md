After sharing market insight, explicitly discuss the pros and cons of alternative solutions to help buyers make sense of their options. This [[concepts/buyer-education|buyer education]] approach reduces anxiety by providing a clear market map. For instance, Help Scout contrasts shared inboxes (easy but limited) with help desk software (feature-rich but cost-focused).
