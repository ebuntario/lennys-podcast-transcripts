A major downside of solo creator work is the loss of spontaneous social interaction and camaraderie found in an office. While you gain control over your schedule, you lose the watercooler chats and collaborative energy. Proactively seeking community through co-working spaces or industry groups is essential to mitigate the [[concepts/remote-work|isolation]] that can accompany this career path.
