A strong [[concepts/product-strategy|product vision]] should be concrete enough for people to picture the future state and differentiated from competitors. It should also explicitly state what the company will *not* do, which provides powerful guardrails and prevents teams from simply copying competitors or pursuing distracting opportunities.
