Releasing a nascent technology to the public early allows creators to observe how people adapt it for unforeseen purposes. For example, a world model designed for creators and simulation was quickly adopted by psychologists for exposure therapy research. This [[concepts/product-discovery|open discovery process]] is vital for understanding the full potential and [[concepts/ai-applications|practical applications]] of a new AI capability.
