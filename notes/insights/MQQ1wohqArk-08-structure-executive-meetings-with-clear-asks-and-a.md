Begin meetings by stating what you need from the group (e.g., a decision, feedback on a specific angle). Frame the discussion with a brief narrative: the current situation, what has changed, and the resulting problem or opportunity. Be prepared with key data points to support your case and answer detailed questions, which builds confidence in the plan. This structure ensures [[concepts/executive-communication|effective executive communication]].
