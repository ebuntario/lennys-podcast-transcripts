The most effective way to give and receive difficult feedback is to establish a collaborative relationship early on. Explicitly agree with colleagues that you want to help each other grow and that open, ongoing feedback is a valued part of your dynamic. This upfront [[concepts/psychological-safety|opt-in]] removes the pressure and defensiveness that can arise when feedback is only given in critical moments.
