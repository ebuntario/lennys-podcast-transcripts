When failures occur, the individuals whose work caused the problem often feel disproportionate shame. Leaders must actively [[concepts/psychological-safety|reassure and support]] these team members, emphasizing that system failures—not individual mistakes—are usually to blame. This prevents talent loss and maintains team morale.
