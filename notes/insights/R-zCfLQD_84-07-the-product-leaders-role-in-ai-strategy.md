Product leaders must own the AI strategy, not delegate it as a "black box" to engineering. They should be able to define the algorithm's objective, specify the features/parameters it learns from, and drive the data collection and fine-tuning strategy. Thinking [[concepts/artificial-intelligence|AI-first]] is a mindset shift where the product leader controls the ingredients and guidelines, while AI powers the non-deterministic experience.
