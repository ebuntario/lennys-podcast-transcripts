A development plan is something the product manager owns, not something the leader creates for them. The leader's role is to help them see gaps between the "definition of good" and their current profile, and to inspire actionable steps for growth. This could involve recommending books, suggesting conference talks, or encouraging reflection on specific practices, all focused on [[concepts/skill-development|skill-building]].
