For effective [[concepts/public-speaking|public speaking]], reframe the physical anxiety you feel before going on stage. Instead of fighting it, recognize that your body is surging with adrenaline to help you perform. Let this feeling wash over you as a natural preparation tool. Combine this with extensive practice (10+ full run-throughs) and a genuine passion for your topic to connect authentically with the audience.
