Amazon's stock-based compensation without performance bonuses reduces short-term financial pressure on leaders. This structure enables [[concepts/risk-taking|risk-taking]] on innovative projects since compensation isn't tied to quarterly business results. Leaders can move from profitable divisions to experimental ventures without financial penalty, aligning incentives with long-term company success rather than individual P&L performance.
