When an engineer or designer personally encounters a bug or problem, they are often highly motivated to fix it quickly and creatively. Leaders should generally not get in the way of this bottom-up energy, as it can be a more effective way to improve quality than top-down mandates. This [[concepts/team-autonomy|autonomy]] fosters a sense of ownership and often leads to faster, more motivated solutions.
