Young people who grow up using AI tools will have a significant edge, similar to how "Googling" became a resume skill. Being [[concepts/ai-skills|AI-native]] allows one person to handle tasks—like video creation, analytics, and multi-platform posting—that previously required a team. This amplifies individual productivity and shifts hiring toward those who can leverage AI as an "Iron Man suit."
