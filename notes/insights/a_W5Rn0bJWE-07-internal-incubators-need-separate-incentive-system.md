For a "startup within a startup" to work, it must operate with a completely different [[concepts/incentive-structures|incentive structure]] and timeframe than the core business. Applying the parent company's biannual performance reviews to a 0-to-1 team will kill innovation and attract the wrong talent. Teams need the freedom to work on longer time horizons and with different success metrics.
