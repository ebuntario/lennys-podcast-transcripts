Trust in a fully autonomous system is built through a holistic experience, not a single feature. Design elements like in-car monitors showing what the vehicle sees, accessible rider support, and predictable, natural driving behavior all contribute to [[concepts/trust-building|trust-building]]. The goal is for the experience to feel credible and uneventful, making the technology feel like a natural part of daily life.
