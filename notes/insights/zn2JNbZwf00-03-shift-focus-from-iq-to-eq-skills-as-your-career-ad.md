Early in your career, prioritize hard [[concepts/product-management-skills|IQ skills]] like execution, product sense, and strategy. As you advance into roles with larger scope and ambiguity, shift focus to [[concepts/emotional-intelligence|EQ skills]] like communication, leadership, and influence. EQ skills are harder to learn, require more self-awareness, and are often what differentiate senior leaders.
