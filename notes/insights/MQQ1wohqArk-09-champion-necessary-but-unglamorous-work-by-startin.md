To secure investment for crucial but non-flashy initiatives (like improving usability or CSAT), start with a small, scrappy team to prove the direction. Use visceral customer feedback and visual prototypes ("show, don't tell") to build excitement and demonstrate the pain and potential. Find low-cost ways for other teams to contribute, creating early momentum that justifies further investment. This is a key tactic for [[concepts/influence|driving strategic change]].
