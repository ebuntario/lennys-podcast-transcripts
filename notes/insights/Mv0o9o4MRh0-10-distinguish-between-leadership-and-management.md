[[concepts/leadership|Leadership]] is about setting vision, direction, and inspiring followership, while [[concepts/management|management]] is the tactical work of enabling execution, coaching, and creating stability. Many people, especially founders, are naturally stronger in one area, and recognizing this distinction allows you to build complementary teams. The goal is to excel in your area while ensuring the other function is covered.
