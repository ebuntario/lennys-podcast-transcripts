GPTs empower non-developers to build custom AI assistants by packaging context, instructions, and tools into a shareable interface. This [[concepts/democratization|democratization]] moves beyond sharing conversation links to creating reusable, specialized agents. The future includes monetization and easier API integrations, further lowering the barrier to creating valuable AI applications.
