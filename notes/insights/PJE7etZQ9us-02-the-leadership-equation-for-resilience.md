Enhanced leadership and greater resilience result from combining practical skills, radical self-inquiry, and shared experiences. While people often seek coaching for the "how-to," the deeper purpose is to grow up without feeling miserable in the process. The ultimate goal is to build the [[concepts/resilience|resilience]] needed to lead effectively without self-destruction.
