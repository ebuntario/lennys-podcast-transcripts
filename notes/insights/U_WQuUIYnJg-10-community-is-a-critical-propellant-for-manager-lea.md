Formal manager training is often lacking in tech. A powerful alternative is participating in authentic, safe communities—whether internal or external—where managers can share challenges and best practices. These communities provide the safety to be vulnerable, the realization you're not alone, and practical advice from peers. This form of [[concepts/continuous-learning|continuous learning]] is often more impactful than traditional corporate L&D programs.
