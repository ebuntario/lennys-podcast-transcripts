Before starting a company, founders should ask if they see themselves working on the idea for a decade. This [[concepts/long-term-thinking|litmus test]] evaluates genuine passion and commitment, even though most startups fail sooner; it helps filter out ventures pursued for transient reasons rather than deep interest.
