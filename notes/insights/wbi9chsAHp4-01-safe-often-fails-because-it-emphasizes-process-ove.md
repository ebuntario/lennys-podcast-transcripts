Organizations adopt SAFe (Scaled Agile Framework) seeking a plug-and-play solution for software development at scale. However, it often leads to teams focusing on "work about work"—endless meetings and rigid ceremonies—instead of delivering customer value. The framework lacks robust guidance for [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] and discovery, which are essential for building the right products.
