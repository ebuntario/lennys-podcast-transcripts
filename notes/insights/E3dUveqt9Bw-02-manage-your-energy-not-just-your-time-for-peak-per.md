Effective [[concepts/productivity|productivity]] stems from [[concepts/energy-management|energy management]], not just time management. Identify your personal energy rhythms—like avoiding difficult tasks during your low-energy periods—and schedule your day accordingly. Simple acts like ensuring you eat lunch can significantly sustain the energy needed to maintain an abundant mindset and tackle complex work.
