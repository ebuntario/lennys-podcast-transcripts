Success in AI is not about being first to market with an autonomous agent. It's about building the right [[concepts/ai-flywheel|flywheels]]—systems for logging human feedback and agent behavior—that allow for continuous calibration and improvement over time. One-click agents that promise immediate ROI are often marketing; real value comes from a pipeline that learns.
