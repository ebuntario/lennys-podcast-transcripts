Instead of leading with the problems you solve, first establish the new game and its object. Then, present the challenges as "obstacles" preventing the customer from achieving that new goal. This [[concepts/problem-framing|problem framing]] imbues your solutions with greater emotional meaning because they are positioned as the "magic gifts" needed to win in the new, high-stakes environment.
