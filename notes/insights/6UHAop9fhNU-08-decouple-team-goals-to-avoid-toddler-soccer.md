Assigning every team the same top-level goal (like revenue) leads to "toddler soccer," where everyone crowds the same plays. Instead, detangle company objectives into complementary [[concepts/team-goals|team goals]] that map to different parts of the customer journey. This allows teams to own distinct input metrics that collectively drive the ultimate outcome.
