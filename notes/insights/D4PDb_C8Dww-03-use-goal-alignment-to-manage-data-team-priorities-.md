When facing inbound requests, data teams should explicitly discuss trade-offs by aligning on shared goals with business partners. Frame the conversation around: "Is this new ask more important than the other items on our prioritized list to hit our shared goals?" This makes [[concepts/prioritization|ruthless prioritization]] a collaborative exercise rather than a simple "no."
