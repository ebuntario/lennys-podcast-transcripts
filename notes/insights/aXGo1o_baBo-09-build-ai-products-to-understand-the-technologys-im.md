One of the best ways to grasp how AI will transform product management is to build an AI product yourself. This hands-on experience teaches you the capabilities and limitations of non-deterministic systems and prepares you to reshape your team's tools and processes. Treat it as a learning lab to stay ahead of the curve in your [[concepts/ai-product-management|domain]].
