A [[concepts/go-to-market-engineer|go-to-market engineer]] is a technical role that brings engineering prowess to sales and marketing workflows. They break down human processes, encode them into deterministic workflows, and build [[concepts/ai-agents|AI agents]] to automate repetitive tasks like lead qualification and personalized outreach. This allows sales teams to shift from spending 30-40% of their time with customers to a goal of 70%, focusing on high-value human interaction.
