A strong [[concepts/brand-building|brand personality]] (like Duolingo's owl) combined with an authentic [[concepts/community-growth|community]] can supercharge growth. When marketing leverages the product's built-in personality through social media and memes, it can drive a significant percentage of new users. This organic, brand-driven growth works in tandem with systematic product experimentation.
