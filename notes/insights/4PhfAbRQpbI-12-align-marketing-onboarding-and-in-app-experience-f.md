Don't separate top-of-funnel marketing from in-app growth mechanics. For a community-focused app, your ads should show that community, the onboarding should let users join it, and the invitation flow should reinforce it. A cohesive [[concepts/user-journey|user journey]] from first touch to activation is critical for the acquisition flywheel to spin effectively.
