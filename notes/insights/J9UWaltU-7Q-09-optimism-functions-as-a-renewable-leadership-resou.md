Effective leaders use [[concepts/optimism|optimism]] to generate energy and renew commitment to a shared mission, especially in competitive talent markets. This involves creating clarity on what needs to be done and fostering a deep belief that the work makes the world better. The resulting cultural vibe helps people choose to dedicate their efforts to something bigger than themselves.
