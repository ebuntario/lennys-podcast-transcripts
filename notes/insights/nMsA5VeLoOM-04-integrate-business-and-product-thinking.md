The most impactful product cultures don't silo product work from business outcomes. At companies like Amazon, product managers cannot divorce their work from revenue growth and go-to-market strategy. This integrated approach fosters a sense of [[concepts/ownership|ownership]] and ensures product decisions are made with a comprehensive understanding of business impact.
