Speed is critical in competitive environments, and many decisions are reversible. A [[concepts/decision-making|bias for action]] means prioritizing momentum while recognizing when a mistake could cause irreversible reputational damage. This principle encourages moving quickly on low-risk bets while applying greater caution to high-stakes choices.
