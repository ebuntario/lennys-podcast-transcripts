Building custom [[concepts/ai-agents|AI agents]] for internal go-to-market workflows is becoming easier, faster, and cheaper than buying point solutions. A single go-to-market engineer can build a powerful agent in weeks for a fraction of the cost of a full team. The unique context and workflows of a company often mean that internally built agents, which can be continuously refined, outperform generic off-the-shelf software.
