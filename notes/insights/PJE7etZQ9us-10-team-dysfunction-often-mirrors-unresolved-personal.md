Teams unconsciously become foils for working out members' unresolved childhood dynamics, like deflecting tension with jokes. The leader with the most power sets the tone; if they avoid their own work, the group manifests collective dysfunction. Creating a high-functioning team starts with the leader's commitment to [[concepts/radical-self-inquiry|radical self-inquiry]].
