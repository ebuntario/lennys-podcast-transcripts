AI tools like ChatGPT can be powerful, personalized tutors that accelerate skill acquisition far faster than traditional methods. You can feed it a curriculum and ask it to customize a learning program based on your preferred style (e.g., examples, analogies). This makes AI an underutilized tool for [[concepts/skill-acquisition|rapid, just-in-time learning]] across many domains.
