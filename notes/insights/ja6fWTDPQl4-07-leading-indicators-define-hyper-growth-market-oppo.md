Hyper-growth is found by focusing on the leading indicators in fast-moving markets, not by forcing [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] into stagnant ones. Identify new pockets of demand where the wealthiest customers are willing to pay "whatever it takes" for a solution. Success comes from being stubborn about your thesis on how the world will change but flexible about the exact form your company takes to serve that change.
