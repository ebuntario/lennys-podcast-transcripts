Top performers are often pulled from job to job by former bosses or colleagues who advocate for them. A simple filter is to ask candidates, "Of your last bosses, how many would get on the phone and say you're amazing?" This [[concepts/hiring|hiring signal]] indicates a history of creating raving fans and exceptional results.
