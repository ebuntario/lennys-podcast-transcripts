Societies and organizations thrive when power is distributed rather than concentrated. Systems that centralize power inevitably become oppressive, regardless of the leader's initial intentions. Healthy [[concepts/organizational-culture|cultures]] operate under consistent rules above any individual, enabling cooperation, knowledge sharing, and innovation that kin-based or authoritarian structures suppress.
