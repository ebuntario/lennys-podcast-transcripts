A robust strategy must answer five linked questions: What is your winning aspiration? Where will you play? How will you win there? What capabilities must you have? What management systems are required to build those capabilities? This [[concepts/strategy-framework|cascade]] ensures choices are mutually reinforcing and focused on compelling customer action.
