Most people try to calm anxiety by reframing thoughts (top-down), but working directly with the physiology (bottom-up) is far more rapid and effective. Since four times more neural signals travel from the body to the brain than vice versa, changing your [[concepts/nervous-system-regulation|body state]] through breath instantly alters your mental and emotional state.
