Process critical feedback by asking "what are they trying to say?" rather than taking it personally. This [[concepts/feedback-processing|feedback processing]] turns perceived attacks into improvement opportunities. Developing this skill through [[concepts/coaching|coaching]] or peer groups builds the [[concepts/resilience|resilience]] needed for long-term career success.
