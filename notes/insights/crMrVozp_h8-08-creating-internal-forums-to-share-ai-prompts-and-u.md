To foster [[concepts/ai-adoption|AI adoption]], create structured forums for [[concepts/knowledge-sharing|knowledge sharing]]. Tactics include weekly meetings where employees share successful prompts, and company-wide emails highlighting usage stats and spotlighting contributors. This system identifies and rewards the innate 10% of early adopters, whose discoveries can then be translated into workflows for the broader 80% who will use AI if shown how it applies to their specific job.
