Decide whether you want to play a high-volume, marketing-intensive game (small business) or a high-value, relationship-intensive game (enterprise). Your choice should align with the problem you're solving, the product you built, your team's experience, and the founder's desired lifestyle, as each path dictates a very different [[concepts/go-to-market|go-to-market]] motion.
