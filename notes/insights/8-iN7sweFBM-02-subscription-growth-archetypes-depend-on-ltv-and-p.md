Companies with high single-player LTVs (e.g., hundreds of dollars) and strong free-to-paid conversion rates (5%+) have a clear opportunity for [[concepts/paid-acquisition|paid acquisition loops]]. Another archetype leverages inherent [[concepts/network-effects|network effects]] for viral or referral loops, while a third focuses on long-tail programmatic SEO if the product lends itself to template or "how-to" searches.
