A powerful final interview technique is a mini case study probing cultural fit. Present a scenario involving a minor interpersonal conflict (e.g., an indirectly offensive Slack comment) and ask the candidate what they would do and what the company should do. Their response and how they handle challenge reveals their alignment with core values like "assuming the most charitable interpretation," helping to [[concepts/values-alignment|opt-in or opt-out]] candidates who won't thrive in your specific culture.
