When relationships are strained, move conversations from email to in-person interaction. It's easier to express anger in writing than face-to-face, so [[concepts/communication|direct human contact]] often leads to more constructive dialogue and faster reconciliation, as demonstrated when Jeff Bezos chose kindness when sitting next to Ethan after a public failure.
