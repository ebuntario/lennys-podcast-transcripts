Researchers must move beyond pure user empathy and explicitly focus on the overlap between user needs and business profit. This requires learning the language of the business by reading quarterly reports, understanding OKRs, and knowing the conversion funnel metrics. Developing strong [[concepts/business-acumen|business acumen]] allows researchers to propose research that directly addresses strategic opportunities and competitive threats.
