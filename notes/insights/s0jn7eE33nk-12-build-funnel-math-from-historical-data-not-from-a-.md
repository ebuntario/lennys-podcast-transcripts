Founders often start with a revenue target and reverse-engineer funnel assumptions, which is fantasy. Instead, analyze recent conversion rates, deal sizes, and cycle lengths, then model realistic improvements. This [[concepts/data-driven-decisions|data-driven approach]] reveals the gap between likely outcomes and goals, prompting smarter interventions.
