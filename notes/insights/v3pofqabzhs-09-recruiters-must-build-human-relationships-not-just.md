Effective recruiters treat candidates as human beings, listen to their preferences, and build genuine rapport over time. They remember personal details and career goals, avoiding tone-deaf, transactional pitches. This [[concepts/relationship-building|relationship-based approach]] builds trust, which is essential for long-term success in talent acquisition.
