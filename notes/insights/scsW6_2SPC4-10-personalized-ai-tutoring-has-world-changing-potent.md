AI models are already capable of providing high-quality, personalized tutoring, which studies show can lead to multiple standard deviation improvements in learning. It's surprising that a ubiquitous, free tutoring application powered by [[concepts/ai-education|AI doesn't yet exist at scale]], given the technology's availability and the profound impact it could have on global education equity.
