When new [[concepts/distribution-platforms|platforms]] open, they establish a [[concepts/value-exchange|value exchange]]: third parties add use cases and engagement to the platform in return for distribution. Understanding this exchange—what the platform needs and what it offers—is crucial for maximizing the opportunity before the platform inevitably shifts toward monetization and control.
