The top factors in interviews are appearance and genuine enthusiasm. Beyond that, candidates must articulate not just what they did, but the [[concepts/personal-branding|business impact]] of their work. Leaders hire to solve problems, so demonstrating how your past contributions created measurable value is more compelling than listing responsibilities.
