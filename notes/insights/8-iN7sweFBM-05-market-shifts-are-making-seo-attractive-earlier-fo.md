With venture funding tightening, the urgency for immediate paid growth has decreased, and startups are focusing on extending runway. This makes [[concepts/seo-strategy|SEO]] more attractive as a sustainable, defensible growth initiative. Companies that have controlled burn and have 18-24 months of runway can now afford to invest in strategies with longer payback periods.
