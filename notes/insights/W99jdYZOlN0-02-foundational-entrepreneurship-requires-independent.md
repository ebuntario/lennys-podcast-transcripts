Successful entrepreneurship begins with asking why you have a unique insight that millions of other smart people don't possess. This requires [[concepts/independent-thinking|independent thinking]] that isn't overly influenced by market noise. Equally important is the willingness to commit to solving the chosen problem for 5 to 10 years, as starting a business based solely on identified customer pain points without deep personal conviction leads to difficult journeys.
