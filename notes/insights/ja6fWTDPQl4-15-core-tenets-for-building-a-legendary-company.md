Three core tenets drive legendary company building: a "can-do" attitude of setting wildly ambitious goals, maintaining incredibly high standards for hiring and output, and fostering an intense, output-oriented culture. This combination, inspired by early cultures at companies like Meta and Google, focuses on achieving outsized results rather than mandating specific hours.
