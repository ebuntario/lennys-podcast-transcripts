Despite the scale of digital, human connection remains powerful. Prospects who meet a team member in person show a 10-30% higher willingness to pay, 20% lower churn, and 15-20% higher expansion revenue. This holds true even for low-cost products. Prioritizing [[concepts/local-marketing|local strategies]] like meetups, coffee chats, or lunch-and-learns creates high-leverage touchpoints that digital channels cannot replicate.
