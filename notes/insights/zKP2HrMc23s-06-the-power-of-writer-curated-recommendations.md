The Substack Recommendations feature demonstrates the power of a [[concepts/network-effects|network effect]] built on goodwill and curation, not algorithms. By allowing writers to manually recommend other newsletters, it creates a viral loop of high-intent discovery that has driven millions of new subscriptions, proving that a human-centric, opt-in system can be a massive growth engine.
