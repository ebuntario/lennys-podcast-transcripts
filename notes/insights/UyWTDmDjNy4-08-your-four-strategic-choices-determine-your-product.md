Four critical, interconnected choices shape your product and future: choose your customers, your competition, your source of validation, and your distribution. Most people gloss over these, sacrificing their agency. For example, choosing your [[concepts/customer-selection|smallest viable audience]] dictates the language, features, and problems your product will solve.
