AI can bridge communication gaps by translating sketches or vague ideas into clearer specifications. This turns AI into a [[concepts/ai-for-collaboration|collaboration tool]] that reduces back-and-forth and aligns stakeholders, creating a universal language for product development across different personas and expertise levels.
