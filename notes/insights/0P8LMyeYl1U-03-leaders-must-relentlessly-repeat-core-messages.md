You must repeat your core talking points far more often than you think is necessary. People fill in blanks with incorrect assumptions if you don't consistently reinforce the mission and key messages. This discipline of [[concepts/internal-communication|internal communication]], even when it feels repetitive, is crucial for alignment and motivation across a diverse team.
