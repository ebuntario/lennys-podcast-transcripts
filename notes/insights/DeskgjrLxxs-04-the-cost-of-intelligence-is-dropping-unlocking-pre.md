Smaller, smarter models are becoming faster and cheaper, increasing access to [[concepts/ai-cost-trends|AI intelligence]] for builders and users. This trend unblocks work constrained by intelligence costs, such as in healthcare diagnostics or education. The democratization of advanced reasoning capabilities will automate redundant tasks and enable new applications across industries.
