When a founder operates effectively in "founder mode," they deeply understand the business and can drive significant change. As a product leader, you can [[concepts/persuasion|activate this founder as an ally]] to champion and push through critical initiatives that require substantial organizational shift. This turns their unique power into a lever for [[concepts/strategic-execution|strategic execution]].
