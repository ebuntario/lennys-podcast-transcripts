The "busy bandwagon" is the pervasive cultural expectation that being busy and responsive is a sign of importance and competence. This internal pressure makes us default to reactive tasks like email and meetings. To break free, you must consciously reject this default and [[concepts/proactive-planning|prioritize proactive, meaningful work]] (Project A) over urgent, shallow tasks.
