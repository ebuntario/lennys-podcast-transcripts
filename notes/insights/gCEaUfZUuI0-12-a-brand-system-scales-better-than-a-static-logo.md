Building a [[concepts/brand-building|brand]] as a scalable system of visual elements, colors, fonts, and voice principles is more powerful than creating a single logo. This approach allows the brand to adapt and manifest consistently across diverse touchpoints, from product interfaces to marketing collateral, creating a cohesive and extensible identity.
