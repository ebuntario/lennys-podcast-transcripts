Your evaluation criteria and product requirements will evolve as you analyze real user interactions. You cannot dream up all failure modes upfront; new expectations and "product smells" emerge only after observing the AI's behavior in the wild. Therefore, [[concepts/ai-evals|evals]] should be a living system derived from continuous error analysis.
