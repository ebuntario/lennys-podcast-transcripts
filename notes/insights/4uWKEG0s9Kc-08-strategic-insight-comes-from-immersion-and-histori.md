[[concepts/strategic-insight|Strategic insight]]—the "aha" moment of seeing a way through a problem—is not magic. It arises from deeply immersing yourself in the nature of the challenge and studying [[concepts/historical-thinking|historical analogies]]. Since there is no precise science of strategy, understanding patterns from business, military, and political history provides a richer mental model for [[concepts/diagnosis|diagnosis]] and creative problem-solving.
