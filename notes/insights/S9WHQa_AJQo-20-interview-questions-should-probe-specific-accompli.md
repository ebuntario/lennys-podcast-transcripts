Effective interviews focus on candidates' most significant professional accomplishments rather than awards or promotions. Using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result), interviewers can spend an entire session exploring one substantial example. This [[concepts/interviewing|deep probing]] reveals how candidates approach problems, influence others, overcome obstacles, and achieve results.
