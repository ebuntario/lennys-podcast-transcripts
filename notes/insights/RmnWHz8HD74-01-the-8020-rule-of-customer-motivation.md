Approximately 80% of customers buy to avoid pain or reduce risk, rather than to increase upside. While founders love to talk about the "art of the possible," this vision-oriented sale primarily resonates with other founders. For most buyers, especially in [[concepts/enterprise-sales|enterprise sales]], the core motivation is avoiding the risk of missing revenue targets or being outdone by competitors.
