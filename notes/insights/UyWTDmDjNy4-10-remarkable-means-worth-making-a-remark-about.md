The word "remarkable" literally means worth making a remark about. To grow, build something where the user's life gets better if they talk about it. You should know in advance what you want them to say. This is the core of the "Purple Cow" concept: designing for [[concepts/remarkability|word-of-mouth]] is a primary growth strategy.
