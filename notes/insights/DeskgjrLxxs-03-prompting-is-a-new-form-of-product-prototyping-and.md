Designers and product managers can use [[concepts/prompting|prompting]] to prototype features like personalized starter prompts or conversation titles without building full implementations. This approach allows rapid experimentation with AI capabilities to explore new interaction paradigms. It democratizes early-stage product development by turning ideas into testable experiences quickly.
