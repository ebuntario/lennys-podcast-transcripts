The worst thing a leader can do is hesitate when faced with two bad options. The psychological muscle required for great [[concepts/leadership|leadership]] is the ability to look into the abyss and choose the slightly better path, even when everyone will criticize the decision. This ability to act decisively in impossible situations is where leaders add their unique value, as easy decisions would have been made without them.
