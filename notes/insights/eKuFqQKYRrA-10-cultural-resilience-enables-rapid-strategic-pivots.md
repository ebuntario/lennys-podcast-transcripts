Companies that excel at [[concepts/organizational-agility|strategic pivots]] cultivate cultures comfortable with throwing away plans when the world changes. This requires avoiding sunk-cost fallacy thinking and maintaining the humility to constantly ask "Is this still the most important thing we can do?" even after significant investment.
