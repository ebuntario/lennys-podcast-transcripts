Instead of writing extensive quality guidelines, Miro's design leadership reviews all shipped work monthly and classifies it as "high quality" or "not high quality." This creates a shared, evolving understanding of excellence through concrete examples, much like a [[concepts/reinforcement-learning|reinforcement learning]] system. It builds a collective muscle for quality judgment across the org.
