The quality of a product improves dramatically when the team building it uses it daily. This creates a sense of [[concepts/personal-accountability|personal accountability]], as engineers and designers feel directly responsible for the user experience. Getting creative to integrate your product into internal workflows (like using Figma for decks) ensures the team encounters and is motivated to fix issues.
