Think about press the same way you think about raising money: only do it when you know what it's for. Common goals include driving awareness for a new product, attracting investors, or establishing executive authority. Without a clear tactical purpose, pursuing press is often a waste of energy and doesn't contribute to meaningful [[concepts/goal-setting|goal setting]] for your business.
