In AI training projects, the top 10% of experts often drive the majority of model improvement, similar to how top performers drive impact in a company. Building a proprietary advantage in identifying and matching these top-tier individuals creates immense value for customers. This underscores the importance of [[concepts/talent-density|talent density]] over sheer volume in expert networks.
