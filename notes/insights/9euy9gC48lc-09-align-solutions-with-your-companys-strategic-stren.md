When solving a problem, evaluate whether the solution aligns with your company's core competencies and makes economic sense. A platform company attempting an operations-heavy solution may fail because it lacks the necessary muscle and the [[concepts/unit-economics|unit economics]] don't scale. It's better to leverage inherent strengths (like user reviews) and find tailored, cost-effective solutions for specific issues rather than applying a single, expensive, blunt instrument.
