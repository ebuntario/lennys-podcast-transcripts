In one-on-ones, have individuals list their priorities and then allocate a hypothetical $10 across them to represent their actual time investment. This visual exercise quickly reveals misalignment between stated priorities and time spent, highlighting areas of overload or underinvestment. It's a powerful tool for [[concepts/priority-management|recalibrating focus]] on the work that moves the business forward.
