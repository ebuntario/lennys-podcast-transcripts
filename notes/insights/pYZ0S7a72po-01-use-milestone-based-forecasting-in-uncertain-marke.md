When visibility is limited, create a conservative annual plan but establish short-term milestones that unlock the ability to lean into growth and spending. This approach prevents floundering between extremes of optimism and pessimism. Pre-determine decisions with your board and team based on hitting or missing these checkpoints to avoid founder [[concepts/optimism-bias|optimism bias]] during volatile periods.
