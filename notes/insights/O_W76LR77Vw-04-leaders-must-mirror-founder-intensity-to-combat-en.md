The purest and most intense source of energy in a business is typically the founder CEO. Each concentric layer of management risks an order-of-magnitude drop in that intensity, which is dangerous for the organization. A leader's job is not to buffer teams from this intensity but to [[concepts/leadership-intensity|mirror and preserve it]] at the highest possible level to fight organizational entropy and decay.
