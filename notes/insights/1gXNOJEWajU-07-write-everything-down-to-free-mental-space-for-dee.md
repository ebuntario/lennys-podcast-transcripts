A core productivity tactic is to get all tasks out of your head and onto a list. Use a system like the Eisenhower Matrix (Do, Defer, Delegate, Delete) to process this list daily. This [[concepts/mind-like-water|mind like water]] state clears cognitive load, reduces stress, and creates space for the deep thinking and problem-solving required in product roles.
