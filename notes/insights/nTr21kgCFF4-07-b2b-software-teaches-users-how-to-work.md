Adopting a B2B tool means adopting the processes and best practices embedded within it. The tool sets a baseline level of organizational competency. Therefore, building an [[concepts/product-opinions|opinionated product]] is about codifying and automating battle-tested practices from high-performing teams, not just solving isolated problems.
