When trying to drive product innovation in a stagnant organization, start by identifying the [[concepts/sacred-cows|sacred cows]]—the features or rules everyone believes are untouchable. These perceived constraints often represent the most fertile ground for impactful change. By systematically questioning and experimenting with these sacred elements, you can unlock new possibilities and shift the team's mindset about what's possible.
