To build effective tools for creators, you must become a user of your own product. This [[concepts/dogfooding|dogfooding]] builds deep [[concepts/empathy|user empathy]] that data and user interviews alone cannot provide. When product team members create their own podcasts, they experience the emotional journey and practical barriers firsthand, leading to better prioritization and advocacy for user-centric features.
