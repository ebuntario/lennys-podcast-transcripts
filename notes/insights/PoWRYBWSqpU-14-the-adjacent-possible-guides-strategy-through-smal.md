The [[concepts/adjacent-possible|adjacent possible]] is the set of actions immediately available that are almost certain to work. Instead of risky leaps, strategy should focus on taking these small, safe steps that reconfigure the landscape for future moves. Combine this with a low-resolution, plausible [[concepts/north-star-metric|North Star]] vision to arc toward transformative outcomes while managing [[concepts/risk-management|risk]].
