The core principles of management—defining clear outcomes, assembling the right resources, and establishing effective processes—are the same skills needed to work well with AI agents. You must be crystal clear on what success looks like, understand the different strengths of various [[concepts/ai-management|AI models and tools]], and design the right workflows to achieve your goal, just as you would with a human team.
