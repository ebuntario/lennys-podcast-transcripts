The [[concepts/builder-mindset|builder mindset]] focuses on executing a predefined plan, which creates linear value limited by the effort invested. In contrast, the [[concepts/gardening-mindset|gardening mindset]] involves planting seeds of potential and nurturing what grows, allowing for exponential outcomes that can appear magical. This approach embraces [[concepts/emergent-strategy|emergent strategy]] by curating organic growth rather than forcing rigid plans.
