When functional experience is similar among many candidates, deep [[concepts/industry-knowledge|industry knowledge]] becomes a powerful differentiator. For example, a product manager applying for a role in automotive tech can stand out by demonstrating understanding of that specific vertical, even if their direct PM experience is in another domain. This signals faster ramp-up time and deeper user empathy.
