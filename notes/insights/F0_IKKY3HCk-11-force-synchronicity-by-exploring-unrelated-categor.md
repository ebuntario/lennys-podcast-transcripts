To generate unexpected name ideas, deliberately explore unrelated categories. If naming a tech product, look through hunting or sailing magazines for words and expressions that spark connections. This [[concepts/forced-synchronicity|forced synchronicity]] creates novel associations that wouldn't emerge from direct category thinking, often yielding the most distinctive and imaginative names.
