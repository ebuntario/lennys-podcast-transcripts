Many business processes remain asynchronous not due to necessity but simply because "that's the way the world is." Building products that deliver [[concepts/consumer-value|instant value]]—like immediate money availability or instant card issuance—creates a powerful competitive wedge. This focus on removing friction and delay can become a core, defensible product advantage.
