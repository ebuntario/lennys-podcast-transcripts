Keeping a "problem journal" to note daily annoyances and inefficiencies is a powerful method for [[concepts/idea-generation|uncovering startup ideas]]. The key is to simply document the problem without immediately jumping to a solution, allowing patterns and genuine opportunities to emerge over time from personal experience.
