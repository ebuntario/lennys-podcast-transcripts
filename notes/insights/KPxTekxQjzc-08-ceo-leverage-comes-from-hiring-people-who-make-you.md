As a CEO, you cannot develop world-class expertise in every function you oversee. Instead of trying to make people great, hire people who are already great and from whom you can learn. [[concepts/managerial-leverage|Managerial leverage]] occurs when your executives tell you how to push the company forward, not when you're constantly directing their departments.
