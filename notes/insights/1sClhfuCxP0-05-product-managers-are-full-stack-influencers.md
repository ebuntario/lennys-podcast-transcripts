A product manager's core function is [[concepts/persuasion|influence]]. They must influence engineers to build, leadership to approve, and ultimately, users to change their behavior. Viewing the role as a **full-stack influencer** expands the scope beyond internal [[concepts/relationship-management|stakeholder management]] to include directly shaping user decisions, akin to marketing and sales.
