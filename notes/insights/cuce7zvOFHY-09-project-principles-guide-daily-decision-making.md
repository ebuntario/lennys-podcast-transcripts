Translate your core differentiators into simple, actionable project principles (e.g., "fast is better than slow" or "helps sellers help each other"). These principles act as a [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making guide]] for the team when evaluating feature ideas or design choices long after the sprint ends. They ensure daily work remains aligned with the foundational strategy and differentiation.
