The path to building something great is messy and often involves setbacks. Persistence—"bloody-mindedness"—is a critical but underrated trait. It's important to be clear-eyed about the odds and open to feedback, but also tenacious in holding onto your core vision. As the saying goes, "Just because I'm losing doesn't mean I've lost."
