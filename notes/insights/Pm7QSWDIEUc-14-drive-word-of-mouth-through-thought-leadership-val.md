Companies can accelerate [[concepts/word-of-mouth|word-of-mouth]] by empowering technical experts to be thought leaders in existing communities, creating a strong content strategy that provides real value (like HubSpot's certifications), and building their own user communities. Atlassian's secret was facilitating local user groups—paying for pizza and beer—where customers could connect, share knowledge, and naturally advocate for the product.
