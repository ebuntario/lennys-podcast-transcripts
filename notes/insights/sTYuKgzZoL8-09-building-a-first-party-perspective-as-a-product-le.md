Product managers must synthesize input from various functions into their own unique, informed perspective. This requires intentionally blocking dedicated time for deep thinking, which may involve solo reflection or discussions with a trusted sparring partner. Developing this [[concepts/systems-thinking|strategic thinking]] muscle is essential and cannot be outsourced to process management alone.
