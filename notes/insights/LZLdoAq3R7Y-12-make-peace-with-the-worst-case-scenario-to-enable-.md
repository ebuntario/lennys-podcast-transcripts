Before embarking on a new venture, practice [[concepts/negative-visualization|negative visualization]]: role-play the worst that could happen and get to a point of peace with that outcome. For example, if a new product fails, the cost might be six months of work and learning. Being at peace with potential failure removes fear and allows you to move forward and experiment boldly.
