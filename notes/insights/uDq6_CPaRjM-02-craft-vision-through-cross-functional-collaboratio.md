Effective visioning requires cross-pollination of ideas from research, design, and engineering from the start, not a linear process. A strong vision is rooted in user insights, visualized through designs and prototypes, and grounded in technical feasibility. This [[concepts/cross-functional-collaboration|collaborative approach]] leads to more innovative and achievable visions than working in silos.
