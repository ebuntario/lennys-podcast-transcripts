A simple, sincere thank you—without asking for anything in return—can leave a lasting positive impression. This practice, modeled from artist culture, focuses on appreciation rather than transaction. It fosters [[concepts/relationship-building|genuine connections]] that can open doors and build a reputation as a thoughtful professional.
