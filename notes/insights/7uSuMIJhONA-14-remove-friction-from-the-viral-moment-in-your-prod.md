Identify the exact moment in your product where sharing naturally occurs, and eliminate any barriers there. For Coda, this meant adopting "Maker Billing" so sharing a doc doesn't trigger costs for recipients. This [[concepts/growth-loops|growth-first]] pricing aligns with how document platforms spread virally through sharing, not through gating collaboration.
