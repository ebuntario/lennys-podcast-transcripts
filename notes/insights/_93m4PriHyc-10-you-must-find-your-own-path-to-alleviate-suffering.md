Due to the immense heterogeneity of human experience, there is no one-size-fits-all solution for psychological suffering. The core message is to find your own unique path, as following someone else's road may not lead you to your necessary destination. This [[concepts/personal-journey|personal journey]] of [[concepts/self-discovery|self-discovery]] is the fundamental work required for healing.
