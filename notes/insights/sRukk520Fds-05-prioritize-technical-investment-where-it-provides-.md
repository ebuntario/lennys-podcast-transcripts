In early-stage companies with operational components, technical resources are scarce and must be invested with extreme intentionality. The focus should be on areas where technology provides the highest leverage for the core business, such as a dispatching or pricing system. This requires being transparent about what you are *not* building to conserve resources for the highest-impact [[concepts/technical-leverage]].
