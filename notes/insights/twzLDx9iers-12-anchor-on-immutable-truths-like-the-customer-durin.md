In a tornado of organizational change, stabilize yourself by focusing on immutable compass points like serving the customer. While internal structures and priorities shift, the customer's needs remain a constant truth. This [[concepts/customer-centricity|customer-centric]] anchor provides clarity and direction when everything else feels unstable, guiding daily decisions and maintaining alignment amid chaos.
