Effective talent evaluation focuses on what people can do exceptionally well rather than fixating on their weaknesses or past failures. When assessing founders or executives, look for world-class strengths that can beat the competition, then help them manage their flaws through complementary team members and coaching. This [[concepts/talent-evaluation|strength-based approach]] uncovers potential that weakness-focused analysis misses.
