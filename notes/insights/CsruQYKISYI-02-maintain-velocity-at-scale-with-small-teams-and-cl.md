To maintain speed as an organization grows, break large problems into small, autonomous teams with crystal-clear missions. This minimizes the communication overhead and complexity that slows down large groups. A key enabler is investing in a clear, easy-to-use [[concepts/platform-strategy|platform]] that reduces decision-making complexity for teams building on top of it.
