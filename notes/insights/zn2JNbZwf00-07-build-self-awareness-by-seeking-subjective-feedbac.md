Objective feedback on work output is common, but subjective feedback on your interpersonal impact is rare and valuable. Proactively ask trusted colleagues how you make them feel in interactions (e.g., "Do I ever come across as angry or disengaged?"). This builds crucial [[concepts/self-awareness|self-awareness]] about blind spots that can hinder [[concepts/emotional-intelligence|EQ]] and leadership growth.
