Systematically looking at real user interaction traces is the most valuable thing you can do to improve an AI product. This [[concepts/error-analysis|error analysis]] process uncovers unexpected failures, inspires product improvements, and grounds all subsequent work. The time investment is minimal compared to the insights gained.
