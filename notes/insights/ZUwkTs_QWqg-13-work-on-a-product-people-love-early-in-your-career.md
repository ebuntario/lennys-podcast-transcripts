Early exposure to a product that generates genuine user passion (e.g., a community that drives hundreds of miles to see it) creates an invaluable internal benchmark for quality. This experience teaches what it feels like to build something people truly love and sets a [[concepts/product-quality|high bar]] for future work, providing a reference point for the effort and empathy required to achieve that level of impact.
