Successful digital transformations must address how strategy is set, how teams are organized, and how products are launched. Simply adopting Scrum or SAFe addresses only the development operating model, leaving gaps in [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]], customer research, and go-to-market execution. Leaders need to build a holistic product operating model that enables teams to deliver value.
