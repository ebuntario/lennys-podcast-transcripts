Companies can drive significant acquisition by incentivizing existing network participants to act as salespeople. For example, offering drivers a bonus to convert cash-paying riders to a digital wallet created a powerful [[concepts/cross-selling|cross-selling]] loop. This approach leverages captive attention and trust within an existing transaction to introduce new services.
