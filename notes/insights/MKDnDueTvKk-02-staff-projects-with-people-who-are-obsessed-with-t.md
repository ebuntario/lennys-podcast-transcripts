For speculative or risky projects, success depends heavily on staffing them with people who genuinely believe in the idea. Teams filled with skeptics or those assigned based solely on availability will lack the necessary drive and creativity. You need individuals with an [[concepts/entrepreneurial-mindset|entrepreneurial mindset]] who are willing to will the project into existence through challenges and uncertainty.
