A healthy product organization allocates its resources across three categories: 70% to the core product (including maintenance and growth), 20% to strategic initiatives adjacent to the core, and 10% to speculative bets. This [[concepts/portfolio-thinking|portfolio approach]] ensures stability while dedicating deliberate resources to future innovation and strategic expansion.
