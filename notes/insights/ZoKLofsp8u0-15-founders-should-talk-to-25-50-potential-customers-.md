Only about 10% of any customer group are [[concepts/early-adopters|early adopters]] willing to try something new. To find them, founders must reach out to a much larger pool and expect a low response rate. Talking to 25-50 people is a realistic volume to gain meaningful insights, and this outreach doubles as early sales work since those who engage are likely to become first customers.
