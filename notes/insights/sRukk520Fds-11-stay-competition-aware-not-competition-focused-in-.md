In massive, non-saturated markets like transportation or real estate, the real competition is often the default behavior (e.g., traditional home selling), not another tech company. The best strategy is to stay deeply focused on serving your core customer well rather than being distracted by competitive moves. This [[concepts/customer-centricity]] provides confidence because the size of the prize is so large.
