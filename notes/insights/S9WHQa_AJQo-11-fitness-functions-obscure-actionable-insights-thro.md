Compound metrics that combine multiple important measurements into single indexes create meaningless abstractions. These [[concepts/metric-aggregation|fitness functions]] prevent teams from understanding which specific actions drive outcomes. Instead, metrics should remain separate so teams can identify causal relationships between specific improvements and business results.
