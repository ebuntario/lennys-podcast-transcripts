Many creators, especially musicians, have a "starving artist" ethos that makes them reluctant to charge their fans, even when fans are willing to pay. This emotional attachment to their art can prevent them from seeing the [[concepts/value-exchange|market value]] of their work. Platforms can help by handling pricing and payments, facilitating a transaction that benefits both creators and their supporters.
