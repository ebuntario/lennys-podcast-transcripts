Top performers internally disqualify prospects by looking for signals they won't buy, avoiding "happy ears" that waste time on dead-end deals. Externally, they maintain a positive, energetic demeanor. This [[concepts/qualification-discipline|qualification discipline]] allows them to prioritize time on genuine opportunities while still building strong relationships with all prospects.
