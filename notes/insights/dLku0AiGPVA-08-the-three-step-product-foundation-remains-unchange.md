The fundamental steps to building products are unchanged: identify a user problem, apply technology to solve it, and find a mechanism to reach people who have that problem. The current AI era is unique because the technology step has unlocked solutions to previously unsolvable problems, creating new opportunities. However, the core discipline of solving real problems and finding the audience remains critical.
