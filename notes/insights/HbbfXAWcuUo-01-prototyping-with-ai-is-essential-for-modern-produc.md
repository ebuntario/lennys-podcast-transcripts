In the current AI era, if you're not [[concepts/ai-prototyping|prototyping and building to see what you want to build]], you're doing product development wrong. Prompt sets are becoming the new PRDs, serving as a high-bandwidth way to communicate and experience ideas quickly. This approach accelerates the feedback loop and moves teams from concept to tangible demo faster than traditional methods.
