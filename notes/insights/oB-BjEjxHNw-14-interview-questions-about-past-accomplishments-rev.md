Asking a candidate to describe something they're proud of and walk through the process uncovers their work ethic, what they value, and their definition of success. This [[concepts/behavioral-interviewing|behavioral insight]] is crucial for understanding how they'll operate within a team. It reveals more about their potential fit than hypothetical or technical questions alone.
