If you want to become a better manager, start by helping your PMs create a development plan, even if you lack a perfect company-wide competency framework. Most PMs have an idea of what they want to learn or improve, such as storytelling or prioritization. Using this as a starting point for [[concepts/skill-gap-analysis|skill-gap analysis]] and action is more effective than waiting for a harmonized company compass.
