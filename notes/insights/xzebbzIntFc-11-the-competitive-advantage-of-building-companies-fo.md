Companies built with a core ethos of promoting human flourishing gain a significant [[concepts/product-market-leadership|competitive advantage]]. When a company's central premise is a clear, values-based promise—like treating customers as you would your own parents—it creates immense [[concepts/customer-loyalty|customer loyalty]] and internal alignment. This focus on creating net new value, rather than extractive profit, often leads to superior financial performance and resilience.
