Leaders like Steve Jobs were deeply involved in curating every element of a product demo, from the music library to the synced devices. This hands-on [[concepts/executive-involvement|attention to detail]] ensures that the presentation perfectly embodies the brand's quality and vision. It sets a standard that elevates the entire [[concepts/user-experience|user experience]] from the first touchpoint.
