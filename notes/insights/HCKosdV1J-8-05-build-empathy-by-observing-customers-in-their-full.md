To develop true [[concepts/empathy|empathy]], product leaders should spend time shoulder-to-shoulder with customers, observing them not just using the product but going about their entire day. This provides context for how the product fits amidst other tasks, meetings, and distractions. This humbling experience builds better intuition and allows makers to effectively "be the customer" when creating solutions.
