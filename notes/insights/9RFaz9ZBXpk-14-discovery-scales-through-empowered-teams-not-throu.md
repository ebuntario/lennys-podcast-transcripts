The fundamental unit of discovery—an empowered trio with an outcome—remains the same as companies grow. Scaling requires managing dependencies and ensuring coherent experiences across teams through shared discovery and design systems, not changing how individual teams operate. Lateral collaboration replaces hierarchical control in mature organizations.
