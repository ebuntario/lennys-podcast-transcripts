The lines between product manager, designer, and engineer are intentionally blurry, as each role should possess some competency in the others' domains. A good [[concepts/product-management|product manager]] needs more than just process; they need a point of view, strategy, and the ability to bring everyone together with frameworks that create a shared understanding of the goal. The ultimate success metric is not just hitting a milestone, but doing so with a team that is stoked and cohesive.
