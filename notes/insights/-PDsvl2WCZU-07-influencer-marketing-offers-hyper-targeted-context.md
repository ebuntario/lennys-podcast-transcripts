Influencer marketing excels at hyper-targeted, contextual reach—similar to early Facebook's granular targeting—but currently involves significant manual relationship management. Tools like Grin help with discovery, CRM, and measurement, yet the process remains tedious. This channel is particularly valuable as [[concepts/targeted-marketing|precision targeting]] becomes harder on traditional platforms, but scaling requires investment in both technology and human coordination.
