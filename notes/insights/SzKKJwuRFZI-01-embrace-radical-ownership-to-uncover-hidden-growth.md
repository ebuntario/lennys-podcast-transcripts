Adopting a mindset where every problem is your problem can reveal significant growth opportunities that the business hasn't explicitly prioritized. By taking [[concepts/ownership-mentality|radical accountability]] for neglected areas—like HubSpot's self-service revenue stream—teams can demonstrate initiative and create outsized impact. This proactive approach builds credibility and expands your team's influence as leadership recognizes your ability to drive meaningful results.
