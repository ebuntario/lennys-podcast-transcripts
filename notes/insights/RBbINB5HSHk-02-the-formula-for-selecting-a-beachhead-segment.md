A viable beachhead segment must be big enough to matter (providing a path to significant revenue), small enough for you to lead (achieving 30-50% market share), and a good fit with your "crown jewels" (core technology). This [[concepts/beachhead-segment|beachhead segment]] should allow you to become the dominant player, attracting partners and creating a repeatable business model without needing to discount.
