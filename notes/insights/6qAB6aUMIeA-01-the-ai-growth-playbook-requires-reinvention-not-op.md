In fast-moving AI categories, traditional [[concepts/growth-strategy|growth strategies]] focused on optimizing existing user journeys become less effective. Success requires investing in bigger bets and creating new growth loops, shifting from spending 5% on innovation to 95%. The competitive landscape demands reinventing solutions rather than just optimizing problems.
