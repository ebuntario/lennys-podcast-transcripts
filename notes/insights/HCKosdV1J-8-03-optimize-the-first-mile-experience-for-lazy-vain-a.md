The [[concepts/first-mile-experience|first mile]] of a product—the onboarding, orientation, and defaults—is critical because new users are lazy, vain, and selfish. They want to accomplish their goal quickly, look good to others, and feel successful immediately. Most teams spend the final mile of development considering this, but world-class teams anchor on getting users through this top of the funnel using psychological principles.
