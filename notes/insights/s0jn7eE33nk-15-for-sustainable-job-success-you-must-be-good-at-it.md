Whether as a founder or employee, lasting success requires three aligned factors: competence in the role, genuine enjoyment of the work, and market demand for the output. If one is missing, you'll eventually struggle or leave. This [[concepts/job-fit|framework]] helps diagnose career dissatisfaction and guide role choices.
