High-performance companies cultivate a laser focus on [[concepts/impact|impact]] rather than rewarding activity or hours worked. This means constantly asking whether strategies and work are driving toward the top-level north star outcome. A culture obsessed with measurable impact, rather than effort, turns teams into execution machines that make real progress on the mission.
