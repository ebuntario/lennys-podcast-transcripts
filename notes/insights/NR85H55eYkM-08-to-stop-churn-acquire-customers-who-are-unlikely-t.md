The most effective way to reduce [[concepts/churn-reduction|churn]] is proactively: analyze which customer segments have the highest longevity and focus your acquisition efforts on attracting more of those profiles. Reactive churn prevention, like offering discounts when a customer wants to leave, is often too late and only provides a temporary stay.
