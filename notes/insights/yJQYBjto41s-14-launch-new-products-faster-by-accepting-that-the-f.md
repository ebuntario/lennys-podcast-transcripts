Founders should prioritize getting a product to market that shows a compelling vision, rather than perfecting every detail. Figma's long pre-launch period is cited as a mistake; the goal is to demonstrate potential quickly. This [[concepts/go-to-market|go-to-market]] approach allows for faster learning and iteration based on real user feedback.
