Instituting a culture where major communication—strategy, reviews, planning—happens through writing forces clarity of thought. You cannot be a good writer without being a clear thinker. This practice, as seen at Stripe, elevates the overall [[concepts/critical-thinking|rigor of thinking]] across an organization and becomes a key mechanism for scaling effective communication and decision-making.
