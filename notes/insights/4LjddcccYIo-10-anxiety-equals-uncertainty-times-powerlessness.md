Most anxiety stems from what you don't know combined with what you can't control. Create an "anxiety balance sheet" with four columns: what you know, what you don't know, what you can influence, and what you can't. This [[concepts/emotional-intelligence|emotional intelligence]] exercise makes free-floating anxiety tangible and manageable.
