In any user base, some people are natural connectors who love to share. Place opportunities to invite others early in the [[concepts/onboarding|user onboarding]] flow and throughout the product to capture this behavior. While some users may say they wouldn't invite without seeing the product first, social users will act if given the chance, driving [[concepts/viral-growth|viral growth]].
