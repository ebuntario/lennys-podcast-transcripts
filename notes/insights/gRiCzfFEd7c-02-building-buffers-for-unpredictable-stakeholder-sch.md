When stakeholders (like journalists) have volatile priorities, always build buffers into your project timelines. The size of the buffer should scale with the scope of work and the likelihood of disruption. This approach acknowledges that your users' primary job takes precedence over your research or testing sessions, requiring flexible [[concepts/planning|planning]].
