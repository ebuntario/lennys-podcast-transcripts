A background in philosophy and literature fosters skills in dialogue, skepticism, and understanding how people communicate and create. These [[concepts/humanities-in-tech|humanities skills]] are directly applicable to product management, especially when navigating complex ethical landscapes, community relations, and designing for human creativity within technological systems.
