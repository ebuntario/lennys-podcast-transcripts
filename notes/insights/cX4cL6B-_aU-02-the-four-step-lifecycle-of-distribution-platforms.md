New [[concepts/distribution-platforms|distribution platforms]] follow a predictable four-step cycle: market consensus forms, a moat is identified, the platform opens to third parties with incentives, and finally closes for control and monetization. Understanding this [[concepts/platform-lifecycle|platform lifecycle]] is essential for timing your entry and exit strategies.
