Create a structured ops cadence (e.g., a spreadsheet) that outlines weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly tasks across key areas like finance, keywords, ad copy, and targeting. This system ensures nothing is missed, holds the team accountable, and provides clear answers to stakeholders about how often optimizations occur. It turns reactive management into a proactive [[concepts/operational-cadence|operational rhythm]].
