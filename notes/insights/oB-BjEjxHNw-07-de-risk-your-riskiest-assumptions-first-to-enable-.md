Using a dual-track [[concepts/agile-frameworks|agile approach]], teams should prioritize validating their biggest, most uncertain ideas before executing on safer bets. This method of [[concepts/risk-prioritization|de-risking]] allows for true innovation by confronting potential failures early. Leaders must create a culture where teams have permission to test and learn from these high-risk assumptions.
