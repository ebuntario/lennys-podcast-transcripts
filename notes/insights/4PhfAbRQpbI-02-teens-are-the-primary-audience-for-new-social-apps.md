For every social app, the number of invitations sent per user drops 20% for each additional year of age from 13 to 18. Teens have malleable habits, see each other daily, and have a high urgency to communicate, making them the ideal audience for new [[concepts/network-effects|network effects]] products. If you build for adults, you will likely need to acquire every user through paid ads.
