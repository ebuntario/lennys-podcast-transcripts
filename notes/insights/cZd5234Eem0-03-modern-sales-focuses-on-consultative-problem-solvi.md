Modern sales is about identifying prospects who have a high proclivity for your solution, not convincing uninterested parties. The seller acts as a consultant, using discovery questions to reveal a prospect's pain points and then presenting a solution. This contrasts with old-school "selling ice to an Eskimo" and aligns with a clear [[concepts/buyer-personas|ideal customer profile]].
