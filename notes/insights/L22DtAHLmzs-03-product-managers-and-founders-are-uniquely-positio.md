The core skill set of a great [[concepts/product-management|product manager]]—defining scope, articulating requirements clearly, and understanding user needs—directly translates to effectively directing AI development agents. As [[concepts/ai-product-development|AI tools]] make building software easier, the bottleneck shifts from writing code to clearly specifying what to build and why, making product thinking more valuable than ever.
