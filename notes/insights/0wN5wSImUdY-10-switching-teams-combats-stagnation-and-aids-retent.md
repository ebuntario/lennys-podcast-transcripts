The Switching pattern, where people move between teams, addresses the "stagnating" phase often missing from traditional team models. Allowing employees to work with new people on different systems can feel like a new job within the same company, renewing their energy and extending their tenure. This intentional [[concepts/professional-growth|career development]] strategy boosts fulfillment and retention.
