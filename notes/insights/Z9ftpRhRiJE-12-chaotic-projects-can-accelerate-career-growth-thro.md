While stressful in the moment, chaotic projects or company downturns can create unique opportunities for rapid [[concepts/professional-growth|career development]]. Being thrust into leadership roles during crises forces accelerated learning and problem-solving. These experiences often form the foundational kernel of a long-term career, even if the business itself doesn't succeed.
