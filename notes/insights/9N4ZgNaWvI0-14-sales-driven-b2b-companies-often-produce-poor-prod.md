Many B2B software companies operate as feature factories driven by sales requests, resulting in products that customers often dislike. While this model can generate revenue, it fails to create beloved, innovative products. Companies that transform to a [[concepts/customer-centricity|customer-centric]], empowered product model can dramatically improve their offerings and better serve both customers and sales teams.
