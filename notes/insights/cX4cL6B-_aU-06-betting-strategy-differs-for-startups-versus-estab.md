Late-stage companies can afford to place multiple bets across emerging platforms, while [[concepts/startup-strategy|startups]] must go all-in on a single platform due to scarce resources. Startups should choose one platform and focus intensely, as spreading resources too thin across multiple platforms typically leads to failure.
