When foundation models reach sufficient scale, further [[concepts/pre-training]] becomes economically inefficient compared to [[concepts/post-training]]. Companies gain more leverage by fine-tuning existing models with proprietary or synthetic data to optimize for specific outcomes. This shift makes reinforcement learning and reward modeling critical product techniques for steering model behavior.
