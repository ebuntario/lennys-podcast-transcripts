A powerful interview question for product roles is "Tell me about the last time you...", such as the last time they conducted user interviews or onboarded a colleague. This [[concepts/behavioral-interviewing|behavioral framing]] prompts candidates to share concrete, recent examples, sparking detailed conversations that reveal their actual practices, thought processes, and [[concepts/skill-assessment|skills]] more effectively than hypothetical questions.
