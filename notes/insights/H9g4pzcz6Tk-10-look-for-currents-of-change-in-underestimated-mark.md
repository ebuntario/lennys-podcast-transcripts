Great marketplace opportunities often start in markets others dismiss as too small. The key is to identify a "current"—a dynamic change or unmet need—within that niche. Founders with an "earned secret" insight into such a market, coupled with intense focus, can build a dominant position and often discover the market is larger than it initially appeared.
