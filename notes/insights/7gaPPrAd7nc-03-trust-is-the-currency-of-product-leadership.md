[[concepts/psychological-safety|Trust]] is the essential currency for product managers and leaders, especially as they grow. It’s built by repeatedly setting and meeting expectations—calling your shots, forecasting, and hitting your goals. Trust directly influences your ability to gain resources and rally support, while behaviors like evasiveness or not shipping what you promised will erode it.
