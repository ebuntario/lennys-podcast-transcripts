A key function of product operations is to streamline [[concepts/go-to-market|go-to-market]] processes by breaking down silos between product, sales, and marketing. This involves creating standardized templates, establishing clear communication cadences, and ensuring consistent processes across the organization. This coordination prevents each product team from reinventing the wheel and improves alignment on launches.
