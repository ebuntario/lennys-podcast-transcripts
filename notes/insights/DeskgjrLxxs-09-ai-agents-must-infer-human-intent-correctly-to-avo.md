Agents operating autonomously need to know when to ask follow-up questions versus completing a task independently. Misunderstanding intent can lead to agents working for minutes on an undesired outcome, degrading [[concepts/trust|trust]]. Teaching models to build mental models of users and care about their preferences is key to effective assistance.
