The absence of a review (silence) often contains meaningful signal about a transaction's quality, sometimes more than a left review. Designing systems to increase review rates, like double-blind prompts, captures more of this passive data. Analyzing engagement metrics alongside explicit ratings can provide a more accurate [[concepts/reputation-systems|reputation assessment]].
