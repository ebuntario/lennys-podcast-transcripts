The most successful AI product builders spend the majority of their time deeply understanding customer workflows and examining the data, not building the fanciest models. AI is a tool to solve a problem; [[concepts/user-centric-design|customer obsession]] and workflow deconstruction are the primary differentiators for creating products that truly work.
