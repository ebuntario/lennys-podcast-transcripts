A culture of radical [[concepts/information-transparency|information transparency]] allows talented people to be fully leveraged, as they can act on correct and complete data. While this requires individuals to develop systems to manage signal from noise, it enables phenomenal bottom-up work by empowering people with the context needed to make good decisions within a strategic framework.
