Your [[concepts/differentiated-value|differentiated value]] is the unique benefit only you can deliver, derived from your distinctive capabilities. It answers the fundamental buyer question of why to choose you over competitors. For example, LevelJump's value was "measuring sales enablement impact on revenue" because they were built on Salesforce.
