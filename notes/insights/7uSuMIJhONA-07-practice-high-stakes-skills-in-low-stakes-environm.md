You can develop critical skills like eigenquestion identification by practicing in frivolous, hypothetical scenarios. For example, the "teleporter device" interview question lets candidates practice discerning the most leverage-giving questions without pressure. This approach builds [[concepts/skill-development|muscle memory]] for strategic thinking that transfers to real, high-stakes decisions.
