AI tools rarely work "off the shelf" for large companies with legacy codebases and unique contexts. Successful integration requires rearchitecting core platforms so AI can reason over them and heavily [[concepts/tool-customization|customizing third-party tools]]. This upfront [[concepts/ai-platform-investment|platform investment]] is a prerequisite for reaping later benefits in velocity and quality.
