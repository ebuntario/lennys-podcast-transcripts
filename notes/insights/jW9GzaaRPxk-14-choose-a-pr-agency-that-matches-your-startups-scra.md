When selecting a [[concepts/pr-agency-selection|PR agency]], prioritize those experienced with startups and a hands-on, fast-paced approach over large agencies used to reactive strategies for household brands. Ask for recent writing samples to assess their style, understand what month one entails, and meet the day-to-day team you'll work with.
