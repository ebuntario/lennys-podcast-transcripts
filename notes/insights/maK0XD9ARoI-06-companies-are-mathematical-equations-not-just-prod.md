Every company represents a mathematical formula that describes how investment converts into returns over time. While product execution influences variables in this equation, if the foundational [[concepts/business-model-math|business model math]] is broken, no amount of iteration can fix it. Understanding this equation helps identify which product optimizations actually move the needle on business outcomes.
