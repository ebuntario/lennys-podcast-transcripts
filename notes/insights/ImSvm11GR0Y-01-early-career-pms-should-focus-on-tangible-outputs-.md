Early in your product management career, prioritize producing clear, tangible outputs like shipping features, running experiments, or creating analysis documents. This builds credibility and demonstrates your ability to execute, which is more valuable than trying to drive high-level strategic impact immediately. As you progress, you can layer on outcome ownership while maintaining strong [[concepts/product-execution|execution skills]].
