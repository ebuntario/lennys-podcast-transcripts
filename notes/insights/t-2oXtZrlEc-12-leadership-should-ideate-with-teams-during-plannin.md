Instead of a top-down or purely bottoms-up planning process, leadership should participate in team ideation sessions. By joining jam sessions and contributing Figma ideas, leaders create high-bandwidth communication, increase team confidence in bets, and ensure top-to-bottom [[concepts/alignment|alignment]] on strategic decisions.
