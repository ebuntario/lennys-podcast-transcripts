Establish clear principles to navigate conflicts between what's best for the customer and what's best for the business. For example, a marketplace might adopt a "customers first, pros second, business last" [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] to ensure long-term health. This framework prevents local optimization (like raising prices for short-term gain) that can damage the broader ecosystem.
