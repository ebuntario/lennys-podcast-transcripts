In the earliest stages, the founder is the product because the actual product may be abstract or an MVP. The founder's novel insight and vision excite the market and allow you to learn what parts of that vision the market accepts, aligning founder vision with market reality. This process is about [[concepts/founder-led-sales|founder-led sales]] to collect research, not just generate immediate revenue.
