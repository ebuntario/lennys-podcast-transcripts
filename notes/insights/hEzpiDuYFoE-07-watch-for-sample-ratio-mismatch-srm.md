A [[concepts/experiment-validity|Sample Ratio Mismatch (SRM)]]—where the actual traffic split deviates from the designed split—is a major red flag indicating an invalid experiment. Common causes include bots or data pipeline issues; implementing automatic SRM checks can prevent trusting flawed results.
