Cold email response rates are at historic lows. Leverage your existing customer base for warm introductions to new prospects. When securing an intro, ask for a text message connection instead of email, and keep the introducer on the thread initially to add social pressure and increase meeting show rates. [[concepts/text-messaging|Text messaging]] is where deals get done; email is where they go to die.
