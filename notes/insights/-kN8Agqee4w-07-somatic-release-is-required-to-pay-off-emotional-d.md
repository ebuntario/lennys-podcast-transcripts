Talk therapy alone often fails to release stored emotional debt because the issue is held in the body. To truly pay it off, you need somatic practices that allow the body to complete stuck stress cycles. This involves feeling and releasing stored emotions through modalities like [[concepts/somatic-therapy|somatic experiencing]], breathwork, or guided body awareness.
