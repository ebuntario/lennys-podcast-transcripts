Theoretical strategy alone is insufficient; [[concepts/strategy-execution|execution]] and action are the starting points. Strategic frameworks provide guideposts for the journey, but value is created through doing, experimenting, and adapting. Life and business are full of surprises, and you often end up in a place you didn't initially expect.
