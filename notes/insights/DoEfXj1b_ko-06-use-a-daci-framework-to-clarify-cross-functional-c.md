A "flying formation" for how teams work together prevents confusion and stepping on toes. Implementing a [[concepts/decision-making|DACI framework]] (Driver, Accountable, Contributor, Informed) clarifies roles, especially the critical distinction between who drives a project and who is the ultimate decision-maker. Combining this with defined operating rhythms (like weekly metric reviews) creates a clear structure for effective [[concepts/cross-functional-collaboration|cross-functional collaboration]].
