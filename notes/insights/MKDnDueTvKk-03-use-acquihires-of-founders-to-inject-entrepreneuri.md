A powerful strategy for driving cultural change and ambitious bets is to acquire small companies led by hungry founders and give them ownership of new initiatives. These [[concepts/entrepreneurial-leaders|entrepreneurial leaders]] bring urgency, ambition, and the ability to operate both within and around existing systems. They can rally teams and navigate the complexities of a large organization to bring speculative visions to life.
