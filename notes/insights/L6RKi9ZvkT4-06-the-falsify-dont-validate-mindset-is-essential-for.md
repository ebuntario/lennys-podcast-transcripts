Teams should approach research with the goal of falsifying their assumptions, not validating them. A [[concepts/learning-mindset|learning mindset]] that actively seeks to be wrong exposes blind spots and leads to better product decisions. In contrast, a validation-seeking approach is often a form of user-centered performance, driven by ego and [[concepts/cognitive-biases|cognitive biases]] like confirmation bias.
