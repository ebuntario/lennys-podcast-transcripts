The core of [[concepts/working-backwards|working backwards]] is starting with the customer problem—not the solution. Many teams get it wrong by retrofitting a problem to a pre-conceived solution or combining existing “ingredients” because they can. True working backwards means being unable to process any information until you’re grounded in a compelling customer problem.
