A truly powerful goal should feel intimidating and make you feel inadequate, as this creates the drive to work extraordinarily hard to achieve it. Unlike reasonable goals, a [[concepts/crazy-big-goals|Crazy Big Goal]] is so important that you persist through inevitable roadblocks and setbacks. It must be something you deeply believe is worth dedicating years or decades of your life to will into existence.
