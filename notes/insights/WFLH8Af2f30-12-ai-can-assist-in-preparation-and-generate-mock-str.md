Use [[concepts/ai-tools|AI tools]] to support the strategy preparation phase by analyzing competitors' release notes, user reviews, and trends. You can also ask AI to generate a "mock strategy," which often provides a comprehensive, well-articulated starting point. The human team's critical role is then to down-select and focus the AI's broad suggestions.
