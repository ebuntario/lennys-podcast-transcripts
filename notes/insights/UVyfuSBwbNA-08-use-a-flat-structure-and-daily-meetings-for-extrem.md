An extremely flat [[concepts/organizational-design|organizational structure]] (e.g., one layer between staff and the CEO) combined with daily leadership meetings enables unprecedented speed. This model ensures no decision is blocked for more than 24 hours and fosters a culture of extreme ownership, where small teams can run billion-dollar businesses by moving mountains in short timeframes.
