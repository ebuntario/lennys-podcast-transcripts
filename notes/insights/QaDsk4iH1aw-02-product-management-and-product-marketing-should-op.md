Treating marketing as a handoff at the end of product development creates misalignment. Instead, product management and [[concepts/product-marketing|product marketing]] should work in lockstep from the beginning of the development cycle. This collaboration ensures the product is built with the right messaging, positioning, and customer language baked in, leading to a stronger go-to-market.
