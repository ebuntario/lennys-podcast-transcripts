When crossing the chasm, position yourself as the technology leader who has specialized and committed to solving a specific, severe problem for a defined segment. Your advantage over incumbents is better technology; your advantage over other tech vendors is deep domain expertise. This focused [[concepts/market-positioning|market positioning]] claims a defensible quadrant.
