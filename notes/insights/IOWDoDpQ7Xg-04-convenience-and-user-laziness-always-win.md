Users are fundamentally lazy and will only adopt new tools if the total effort required is significantly less than the value gained. Minimize every point of friction, from sign-up to daily use, because convenience ultimately defeats features. A product must make life noticeably easier, not just be technically impressive, to achieve [[concepts/user-adoption|widespread adoption]].
