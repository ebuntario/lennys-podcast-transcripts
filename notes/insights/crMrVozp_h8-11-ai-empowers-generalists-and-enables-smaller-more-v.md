[[concepts/ai-tools|AI tools]] empower [[concepts/generalists|generalists]] by providing deep, on-demand expertise across many domains (coding, writing, design). This allows small teams to maintain a broad scope without immediate specialization, enabling a 15-person company to run multiple products. The future may see more smaller organizations of versatile generalists, as AI reduces the coordination cost that historically favored large corporations of hyper-specialists, increasing overall [[concepts/team-leverage|team leverage]].
