Evaluate your AI product on two axes: autonomy (how much human involvement is needed) and attribution (how well you can prove value impact). The ideal quadrant is high autonomy and high attribution, which enables [[concepts/outcome-based-pricing|outcome-based pricing]]. Most companies today use hybrid models, but the goal should be to move toward outcome-based pricing for maximum pricing power.
