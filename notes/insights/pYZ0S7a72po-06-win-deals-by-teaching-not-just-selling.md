The most effective sales approach is to provide unique, valuable insights that change the buyer's perspective on their business. Conduct deep research on the prospect's company and industry, then present findings that educate them. This transforms the interaction from a sales pitch into a strategic consultation, building immense trust and [[concepts/deal-momentum|deal momentum]].
