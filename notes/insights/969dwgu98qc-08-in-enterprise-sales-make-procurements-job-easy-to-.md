When dealing with [[concepts/procurement|procurement]], proactively offer to fill out their required forms and provide clear, simple explanations of what you do. Classifying your offering ambiguously leads to high-risk contracting terms and delays. Making their job easy prevents your deal from being sidelined in their queue.
