For long-term, capital-intensive projects like autonomous vehicles, maintaining investor buy-in requires demonstrating meaningful progress in commercial deployment, not just technological milestones. Showing real-world service growth, user adoption, and operational metrics proves the business model is viable. The results must speak for themselves to sustain confidence and investment.
