To maintain momentum on a long-term journey, create fun, concrete celebrations for when you achieve major milestones. Rituals like smashing plates, releasing doves, or holding a festival give the team a moment to pat themselves on the back for conquering something hard. This [[concepts/celebration|celebration]] reinforces focus, provides a sense of accomplishment, and makes the arduous climb feel rewarding.
