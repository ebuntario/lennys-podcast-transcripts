Simply making developers, product managers, and security teams aware that [[concepts/prompt-injection|prompt injection]] and jailbreaking are possible and unsolved problems can prevent dangerous deployment decisions. Knowledgeable teams are less likely to grant AI systems unnecessary permissions or over-rely on marketed "solutions" that don't work.
