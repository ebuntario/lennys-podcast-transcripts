Pursuing [[concepts/excellence|excellence]] means focusing on the quality of the outcome, not just the hours worked or the polish of a deliverable. Sometimes, spending extra time on final polish is a poor use of resources if the core objective is already met. The goal is thoughtful, high-impact work that drives the desired [[concepts/outcome-orientation|business outcome]].
