When reviewing decisions, ask questions that uncover the insights and perspectives that led to them. Inquire about customer interactions, market evolution, and competitive analysis that informed the choice. This [[concepts/empathetic-inquiry|empathetic inquiry]] assumes good intent and builds trust, turning potential conflicts into learning opportunities.
