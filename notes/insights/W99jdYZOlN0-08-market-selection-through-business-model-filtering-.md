Before committing to a new venture, apply filters based on proven business model characteristics: marketplaces, SaaS with recurring revenue, network effects, and businesses that become more valuable at scale. This analytical approach quickly eliminates ideas with structural challenges, allowing entrepreneurs to focus energy where models suggest potential for outsized outcomes. At Uber, this filtering process examined numerous concepts (convenience delivery, point-to-point shipping) before identifying food delivery's unique combination of [[concepts/unit-economics|unit economics]], incremental demand value, and market readiness.
