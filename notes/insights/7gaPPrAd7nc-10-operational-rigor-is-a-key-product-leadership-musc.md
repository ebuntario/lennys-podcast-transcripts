Product leadership isn’t just about building new things; it’s about how well you run what you’ve already built. Implementing rigorous operational forums like Weekly Business Reviews builds a culture where leaders deeply understand their metrics and can speak to variances. This [[concepts/operational-excellence|operational rigor]] cascades through teams and drives continuous improvement.
