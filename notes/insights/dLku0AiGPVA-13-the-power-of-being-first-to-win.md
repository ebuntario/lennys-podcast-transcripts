A key operating principle is that the easiest way to be the best is to be first. In fast-moving fields like AI, [[concepts/first-mover-advantage|being first]] with a novel application of technology allows you to define the category, capture early users, and build momentum before competitors catch up. This focus on speed and novelty is a primary competitive strategy.
