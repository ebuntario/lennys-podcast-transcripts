When evaluating new initiatives, apply three tests: Is it a big idea? Is it something your company should be doing? Is there a legitimate plan to succeed? The [[concepts/working-backwards|working backwards]] FAQ document serves as that early-stage “legitimate plan” test, ensuring you’ve thought through key hurdles before committing resources.
