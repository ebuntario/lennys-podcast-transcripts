Companies that support focus provide psychological safety to discuss distraction problems, create forums for feedback, and have leadership that models indistractable behavior. Since culture flows downhill, managers must exemplify [[concepts/workplace-culture|healthy boundaries]], like not sending off-hours messages, to make focused work culturally acceptable.
