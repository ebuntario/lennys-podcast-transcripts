Focusing on [[concepts/operational-efficiency|efficiency]] rather than growth allows a business to maintain healthy margins with a small team. By imposing constraints like a maximum team size of two people per project and a six-week time limit, companies can avoid the complexity and slowness that comes with large organizations. This approach fosters [[concepts/simplicity|simplicity]] and clarity, leading to better work and sustainable profits.
