[[concepts/ai-evaluation|Evals]] serve a dual purpose: they are the PRDs guiding researchers on what to build, and they become the sales collateral demonstrating a model's capabilities. As focus shifts from academic benchmarks to practical business automations, labs and application companies will increasingly use evals to prove their model's effectiveness at specific, valuable tasks.
