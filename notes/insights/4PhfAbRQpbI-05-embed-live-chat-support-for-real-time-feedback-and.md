Putting 24/7 live chat support in your app is a powerful tool for [[concepts/customer-research|user research]] and eliminating support-related confounding variables. Users will directly tell you their problems and ideas, providing a constant stream of feedback for iteration. This white-glove experience also ensures users feel treated well and are more likely to promote your app.
