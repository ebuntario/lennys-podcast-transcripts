An Ideal Customer Profile has two components: the characteristics of the target company (e.g., size, industry) and the personas you'll interact with (e.g., user, budget owner, technical evaluator). Being crisp on your [[concepts/ideal-customer-profile|ICP]] ensures you spend time with prospects in the "white-hot center" of your target market and understand the buying committee.
