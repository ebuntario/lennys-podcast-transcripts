A core engineering goal is for every engineer to ship a marketable product or feature every week. A "marketable" feature is one a user might subscribe or come to the app for, not just a table-stakes improvement. This forces extreme [[concepts/mvp-scoping|scope slicing]]—cutting a design down until removing anything more would make it useless—to achieve a weekly cadence and generate rapid user feedback.
