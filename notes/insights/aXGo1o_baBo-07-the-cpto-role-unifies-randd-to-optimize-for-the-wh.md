The Chief Product and Technology Officer (CPTO) role combines product, engineering, and design under one leader to eliminate functional debates and optimize for overall business outcomes. This structure provides the CEO with a single point of accountability for the significant R&D investment. Success in this role requires deep technical understanding, operational skill in [[concepts/org-design|organization design]], and a founder-like breadth of perspective.
