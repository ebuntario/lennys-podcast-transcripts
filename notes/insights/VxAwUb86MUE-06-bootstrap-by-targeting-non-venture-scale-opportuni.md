You can build a large, profitable company through [[concepts/bootstrapping|bootstrapping]] by deliberately targeting markets that are too small for venture capital interest. Focus on businesses with a strong [[concepts/business-model|moat]] (like a brand or network effect) in a niche that can grow organically without burning capital. This allows for a high-quality life and control, avoiding the growth-at-all-costs pressure of venture-backed competition.
