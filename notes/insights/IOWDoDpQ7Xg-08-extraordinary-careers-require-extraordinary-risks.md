You can have a linear career by playing it safe and avoiding failure, but extraordinary impact requires taking extraordinary risks. Embrace the possibility of failure and embarrassment as the cost of doing interesting, disruptive work. The willingness to take [[concepts/intelligent-risks|calculated risks]] separates incremental from transformative career paths.
