As an immigrant or outsider, perceived differences—accent, background, perspective—can be reframed as unique strengths that set you apart. In environments where you are rare, your distinct viewpoint becomes a source of innovation and connection. Embrace this [[concepts/authenticity|authenticity]] rather than conforming; it can become your most powerful professional asset.
