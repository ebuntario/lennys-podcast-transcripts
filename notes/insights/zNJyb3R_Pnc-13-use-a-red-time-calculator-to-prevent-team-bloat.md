Calculate your team's capacity by adding up days spent on meetings, PTO, optimizations, and reporting. If the total required days exceed the days in the quarter (putting you "in the red"), first try to cut low-value work. If the red persists for multiple quarters, you have a data-backed case to hire, preventing unnecessary [[concepts/team-bloat|team bloat]].
