Even when founders know they should talk to users, a pervasive fear of rejection often prevents them from making enough outreach attempts. Technical founders, in particular, may believe software alone is the solution. Overcoming this fear and embracing the grind of [[concepts/customer-discovery|customer development]] is a non-negotiable hurdle for early-stage success.
