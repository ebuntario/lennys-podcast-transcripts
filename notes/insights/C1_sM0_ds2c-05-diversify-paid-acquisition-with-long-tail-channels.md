Beyond major ad platforms, invest in long-tail channels like niche websites, review sites, podcast ads, and newsletter sponsorships. Individually, these may not drive significant volume, but collectively they can contribute a substantial portion of lead flow. This [[concepts/channel-diversification|diversification]] reduces dependency on any single source and builds a more resilient acquisition strategy.
