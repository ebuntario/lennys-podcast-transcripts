AI allows individuals to perform tasks that previously required specialized team members, moving us toward a model where traditional roles like designer, engineer, and product manager are less distinct. This enables smaller, more nimble teams where everyone can be a [[concepts/builder-mindset|builder]] who uses AI tools to supplement their skill set. The goal is to empower each person to take on more of the end-to-end process of creating value.
