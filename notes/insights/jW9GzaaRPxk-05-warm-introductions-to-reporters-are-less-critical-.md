While relationships can get you an answer, they don't guarantee coverage. Editorial decisions are based on the story's merit, not friendships. Effective [[concepts/cold-outreach|cold outreach]] with a thoughtful, concise pitch is often just as successful. Relationships become valuable *after* initial coverage for future sourcing and nurturing.
