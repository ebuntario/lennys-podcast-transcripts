Improving [[concepts/user-retention|retention]] often requires fixing the step immediately before a user converts or completes a key action. Teams should analyze the user journey to identify friction points, such as lack of trust or slow performance, that block progression. Solving these setup moments is more actionable than targeting the abstract goal of "increasing retention."
