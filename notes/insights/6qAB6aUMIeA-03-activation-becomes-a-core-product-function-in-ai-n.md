For AI products where the core interaction is with an agent, [[concepts/activation|activation]] is woven into the company's DNA rather than being a separate growth function. The team improving the AI agent is inherently obsessed with the first experience and the "wow moment," as improving the agent improves the entire user lifecycle simultaneously.
