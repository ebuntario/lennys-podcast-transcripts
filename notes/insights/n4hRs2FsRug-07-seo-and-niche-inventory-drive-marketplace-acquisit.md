A marketplace with a long tail of unique, niche inventory is naturally suited for search-driven acquisition. Buyers often come via [[concepts/seo-strategy|SEO]] or Google Shopping when looking for something specific, and the platform can meet that need precisely. This creates a strong top-of-funnel engine where the breadth and specificity of supply inherently attract qualified demand.
