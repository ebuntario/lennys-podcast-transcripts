Adopt the motto "hard choices, easy life; easy choices, hard life." Procrastinating on difficult decisions like firing an underperforming employee or shutting down a project creates more long-term stress and complexity. Making the tough call upfront, while painful in the moment, simplifies your operational reality and paves the way for a more manageable and focused [[concepts/entrepreneurial-journey|entrepreneurial journey]].
