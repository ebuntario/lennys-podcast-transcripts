While intuition is a valuable pattern-matching tool for leaders, it is also where cognitive biases reside. To check your gut, engage in systematic, analytical thinking (System 2) and leverage the [[concepts/wisdom-of-crowds|wisdom of crowds]] by bringing together people with diverse information and perspectives. Researchers are particularly skilled at facilitating these discussions and providing evidence that challenges ingrained assumptions.
