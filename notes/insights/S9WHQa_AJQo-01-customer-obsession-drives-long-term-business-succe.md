Amazon operates on the principle that prioritizing customer needs leads to sustainable business outcomes like revenue and growth. This [[concepts/user-centric-design|customer obsession]] requires faith that financial metrics will follow if you consistently deliver value to customers. Starting decisions with "what's best for the customer" creates a backward-working approach that informs all subsequent work.
