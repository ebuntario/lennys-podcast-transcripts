To move fast in a new bet, you may need to break established rules (e.g., hiring contractors, skipping standard processes). Do this strategically by using your [[concepts/social-capital|social capital]] and ensuring leadership support. The goal isn't to rebel, but to consciously bypass constraints that are designed for scaling mature products, not for validating early-stage hypotheses with speed.
