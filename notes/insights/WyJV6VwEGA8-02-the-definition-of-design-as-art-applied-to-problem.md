Design is art applied to problem solving, combining creativity and unique expression with addressing a specific user need. If you lose the art, the result becomes purely utilitarian and lacks soul, while losing the problem-solving aspect removes its practical value. This combination is what makes [[concepts/design-philosophy|design]] beautiful and effective.
