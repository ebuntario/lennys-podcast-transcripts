Beyond individual product contributions, product managers play a crucial role in building community within their teams and organizations. This becomes especially important in distributed or remote work environments where natural connections are harder to form. Fostering this [[concepts/team-culture|collaborative culture]] helps align everyone toward shared missions and improves overall team effectiveness and satisfaction.
