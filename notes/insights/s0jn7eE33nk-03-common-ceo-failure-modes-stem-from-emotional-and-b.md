CEOs often fail due to recognizable patterns like being a robot (suppressing emotion), a pleaser (avoiding hard decisions), a perfectionist (fearing mistakes), or laissez-faire (ignoring management). These [[concepts/leadership-failures|failure modes]] are rooted in lacking self-awareness and emotional intelligence, which erode team trust and company performance.
