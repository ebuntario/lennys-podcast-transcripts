Executive coaching is a creative partnership where the client defines success, and the coach helps them reach their potential across all life domains. It utilizes tools like deep listening, curiosity, and intuition to challenge assumptions and connect dots. The work is often about internal transformation and [[concepts/personal-growth|personal development]], not just acquiring new professional skills.
