Organizational bottlenecks often stem from people not understanding the [[concepts/mental-models|mental models]] of key decision-makers. Share verbatim quotes from leadership meetings along with your interpretation of the underlying thinking. This helps your team internalize decision-making criteria, enabling them to act autonomously and align their work without constant approval loops.
