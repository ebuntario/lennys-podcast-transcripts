A marketplace carves out its unique position through a clear definition of acceptable supply and rigorous enforcement of those policies. The brand is fundamentally shaped by what is allowed to be sold. Maintaining this integrity through [[concepts/trust-and-safety|policy enforcement]] is a continuous investment but is essential for differentiating from competitors and sustaining user trust.
