In technology companies, technical architecture often determines strategic options more than feature decisions do. Investing in flexible, adaptable systems creates optionality for future directions, even if it means shipping features more slowly initially. This [[concepts/long-term-planning|long-term orientation]] prioritizes building foundations that support evolution over decades rather than optimizing for immediate feature delivery.
