Conclude the setup phase by jointly defining the characteristics of an ideal solution with the buyer. This [[concepts/buyer-alignment|buyer alignment]] step creates agreement on what matters before introducing your product. If done well, you've essentially won the deal by framing your solution as the only one that meets these criteria.
