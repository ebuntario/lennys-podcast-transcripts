A mature decision framework moves beyond minimizing regret to [[concepts/maximizing-option-value|maximizing future option value]]. This perspective values health, relationships, and continuous learning as investments that compound over time. It recognizes that rest and family time aren't trade-offs against productivity but foundations for sustaining long-term adventure and impact.
