Paid search is user-driven, meaning someone must type a relevant keyword for your ad to appear. This makes it a foundational channel for almost every business, unlike disruptive media where ads interrupt unrelated content. Starting with [[concepts/paid-search|Google Search]] allows you to capture high-intent users who are actively looking for solutions.
