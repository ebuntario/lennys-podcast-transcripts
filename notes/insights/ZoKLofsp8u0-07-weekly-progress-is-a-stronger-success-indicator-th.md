Consistent, tangible progress on a weekly or biweekly basis is a better predictor of startup success than being in a hot industry or having investor buzz. Founders who continuously hit their goals and learn new things each week are on a strong track. This focus on [[concepts/strategy-execution|execution]] and learning outweighs external validators.
