You don't need an eval for every possible failure. Many issues can be fixed directly in the prompt or code. Use your error analysis to identify the most prevalent or critical failure modes, and build [[concepts/ai-evals|evals]] only for those. This [[concepts/prioritization|prioritization]] ensures you invest effort where it has the highest ROI for product quality.
