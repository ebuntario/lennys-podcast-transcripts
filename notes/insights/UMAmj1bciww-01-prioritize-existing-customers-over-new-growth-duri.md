During a supply crisis, the highest priority is ensuring you do not run out of product for your current, committed customers. This may require the counterintuitive action of halting new customer acquisition entirely. Protecting your [[concepts/retention|customer base]] builds immense long-term loyalty and brand trust, which is more valuable than short-term growth.
