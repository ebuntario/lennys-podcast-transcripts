A company wins by getting an A+ in the one class that matters and being willing to get an F in all others. This is the opposite of school, where you optimize for total GPA. [[concepts/ruthless-prioritization|Ruthless prioritization]] on the single most important objective is a forcing function for excellence and a defense against dilution of effort.
