It's easier to build a following for a person than a brand. Invest in building the [[concepts/social-media-strategy|social presence]] of founders or key executives who can share bold opinions and personality. Corporate channels are hard to grow unless they offer unique value, while an executive's platform can directly influence press and industry conversations.
