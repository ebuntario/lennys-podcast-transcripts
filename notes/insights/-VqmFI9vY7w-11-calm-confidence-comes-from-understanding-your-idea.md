Develop confidence by deeply understanding who your solution is perfect for and who it isn't. This allows you to honestly advise prospects when they might be better served by alternatives. This [[concepts/sales-mindset|calm confidence]] builds trust and makes you more effective with qualified prospects while saving time on poor fits.
