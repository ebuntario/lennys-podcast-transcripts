In the AI era, [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] must be recaptured every three months due to rapid changes in underlying LLM capabilities and consumer expectations. Companies must constantly reinvent and scale in short cycles, operating on a "product-market fit treadmill" where even massive revenue doesn't guarantee durability.
