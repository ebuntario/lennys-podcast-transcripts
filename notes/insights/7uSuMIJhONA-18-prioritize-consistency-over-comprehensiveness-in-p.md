In platform-driven markets like online video, users value a consistent, reliable experience more than access to every piece of content. This eigenquestion guided YouTube to stop linking out to external sites and even retake control of its iPhone app from Apple. Making [[concepts/strategic-tradeoffs|strategic tradeoffs]] for consistency builds stronger user trust and product integrity.
