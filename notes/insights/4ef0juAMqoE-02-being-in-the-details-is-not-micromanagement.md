Effective leaders stay deeply involved in project details to understand progress and quality, similar to how a board oversees a CEO. This involvement is distinct from [[concepts/micromanagement|micromanagement]], which dictates specific actions, and is essential for ensuring teams are performing well and aligned with company goals.
