The litmus test for a modern salesperson is that if placed among 10 engineers, it should take 10 minutes for them to figure out you aren't a product manager. This requires incredible product depth, which builds credibility with engineering and enables the sales org to act as an extension of R&D. The best go-to-market teams are equal parts revenue drivers and sources of high-quality customer signal for the product roadmap.
