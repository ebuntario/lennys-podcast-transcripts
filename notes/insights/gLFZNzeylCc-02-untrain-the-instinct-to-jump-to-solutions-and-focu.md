A common mistake for new product managers is becoming overly attached to a specific solution before understanding the user's problem. The role requires focusing first on understanding users and their real-world problems to identify genuine opportunities. This shift from a solution-first to a [[concepts/problem-discovery|problem-discovery]] mindset is a critical retraining for effective product work.
