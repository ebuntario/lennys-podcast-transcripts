Instead of passively "playing with" new tech like AI, accelerate your learning by picking a specific, slightly unreasonable goal to achieve with it. The process of stubbornly working toward that goal, even if you fail, forces deeper understanding than unstructured exploration. This [[concepts/goal-directed-learning|goal-directed learning]] approach reveals both capabilities and limitations more effectively.
