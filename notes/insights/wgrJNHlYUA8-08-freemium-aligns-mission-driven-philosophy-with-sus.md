A [[concepts/freemium-model|freemium model]] can serve both a democratizing mission and a viable business. It allows a product to reach users who cannot pay while identifying features that specific segments value enough to upgrade. The model naturally supports [[concepts/product-led-growth|product-led growth]] by letting the product experience demonstrate its own value.
