Palantir minimized formal titles to avoid unproductive internal competition and people gaming promotion systems. With mostly uniform titles like "forward deployed engineer," roles and leadership on projects were fluid and based on merit. This enforced a culture where you had to continually earn your place and the right to work on important projects, rather than relying on a static [[concepts/organizational-structure|organizational structure]].
