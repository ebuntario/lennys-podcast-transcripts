Product frameworks are tools in a toolkit to be pulled out when relevant, not a rigid set of rules to follow without thinking. The core job of a product manager is to add value to customers that translates to business value, not to perfectly execute a prescribed process. Over-reliance on frameworks can stifle [[concepts/risk-taking|risk-taking]] and creative problem-solving.
