For building a great growth engine, culture and rituals matter more than team structure. The key is creating a culture of reducing cycle time and instilling a [[concepts/bias-to-action|bias to action]]. This involves getting teams to think in smaller units of time (like days, not quarters) and reinforcing that you should never postpone something that can be done today.
