When working with a strongly opinionated, product-minded founder, success hinges on establishing shared [[concepts/product-principles|product principles]]. These principles become the common language of the company, allowing teams to make creative decisions without constant top-down approval. This framework transforms subjective "gut feel" reviews into constructive discussions about whether work aligns with the agreed-upon vision and craft standards.
