Identify and work on problems that have a potential 10x positive or negative impact on the business. To solve these [[concepts/high-leverage-work|high-leverage problems]], leaders must have the humility to dive into the details themselves. This hands-on approach signals to the team the importance of the problem and ensures execution stays on track.
