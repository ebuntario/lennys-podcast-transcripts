In product, prioritize leaders who have a strong track record of being right in ambiguous, high-stakes situations. This ability to synthesize incomplete information and make a correct call is a peculiar and critical skill. Frameworks and process should enable this [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]], not substitute for deep thinking and judgment.
