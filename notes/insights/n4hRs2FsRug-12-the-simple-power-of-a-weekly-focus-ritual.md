A simple, consistent practice of defining a weekly focus, sharing it with a team, and reflecting on the previous week's focus builds accountability and self-awareness. This low-fi ritual helps individuals understand how they spend time, identify patterns, and improve their planning. The act of writing down priorities and creating social accountability is a powerful [[concepts/personal-organization|productivity tool]].
