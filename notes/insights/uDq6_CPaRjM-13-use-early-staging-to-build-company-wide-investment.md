Putting a product on staging early has a dual benefit: it provides crucial feedback to mature the product, and it makes people across the company feel invested because they helped shape it. This [[concepts/dogfooding|dogfooding]] process opens the doors to product development, creating a collective drive for success and ensuring the product is ready for launch.
