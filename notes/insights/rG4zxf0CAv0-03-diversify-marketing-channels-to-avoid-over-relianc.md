Treat performance marketing as one investment within a broader portfolio, not a sole growth engine. Over-reliance on a single channel creates volatility, similar to investing all capital in one stock. A balanced [[concepts/marketing-mix|marketing mix]] including SEO, email, and offline channels reduces dependency and mitigates risk from platform algorithm changes.
