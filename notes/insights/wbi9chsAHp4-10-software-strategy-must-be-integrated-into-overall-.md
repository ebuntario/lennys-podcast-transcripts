For non-software-native companies, treating software as merely an IT function leads to missed opportunities. The C-suite needs a chief product officer or equivalent to ensure [[concepts/product-strategy|software strategy]] is woven into business strategy, enabling competitive advantage and innovation. This shift is critical for competing with digital-first disruptors.
