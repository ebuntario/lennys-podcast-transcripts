Many people treat estimation as a mysterious talent rather than a learnable skill. Regular practice with OKR grading and retrospective analysis develops [[concepts/planning-skills|estimation capabilities]] over time. This learned skill becomes increasingly valuable as organizations scale, enabling more predictable delivery and better resource allocation decisions.
