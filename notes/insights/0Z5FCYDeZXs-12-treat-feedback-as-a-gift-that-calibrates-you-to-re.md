Feedback is essential because our self-perception is often biased (e.g., Dunning-Kruger effect, imposter syndrome). Treat feedback as a valuable gift that reflects an external perspective you cannot see yourself, helping you calibrate your understanding of reality. Embracing [[concepts/feedback-culture|regular, constructive feedback]] is the fastest way for individuals and teams to improve.
