Cultivating a culture of [[concepts/first-principles-thinking|first-principles thinking]]—breaking down problems to their fundamentals and building frameworks—teaches strategic rigor. Giving product managers broad autonomy and ownership, akin to "mini-CEOs," allows them to develop the full spectrum of skills needed to build and run a business, preparing them for future leadership roles.
