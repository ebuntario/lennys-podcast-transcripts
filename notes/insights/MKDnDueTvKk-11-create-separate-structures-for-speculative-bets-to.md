To give risky, long-term projects a chance to succeed, sometimes you need to structure them like internal startups—siloed from the core organization's metrics and processes. This protects them from being suffocated by short-term incentives and allows teams to operate with the [[concepts/entrepreneurial-freedom|entrepreneurial freedom]] and focus needed to validate a hypothesis.
