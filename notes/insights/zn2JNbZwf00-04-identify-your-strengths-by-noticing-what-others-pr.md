A key to [[concepts/strengths-based-development|strengths-based development]] is identifying what people consistently compliment you on that you consider trivial or easy. This often points to a natural talent. Understanding the "shadow side" of this strength—how it can manifest as a weakness in certain contexts—allows you to dial it appropriately and leverage it more effectively.
