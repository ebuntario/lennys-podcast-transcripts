The fundamental skill of product management is the ability to start with a blank sheet and create. To prevent this "builder" muscle from atrophying, PMs should regularly work on [[concepts/skill-development|side projects or art]] completely separate from their day job. This practice of molding new clay maintains creative energy and the core capability to envision and articulate new products.
