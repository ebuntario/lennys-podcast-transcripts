The skills required for a job are projected to change by 70% by 2030, meaning roles are transforming whether individuals seek change or not. This rapid [[concepts/skill-obsolescence|skill obsolescence]] creates a necessity for organizations to become more nimble and adaptive to survive. Companies must reimagine their structures and processes to match the accelerating pace of external change.
