When involving teams in redesigning their structure, time-box the collaborative process with a bias towards shorter durations. Prolonged deliberation becomes distracting and counterproductive. Clear deadlines help maintain momentum and ensure that [[concepts/decision-making|decision making]] is expedient while still incorporating valuable input.
