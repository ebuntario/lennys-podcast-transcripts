When a company makes big, long-term bets (like AI or hardware), it must proactively communicate the strategy and timeline to the market. During economic downturns, external pressure will focus on short-term core business performance. Clear, consistent [[concepts/strategic-communication|explanation of the investment portfolio]] is crucial to maintain support for future growth initiatives.
