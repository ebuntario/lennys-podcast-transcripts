When pushing for cultural transformation, leaders must quickly identify whether team members are "on the wagon" (excited and bought into the new vision) or "off the wagon" (resistant or pessimistic). You must either convince them to get on board or make the difficult decision that they shouldn't be there, as [[concepts/misalignment|misalignment]] and low tolerance for change can drag down high performers and slow progress.
