The root cause of most distraction is not technology, but our inability to deal with internal discomfort like boredom, loneliness, or anxiety. Research shows that 90% of distractions originate from these [[concepts/internal-triggers|internal triggers]], not from external pings or notifications. Recognizing distraction as an emotional regulation problem empowers you to address the underlying feelings rather than blaming your tools.
