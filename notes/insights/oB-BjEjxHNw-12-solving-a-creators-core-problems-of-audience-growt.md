Every creator fundamentally needs to grow an audience and get paid to make a living. New platforms should focus on solving acute, real [[concepts/creator-pain-points|pain points]]—like access to financing or health insurance—that are unique to freelance creative work. Validating that a problem is deeply felt is more important than chasing trendy verticals.
