[[concepts/word-of-mouth|Word-of-mouth]] growth naturally occurs with products that have high frequency of use, giving users daily opportunities to recommend them. For low-frequency products, growth must rely on other channels like paid acquisition unless the experience is exceptionally "cool" or unique. Understanding this dynamic early shapes your go-to-market strategy and resource allocation.
