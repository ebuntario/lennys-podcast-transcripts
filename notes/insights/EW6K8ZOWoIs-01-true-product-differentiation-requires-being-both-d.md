Being different is not enough, as it's easy to build something that simply diverges from existing solutions. Being better is also insufficient if it only means charging more for incremental improvements. True [[concepts/product-differentiation|product differentiation]] requires being better in a way that matters deeply to the end user, creating a compelling reason for them to choose your product.
