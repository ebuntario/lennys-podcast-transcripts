The ideal hire for product ops depends on the targeted pillar. For business data and insights, seek someone with strong [[concepts/data-analysis|data analysis]], storytelling, and BI tool expertise, often from a consulting or analyst background. For process and practices, high EQ and experience with product management systems are key. For customer insights, look for a user research or research ops background with a process-improvement mindset.
