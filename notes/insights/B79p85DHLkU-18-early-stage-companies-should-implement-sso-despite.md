Implementing Single Sign-On (SSO) early prevents security incidents and reduces future administrative overhead, despite the upfront investment. The $2,000 cost and two-day implementation time represent a worthwhile investment compared to potential data breaches or the eventual need to hire IT staff specifically for SSO management. This [[concepts/future-proofing]] approach balances immediate costs against long-term risks and expenses.
