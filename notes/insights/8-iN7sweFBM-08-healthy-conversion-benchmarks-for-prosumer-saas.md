For prosumer premium SaaS products (like Grammarly, Canva), a healthy website-to-free-user conversion rate at scale is 20-35%. A healthy free-to-paid conversion rate should be north of 5%, ideally over 7%, for long-term independent company growth. These benchmarks help assess whether a product is ready to support efficient [[concepts/paid-acquisition|paid acquisition]] strategies.
