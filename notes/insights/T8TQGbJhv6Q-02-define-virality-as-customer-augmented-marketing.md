A powerful way to think about [[concepts/virality|virality]] is as customer-augmented marketing, where your users become your marketers. This allows companies to spend significantly less on traditional marketing and reinvest those savings into product improvement. Atlassian, for example, spends 10-20 percentage points less on marketing than competitors because its products are inherently viral.
