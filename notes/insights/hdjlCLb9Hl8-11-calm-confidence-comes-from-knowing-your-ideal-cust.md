The optimal sales posture is calm confidence, which stems from deeply understanding who you're perfect for and who you're not. Be honest about where competitors excel and where you excel, and willingly disqualify poor fits. This [[concepts/sales-mindset|sales mindset]] builds trust and ensures you fight hard for the right deals while avoiding wasted effort on mismatched opportunities.
