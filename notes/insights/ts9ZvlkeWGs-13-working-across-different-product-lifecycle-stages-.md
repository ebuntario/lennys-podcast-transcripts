Maintain entrepreneurial energy by actively working on products at various stages of maturity within a portfolio. Having projects in early startup phases, growth phases, and mature phases provides diverse challenges and prevents stagnation. This allows leaders to experience the thrill of building something new while also steering established products, sustaining long-term [[concepts/entrepreneurial-energy|passion and innovation]].
