Build teams by pairing people whose energy drivers and strengths cover each other's energy drains and weaknesses. This creates a balanced, productive unit where individuals can operate in their superpower zones. The goal is [[concepts/team-building|complementary diversity]], not hiring clones, which often leads to collective blind spots.
