Employee motivation follows a hierarchy: compensation forms the base, recognition sits in the middle, and meaning resides at the top. While money matters most in some roles, differentiation often comes from recognition and [[concepts/employee-motivation|meaningful work]]. Understanding where your organization invests in this hierarchy helps attract and retain talent.
