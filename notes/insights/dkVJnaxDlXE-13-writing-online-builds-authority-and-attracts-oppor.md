Consistent [[concepts/thought-leadership|content creation]] on platforms like Medium or LinkedIn allows you to build an audience and authority without an editorial intermediary. While a "viral" post can be a breakthrough, sustained writing over time is what compounds into significant [[concepts/professional-growth|career development]] opportunities. Solving your own problems publicly often leads to unexpected professional paths.
