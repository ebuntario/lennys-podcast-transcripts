Protect an early-stage project by demonstrating consistent velocity. Share weekly updates that blend data, demos, and short video snippets of customer feedback. This [[concepts/internal-communication|rhythm of communication]] builds momentum, shows tangible learning, and makes it harder for stakeholders to interrupt a "high-speed train" of progress, buying the team the time needed to mature the product.
