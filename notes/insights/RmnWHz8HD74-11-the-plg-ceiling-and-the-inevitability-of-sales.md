While [[concepts/product-led-growth|product-led growth (PLG)]] is an excellent starting motion for many companies targeting startups, it typically has a ceiling. Customers are generally unwilling to give a company $1 million through a self-serve flow. To sustain high growth rates, companies must eventually build a sales function to drive larger deal sizes, and they often get stuck by waiting too long to make this transition.
