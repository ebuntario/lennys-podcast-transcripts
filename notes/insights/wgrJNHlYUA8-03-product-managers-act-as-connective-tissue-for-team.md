Effective [[concepts/product-leadership|product managers]] connect teams, ideas, data, and constraints to move toward a shared vision. Their role involves constant adaptation, compromise, and reorienting around new limitations. They are less about rigid process and more about facilitating movement and alignment across disparate areas.
