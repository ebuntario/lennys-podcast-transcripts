A marketplace's primary focus often evolves from deeply understanding and serving suppliers to creating a world-class experience for buyers. Early immersion in the supplier's world builds a strong foundation, but ultimately, the platform must facilitate transactions. The job of a marketplace is to help sellers make sales, which is fundamentally driven by attracting and satisfying qualified [[concepts/marketplace-strategy|buyer demand]].
