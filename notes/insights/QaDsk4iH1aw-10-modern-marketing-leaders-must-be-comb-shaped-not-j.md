The traditional "T-shaped marketer" (deep in one pillar) is evolving. Modern [[concepts/marketing-leadership|marketing leaders]] need to be comb-shaped—proficient across analytics, creativity, product, and growth—to diagnose and address diverse company needs. AI tools can help bridge skill gaps (e.g., aiding creative marketers with data analysis), but foundational understanding across domains is crucial.
