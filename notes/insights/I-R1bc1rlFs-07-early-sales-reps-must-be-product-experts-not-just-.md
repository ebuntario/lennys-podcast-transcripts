In the early days, sales reps need to be product savants who understand the intricacies, feature gaps, and unique 10x advantages of your immature product. They must be able to box around limitations and sell the vision. Later-stage reps at large companies can rely on sales engineers and simple scripts, but early reps must embody deep product expertise.
