The most innovative storytelling formats often begin as one-off experiments created by interdisciplinary teams of journalists, engineers, and designers. Product teams should observe these experiments to identify patterns that can become scalable [[concepts/product-platforms|product platforms]]. This dual-mode approach—supporting both experimental storytelling and systematic platform building—creates sustainable innovation.
