Shopify's "Meeting Armageddon" annually deletes all recurring internal meetings with more than two people, followed by a two-week moratorium on adding new ones. This breaks the inertia of unnecessary meetings and forces teams to reconsider what gatherings truly add value. The practice significantly reduces meeting hours while increasing [[concepts/productivity|focused work time]].
