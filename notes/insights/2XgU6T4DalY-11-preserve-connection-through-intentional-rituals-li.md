As you scale in leadership, staying connected to individual contributors requires intentional rituals. Maintaining regular office hours and "Ask Me Anything" sessions forces you to preserve time for these connections. This [[concepts/leadership-communication|communication]] practice builds approachability and ensures you receive ground-level input, which is vital for informed leadership.
