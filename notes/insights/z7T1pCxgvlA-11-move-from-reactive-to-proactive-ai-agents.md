The next evolution of [[concepts/ai-agents|AI agents]] will be proactive, moving beyond responding to user prompts. By being integrated into workflows and understanding context, agents will anticipate needs—like a coding agent reviewing pull requests overnight or a personal agent surfacing relevant information at the start of your day—creating significantly more value.
