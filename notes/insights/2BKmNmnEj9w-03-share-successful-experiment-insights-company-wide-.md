When an experiment yields a significant insight—like discovering users prefer positive reinforcement after losses—share it broadly across the company. This allows adjacent teams to apply the learning to their domains, turning a single win into a pattern that can be exploited 10x across the organization through systematic [[concepts/knowledge-sharing|knowledge sharing]].
