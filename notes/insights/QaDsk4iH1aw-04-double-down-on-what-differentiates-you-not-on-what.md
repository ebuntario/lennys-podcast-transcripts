When analyzing competition, the goal is not to copy but to find a unique path. For example, Retool's competitors focused on content and events, but Retool's key differentiator was its impressive enterprise customer base. They doubled down on [[concepts/customer-marketing|customer marketing]] and storytelling, leveraging logos and testimonials that competitors couldn't replicate, which became a superpower.
