Good OKRs or goal-setting frameworks must have three key attributes. They must be *legible* (easily understood), *actionable* (inspiring specific action), and *authentic* (honestly reflecting the team's daily work). If a goal is just a post-rationalization of planned work, it loses its power to drive meaningful [[concepts/team-alignment|alignment and focus]].
