When receiving feedback, respond with enthusiastic gratitude externally, even if internally you feel defensive. This creates a persona that makes others comfortable giving you more constructive criticism, which accelerates your [[concepts/skill-development|skill development]]. People are more likely to share honest, subjective feedback—like how you make them feel—if they trust you'll receive it well.
