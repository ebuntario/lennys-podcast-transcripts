Choosing the right [[concepts/value-metric|value metric]]—the unit of value a customer derives—creates alignment between price and value. This creates a natural escalator where customers pay more as they get more value, whether through seats, API calls, or storage used. This usage-based approach is a core component of modern [[concepts/product-led-growth|product-led growth]].
