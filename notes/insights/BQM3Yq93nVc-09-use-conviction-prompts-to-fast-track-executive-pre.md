Integrating prompts like "This matters a ton" or "I genuinely believe" forces you to complete sentences with more conviction, altering your state and content. This builds [[concepts/executive-presence|executive presence]] by helping you stand behind your ideas more fully. It's a tool to ensure your ideas are heard with the weight they deserve, countering the common tendency to hedge.
