A minimum viable product should elicit an emotive response and [[concepts/user-delight|delight]], not just solve a problem functionally. Early users need to be excited enough to tell others, which drives organic [[concepts/word-of-mouth-growth|word-of-mouth growth]]. The bar for launch is creating an experience that lights up users' eyes and makes them want to share it.
