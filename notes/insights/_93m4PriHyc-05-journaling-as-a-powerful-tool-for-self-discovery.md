Pen and paper are a deeply underrated technology for [[concepts/self-discovery|self-discovery]]. Unstructured writing allows subconscious thoughts to flow, while a structured approach can involve listing recent emotional reactions and interrogating their root causes. This practice helps edit and rewrite deep-seated internal narratives over time.
