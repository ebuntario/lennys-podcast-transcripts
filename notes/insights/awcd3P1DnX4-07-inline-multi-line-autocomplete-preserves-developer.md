Early experiments with AI code suggestions involved side panels, but these required developers to shift focus away from their cursor. The winning [[concepts/inline-autocomplete|inline autocomplete]] experience—using gray, italicized text—keeps suggestions in the direct line of sight and workflow. This minimizes context switching and aligns with the goal of maintaining developer flow.
