Growth teams need time to validate hypotheses and discover what works, given the high inherent failure rate of experiments. Commit to outcomes over a year using low/medium/high confidence buckets, rather than demanding weekly metric movements. This prevents vanity metrics and data manipulation, creating a healthier environment where the team can focus on real [[concepts/testing|learning and impact]].
