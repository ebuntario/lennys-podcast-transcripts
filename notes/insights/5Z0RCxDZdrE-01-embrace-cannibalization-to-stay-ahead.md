A company should actively work to make its own product look obsolete every 6-12 months. This requires making long-term bets on features that may be 6-9 months out, not just incremental improvements. The goal is to [[concepts/innovation|cannibalize]] the current form factor before competitors do, ensuring you stay ahead.
