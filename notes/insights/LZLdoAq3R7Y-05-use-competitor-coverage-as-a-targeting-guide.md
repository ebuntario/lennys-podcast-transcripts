Look at where your competitors have been featured to identify relevant publications and writers. This reveals which audiences are being reached and how stories are framed. This [[concepts/competitive-analysis|competitive analysis]] provides a practical roadmap for your own outreach, helping you target the right journalists and tailor your pitch effectively.
