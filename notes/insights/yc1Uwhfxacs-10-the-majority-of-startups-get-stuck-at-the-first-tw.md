Roughly 60-70% of startups never progress beyond nascent or developing product-market fit (levels one and two). Getting stuck often manifests as plateauing growth, high churn, long sales cycles, or difficulty finding the next customer. Recognizing these stagnation signals and being willing to make significant pivots using the [[concepts/pivot-framework|Four Ps framework]] is crucial to breaking through.
