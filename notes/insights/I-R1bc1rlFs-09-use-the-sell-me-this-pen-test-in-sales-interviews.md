A crucial test for sales hires is having them "sell you this pen"—or rather, sell your product. Give them time to prepare, watch explainer videos, and understand the core problem you solve. Their ability to deliver a coherent, confident pitch reveals their work ethic, preparation skills, and fundamental understanding of [[concepts/sales-skills|solution-selling]].
