Working from an office most of the time enables high-bandwidth, unstructured communication that accelerates problem-solving. Shared meals like lunch become surprisingly productive hours for cross-pollination of ideas, as people subconsciously process challenges and solutions. This environment fosters the spontaneous conversations that help small teams move exceptionally fast.
