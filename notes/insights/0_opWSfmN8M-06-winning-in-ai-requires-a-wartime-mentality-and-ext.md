To compete with new AI-native startups, established companies must adopt a "wartime" mentality and be prepared to work at an unprecedented pace. Young competitors often work 12-hour days, 365 days a year, and use AI tools pervasively in their own workflows. Matching this [[concepts/competitive-intensity|competitive intensity]] and deeply integrating AI into operations is the price of entry.
