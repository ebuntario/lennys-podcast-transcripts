A healthy product portfolio should allocate roughly 5-10% of capacity to bold, experimental [[concepts/moonshot-projects|moonshot projects]], about 25-30% to maintaining and operating existing products, and the remaining 60% to incremental improvements on in-market products. This balance allows for future bets while realizing payoff from past investments and keeping current customers satisfied.
