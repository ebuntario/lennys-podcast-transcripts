Traditional sales comp plans reward only new business, ignoring whether customers churn or renew. In a downturn, align incentives by rewarding reps for customer renewals, upsells, and advocacy. Modern compensation should reflect metrics like [[concepts/net-dollar-retention|net dollar retention]] to ensure reps are motivated to find the right, long-term customers, not just any logo.
