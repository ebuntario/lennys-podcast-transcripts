A product leader's job isn't to manage features but to own the product and become its foremost expert. This requires going deep—studying tax codes, regulations, or user workflows firsthand—rather than delegating the learning. This deep [[concepts/expertise|expertise]] enables fast, confident decisions and is more valuable than any amount of process or delegation.
