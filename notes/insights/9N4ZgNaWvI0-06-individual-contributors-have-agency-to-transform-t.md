Product managers often feel like victims trapped in feature teams, but they have significant [[concepts/career-agency|agency]] to drive change. By proactively developing the skills of a true product manager (customer expertise, business acumen, data analysis), they can raise their own game. This often leads to recognition and can inspire pilot efforts to transform how teams operate, even from the bottom up.
