When moving a project from an R&D team to a core product team, it's critical to seed the new team with researchers for knowledge transfer. The transition should be based on having a fully capable replacement team in place, not a calendar date. This ensures continuity of expertise while allowing the R&D team to return to exploring new [[concepts/horizon-three-projects|horizon three projects]].
