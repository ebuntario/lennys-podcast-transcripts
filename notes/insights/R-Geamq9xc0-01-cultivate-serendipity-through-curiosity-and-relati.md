Great careers are often built non-linearly through [[concepts/networking|networking]] and seizing unexpected opportunities. It's crucial to be curious, understand what others are working on, and build relationships within and outside your company. These connections create a reservoir of goodwill and open doors to serendipitous career leaps that wouldn't arise from linear promotion paths alone.
