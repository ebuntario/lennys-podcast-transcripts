Companies unwilling to share financials with director-level candidates demonstrate either hidden problems or a culture of distrust. For senior marketing technology roles requiring strategic planning and budget management, financial transparency is essential. This [[concepts/transparency]] issue often indicates deeper organizational problems that could hinder effective technology decision-making and resource allocation.
