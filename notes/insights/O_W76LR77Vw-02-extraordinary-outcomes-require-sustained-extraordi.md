Achieving 99th percentile outcomes demands consistent, uncomfortable effort; if a team finds itself in a comfort zone, it is a sign of underperformance. This [[concepts/high-performance|high-performance]] mindset is necessary in competitive markets, as any relaxation creates an opening for more ambitious competitors. The leader's role is to remind the team that their extraordinary effort is contributing to a rare and valuable outcome.
