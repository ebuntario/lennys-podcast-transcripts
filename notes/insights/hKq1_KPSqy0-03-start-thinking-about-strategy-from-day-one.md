Founders should consider strategic principles even before achieving [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]]. Early conversations about the underlying characteristics of a business proposition can tilt probabilities toward the eventual availability of power. Strategy isn't a phase that comes after validation; it's a lens to apply from the very beginning to shape more durable opportunities.
