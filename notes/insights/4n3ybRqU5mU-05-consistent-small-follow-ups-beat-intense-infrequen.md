The final ingredient for effective coaching is consistent follow-up, which can be as simple as a regular nudge about a personal development plan. Small, weekly investments in people development are more sustainable and effective than large, infrequent reviews. This principle of [[concepts/consistency|consistency]] applies both to the manager's coaching time and the PM's learning time.
