Include a simple, non-intrusive mention of your podcast in email signatures, social profiles, and other routine communications. This [[concepts/consistency|consistent]] but subtle promotion occasionally converts unexpected contacts into listeners. The cumulative effect of these micro-exposures builds awareness over time without requiring active promotion efforts.
