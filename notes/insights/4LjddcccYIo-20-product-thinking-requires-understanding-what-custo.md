When joining Airbnb, Chip initially thought the "product" was the homes and apartments, while the tech team focused on digital features. This highlights how different perspectives can reveal what customers truly value. Effective [[concepts/organizational-product-strategy|product strategy]] requires bridging these viewpoints to build what users actually need rather than what internal teams assume.
