[[concepts/product-frameworks|Product frameworks]] should be treated as learning tools and job aids rather than rigid prescriptions to install. Their effectiveness depends heavily on your specific [[concepts/organizational-context|organizational context]], including company size, industry, and cultural norms. Successful teams adapt frameworks to their circumstances rather than expecting one-size-fits-all solutions.
