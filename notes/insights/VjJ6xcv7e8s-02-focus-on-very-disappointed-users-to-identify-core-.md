Ignore feedback from users who would be "somewhat disappointed," as they view the product as a nice-to-have. Instead, deeply analyze the "very disappointed" cohort to understand the core [[concepts/value-proposition|value proposition]]. This insight should drive [[concepts/product-positioning|product positioning]], onboarding, and roadmap decisions to avoid diluting the experience for must-have users.
