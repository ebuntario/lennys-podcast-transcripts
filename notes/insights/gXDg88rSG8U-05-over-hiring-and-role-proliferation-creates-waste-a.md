Many companies, especially outside Silicon Valley, have created excessive roles like agile coaches, product owners, and product ops, leading to bloated teams and high cost with little outcome. The best companies achieve more with leaner, cross-functional teams, and reducing this [[concepts/process-overhead|process overhead]] can actually increase velocity and innovation.
