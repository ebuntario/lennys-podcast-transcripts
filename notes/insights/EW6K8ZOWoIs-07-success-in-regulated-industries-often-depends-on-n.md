In complex fields like healthcare and fintech, getting deals done or accessing critical data can be heavily dependent on who you know. Many startups struggle because they are stuck waiting for introductions to the right decision-makers within large, incumbent organizations. Understanding whether your [[concepts/b2b-sales|business model is network-dependent]] is crucial for navigating these environments effectively.
