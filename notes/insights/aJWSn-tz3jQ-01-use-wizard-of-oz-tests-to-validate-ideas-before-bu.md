You can validate a product idea by creating a facade that simulates the experience without building the full backend. For the Gmail tabbed inbox, the team used a simple HTML mockup and manually sorted emails behind the scenes during user interviews. This [[concepts/product-validation|Wizard of Oz test]] provided strong evidence of user delight with minimal engineering effort, de-risking the decision to build.
