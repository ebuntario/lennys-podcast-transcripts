A transformative piece of advice is to overcome the instinct that leaders must have all the answers. Being honest about what you don't know and proactively [[concepts/continuous-learning|asking for help]] from peers, your team, and mentors leads to better decisions and product outcomes. This builds trust and ensures you're not limited by your own perspective when solving complex problems.
