For high-velocity, high-craft work, co-locate or closely integrate designers, engineers, and product managers. This [[concepts/team-structure|multidisciplinary setup]] allows for rapid batting of ideas back and forth, where an engineer's technical insight and a designer's creative push combine to elevate the final product.
