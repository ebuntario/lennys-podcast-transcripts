When transitioning from a structured corporate job to self-employment, creating external [[concepts/deadlines|deadlines]] is crucial for maintaining productivity. Announcing a regular publishing schedule to an audience creates a powerful form of accountability that mimics workplace pressure. This external commitment forces you to deliver consistently, combating the tendency to procrastinate when you have full control over your time.
