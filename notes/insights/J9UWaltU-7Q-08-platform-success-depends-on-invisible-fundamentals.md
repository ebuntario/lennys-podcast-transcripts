Winning platforms prioritize often-invisible [[concepts/platform-fundamentals|fundamentals]] like data residency, reliability, privacy, and performance over feature counts. Just as messaging apps won through phone book integration and encryption, AI platforms must excel at core infrastructure. This requires a game-of-inches focus on optimizing the underlying engine that delivers value.
