Healthier organizations address chronic issues systematically, which better prepares them to handle acute stressors like economic downturns or pandemics. Companies that proactively design responses to challenges outperform those that simply wait for conditions to normalize. This distinction in [[concepts/organizational-health|organizational health]] becomes particularly visible during periods of significant change.
