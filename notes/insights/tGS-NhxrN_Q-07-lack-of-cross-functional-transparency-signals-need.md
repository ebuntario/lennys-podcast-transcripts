A clear signal that an organization needs [[concepts/product-operations|product operations]] is when product launches suffer due to poor cross-functional transparency and readiness. When teams know something is coming but don't understand how to prepare customers or adjust their workflows, it indicates a systemic communication gap. Product ops addresses this by creating feedback loops and ensuring all stakeholders understand both what's coming and what to do with that information.
