With the decline of deterministic matching due to privacy changes like Apple's ATT, marketers must adapt to [[concepts/probabilistic-modeling]]. This involves creating models based on available data from 30% of users and extrapolating to the full population. As browsers strip URL parameters and cookie blockers increase, probabilistic approaches become necessary for understanding campaign performance in an increasingly opaque advertising landscape.
