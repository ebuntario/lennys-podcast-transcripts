Brand is not just marketing; it's the amalgamation of every interaction a customer has with your company—product, support, sales, and recruiting. Consistency across these touchpoints builds [[concepts/brand-trust|brand trust]], which in turn makes every future initiative (like launching a new product) easier because customers start with positive expectations.
