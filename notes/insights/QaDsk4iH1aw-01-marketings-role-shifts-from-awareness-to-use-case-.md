For products with inherent awareness, like ChatGPT, the core marketing challenge is not top-of-funnel demand but helping users understand practical applications. The work becomes creating "use case epiphany," moving customers from "I know what this is" to "I know exactly how to use it for my needs." This requires a diagnostic approach to [[concepts/customer-insights|customer understanding]] rather than blindly applying standard demand-generation playbooks.
