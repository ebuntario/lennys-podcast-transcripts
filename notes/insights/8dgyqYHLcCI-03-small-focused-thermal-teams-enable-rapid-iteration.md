The project started as a "thermal" team—a small, dedicated group with a single decision-maker and 100% focus. This [[concepts/team-structure|structure]], free from heavyweight processes and bureaucracy, allowed for goals to be set dynamically and for features to be built and shipped in weeks instead of months or years.
