Your champion is the person inside the acquirer who has the fantasy and fights for you. The buyer cares only about the business case math. Advocates are passive supporters who provide intel, and blockers (like Legal or Procurement) can say no but not yes. Effective [[concepts/stakeholder-management|stakeholder management]] requires tailoring your approach to each character's motivations.
