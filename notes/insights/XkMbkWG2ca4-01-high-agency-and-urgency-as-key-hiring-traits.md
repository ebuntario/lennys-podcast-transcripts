When building a fast-moving team, prioritize hiring individuals with [[concepts/high-agency|high agency]] and a sense of urgency. These people proactively identify and solve problems without waiting for consensus, allowing the organization to move quickly and adapt to customer needs. This trait is more valuable than extensive process or coordination in dynamic environments.
