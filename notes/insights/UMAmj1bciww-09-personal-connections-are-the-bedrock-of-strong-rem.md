In a remote environment, intentionally create space for personal check-ins and sharing beyond work topics. Knowing colleagues personally—their families, interests, and lives—fosters the trust and camaraderie needed for a resilient, motivated team. This human connection is a non-negotiable element of [[concepts/remote-work|remote culture]].
