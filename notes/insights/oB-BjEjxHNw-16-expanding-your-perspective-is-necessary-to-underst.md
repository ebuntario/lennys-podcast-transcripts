The Japanese proverb "a frog in a well cannot know the ocean" warns against limited thinking confined to immediate experience. Actively seeking new experiences and challenges pushes the boundaries of what you believe is possible. This [[concepts/mindset|growth mindset]] is essential for innovation and [[concepts/career-development|career advancement]].
