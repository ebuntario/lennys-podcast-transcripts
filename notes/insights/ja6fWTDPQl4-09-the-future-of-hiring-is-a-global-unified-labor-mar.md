Current labor markets are inefficient and disaggregated, with candidates applying to dozens of jobs and companies reviewing only a fraction of available talent. Automating the matching process at software cost enables a future of a global, unified [[concepts/labor-marketplaces|labor marketplace]] where every candidate can apply and every company can hire from a single, perfect-information system.
