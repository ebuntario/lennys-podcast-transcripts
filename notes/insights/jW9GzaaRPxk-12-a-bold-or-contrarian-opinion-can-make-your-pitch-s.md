When you lack hard news, having a [[concepts/contrarian-viewpoint|bold or controversial stance]] on a trending topic can attract reporter interest. Reporters often seek balanced perspectives for their stories, so a well-reasoned opposing view can make you a valuable source. This approach works well for op-eds and commentary.
