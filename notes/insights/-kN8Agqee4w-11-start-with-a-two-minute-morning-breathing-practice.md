Begin by experimenting with different practices to see what feels good. Then, commit to just two minutes of a chosen [[concepts/breathwork|breathing exercise]] each morning. This builds the neural pathway so you're more likely to remember and use the tool during stressful moments. Consistency with tiny practices is more sustainable than aiming for lengthy sessions.
