Craft a winning aspiration by asking the team to imagine a newspaper headline in two years describing the product's success based on the strategy. This exercise forces simple, benefit-oriented language and describes the ultimate impact. Blending individual headlines into one statement creates a shared [[concepts/vision|vision]] of success.
