Culture isn't accidental; it's the deliberate embedding of founder values into rituals, hiring, and decision-making. To scale [[concepts/company-culture|culture]], codify what matters (e.g., through core values) and create consistent rituals that reinforce those values. Hire people who embody them, using dedicated interviewers to assess fit.
