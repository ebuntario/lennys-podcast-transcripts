Instead of creating rigid five-year plans, successful careers are built by taking small, experimental steps called [[concepts/little-bets|little bets]]. You take action on things that give you energy, learn from the outcomes, and pivot rapidly. This method of rapid recovery from failure is more effective than analysis paralysis when pursuing opportunities.
