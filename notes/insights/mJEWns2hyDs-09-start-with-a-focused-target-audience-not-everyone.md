Early-stage companies should avoid trying to be everything to everyone. Instead, identify a [[concepts/target-audience|target audience]] that is large enough to build a significant business if you achieve strong market share within it. This focused group is who you will actively try to acquire for the next 18 months, though you may still attract users outside this circle.
