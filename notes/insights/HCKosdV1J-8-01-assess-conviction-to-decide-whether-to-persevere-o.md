When deciding whether to continue a struggling venture, founders should ask themselves if they have more or less [[concepts/conviction|conviction]] in their solution now than when they started. If learning and customer validation have increased your belief, you are likely in the [[concepts/messy-middle|messy middle]] and should persevere. If you would not start the venture knowing what you know now, it's a sign to quit or pivot, as life is too short to work without deep belief.
