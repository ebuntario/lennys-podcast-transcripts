Avoid building a product team in your own image by hiring people with similar strengths. Instead, consciously build a balanced [[concepts/talent-portfolio|talent portfolio]] with complementary skillsets. For example, pair homegrown PMs who deeply understand the product and culture with externally hired PMs who bring rigorous methodologies from other companies, creating a stronger, more versatile whole.
