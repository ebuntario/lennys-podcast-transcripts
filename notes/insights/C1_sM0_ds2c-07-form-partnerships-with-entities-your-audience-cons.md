Effective partnerships require more than shared audiences; partner with organizations your target customers view as trusted advisors for their specific problems. For example, venture capital firms are valuable partners for HR/payroll solutions because portfolio companies ask them for tool recommendations. Prioritize partners who influence purchase decisions within your niche.
