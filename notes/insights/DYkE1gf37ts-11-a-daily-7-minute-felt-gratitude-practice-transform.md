Practice gratitude for seven minutes daily with another person, speaking from the felt sensation of gratitude, not just a list of thoughts. After establishing the practice, focus gratitude on areas where you feel lack (time, money, love). This [[gratitude-practice|gratitude practice]] redefines your identity from someone who lacks to someone who has, fundamentally changing your reality and attracting more abundance.
