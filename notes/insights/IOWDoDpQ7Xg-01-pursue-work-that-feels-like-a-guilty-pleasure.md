People often undervalue what comes easily to them, assuming work must be unpleasant to be valuable. Instead, you should identify the activity you feel "guilty to get paid for" and pursue it with intensity. Doing what you're naturally good at and enjoy leads to greater [[concepts/impact|impact]] and career satisfaction than grinding through unpleasant tasks.
