The "working backwards" process involves writing a press release and FAQ for a product before building it. This forces concrete, specific language—replacing adjectives like "great" with measurable outcomes—and clarifies the customer problem, solution, and launch plan. This [[concepts/planning|planning]] method aligns teams and de-risks launches by simulating the end state.
