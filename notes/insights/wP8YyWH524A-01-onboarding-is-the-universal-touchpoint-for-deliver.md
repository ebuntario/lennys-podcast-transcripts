Onboarding is the only part of a product experience that 100% of users will touch, making it a critical [[concepts/user-onboarding|onboarding]] lever. It's the first opportunity for a company to deliver on its [[concepts/brand-promise|brand promise]] made in the marketplace. When the brand promise and product experience are misaligned, it creates mismatched expectations and disappointed customers.
