The product manager's role is not to be the "mini-CEO" who solely owns commercial thinking, but to facilitate the *entire team* thinking like a CEO. Engineers, designers, and researchers often have unique insights into systems, customer needs, and how pieces fit together to drive impact. The PM's job is to bring in and steward conversations about business success, enabling the collective intelligence of the team to align on [[concepts/commercial-thinking|commercial outcomes]].
