Operators are constantly in the "trees," dealing with immediate problems. [[concepts/forest-time|Forest time]] is the intentional practice of taking a day off monthly to elevate perspective, see the entire landscape, and identify alternative paths. This improves strategic aim, which is critical because a small misdirection in product strategy can waste millions in development effort. Provide teams with a structured worksheet to guide this reflection.
