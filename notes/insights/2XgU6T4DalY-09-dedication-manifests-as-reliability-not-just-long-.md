[[concepts/dedication|Dedication]] to work and team is demonstrated through consistent [[concepts/reliability|reliability]], not merely through long hours. This means being responsive, following through on commitments on time, showing up prepared for meetings, and holding yourself to a high standard because others are relying on you. It's a form of respect and [[concepts/teamwork|teamwork]].
