Customers often adapt to pain points with workarounds and may not articulate the core problem directly. Effective research involves observing behavior more than asking leading questions like "Would you love this?" Search for the problem indirectly and from multiple angles, as exemplified by products like the Swiffer, which solved an unstated mopping frustration.
