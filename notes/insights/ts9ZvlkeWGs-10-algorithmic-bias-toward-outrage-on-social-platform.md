Social media algorithms often promote outrage and controversy, making nuanced discussions difficult and allowing misinformation to spread rapidly. A negative narrative can achieve massive reach while corrections gain little traction, distorting public perception. Engaging in long-form, contextual conversations is more effective for complex topics than battling [[concepts/misinformation|algorithmically amplified negativity]] in short-form feeds.
