For products that democratize professional tools (like design or video editing), [[concepts/onboarding|onboarding]] is critical to bridge the gap between simplicity and robust functionality. Early-stage companies can often 2-4X activation rates through onboarding improvements, while later-stage companies can still achieve 20-30% lifts. Customizing the landing experience based on user intent is key.
