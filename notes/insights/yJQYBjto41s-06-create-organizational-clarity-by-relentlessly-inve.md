Leadership is not about constant positivity but about unpacking complex contexts and trade-offs. A leader's role is to ask difficult questions until the team understands the options, even if they don't all agree. This pursuit of [[concepts/clarity|clarity]] ensures everyone is aligned on the goal and the path forward.
