Begin sales conversations by sharing your unique perspective on the market rather than immediately demonstrating features. This establishes you as a knowledgeable guide who understands the buyer's world. The insight should frame why your [[concepts/positioning|positioning]] matters and create context that makes your solution's value obvious before you even mention your product.
