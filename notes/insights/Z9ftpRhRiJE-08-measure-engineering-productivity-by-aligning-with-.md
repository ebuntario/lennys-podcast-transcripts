Rather than relying solely on internal metrics like deployment frequency, evaluate [[concepts/engineering-culture|engineering productivity]] by its contribution to product and business goals. Maintain a roadmap of valuable, impactful work delivered over the past six months. This demonstrates effectiveness in terms that matter to stakeholders and the board.
