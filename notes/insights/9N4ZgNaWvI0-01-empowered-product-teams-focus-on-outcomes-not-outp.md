Feature teams are given a roadmap of features (output) to design, build, and ship, often with fixed deadlines. In contrast, [[concepts/empowered-product-teams|empowered product teams]] are given problems to solve and are measured on whether they deliver business and customer outcomes. Strong product companies understand that success is about delivering value, not just shipping features.
