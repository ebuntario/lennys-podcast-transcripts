Human intervention in onboarding can reveal powerful insights that should be [[concepts/productizing-learnings|productized]] for scalability. At Patreon, connecting high-potential creators with human support increased their second-month revenue by 25%. These learnings were then translated into "opinionated defaults" within the product, making it easy to do the right thing while still allowing user choice.
