The [[concepts/strategic-narrative|strategic narrative]] framework is particularly powerful for B2B enterprise technology companies. These environments involve complex products, group buying decisions, and rapidly evolving solutions where simple feature comparisons are less meaningful. The narrative provides a unifying story for the buying committee and frames the product within a larger, necessary shift.
