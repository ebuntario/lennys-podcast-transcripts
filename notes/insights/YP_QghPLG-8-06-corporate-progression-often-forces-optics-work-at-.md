As teams grow beyond approximately 50 people, corporate structures inevitably require leaders to spend increasing time on [[concepts/optics-work|optics work]] rather than impact-focused activities. Recognizing this threshold allows product leaders to make intentional career choices rather than following traditional progression paths that may lead to frustration.
