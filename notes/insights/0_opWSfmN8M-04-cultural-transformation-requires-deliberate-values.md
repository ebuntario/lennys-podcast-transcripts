To reshape a company's culture, you must explicitly rewrite core values to act as a "sharp knife" for cutting out ineffective behaviors and people. Implementing a structured quarterly performance process that scores both goals and adherence to these new [[concepts/company-values|company values]] allows you to systematically shape the organization. This deliberate approach, while painful in the short term, builds a highly aligned and performant team.
