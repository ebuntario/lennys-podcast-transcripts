Strategy must ultimately align with where customers want to go. Attempting to hold back a [[concepts/market-forces|customer-driven shift]]—like digital channels or new product formats—is like trying to hold back the tide with a sandcastle. Even powerful incumbents fail if they resist too long, as customers will find a way to get what they want.
