The goal of the [[concepts/full-stack-builder|full stack builder]] model is to enable any builder to take an idea to market, regardless of their formal role. It emphasizes a fluid [[concepts/human-machine-collaboration|human-machine collaboration]] where individuals combine skills across traditional domains. This approach creates smaller, mission-focused pods that are nimble and can be assembled or reassembled quickly.
