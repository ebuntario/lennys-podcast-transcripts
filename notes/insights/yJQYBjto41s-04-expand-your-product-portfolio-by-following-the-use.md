New product lines should emerge naturally from observing what users are trying to accomplish with your existing tools. Figma created FigJam, Slides, and Buzz by identifying workflows that were awkward within the main product and deserved a dedicated surface. This [[concepts/user-centric-design|user-centric]] approach often reveals opportunities that traditional TAM (Total Addressable Market) analysis would miss.
