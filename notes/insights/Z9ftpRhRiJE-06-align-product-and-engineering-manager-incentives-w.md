A powerful method to improve [[concepts/product-leadership|product]] and [[concepts/engineering-management|engineering]] collaboration is to give EM/PM pairs the same performance rating. This shared incentive forces both roles to solve for the full set of business and technical constraints together. It reduces friction by making success interdependent, moving beyond functional silos.
