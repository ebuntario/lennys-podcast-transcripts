Periodically use the quest framework to diagnose if you're being pulled out of alignment by tasks that drain your energy or misalign with your strengths. This [[concepts/self-assessment|regular check-in]] helps you delegate or eliminate misaligned work, restoring energy and focus. It turns career management into an ongoing process, not a reactive crisis.
