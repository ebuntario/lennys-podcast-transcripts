Revolut emphasizes [[concepts/direct-customer-connection|direct communication channels]] between product owners and customers, avoiding filtered feedback from intermediaries. They build tools for easy access to user panels and encourage product teams to gather firsthand insights about emotions, pain points, and usage contexts that get lost in refined reports.
