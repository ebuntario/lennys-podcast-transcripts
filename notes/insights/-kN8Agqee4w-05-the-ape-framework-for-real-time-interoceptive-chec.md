Use the APE acronym to quickly tune into your body: check your **A**wareness (narrow vs. expanded), **P**osture, and **E**motion/sensation. Doing this before meetings or when feeling "off" provides valuable data about your state. This cultivates [[concepts/interoception|interoception]], helping you catch early signs of stress or intuitive nudges.
