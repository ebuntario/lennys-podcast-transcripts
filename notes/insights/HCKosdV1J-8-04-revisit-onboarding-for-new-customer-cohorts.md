Onboarding experiences must be reimagined as new customer cohorts enter the funnel. Early adopters are more forgiving, but later pragmatist customers are more skeptical and less tolerant of friction. As a product scales and its audience broadens, the [[concepts/onboarding|onboarding]] process must change to meet these new users where they are, which is a continuous opportunity for improvement.
