PR rarely drives direct user acquisition. Instead, use it for targeted credibility goals like improving candidate response rates in hiring or boosting cold outbound email replies. Have clear, measurable objectives for how you'll leverage the coverage asset. A thoughtful [[concepts/public-relations|public relations]] strategy focused on specific outcomes is far more valuable than chasing general press.
