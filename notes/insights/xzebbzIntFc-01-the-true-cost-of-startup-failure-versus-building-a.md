A startup failing is not the worst outcome for a founder. Far worse is building a [[concepts/zombie-company|zombie company]] that you hate but feel you cannot leave. The mental health crisis among founders is exacerbated when so-called "successes" involve selling a company that becomes something abhorrent, leaving founders feeling complicit or needing to distance themselves from their own creation.
