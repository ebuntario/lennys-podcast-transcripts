A single premium tier often leaves money on the table. Different user personas derive vastly different value from a product. Research to understand these segments—from casual users to power users—and create pricing plans that match their [[concepts/willingness-to-pay|willingness to pay]] and perceived value, maximizing revenue across the spectrum.
