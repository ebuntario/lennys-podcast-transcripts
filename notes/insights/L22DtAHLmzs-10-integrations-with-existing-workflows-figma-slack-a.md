For a new, disruptive tool to be adopted, it must seamlessly fit into existing [[concepts/workflow-tools|workflows]]. Building integrations that allow the tool to act within environments people already use (like converting a Figma design via a URL or acting as a developer bot in Slack) reduces friction and demonstrates immediate utility, accelerating [[concepts/user-adoption|user adoption]] within companies.
