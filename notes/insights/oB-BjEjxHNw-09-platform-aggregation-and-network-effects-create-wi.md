Large platforms win in the [[concepts/creator-economy|creator economy]] by aggregating supply or demand, creating powerful [[concepts/network-effects|network effects]] that are difficult for new entrants to break. Their strategic features and tooling are designed to retain creators and prevent churn. However, vertical-specific platforms with tailored features can still find success by serving niche communities.
