When co-founding with a partner or close friend, define complementary domains and clear decision-making rights to prevent overlap disputes. Cultivate [[concepts/constructive-conflict|constructive conflict]] by focusing on problems, not personal attacks, and assuming the most respectful interpretation of feedback. Regular check-ins on the health of the relationship ensure the partnership remains a priority.
