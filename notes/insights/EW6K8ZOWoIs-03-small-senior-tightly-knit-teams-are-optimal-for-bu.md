When launching a new initiative inside an established organization, a small team of senior, trusted individuals reduces miscommunication and increases focus. This structure helps counteract the lack of existential financial pressure that startups face, forcing disciplined resource allocation. A [[concepts/team-building|tightly-knit senior team]] can achieve real scale and business impact before significantly expanding headcount.
