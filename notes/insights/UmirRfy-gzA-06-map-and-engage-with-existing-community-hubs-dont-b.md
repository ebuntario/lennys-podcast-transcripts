Go where your audience already congregates instead of trying to bring them to you. Figma identified Twitter as the design community's hub and used a scraper to build a node graph of influencers and topic clusters. This [[concepts/influencer-identification|influencer identification]] allowed targeted outreach for feedback and helped passively build relationships over time before asking for adoption.
