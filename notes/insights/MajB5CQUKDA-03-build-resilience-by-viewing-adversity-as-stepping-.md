The most successful people are not those with charmed lives, but those who learn to turn stumbling blocks into stepping stones. Building [[concepts/resilience|resilience]] comes from processing hard feedback and product failures, then using those lessons to grow stronger. This adaptability is more valuable than perfectionism in a nonlinear career path.
