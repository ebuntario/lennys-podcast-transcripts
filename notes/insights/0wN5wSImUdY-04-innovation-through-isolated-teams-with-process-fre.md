Creating an isolated team with process freedom is a powerful pattern for catalyzing new product lines or pivots. This "beneficial silo" allows the team to work with faster iteration loops, free from the cadence and constraints of mature product teams. Executive sponsorship is critical to protect the team from distractions and grant [[concepts/team-autonomy|team autonomy]] for rapid experimentation.
