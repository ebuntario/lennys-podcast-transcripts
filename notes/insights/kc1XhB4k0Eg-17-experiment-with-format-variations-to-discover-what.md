Regularly test different podcast formats like solo episodes, Q&A sessions, deep dives, or co-hosted conversations to discover what you genuinely enjoy creating. This [[concepts/experimentation|experimentation]] prevents creative burnout by ensuring your show evolves with your interests. Since content creation often requires years of unpaid work before monetization, enjoying the process is essential for [[concepts/creator-sustainability|sustainability]].
