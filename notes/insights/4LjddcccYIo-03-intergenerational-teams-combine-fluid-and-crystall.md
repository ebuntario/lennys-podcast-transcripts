Younger brains typically excel at fluid intelligence—fast, focused, linear problem-solving. Older brains often leverage crystallized intelligence—holistic, systemic thinking and pattern recognition. Combining these creates powerful [[concepts/team-dynamics|team dynamics]] where speed and focus meet broad perspective and [[concepts/cognitive-diversity|cognitive diversity]].
