For marketplaces with unique inventory from independent sellers, building buyer confidence is paramount. Elevating signals like customer reviews, photos from past buyers, and scarcity indicators (e.g., "only one left") are highly effective conversion tactics. These elements provide the [[concepts/social-proof|social proof]] and urgency needed for a buyer to choose one unique item from millions of options.
