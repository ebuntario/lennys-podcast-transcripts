When scaling, avoid simply importing processes from other successful companies. Instead, reinvent systems that are authentic to your own culture and history, like building a house where every brick matches. Trying to insert another company's "bricks" often fails because it clashes with your foundational [[concepts/company-culture|company culture]]. Have confidence in developing what works uniquely for your team at each new stage of growth.
