As a company grows, a founder must evolve from one-to-one storytelling to one-to-many storytelling. This is a skill to be developed like any other craft, necessary to shape the company's direction and market perception. Treat it as a [[concepts/strategic-skill|strategic skill]] required to unlock the next phase of growth.
