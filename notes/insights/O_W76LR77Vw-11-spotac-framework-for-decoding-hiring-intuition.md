After developing intuition from interviewing thousands of candidates, use the SPOTAC framework to decode it: Is the candidate Smart, Passionate, Optimistic, Tenacious, Adaptable, and Kind? This framework helps translate gut feelings into articulable reasons for a hiring decision, moving beyond vague impressions to structured [[concepts/talent-evaluation|talent evaluation]].
