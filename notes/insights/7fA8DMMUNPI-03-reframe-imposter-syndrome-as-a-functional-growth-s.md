Instead of trying to eliminate [[concepts/imposter-syndrome|imposter syndrome]], ask how it serves you. Often, the feeling of being an imposter signals a growth edge and motivates you to learn and work harder. The key is to leverage this energy without letting it lead to burnout or unnecessary overwork.
