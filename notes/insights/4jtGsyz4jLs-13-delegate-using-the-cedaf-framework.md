Ensure Comprehension (provide all needed context), generate Excitement (explain the why), De-risk (identify and mitigate obvious pitfalls), Align (verify understanding), and shorten the Feedback loop. This [[concepts/cedaf-framework|structured approach]] to delegation increases success rates and maintains quality standards.
