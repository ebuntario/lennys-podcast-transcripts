Deeply internalized negative self-beliefs are like well-worn neural pathways that take time to rewire. Building [[concepts/self-compassion|self-compassion]] starts with awareness from seeking truth, then involves daily practice of intercepting [[concepts/conditioned-behavior|conditioned behaviors]]. Small, consistent actions, like simply accepting a compliment, accumulate to form a new, kinder internal narrative.
