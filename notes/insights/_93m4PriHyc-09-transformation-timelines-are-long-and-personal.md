Profound identity shifts are not overnight events but often involve a long "[[concepts/valley-of-transition|valley of transition]]" between the old and new self, which can span years. Historical examples like Buddha or modern teachers like Eckhart Tolle illustrate journeys lasting seven years or more. This underscores that deep [[concepts/personal-transformation|personal transformation]] is a marathon, not a sprint, requiring sustained dedication.
