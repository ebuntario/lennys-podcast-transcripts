Starting a company is incredibly lonely, as founders can't share their deepest anxieties with employees. YC's group office hours place founders together to share problems, realizing everyone's company is "broken" in some way. The structured [[concepts/accountability|accountability]] of stating goals and reporting progress every two weeks leverages founders' competitive nature to drive execution.
