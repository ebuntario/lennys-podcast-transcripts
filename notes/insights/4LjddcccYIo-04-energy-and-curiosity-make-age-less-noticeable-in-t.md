People notice your energy more than your wrinkles. Showing up with passionate engagement, curiosity, and positive energy creates "age fluidity" where chronological age becomes less relevant. This [[concepts/mindset|mindset]] helps older professionals remain valuable in fast-paced environments by focusing on contribution rather than years.
