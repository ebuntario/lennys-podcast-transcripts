Choosing not to raise venture capital allows a company to avoid the "Silicon Valley game" of constant fundraising, PR, and pivoting for valuation. This path is harder initially but leads to stronger [[concepts/product-development|product development]] driven by genuine customer need and word-of-mouth from experts, rather than hype. It fosters a culture focused on building a 10x better product for a deeply aligned customer base.
