As trust in institutions declines, audiences increasingly form relationships with individual experts and creators. Successful media organizations must enable journalists to build personal platforms while maintaining organizational scale and support. This hybrid approach combines the [[concepts/creator-economy|creator economy]] model with institutional resources to help experts reach wider audiences sustainably.
