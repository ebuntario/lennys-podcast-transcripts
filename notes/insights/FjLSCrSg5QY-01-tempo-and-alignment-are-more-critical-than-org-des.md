Real professionals ship at a high frequency, regardless of their role. Establishing a clear [[concepts/operational-tempo|tempo framework]] for what "good looks like" in terms of output is more important than organizational structure. Misalignment often occurs when leadership understands the goals, but teams lack clarity on the expected pace and quality of delivery, leading to friction and underperformance.
