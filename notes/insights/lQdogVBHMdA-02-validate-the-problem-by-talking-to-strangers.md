To validate if a problem is real and worth solving, speak with at least 20 people you don't know, not just friends and family. A strong signal is when someone rephrases the problem in their own words, indicating they personally experience it. This [[concepts/customer-discovery|customer discovery]] process helps confirm whether the problem is widespread enough to build a startup around.
