As a product manager or leader, proactively challenging your own assumptions is critical for effective [[concepts/listening-skills|listening]]. If there is no conflict or contention in discussions, it may indicate you are not fully absorbing diverse perspectives. This practice helps avoid complacency and ensures you are genuinely working backwards from the customer problem.
