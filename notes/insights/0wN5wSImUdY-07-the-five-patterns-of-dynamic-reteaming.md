Teams change in five core patterns: One-by-One (people join/leave the company), Grow-and-Split (teams expand and divide), Merging (teams consolidate), Isolation (creating a separate team for innovation), and Switching (people move between teams). Recognizing these [[concepts/change-patterns|change patterns]] helps leaders anticipate and design organizational evolution rather than resist it.
