A powerful framework for any strategic decision is to ask: "Knowing what I know today, if I were to start this company now, what would I do?" If the answer differs from your current path, you should strongly consider changing course today. This [[concepts/scenario-planning|forward-looking assessment]] prevents clinging to outdated assumptions.
