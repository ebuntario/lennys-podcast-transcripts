When transforming an entrenched product ("minus one to one"), internal resistance from teams reliant on existing metrics can stall progress. A effective tactic is to carve out a small, randomized cohort of users (e.g., 2 million) to build the new experience for, isolated from core business metrics. This allows for [[concepts/experimentation|rapid learning and proof]] of the new model's potential before scaling it company-wide.
