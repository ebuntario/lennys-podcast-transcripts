AI models will become increasingly differentiated based on the values and [[concepts/objective-functions|objective functions]] of the companies that build them. A model can be designed to optimize for user engagement (leading to lengthy, sycophantic interactions) or for user productivity and time-saving (encouraging efficiency). This choice in [[concepts/ai-alignment|AI alignment]] fundamentally shapes the model's behavior and its long-term impact.
