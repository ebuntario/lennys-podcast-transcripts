A powerful narrative starts by concisely naming the shift from an old way of operating to a new one, such as "transactions to subscriptions" or "opinions to reality." This compact naming, while an overstatement, creates a clear and memorable [[concepts/messaging|messaging]] anchor. It frames your company not just as a vendor, but as a guide helping customers navigate this essential change.
