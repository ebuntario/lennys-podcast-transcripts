Instead of trying to craft a perfect strategy from scratch, identify the most painful gap between your current outcomes and desired outcomes. Use the strategy cascade to make different choices that close that single gap. This [[concepts/iteration|iterative betterment]] approach is more manageable and leads to continuous improvement over time.
