Break down a complex decision (like a venture investment) into component forecasts (e.g., probability of Series A, market quality rating). By tracking these explicit forecasts against outcomes, you can give experts feedback on their accuracy in specific areas, allowing them to refine their [[concepts/intuition|intuition]] and decision-making over time.
