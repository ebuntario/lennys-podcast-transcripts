Product managers should follow three key principles when working with stakeholders: raise difficult issues without being difficult to work with, bring out important topics without drawing importance to yourself, and be responsible for getting decisions made without needing to make all decisions yourself. These tenets help maintain productive relationships while advancing product work effectively through strong [[concepts/stakeholder-management|stakeholder collaboration]].
