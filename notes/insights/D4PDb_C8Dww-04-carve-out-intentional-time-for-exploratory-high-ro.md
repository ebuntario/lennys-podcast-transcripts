Exploratory work and deep dives are often the first tasks sacrificed when inbound requests pile up. To prevent this, be intentional: set goals for self-directed work, run hackathons, and secure partner buy-in by demonstrating how these explorations have driven future roadmaps. This [[concepts/proactive-analysis|proactive analysis]] uncovers significant opportunities that reactive work misses.
