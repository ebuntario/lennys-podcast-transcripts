Products never remain static; they either improve or degrade with each release. From a customer's perspective, every feature you ship is compared against competitors' moves, making each release a strategic play in a [[concepts/benchmarking|competitive chess game]]. This framework forces clarity on whether your work gains or loses ground relative to the market.
