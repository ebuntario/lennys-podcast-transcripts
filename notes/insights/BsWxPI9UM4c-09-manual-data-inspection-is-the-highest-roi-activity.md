Looking directly at user traces is the most valuable and underutilized practice for improving AI products. Product managers and builders should regularly sample and annotate interactions to develop an intuition for what's working and what's not. This hands-on [[concepts/error-analysis|error analysis]] consistently reveals critical issues that aggregate metrics miss.
