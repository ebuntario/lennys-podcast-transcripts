Examining gross margins provides a coarse but effective instrument to assess whether a business adds sufficient value and possesses differentiation. If you can't command healthy margins, it often indicates customers have alternatives or you're not solving an urgent enough problem. This analysis short-circuits to the core competitive issue and helps founders understand the economic machine they'll need to build within 2-3 years before inevitable [[concepts/margin-compression|margin compression]] occurs.
