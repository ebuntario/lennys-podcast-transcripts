Effective low-cost growth involves finding where your target audience asks questions and providing genuine answers. Platforms like Reddit, Quora, and niche forums are ideal for [[concepts/community-building|community engagement]]. Focus on solving people's problems first, then subtly present your solution, rather than leading with a sales pitch, to build trust and avoid being removed from the community.
