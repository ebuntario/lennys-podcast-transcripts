The most critical skill for a product manager is crystallizing what problem truly matters. Clear [[concepts/problem-framing|problem definition]] guides everything from feature prioritization and team motivation to business model and strategy. It moves debates from abstract "this vs. that" choices to focused discussions on the core issue you are solving for the customer.
