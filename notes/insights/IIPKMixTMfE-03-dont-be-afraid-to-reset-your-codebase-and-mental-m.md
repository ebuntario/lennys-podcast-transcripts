Progress can be achieved faster through better abstractions than through incremental fixes on a flawed foundation. If you find a fundamentally better way to solve the core problem, you can recover lost ground quickly. This mindset frees you to discard work and restart, which is a powerful form of [[concepts/strategic-reset|strategic reset]].
