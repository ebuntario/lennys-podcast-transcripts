Treat anything less than enthusiastic, wholehearted agreement as a "no." A lukewarm "maybe" or reluctant "yes" often leads to unmet expectations and failed commitments. To get a genuine [[concepts/commitment|hell yes]], ask "What would it take for you to be fully in on this?"
