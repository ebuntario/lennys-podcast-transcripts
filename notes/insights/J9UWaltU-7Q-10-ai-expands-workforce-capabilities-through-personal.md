Employees will bring personalized [[concepts/agent-stack|agent stacks]] to work, similar to bringing your own device, granting access to skills they previously lacked. This [[concepts/skill-augmentation]] can exponentially increase GDP by making millions of workers significantly more capable. The focus shifts from hierarchical management to enabling individuals with tools that expand their potential.
