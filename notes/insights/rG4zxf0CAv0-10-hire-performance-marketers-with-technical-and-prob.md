Look for candidates with backgrounds in technical fields like engineering, math, or physics, as they often excel in data-driven marketing. Beyond channel experience, assess their ability to solve tracking, attribution, and modeling challenges. Strong [[concepts/problem-solving|problem-solving skills]] are more critical than years of experience alone.
