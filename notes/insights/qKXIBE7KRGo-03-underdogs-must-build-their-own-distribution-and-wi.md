As an underdog, you cannot rely on the playbook that requires more resources or institutional backing. You must build your own distribution from day one by winning hearts and minds. This involves shaping your story to fit your audience's passions and finding the key [[concepts/audience-understanding|influencers]] who will help spread it for you, as you likely won't have immediate access to traditional media.
