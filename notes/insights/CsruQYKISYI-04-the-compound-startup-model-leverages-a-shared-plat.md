A "compound startup" builds multiple distinct business verticals (e.g., payroll, benefits, IT) on top of a single, shared platform and [[concepts/system-of-record|system of record]]. While the initial platform investment is high, it allows new product teams to launch rapidly by reusing core data and capabilities. This model creates defensible differentiation that competitors stitching together separate systems cannot match.
