Effective [[concepts/natural-language-interface|natural language interfaces]] require intentional design decisions about how much progress to show, what follow-up questions to suggest, and how to structure conversations. Showing too little work creates uncertainty, while showing too much feels verbose; the right balance depends on context and may evolve toward personalization over time.
