Design choices are heavily influenced by what teams measure and reward; short-term [[concepts/incentives|incentives]] (like quarterly conversion bumps) can lead to predatory patterns. To ensure ethical [[concepts/product-ethics|product design]], tie incentives to specific user behaviors that correlate with long-term customer value, not just intermediate metrics like login counts.
