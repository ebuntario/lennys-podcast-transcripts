Strategic thinking means seeing the often-invisible systems that govern how things work. Systems create culture ("the way things are around here") to protect themselves. If an action feels scary, ask what system is being threatened. To innovate, you must [[concepts/systems-thinking|understand the system]] you're operating within and find leverage points to change the rules.
