Effective growth work begins with deeply understanding the current state through comprehensive data instrumentation. This allows teams to identify the key opportunities and barriers impacting metrics like activation or retention. Only then should they execute on building products to address those specific levers, ensuring a [[concepts/data-driven-decisions|data-driven]] approach rather than guesswork.
