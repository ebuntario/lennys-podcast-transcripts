As a CEO, you can define your role based on where you provide the most value. By hiring a president to handle operations and executive team management, you can shift your focus from administrative tasks to areas like product and marketing. This [[concepts/organizational-design|organizational design]] change can increase time spent on high-impact work from single digits to over 60% of your week.
