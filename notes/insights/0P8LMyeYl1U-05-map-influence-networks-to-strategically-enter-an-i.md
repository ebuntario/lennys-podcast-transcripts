To break into a new industry, analyze the "flocking patterns" of influential people—tracking how key individuals (like VCs or executives) move between companies and shape ecosystems. This [[concepts/industry-analysis|industry analysis]] helps identify strategic entry points, such as smaller companies connected to influential networks, where you can offer unique value.
