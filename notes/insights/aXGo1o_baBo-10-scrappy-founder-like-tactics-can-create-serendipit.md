You can engineer opportunities by creatively maneuvering to get into the right room with the right people. This requires a scrappy, resourceful mindset focused on achieving an outcome "by any means necessary." Demonstrating this kind of [[concepts/agency|proactive hustle]] is a highly valuable trait in both founders and product leaders.
