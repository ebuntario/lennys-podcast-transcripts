If you are unsure who the decision-maker is, assume it is you and act accordingly to avoid slowing the company down. This mindset fosters [[concepts/ownership|ownership]] and accelerates progress, which is critical for maintaining [[concepts/company-velocity|company velocity]]. If you are genuinely concerned it is not your decision, simply ask for clarity rather than getting stuck in paralysis.
