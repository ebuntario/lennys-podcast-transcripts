Simple physical tools like multi-color pens and clipboards can be more effective for certain types of thinking and planning than complex digital systems. The tactile experience and freedom from software constraints can enhance [[concepts/cognitive-processing|cognitive processing]] and creativity. Tool choice should be driven by effectiveness for the specific thinking task, not technological sophistication.
