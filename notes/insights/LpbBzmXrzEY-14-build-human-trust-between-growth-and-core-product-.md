When growth teams (focused on metrics) and core product teams (focused on vision) work together, success depends on building human trust through consistent delivery of high-quality work. Growth teams must demonstrate they'll maintain product quality standards while testing hypotheses, and core teams must trust that growth experiments will be properly evaluated and reverted if harmful. This [[concepts/team-collaboration|collaboration]] requires ongoing relationship building more than rigid process rules.
