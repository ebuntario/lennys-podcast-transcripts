Building a [[concepts/growth-loops|growth model]] quickly reveals that business growth is exceptionally sensitive to [[concepts/retention|customer retention]], more so than intuition suggests. A healthy retained base improves referral rates, content generation, and contribution margin, creating compounding effects. This insight often exposes misallocated resources, showing that smaller percentage gains in retention can be more valuable than larger changes elsewhere.
