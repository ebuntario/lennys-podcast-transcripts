Establishing a recurring, time-boxed meeting specifically for addressing disagreements—like a weekly "Fight Club"—normalizes conflict as a healthy part of problem-solving. This ritual prevents small issues from festering and builds stronger relationships by ensuring leaders proactively tackle difficult conversations. It creates a structured space for [[concepts/conflict-resolution|constructive conflict]] within a framework of psychological safety.
