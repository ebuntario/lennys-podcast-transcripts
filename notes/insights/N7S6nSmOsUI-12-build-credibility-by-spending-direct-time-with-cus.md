When starting a new product role, immediately become best friends with a researcher and spend time watching customers use the product firsthand. This direct observation provides insights that reports and studies often miss and quickly builds a foundation of [[concepts/credibility|credibility]]. It also counteracts the "navel-gazing" risk in organizations with strong dogfooding cultures, where teams can mistakenly believe they are the customer.
