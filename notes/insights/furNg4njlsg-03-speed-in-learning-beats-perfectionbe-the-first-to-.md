In competitive markets, speed of learning is a critical advantage. The goal is to "be the first to hit the brick wall"—to rapidly test hypotheses and discover what doesn't work before competitors do. This approach prioritizes [[concepts/testing|fast experimentation]] over slow, perfect execution for new, innovative experiences.
