Take control of external triggers like notifications, interruptions, and unnecessary meetings. Set devices to permanent do-not-disturb during work blocks, use physical signs to signal focus time, and establish protocols with family or colleagues. [[concepts/environment-design|Designing your environment]] this way prevents unauthorized access to your attention.
