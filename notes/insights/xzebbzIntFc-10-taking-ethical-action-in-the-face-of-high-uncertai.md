When the future is highly uncertain and the "right" path is unknowable, the best [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]] strategy is to pick actions that make ethical sense across a wide variety of possible future scenarios. Initiatives that increase [[concepts/transparency|transparency]], build state capacity, and foster competent leadership are valuable regardless of which specific future unfolds, allowing for positive [[concepts/ethical-action|ethical action]] without needing perfect prediction.
