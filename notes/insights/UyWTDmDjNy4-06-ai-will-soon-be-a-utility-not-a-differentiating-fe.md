AI will soon stop being a feature, just as electricity is not a feature. Companies using AI must focus on the promise they make to the user, not on the technology itself. The key question is: what's in this for the user? [[concepts/artificial-intelligence|AI]] is becoming a baseline utility upon which remarkable promises are built.
