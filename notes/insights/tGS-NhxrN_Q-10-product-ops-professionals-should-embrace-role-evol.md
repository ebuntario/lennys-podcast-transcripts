People considering [[concepts/product-operations|product operations]] careers should be comfortable with role evolution and eventual automation of their initial responsibilities. The value of the role increases as operational tasks become systematized, freeing capacity for more strategic advisory work. This requires adaptability and willingness to continuously identify new areas where operational support can drive product team effectiveness and business outcomes.
