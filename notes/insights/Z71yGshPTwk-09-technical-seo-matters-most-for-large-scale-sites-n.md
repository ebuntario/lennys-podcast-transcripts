[[concepts/technical-seo|Technical SEO]] is crucial for sites with millions of pages (like Zillow or Airbnb) but often overemphasized for smaller sites. Most SEO problems stem from poor user experience or irrelevant content rather than technical issues. Focus on creating valuable content and user journeys before optimizing technical elements like page speed.
