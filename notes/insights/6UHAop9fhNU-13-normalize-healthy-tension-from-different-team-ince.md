Disagreement between teams is often a sign of healthy tension, not failure. Different functions have different incentives and information. Acknowledge this openly, agree on the shared customer outcome, and then rationally debate the best path using your combined knowledge. This turns friction into productive [[concepts/conflict-resolution|conflict resolution]].
