Founders should proactively chat with top performers to understand benchmark candidate profiles, even without an immediate hiring need. Engaging these individuals for advice or involving them in the product builds rapport and creates a pipeline. This long-term [[concepts/relationship-building|relationship building]] makes future recruitment feel less transactional and more collaborative.
