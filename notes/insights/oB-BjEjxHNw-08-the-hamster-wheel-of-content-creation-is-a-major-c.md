Once creators start earning through [[concepts/subscription-models|subscriptions]], they can feel trapped in a cycle of constant output to maintain revenue. Platforms have an opportunity to address this by offering tools for financing, content automation, or revenue smoothing. Solving this problem is key to enabling long-term, sustainable creative careers.
