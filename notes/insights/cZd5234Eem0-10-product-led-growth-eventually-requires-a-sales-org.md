Never mistake your lead generation for your entire business. While PLG is excellent for user adoption and permeation, large contracts require human interaction to navigate budgetary authority and organizational complexity. Most successful B2B companies eventually build a [[concepts/sales-organization|sales organization]]; it's a question of when, not if.
