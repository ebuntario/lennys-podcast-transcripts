In European tech markets, less job liquidity and stricter employment laws can lead to less autonomy for product managers and more founder control. Additionally, [[concepts/equity|equity]] is often undervalued and seen as a bonus rather than a core part of compensation. To foster a stronger [[concepts/ownership|ownership mindset]], companies should educate employees on equity value and create teams with clear business accountability.
