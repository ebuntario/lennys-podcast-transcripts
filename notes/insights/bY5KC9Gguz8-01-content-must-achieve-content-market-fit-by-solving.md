The way you think about [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] must be applied to content as well. You need to deeply understand your target audience: what causes them anxiety, what they need to avoid failure, or what they need to get promoted. Effective [[concepts/content-strategy|content marketing]] acts as a painkiller, not a vitamin, by directly addressing these core needs.
