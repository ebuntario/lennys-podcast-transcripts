Truly disruptive innovations often generate a "love it or hate it" binary reaction, not mild indifference. If some people are passionately excited while others dismiss it as a "toy" or want it to "die in a fire," that's a strong signal you're onto something impactful. This polarization is more indicative of potential [[concepts/innovation-signals|disruptive potential]] than lukewarm acceptance.
