Training AI with high-quality data is less like simple labeling and more like raising a child. It involves teaching models about values, creativity, beauty, and the subtle aspects of what constitutes good judgment and helpful behavior. This philosophical approach to [[concepts/ai-alignment|AI alignment]] is critical for shaping AI that benefits humanity rather than just optimizing for engagement metrics.
