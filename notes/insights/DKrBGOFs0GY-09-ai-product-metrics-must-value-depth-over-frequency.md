Traditional engagement metrics like time spent or message count are misleading for AI products, where a valuable interaction could be two messages or two hundred. The focus should be on whether the AI helped users accomplish meaningful work or have meaningful moments. Product teams should resist [[concepts/ai-ethics|optimizing for addiction]] and instead value quiet, impactful interactions that don't show up in dashboards.
