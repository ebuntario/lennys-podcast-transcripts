For network-effect marketplaces in greenfield opportunities, speed to market is paramount. For mission-critical products like email, where reliability directly impacts user trust and professional reputation, extensive polish before launch is necessary. Your [[concepts/launch-strategy|launch strategy]] should be calibrated to your product's role in users' lives and the competitive dynamics of your market.
