Metrics like "seven friends in ten days" are valuable primarily for creating extreme clarity and a unified team goal. While they should correlate with retention, the exact point on the curve matters less than having a clear target everyone optimizes toward. This focus often reveals the need for foundational work, like building a robust [[concepts/user-onboarding|user onboarding]] experience.
