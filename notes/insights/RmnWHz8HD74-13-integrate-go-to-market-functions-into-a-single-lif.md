Historically, marketing, sales, support, and success have operated with overlapping but misaligned Venn diagram strategies. The future of world-class go-to-market is to integrate these functions into a single, orchestrated [[concepts/customer-lifecycle|customer lifecycle]]. Map the jobs to be done from prospect awareness to high long-term value, and organize your team and processes around this holistic journey rather than functional silos.
