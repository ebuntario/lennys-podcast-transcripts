Your ability to laser-focus on your highlight depends on physical energy. Prioritize [[concepts/sleep|sleep]] by creating a phone-free bedroom and consider tools like an eye mask. Incorporate consistent exercise, using accountability (like a trainer or app) to overcome excuses. Viewing health as part of your productivity system ensures you have the fuel for peak attention.
