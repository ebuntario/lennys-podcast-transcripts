A [[concepts/product-leadership|product management]] background provides excellent foundational skills for entrepreneurship, as it exposes you to multiple business functions and leadership challenges. Don't let the fear of not knowing everything stop you; many less capable people have figured it out. The key is to surround yourself with a network of advisors you can turn to for specific problems as they arise.
