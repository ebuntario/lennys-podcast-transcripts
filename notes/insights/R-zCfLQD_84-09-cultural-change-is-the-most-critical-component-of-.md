Building the platform and tools is a prerequisite, but not sufficient, for organization-wide AI adoption. Success requires investing in [[concepts/cultural-transformation|cultural transformation]]: changing hiring practices, performance evaluations, and incentives. Celebrating wins, showcasing internal success stories, and fostering a [[concepts/mindset|growth mindset]] are essential to drive behavioral change.
