Implementing OKRs with struggling teams often makes both the team and the methodology look bad. High-performing teams can adapt the process to their context and demonstrate its value. This [[concepts/change-management|change management]] approach creates internal advocates who can help other teams adopt the practice effectively based on real experience rather than theoretical implementation.
