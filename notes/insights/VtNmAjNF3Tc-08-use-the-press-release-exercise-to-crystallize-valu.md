A powerful technique for ensuring customer-centricity is to write a press release for the finished product before any building begins. This exercise, popularized at Amazon, forces the team to thoroughly define the value proposition and articulate what they are telling users. It is a concrete method to operationalize the principle of working backwards from the customer problem.
