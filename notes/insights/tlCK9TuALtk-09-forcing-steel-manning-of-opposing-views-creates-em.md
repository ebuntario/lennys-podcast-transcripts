In debates between strongly opinionated leaders, ask each side to articulate the best arguments for the *opposing* view. This [[concepts/empathy|empathy]]-building exercise helps people understand different motivations and goals, and sometimes they persuade themselves. It's a critical first step before clarifying the decision-maker and timeline.
