In an AI-powered world, [[concepts/agency|agency]]—the drive to build and execute without being told exactly what to do—is becoming the most critical skill. Traditional education often prioritizes following well-defined paths, but the future belongs to those who can identify problems and leverage new tools to solve them independently.
