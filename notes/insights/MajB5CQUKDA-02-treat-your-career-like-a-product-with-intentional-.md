Apply [[concepts/product-leadership|product management]] principles to your career by writing a spec with clear milestones, success metrics, and required skills. This intentional approach prevents drifting and allows you to evaluate opportunities against long-term goals. Many successful product managers excel at building products but lack this same [[concepts/intentionality|intentionality]] for their own professional journeys.
