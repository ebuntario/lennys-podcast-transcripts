Founding a company requires a wide range of activities, many of which may be energy drains for you. Use your [[concepts/self-awareness|self-awareness]] of energy drivers/drains and core strengths to prototype the founder role through conversations. This helps you design a founder path that leverages your superpowers and builds a team to cover your gaps.
