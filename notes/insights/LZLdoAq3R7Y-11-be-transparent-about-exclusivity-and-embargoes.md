If offering an exclusive, be upfront with journalists about your plans. Propose clear terms, such as a specific window for their exclusive story before you engage with other outlets. Honest [[concepts/exclusivity-management|exclusivity management]] builds trust and prevents journalists from feeling manipulated, which is crucial for maintaining positive long-term relationships.
