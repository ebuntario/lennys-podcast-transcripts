A clear [[concepts/brand-purpose|brand purpose]] explains why your company exists beyond financial gain and exists on a 10-year timeframe. It helps align employees, guides decision-making, and makes the public want to root for you. A well-articulated purpose should be a single sentence you can naturally say when introducing your company, such as "We exist to..."
