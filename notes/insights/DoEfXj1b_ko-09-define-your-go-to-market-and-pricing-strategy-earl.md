A common growth pitfall is not being strategic about your [[concepts/go-to-market|go-to-market]] motion and pricing from the start. Determine early whether your product is conducive to a product-led or sales-led motion based on factors like onboarding complexity and virality. Also, establish your value metric and pricing tiers early; changing pricing later at scale involves complex challenges like grandfathering legacy customers and can disrupt growth momentum.
