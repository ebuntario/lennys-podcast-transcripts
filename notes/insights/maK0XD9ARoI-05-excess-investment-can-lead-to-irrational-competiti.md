When multiple competitors are overfunded, they often engage in an unwinnable arms race of spending. This dynamic can force companies to make irrational decisions, like expensive national advertising campaigns, that drain resources without creating sustainable advantages. Having too much capital can sometimes be an albatross that distorts sound [[concepts/business-strategy|business strategy]].
