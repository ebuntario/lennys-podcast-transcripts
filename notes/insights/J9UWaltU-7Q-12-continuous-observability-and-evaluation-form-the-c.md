As products become learning organisms, [[concepts/observability|observability]] and rigorous evaluation become central to team culture rather than afterthoughts. Teams need systems to monitor agent performance, conduct A/B tests, and implement self-healing mechanisms. This continuous feedback loop is essential for managing the work of thousands of deployed agents effectively.
