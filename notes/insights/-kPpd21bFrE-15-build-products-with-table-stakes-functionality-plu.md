Successful products at Revolut combine [[concepts/table-stakes-features|table-stakes functionality]] (meeting basic customer expectations) with delightful differentiators that create love. For savings accounts, this means competitive rates (table stakes) plus multi-currency options, daily interest payments, and beautiful UX (differentiators) that exceed expectations.
