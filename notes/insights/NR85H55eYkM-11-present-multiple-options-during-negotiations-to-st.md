Instead of presenting a single product and price, offer options (e.g., good/better/best or different pricing models). This shifts the discussion from "Is the price fair?" to "Which value package is right for us?" It also provides [[concepts/anchoring|anchoring]], making higher price points seem more reasonable and giving you the courage to ask for what you're worth.
