Cultivating deep [[concepts/self-trust|self-trust]] is foundational for making bold decisions and maintaining conviction amidst criticism. This means believing in the value of your own perspective formed by direct observation and intellect. Trusting yourself also includes trusting your ability to handle the consequences when risks don't pay off, which is essential for innovation.
