A shaky technical foundation is a product problem, not solely an engineering concern. Product managers must treat [[concepts/technical-debt|technical debt]] and infrastructure limitations as core issues in their "top problems" list. You cannot build a skyscraper on a weak foundation, so prioritizing foundational health is essential for long-term product success.
