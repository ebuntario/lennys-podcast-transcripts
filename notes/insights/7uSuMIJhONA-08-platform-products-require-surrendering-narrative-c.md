When building a platform, you must accept that your users—not you—control the narrative and how new users experience the product. This means designing for how your community will onboard and advocate for others, rather than trying to own every touchpoint. Embracing this [[concepts/platform-strategy|platform dynamic]] is essential for scalable, organic growth.
