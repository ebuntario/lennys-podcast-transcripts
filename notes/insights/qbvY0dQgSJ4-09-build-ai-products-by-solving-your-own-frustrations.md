To overcome the "idea crisis" in an era of powerful AI tools, focus on your own daily frustrations as a source for [[concepts/idea-generation|product ideas]]. Pay attention to tasks that annoy you for a week, then ask if AI can build a solution to make that process better, leading to practical, user-validated micro-tools.
