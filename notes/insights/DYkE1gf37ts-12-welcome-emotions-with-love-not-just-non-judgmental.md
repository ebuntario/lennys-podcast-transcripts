While non-judgmental awareness of emotions is a good step, welcoming them with love and excitement is more transformative. Joy will not reside where other emotions are merely tolerated; it comes when all emotions are deeply welcomed. This shift from acceptance to active welcoming accelerates [[emotional-acceptance|emotional fluidity]].
