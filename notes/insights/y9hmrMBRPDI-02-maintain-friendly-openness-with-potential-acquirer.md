When positioning for [[concepts/mergers-and-acquisitions|M&A]], it's a mistake to take an overly competitive stance and prematurely shut down conversations with incumbents or potential buyers. M&A is about creating plan Bs and maintaining optionality, so you should keep doors open and relationships friendly, as you may not ultimately succeed as an independent company.
