Asking users about their problems often yields inaccurate answers because people adapt to inefficient workflows. The most effective method is to watch them perform their tasks through screen shares or in-person observation. This [[concepts/customer-discovery|customer development]] technique reveals the true intensity of a problem that users may not even recognize themselves.
