During user testing, the segment that exhibits the most emotive, joyful reaction to the product often reveals the ideal initial target market. Canva identified social media managers because their excitement and clear "job to be done" were magnitudes greater than other groups. Leaning into this early adopter segment provides a strong foundation for [[concepts/go-to-market|go-to-market]] strategy.
