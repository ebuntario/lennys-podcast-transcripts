Regularly talking to customers, especially through support rotations, reveals recurring themes, pain points, and the exact language customers use. This is a shortcut to effective [[concepts/product-marketing|product marketing]] and messaging. By addressing these thematic questions in educational content (like landing pages or docs), you meet customers where they are and preemptively solve their confusion.
