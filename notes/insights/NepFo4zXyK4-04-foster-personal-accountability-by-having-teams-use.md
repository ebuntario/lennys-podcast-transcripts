The most powerful driver of product quality is when team members feel personally accountable for the user experience. This is best achieved by ensuring everyone uses the product extensively in their daily work. For example, Figma shifted from memos to decks built in Figma, giving the entire company firsthand experience and motivation to improve the tools they rely on through [[concepts/internal-dogfooding|internal dogfooding]].
