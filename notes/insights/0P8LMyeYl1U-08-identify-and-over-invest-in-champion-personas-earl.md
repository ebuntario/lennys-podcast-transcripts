For horizontal products, manually reach out to early users to identify champion personas—like "tinkerers" who enjoy building systems. Over-investing in their success turns them into internal evangelists who drive [[concepts/product-led-growth|product-led growth]] within their companies. This hands-on approach builds the mental models needed for scalable playbooks later.
