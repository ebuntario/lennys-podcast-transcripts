In large organizations, you can create alignment by causing [[concepts/internal-virality|internal virality]]. Share compelling prototypes or builds internally; if they are interesting, they will spread organically from engineers to managers to leadership. This creates instant, bottom-up excitement and alignment around an idea, reducing the need for extensive stakeholder management and proving internal demand before major investment.
