Aim to distill your strategy down to three [[concepts/strategic-priorities|strategic pillars]]. This number creates maximum clarity and focus, forcing the toughest but most valuable prioritization decisions. While some suggest three to five, three is a forcing function that ensures you are concentrating resources on the areas of highest leverage.
