The entire software market is moving towards [[concepts/ai-agents|AI agents]] that autonomously accomplish jobs, which will drive a fundamental shift in business models. This shift enables [[concepts/outcomes-based-pricing|outcomes-based pricing]], where customers pay for measurable business results rather than software usage or seats. This model aligns vendor and customer incentives more effectively than traditional productivity software, which has historically been difficult to sell and measure.
