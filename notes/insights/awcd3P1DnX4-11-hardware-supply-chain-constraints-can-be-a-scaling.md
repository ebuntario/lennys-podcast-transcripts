Scaling an AI product like Copilot depends heavily on securing rare, high-performance GPUs for both training and inference. Global supply chain disruptions can directly limit growth and capacity. Successful scaling requires proactively earmarking and aggressively acquiring [[concepts/ai-hardware|AI hardware]] capacity as a core part of the operational strategy.
