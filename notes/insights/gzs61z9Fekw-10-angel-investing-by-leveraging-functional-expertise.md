To become a sought-after [[concepts/angel-investing|angel investor]], clearly define and communicate your specific functional expertise as your value proposition (e.g., "I will help you build your marketing team"). Founders actively seek investors who can provide deep, practical help in areas where they lack internal expertise. Delivering on this promise builds reputation and generates deal flow through referrals.
