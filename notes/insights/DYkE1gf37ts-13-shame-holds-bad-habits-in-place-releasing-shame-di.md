Often, the problem isn't a behavior (like being an "asshole" or smoking), but the shame attached to it. When you stop resisting and fully accept the behavior without shame, the [[shame|shame]] falls away and the habit often naturally diminishes. The energy of resistance is what perpetuates the unwanted pattern.
