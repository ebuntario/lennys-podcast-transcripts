Large, successful companies are susceptible to the innovator's dilemma, where they focus on incremental improvements while startups disrupt them. To avoid this, product leaders must cultivate a mindset of [[concepts/innovation|self-disruption]]. This involves constantly challenging the status quo and investing in transformational capabilities, even if they threaten existing profitable models.
