If you're repeatedly stuck on the same problem or experiencing frequent interpersonal conflict, examine how you ask for what you want. These patterns often stem from either people-pleasing (not asking) or entitled demanding (ordering). Changing your [[concepts/team-communication|asking style]] can break the cycle.
