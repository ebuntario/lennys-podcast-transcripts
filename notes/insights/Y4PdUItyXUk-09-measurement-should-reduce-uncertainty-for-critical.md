Effective [[concepts/measurement|measurement]] focuses on reducing uncertainty enough to make the next important decision, not achieving perfect information. Teams should identify what specific uncertainties matter most to their current challenges and design measurements accordingly. This pragmatic approach avoids analysis paralysis while supporting [[concepts/data-informed-decisions|data-informed decisions]].
