Collecting more information during signup than you might initially think necessary enables highly personalized onboarding guidance that significantly improves long-term success rates. This upfront investment in understanding user context pays dividends throughout the customer journey by delivering relevant guidance exactly when users need it. Well-executed [[concepts/onboarding|onboarding]] remains one of the highest-impact growth levers across different business models.
