Implementing new organizational processes requires [[concepts/leadership-commitment|CEO-level commitment]] and months of disciplined practice. These changes often fail when attempted as pilot programs without executive buy-in. Organizations should expect initial difficulty and commit to working through the challenging adoption period rather than abandoning processes at first obstacles.
