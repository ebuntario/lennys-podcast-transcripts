When guiding others, don’t just state the right decision—take a moment to teach the underlying mental model or principle. Explaining the “why” helps people apply the lesson to future situations and fosters deeper alignment. This [[concepts/leadership|leadership]] practice, observed in leaders like Jeff Wilke, has a more lasting effect than prescriptive instructions.
