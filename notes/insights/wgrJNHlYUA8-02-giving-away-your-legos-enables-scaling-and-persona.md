The "giving away your Legos" philosophy means voluntarily handing off tasks you enjoy to create capacity for higher-level impact. This is essential for [[concepts/scaling-organization|scaling an organization]] as complexity multiplies. Finding joy in building teams and mentoring others becomes the new source of fulfillment and enables company growth.
