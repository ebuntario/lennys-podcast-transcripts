The "thinking gray" framework involves delaying decisions as long as possible before committing to a course of action. This [[concepts/patience]] allows marketing technologists to gather more information, understand stakeholder perspectives, and avoid premature tool selections. Applied to systems design, this approach prevents reactive decisions driven by immediate pain points in favor of more thoughtful, future-proof solutions.
