While there are millions of podcasts, only about 150,000 have published 10 episodes within the last 10 days. Simply committing to a weekly schedule for 10 weeks can place a podcast in the top 3-4% of all shows created. This demonstrates that [[concepts/consistency|consistency]] is a primary differentiator, as most creators fail to persist beyond the initial launch phase.
