The current gap in AI adoption between genders risks widening the technology accessibility divide. Proactive initiatives like women-only hackathons (e.g., SheBuilds) are essential to bring diverse voices into the [[concepts/ai-skills|AI skills]] wave and ensure software is built for and by a representative sample of people.
