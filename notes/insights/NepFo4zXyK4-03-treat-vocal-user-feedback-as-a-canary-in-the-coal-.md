Individual tweets or loud customer voices should be treated as signals, not commands. They act as a "canary in the coal mine" indicating a potential issue. Effective product management requires balancing this vocal minority with broader data from support tickets, sales conversations, and research to build a complete picture and avoid blind spots in the [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]].
