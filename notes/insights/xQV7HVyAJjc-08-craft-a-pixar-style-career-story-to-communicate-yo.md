Use a simple narrative template ("Once upon a time... Every day... One day... Because of that...") to distill your career journey into a compelling story. This [[concepts/career-narrative|framing]] highlights your core skills, pivotal moments of change, and future direction in a memorable way. It transforms a resume into a human-centered pitch that intrigues interviewers.
