When you struggle to use your own product, other users likely face similar difficulties. [[concepts/dogfooding|Dogfooding]] (using your product regularly) builds [[concepts/product-sense|product intuition]] that complements formal user research. This is especially valuable for identifying friction points that might not emerge in interviews but significantly impact real-world usage.
