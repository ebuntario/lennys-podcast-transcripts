Product operations is structured around three key pillars: business and data insights (quantitative), customer and market insights (qualitative), and process and practices (governance). The function's focus depends on the company's stage and needs, but its ultimate goal is to increase the speed and quality of [[concepts/strategic-work|strategic decision-making]] across the product organization.
