To achieve [[concepts/hypergrowth|hypergrowth]], a company must have organic, inbound, and viral word-of-mouth, as you cannot pay enough to grow at those rates sustainably. The foundation is an amazing product that people love to use, which naturally encourages users to share it with others. This combination of a beloved product and [[concepts/virality|virality]] is essential for viable, efficient growth.
