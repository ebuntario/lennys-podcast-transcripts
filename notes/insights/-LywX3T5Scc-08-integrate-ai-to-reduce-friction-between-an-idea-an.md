The most effective AI features are those deeply embedded into the user's workflow to help them achieve their goals faster. Instead of adding AI for its own sake, focus on how it can minimize the steps between having an idea and creating a finished design. This [[concepts/user-experience|user-centric]] approach ensures the technology serves a genuine need and feels like a natural collaborator within the product.
