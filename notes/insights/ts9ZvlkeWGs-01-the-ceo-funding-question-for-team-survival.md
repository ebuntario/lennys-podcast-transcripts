Product teams should regularly ask themselves: "If you were the CEO of this company, would you fully fund your own team?" This question forces a perspective shift from day-to-day execution to evaluating your team's commercial value. If you cannot confidently answer "yes," it indicates a risky position where your team's existence may not be justified by its contribution to [[concepts/business-impact|business impact]].
