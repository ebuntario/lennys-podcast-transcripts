For marketplace or service-based companies, the true product is the real-world outcome, not just the app. At Airbnb, the product was the memories created in a home, requiring deep integration with operations, physical spaces, and customer experience. Building such products demands collaboration across software, operations, and service design to deliver the complete [[concepts/customer-experience|customer experience]].
