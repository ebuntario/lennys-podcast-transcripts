Managers often spend excessive time on struggling employees, but the highest return comes from actively investing in [[concepts/high-performers|high performers]]. Don't just leave them alone; run experiments to expand their responsibilities and test their potential. This investment unlocks 10x returns for the company and accelerates their growth, turning individual contributors into future leaders who can own larger parts of the business.
