A key to effective scaling is actively seeking out people who have solved similar problems before you. Making "enough phone calls" to learn from others' experiences five years ago accelerates your own [[concepts/organizational-excellence|operational excellence]] and prevents reinventing the wheel. This disciplined approach to [[concepts/learning|learning]] is a high-leverage activity for leaders.
