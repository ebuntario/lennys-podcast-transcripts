The real secret behind [[concepts/virality|virality]] is not viral mechanics like address book imports, which have a limited lifetime impact. Sustainable growth comes from spontaneous [[concepts/word-of-mouth|word-of-mouth]] where users genuinely want to tell others about your product. This insight shifts the focus from engineering viral features to creating a remarkable product that people naturally share.
