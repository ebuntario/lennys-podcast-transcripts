Successful subscription businesses require building direct relationships with customers rather than relying solely on platform distribution. This necessitates creating compelling destination experiences that rebundle value previously found in traditional formats like newspapers. The [[concepts/product-bundling|product bundling]] strategy at The New York Times demonstrates how to extend reach while maintaining control over the customer relationship.
