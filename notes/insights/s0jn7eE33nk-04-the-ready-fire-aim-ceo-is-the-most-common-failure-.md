This CEO type avoids planning and operates purely improvisationally, which works early but fails at scale. The antidote is basic [[concepts/planning|business design]]: start by defining what success looks like, quantify required resources, and establish short feedback loops to track progress. Planning is not corporate bureaucracy but a thoughtful exercise in aligning effort with outcomes.
