Hypergrowth companies compress years of scaling into months, rapidly moving through stages like going from 10 to 50 to 100 employees. To navigate this, they must continuously hire "2X and 3X leaders" who have already experienced the next stage of growth. Bringing in this external [[concepts/leadership-hiring|experienced leadership]] provides the foresight needed to manage the accelerating challenges.
