[[concepts/game-design|Game design]] focuses on creating intrinsically motivating experiences through goals, emotions, and flow, unlike gamification which adds extrinsic rewards like points and badges. Build "toys"—features that indulge playful exploration and create pleasant surprises—then combine them into engaging experiences. This approach makes software fun and shareable without undermining [[concepts/intrinsic-motivation|intrinsic motivation]].
