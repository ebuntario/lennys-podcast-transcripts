Your [[concepts/professional-reputation|professional reputation]], built through daily decisions on how you treat people and show up, compounds over time. This reputation can open surprising doors through relatively light connections and is crucial for long-term [[concepts/career-development|career development]]. Conversely, a damaged reputation takes a long time to repair, so integrity in small matters is a high-leverage investment.
