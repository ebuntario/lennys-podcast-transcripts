Research shows ambitious goals motivate teams when they feel challenging but achievable, while impossible-seeming goals demotivate. The sweet spot is objectives that make teams say "This will be tricky but we can do it." This [[concepts/performance-psychology|performance psychology]] principle suggests calibrating stretch goals based on team confidence rather than arbitrary percentages.
