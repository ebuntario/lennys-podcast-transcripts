Write short, personalized emails that signal you've done your homework on the journalist and their work. Reference a specific article they wrote or demonstrate understanding of their beat to stand out from mass blasts. A human, customized [[concepts/email-pitching|email pitching]] approach shows respect for their time and increases the likelihood they'll pay attention to your story.
