When new [[concepts/distribution-platforms|distribution platforms]] emerge, startups can achieve escape velocity before incumbents can react. This creates a temporary but powerful window where startups can leverage novel distribution to build defensibility and market share. The key is recognizing these shifts early and placing focused bets.
