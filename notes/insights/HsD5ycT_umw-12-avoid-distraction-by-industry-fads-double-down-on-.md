Startups often chase investor-driven trends (like the "sharing economy" or AI), diverting resources from their core value proposition. This [[concepts/startup-focus|startup focus]] mistake dilutes execution and confuses customers. Instead, relentlessly pursue what your customers actually pay for and request, even if it seems less glamorous than the latest tech trend.
