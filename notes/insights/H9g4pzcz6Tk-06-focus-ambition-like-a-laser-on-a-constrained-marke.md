When building a marketplace, ambitious founders should focus their effort like a laser beam on a very constrained "thimble" of a market, rather than trying to warm the entire ocean. This constraint of capital and attention allows you to achieve deep [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] and create a "white hot center" of happy users before expanding. DoorDash's initial focus on suburbs, not cities, is a prime example.
