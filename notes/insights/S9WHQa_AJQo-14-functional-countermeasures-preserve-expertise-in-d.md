When implementing single-threaded teams, organizations risk losing [[concepts/functional-excellence|functional excellence]] as specialists report to generalists. Amazon created countermeasures like centralized functional leadership for core services and additional responsibilities for experts. These structures maintain standards while enabling autonomous teams, balancing decentralization with necessary specialization.
