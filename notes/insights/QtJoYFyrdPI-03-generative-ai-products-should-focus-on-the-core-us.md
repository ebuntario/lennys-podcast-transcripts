When building with [[concepts/ai-generation|generative AI]], resist the urge to make the technology the star. For example, an AI DJ should talk minimally and focus on getting users to the music quickly. The principle is to "do as little as possible and get out of the way," ensuring the product serves the core user need rather than showcasing its own capabilities.
