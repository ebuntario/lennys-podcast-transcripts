Launch communities around a passionate niche, not a broad vision, and use non-scalable tactics to nurture early members. Simultaneously, consider [[concepts/monetization|monetization]] strategies early—before reaching scale—to avoid disruptive business model pivots later. This approach ensures the community's economic sustainability aligns with its growth trajectory.
