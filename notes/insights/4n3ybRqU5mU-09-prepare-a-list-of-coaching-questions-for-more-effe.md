A simple tip to improve coaching is to curate a personal list of great questions to ask in one-on-ones, especially when unprepared. Questions like "How are you *really* doing?" or "What would make you more successful in your role?" serve as a health check and make PMs feel valued. This [[concepts/preparation|preparation]] elevates the quality of conversations beyond just status updates.
