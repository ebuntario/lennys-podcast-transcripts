People don't talk about products that simply meet expectations; they talk about products that do the unexpected. To drive [[concepts/product-led-growth|word-of-mouth growth]], optimize for features that create surprise and delight, like Easter eggs or magical capabilities. This principle applies to both consumer and enterprise products, as unexpected positive experiences forge stronger relationships and generate organic discussion.
