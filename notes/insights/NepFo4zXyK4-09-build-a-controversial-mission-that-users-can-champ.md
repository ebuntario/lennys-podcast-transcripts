A powerful product vision often challenges the status quo and can be initially controversial (e.g., real-time collaborative design). This narrative tension signals you're part of a revolution. When users adopt your tool, they're also championing a new way of working. Equipping users with this [[concepts/product-strategy|larger mission]] transforms them from tool users into passionate advocates for systemic change.
