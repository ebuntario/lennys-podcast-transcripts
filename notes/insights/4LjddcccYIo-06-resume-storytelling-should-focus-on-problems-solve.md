Instead of listing job titles and responsibilities, structure your resume around specific problems you've solved. Describe the challenge, the skills you applied, and the measurable outcome. This [[concepts/team-communication|communication]] approach demonstrates value more effectively than chronological role descriptions, especially for experienced professionals.
