Extreme product-market fit requires a product that has widespread [[concepts/demand-generation|demand]], deeply satisfies a critical customer need, and can be delivered repeatably and efficiently. Many startups fail by achieving demand and satisfaction but neglecting [[concepts/saas-metrics|efficiency]], leading to an unsustainable business model. All three dimensions must be optimized, but the priority among them shifts at each level of the journey.
