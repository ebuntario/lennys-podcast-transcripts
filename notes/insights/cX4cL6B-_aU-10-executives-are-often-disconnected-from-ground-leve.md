There's often a significant disconnect between executive declarations about AI transformation and actual ground-level adoption. Leaders must go beyond issuing mandates and actively measure real usage, talk to individual contributors, and understand the friction points preventing [[concepts/ai-adoption|widespread adoption]] within their organizations.
