Establish clear roles and responsibilities with your engineering and design partners to avoid confusion in the organization. Spend significant time together, document agreements, and present a united front. This [[concepts/team-alignment|alignment]] and mutual respect prevent team members from playing leaders against each other and ensures things get done.
