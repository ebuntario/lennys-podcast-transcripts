In an era of too many possibilities, prioritize by observing what has [[concepts/virality|organic virality]] on social media. What people share and talk about reveals a core element that resonates. This acts as a pre-validation tool; you can gauge how a product might be received before building it. Spending time on social platforms to identify trends provides strong signals for what to build next.
