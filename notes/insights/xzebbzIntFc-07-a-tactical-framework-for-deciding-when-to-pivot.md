If you're asking whether you should pivot, you likely already know the answer. A practical [[concepts/decision-frameworks|decision framework]] is to give yourself a fixed period of time for decisive action on the current plan. If that fails, have each founder state, "If I could start this company over, the thing I wish we were doing is..." This often reveals a shared, unspoken desire for a new direction that the team can then pursue with focused [[concepts/experimentation|experimentation]].
