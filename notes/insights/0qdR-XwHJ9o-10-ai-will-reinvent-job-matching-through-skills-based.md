The hiring process will transform with AI interviewers that assess skills through work simulations and dynamic questioning, moving beyond resume reviews. This enables better matching between candidates and roles, saving employers time and helping candidates demonstrate abilities more effectively. The future of [[concepts/ai-in-hiring|talent marketplaces]] lies in AI-driven, skills-based evaluation.
