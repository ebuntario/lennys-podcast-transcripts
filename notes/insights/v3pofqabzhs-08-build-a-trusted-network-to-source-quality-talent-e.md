The most efficient hiring comes from tapping a trusted network of top performers who can recommend other great people. This method yields higher-quality leads than blind outreach, as these referrals come with implicit vetting. Investing in [[concepts/network-leveraging|network leveraging]] saves time and increases the likelihood of finding exceptional candidates.
