Instead of asking "what should we do?" present "here's what I think we should do" with supporting evidence. This [[concepts/proactive-contribution|point-of-view sharing]] reduces your manager's mental lift while demonstrating strategic thinking. Even junior staff can do this by sharing observations from customer interactions or data analysis.
