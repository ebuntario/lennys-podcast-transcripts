Strong product organizations avoid putting PMs on a pedestal as the sole source of product ideas. Instead, they cultivate an environment where engineers, support, sales, and other functions all feel responsible for [[concepts/product-thinking|product thinking]]. This distributed ownership leads to better outcomes and more engaged teams.
