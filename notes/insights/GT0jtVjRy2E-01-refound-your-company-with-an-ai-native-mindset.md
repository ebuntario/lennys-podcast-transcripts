To navigate the AI era, leaders should ask: "If we were founding a new company from scratch with the same mission, how would we execute using a fully AI-native approach?" This clean-slate thinking forces a radical re-evaluation of your product and strategy. If your existing assets don't give you an advantage in this new paradigm, it may be time for a more drastic change.
