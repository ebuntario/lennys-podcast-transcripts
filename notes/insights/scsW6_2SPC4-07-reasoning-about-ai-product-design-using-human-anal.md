When designing AI interactions, you can often gain useful intuition by asking, "How would a human do this?" For example, designing the UX for a "thinking" model involved considering how a person behaves when pondering a difficult question for 20 seconds. This [[concepts/human-analogy|human-centric reasoning]] provides a rich prior art for creating natural and effective AI experiences.
