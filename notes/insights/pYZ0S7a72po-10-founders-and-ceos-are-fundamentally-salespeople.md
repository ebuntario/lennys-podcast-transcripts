A CEO's core function is selling: selling the vision to yourself, to co-founders, to investors, to early customers, to hires, and to the market. Embracing this [[concepts/sales-identity|sales identity]] is crucial. The skills of balancing optimism with realistic qualification, building genuine relationships, and creating delight are the same whether selling a product or a company.
