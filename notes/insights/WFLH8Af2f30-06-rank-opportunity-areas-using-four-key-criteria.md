To select strategic pillars, rank candidate opportunity areas on four dimensions: expected impact, certainty of that impact, clarity of solution levers, and whether those levers are unique/differentiated for your team or company. Debating these scores as a team builds alignment and creates a defensible rationale for the final choice.
