AI's ability to reason and search is most powerful when it has access to a unified information surface area. A horizontal platform that bundles multiple workstreams provides a richer context for AI, creating a significant advantage. This turns a historical weakness of horizontal tools (complexity) into a strength in the [[concepts/ai-era|AI era]].
