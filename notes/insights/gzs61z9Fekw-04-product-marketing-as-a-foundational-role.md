A [[concepts/marketing-roles|product marketer]] understands the product, audience, and market to determine how to communicate the right message at the right time. They often serve as a bridge between fuel and engine, handling positioning, messaging, and launch strategy. Look for candidates who have worked in environments where they weren't siloed and have seen "what great looks like" in terms of marketing quality.
