Effective [[concepts/seo-strategy|SEO]] is not a minor, linear channel; it can be exponential and company-defining. Entire businesses can be built or acquired based on ranking #1 for a single, high-intent keyword. Don't underestimate the traffic potential; winning a top spot for a core industry term can drive massive, sustainable growth.
