The most common mistake in [[concepts/ai-evals|AI evals]] is jumping straight to writing automated tests. Instead, you should begin with manual [[concepts/error-analysis|error analysis]] by sampling and reviewing real user interaction traces. This foundational step reveals the actual failure modes in your application, which then informs what specific evals you need to build.
