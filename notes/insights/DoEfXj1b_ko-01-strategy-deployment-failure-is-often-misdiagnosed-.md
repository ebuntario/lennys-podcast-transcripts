Many organizations incorrectly attribute their issues to a lack of [[concepts/product-management-training|product management training]]. In reality, the core problem is usually how they set goals and deploy [[concepts/strategy-deployment|strategy]]. Trained individuals without clear strategic context have no direction on what to work towards, rendering the training ineffective.
