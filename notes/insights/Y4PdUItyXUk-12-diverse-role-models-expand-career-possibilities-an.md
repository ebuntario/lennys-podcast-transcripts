Exposure to [[concepts/role-models|role models]] from varied backgrounds and company types helps professionals see more [[concepts/professional-growth|career development]] paths as viable. Many assume success requires adopting specific Silicon Valley mindsets, but diverse leadership styles succeed across industries. This expanded perspective increases both opportunity recognition and self-efficacy.
