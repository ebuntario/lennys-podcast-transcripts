Adding more people to an organization creates geometric [[concepts/coordination-overhead|coordination overhead]] and information flow friction, which can reduce morale and effectiveness. Companies like WhatsApp, Instagram, and Linear demonstrate that keeping teams super small after achieving product-market fit often leads to better performance, as there is less need for constant context-sharing and alignment.
