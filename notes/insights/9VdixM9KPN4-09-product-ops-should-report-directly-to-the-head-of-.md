For optimal alignment and impact, the product operations function should report directly to the Chief Product Officer or head of product. This structure ensures product ops is tightly coupled with product strategy and can act as a right-hand function to the product leader, helping to operationalize goals and provide the strategic insights needed for board meetings and planning.
