Rebranding or redesigning a marketing site almost never improves acquisition metrics and often causes a temporary performance drop. Treat such projects as foundational resets requiring 3-6 months of post-launch [[concepts/performance-optimization|optimization]] to recover baseline performance. Avoid promising immediate growth from aesthetic changes driven by personal taste rather than strategic needs.
