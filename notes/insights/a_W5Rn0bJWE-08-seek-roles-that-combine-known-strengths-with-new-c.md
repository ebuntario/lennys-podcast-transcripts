Prioritize career opportunities that will cause significant growth and learning, which is often painful. To maintain balance, ensure the role includes one or two areas where you are already strong and confident. This foundation provides stability while you stretch into new, unfamiliar territories, making the overall [[concepts/skill-development|skill development]] process more sustainable and healthy.
