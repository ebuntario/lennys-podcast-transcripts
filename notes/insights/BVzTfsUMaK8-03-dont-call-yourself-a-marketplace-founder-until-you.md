The litmus test for being a marketplace is having scaled liquidity on both the supply and demand sides. If you lack liquidity on both sides, focus on scaling one side using general startup growth tactics. Only when you have one side scaled should you focus on leveraging it to attract the other side and spin up the [[concepts/growth-flywheel|marketplace flywheel]].
