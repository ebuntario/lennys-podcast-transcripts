Many people avoid speaking freely because they fear coming across as inauthentic or manipulative. However, if you're worried about being a bullshitter, you likely have a strong internal compass for authenticity that will keep you in check. Developing communication skills allows you to present your genuine ideas in the best light, not to deceive.
