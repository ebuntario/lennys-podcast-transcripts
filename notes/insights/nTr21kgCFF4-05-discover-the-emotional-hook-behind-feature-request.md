The goal of customer discovery is to feel the same negative emotion the user feels. Dig beyond surface-level requests to understand the "schlep blindness" or moment of frustration that motivates the ask. Building a solution that alleviates that specific bad feeling creates a powerful [[concepts/empathy|emotional hook]] and deeper product loyalty.
