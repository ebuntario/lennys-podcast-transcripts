The SPACE framework (Satisfaction, Performance, Activity, Communication, Efficiency) remains valuable for [[concepts/developer-experience|developer experience]] because it's descriptive, not prescriptive. It allows teams to adapt measurements to new contexts like AI, where dimensions like trust become critical.
