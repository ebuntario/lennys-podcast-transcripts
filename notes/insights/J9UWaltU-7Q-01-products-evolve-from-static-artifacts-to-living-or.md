Modern AI-powered products are no longer static artifacts but living [[concepts/product-as-organism|organisms]] that improve with more user interactions. The key performance indicator becomes a team's ability to ingest data, digest reward models, and tune for specific outcomes like price or quality. This continuous learning loop represents the new intellectual property for companies.
