Resist the "ego monster" that pushes you toward impressive titles and logos. Use your [[concepts/values-clarification|inner scorecard]]—personal values like adventure, kindness, or growth—to evaluate opportunities instead of an outer scorecard of status. This prevents late-career regret where you feel trapped in a prestigious but unfulfilling job.
