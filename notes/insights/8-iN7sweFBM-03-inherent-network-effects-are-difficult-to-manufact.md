It's very hard to manufacture product network effects if they aren't present from the product's inception. Collaboration tools and marketplaces naturally have this dynamic, whereas single-player tools do not. For products without inherent network effects, [[concepts/referral-programs|referral programs]] can still work if the brand is beloved and offers strong incentives, but it's an uphill battle.
