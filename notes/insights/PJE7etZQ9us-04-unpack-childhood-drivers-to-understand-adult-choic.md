Consciousness about why you pursue certain goals is key to breaking automatic patterns. For example, asking "What will achieving this goal do for you?" can reveal childhood drivers, like associating safety with an endless supply of lemon drops. This [[concepts/radical-self-inquiry|radical self-inquiry]] helps you make adult choices rather than replaying learned behaviors.
