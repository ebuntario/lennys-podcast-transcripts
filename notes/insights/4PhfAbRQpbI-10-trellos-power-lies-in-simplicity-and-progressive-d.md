Trello's core strength is starting with extreme simplicity for small problems (like personal task management) and growing with the user as their needs become more sophisticated. This [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] of progressive disclosure contrasts with tools that start complex and require simplification, making Trello ideal for ideation, discovery, and teams that haven't fully defined their process yet.
