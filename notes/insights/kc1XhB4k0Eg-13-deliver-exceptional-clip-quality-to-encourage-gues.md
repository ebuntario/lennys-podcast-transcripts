Invest in producing high-quality video clips that make guests look exceptional, then provide these assets to them post-interview. When guests share professionally edited clips to their audiences, it creates [[concepts/viral-potential|viral potential]] through borrowed credibility. This turns guests into promoters, exponentially expanding your reach through their established networks.
