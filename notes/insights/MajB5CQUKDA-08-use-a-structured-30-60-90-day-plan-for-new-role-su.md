A successful [[concepts/user-onboarding|onboarding]] plan dedicates the first 30 days to listening, the next 30 to aligning on vision, and the final 30 to execution. Conducting comprehensive [[concepts/listening-tours|listening tours]] and summarizing findings builds [[concepts/trust-building|trust]] while ensuring you understand the landscape before making changes. This "diagnose before treat" approach prevents early missteps.
