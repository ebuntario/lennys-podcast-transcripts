The initial adopters of new AI-augmented workflows are often a company's top talent, who have an innate desire to improve their craft. This leads to [[concepts/talent-amplification|talent amplification]], where the best get better faster, rather than elevating average performers. This pattern necessitates intentional [[concepts/change-management|change management]] to bring the broader organization along.
