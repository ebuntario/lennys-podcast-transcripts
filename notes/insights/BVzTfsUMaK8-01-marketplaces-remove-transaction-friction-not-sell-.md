A marketplace's core value proposition is removing the [[concepts/transaction-costs|transaction costs]] or frictions of finding a counterparty, not selling the underlying goods or services. Both sides of the market are customers of the platform, which intermediates to reduce search and matching costs. This fundamental shift in perspective is critical for understanding the business model.
