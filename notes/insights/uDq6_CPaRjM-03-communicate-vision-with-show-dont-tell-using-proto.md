Words alone are insufficient for communicating a compelling product vision. Structure pitches as a sequence of pain point, solution, and proof point, using prototypes, mocks, and user testimonials to make the future tangible. At Figma, they "lean heavily into designing and prototyping even before a project gets a green light" to create an emotional pull and secure [[concepts/internal-buy-in|internal buy-in]].
