A key indicator of a strong product manager is their ability to tell a compelling story. In interviews, ask candidates to describe a controversial product decision. A great PM can articulate the conflict, represent multiple perspectives fairly, and explain why the problem was important. This [[concepts/narrative|storytelling]] skill is crucial for rallying teams and communicating vision effectively.
