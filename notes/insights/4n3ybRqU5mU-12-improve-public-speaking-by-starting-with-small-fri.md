To become a better and less nervous public speaker, start by presenting to very small, supportive groups like your team or a local meetup. Gradually increase the audience size as your confidence grows. Having a friendly face in the front row for feedback and practicing physical techniques (like the Superman pose) backstage can also help manage [[concepts/confidence|nerves]].
