Small, thoughtful changes to product copy can sometimes drive unexpectedly large conversion wins. At Etsy, adding a single line about carbon offsetting for deliveries resonated deeply with their buyer base and significantly improved conversion rates. This highlights the importance of [[concepts/a-b-testing|A/B testing]] even minor changes and aligning messaging with core brand values that matter to customers.
