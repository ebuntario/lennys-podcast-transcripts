For mission-critical products, one-to-one concierge [[concepts/user-onboarding|onboarding]] creates super fans and excellent early metrics for engagement and retention. This strategy uses capital to avoid building complex self-service flows prematurely, allowing engineering focus on finding [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] instead. It's a powerful way to kickstart a brand through direct human connection.
