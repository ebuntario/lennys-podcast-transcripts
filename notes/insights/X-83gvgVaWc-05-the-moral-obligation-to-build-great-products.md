People have hundreds of frustrating digital interactions daily, which drain their emotional energy. As product creators, we have a moral obligation to put that energy back by building intuitive, respectful software. Recognizing [[concepts/software-as-a-medium|software as a medium]] with an emotional component means we must be purposeful about the experience we deliver, as we are present in those moments with users, whether making a grandmother feel like a superhero or a fool.
