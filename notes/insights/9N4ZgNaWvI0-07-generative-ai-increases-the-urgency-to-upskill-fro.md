Administrative roles focused on backlog management and project coordination are highly vulnerable to automation by [[concepts/ai-disruption|generative AI]]. This makes roles like product owners and feature team PMs precarious. The long-term opportunity lies in developing skills that AI cannot easily replicate, particularly those centered on complex judgment around value, viability, ethics, and business constraints.
