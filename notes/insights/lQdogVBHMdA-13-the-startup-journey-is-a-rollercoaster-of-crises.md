Building a startup is a long journey marked by a rollercoaster of ups and downs, a series of failures, and moving from one crisis to the next. Embracing this reality is part of the [[concepts/founder-mindset|founder's mindset]]; if you haven't nearly died multiple times, you're likely too early in your journey.
