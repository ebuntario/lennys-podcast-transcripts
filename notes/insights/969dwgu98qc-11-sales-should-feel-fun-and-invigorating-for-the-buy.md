Bring passion and energy to sales conversations; the buyer should feel excited, like you're going to change their world, get them promoted, or increase their internal influence. [[concepts/psychological-safety|Trust]] is the primary currency in sales, and being an invigorating, trusted partner leads to referrals and word-of-mouth growth.
