AI doesn't replace human creativity but acts as a powerful collaborator that helps explore more ideas and variations quickly. For example, a director can use a video model to generate 50 versions of a scene transition for brainstorming, leading to a more creative and refined final product. This [[concepts/human-augmentation|augmentation]] allows creators to achieve outcomes that were previously too time-consuming or costly to iterate on.
