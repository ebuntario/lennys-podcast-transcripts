Stories impact the brain and release hormones when they use natural, sensory language that speaks to the heart and mind. Avoid business lingo and three-letter abbreviations that cause "banner blindness." Instead, describe how something smells, feels, or how a user's life improves, as this [[concepts/audience-engagement|engages audiences]] on a deeper level than technical or overused terms.
