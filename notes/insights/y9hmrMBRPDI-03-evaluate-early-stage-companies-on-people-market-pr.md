When assessing a startup, examine four key dimensions: the founder's leadership and vision, the growth and chaos of the [[concepts/market-dynamics|market]], the product's uniqueness and user enthusiasm, and the company's distribution advantage. You won't score perfectly on all four, so consider how your own strengths could de-risk their weaknesses if joining.
