To evangelize a vision, start with your core team to build conviction, then expand to key stakeholders whose buy-in is critical for success. Finally, present the vision to leadership as high up as possible. This [[concepts/stakeholder-buy-in|stakeholder alignment]] process incorporates feedback at each stage and turns the vision into a polished, shared belief.
