Reframing [[concepts/career-communication|career communication]] from self-promotion to educating others about your team's work makes it more palatable for introverts. This mindset shift helps secure resources and support by focusing on the collective impact rather than individual credit. It transforms an uncomfortable task into a necessary function of leadership and team advocacy.
