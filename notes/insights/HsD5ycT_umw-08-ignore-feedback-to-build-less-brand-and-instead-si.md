In large organizations, the advice to "keep your head down and let your work speak for itself" often hinders career advancement. Proactively building a [[concepts/personal-branding|personal brand]] through content, talks, or internal visibility acts as a bat-signal, attracting opportunities and distinguishing you from thousands of peers. This external signaling creates serendipity that pure execution often cannot.
