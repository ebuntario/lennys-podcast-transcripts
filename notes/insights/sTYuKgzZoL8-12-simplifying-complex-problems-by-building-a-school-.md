Tackle complex projects by first establishing a kindergarten-level understanding of the basic building blocks. From there, build up curricula for elementary, high school, and college levels of complexity. This "school pyramid" approach ensures everyone, regardless of their starting point, can get aligned and progress together in their [[concepts/creative-problem-solving|problem solving]].
