Creating a visual [[concepts/customer-journey-mapping|customer journey map]] fosters a [[concepts/shared-understanding|shared understanding]] of the customer's experience across marketing, product, and customer success. This shared language improves collaboration, helps non-customer-facing teams grasp their impact, and aligns everyone around delivering value at each milestone rather than siloed metrics.
