[[concepts/ai-integration|AI integration]] should focus on helping users achieve their goals faster and with higher quality, not on showcasing the technology itself. A balanced approach combines building proprietary AI for core advantages, partnering with best-in-class providers for commodities, and leveraging an ecosystem for extended capabilities. The user's desired outcome must remain the central focus.
