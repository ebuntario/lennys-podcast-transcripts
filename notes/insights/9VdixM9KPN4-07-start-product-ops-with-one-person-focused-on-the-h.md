When launching a product ops function, begin with a single hire focused on the pillar that addresses the organization's most pressing pain point. This person should conduct a listening tour to identify where they can deliver quick, visible wins. Demonstrating tangible impact builds momentum and makes the case for further investment in the [[concepts/product-operations|product operations]] team.
