Leading a strategy process requires being a good integrator who can connect diverse viewpoints and maintain forward momentum with low ego. The process is deeply satisfying at the end but involves frustration, dead ends, and self-doubt throughout. Maintaining a playful, light touch can help the team tolerate the inherent grind.
