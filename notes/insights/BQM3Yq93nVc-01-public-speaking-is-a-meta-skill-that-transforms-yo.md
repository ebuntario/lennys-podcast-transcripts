Public speaking is a [[concepts/meta-skills|meta-skill]], meaning that improving it elevates other areas of your life, similar to how fitness improves energy and confidence. Because we are naturally evolved to communicate, the anxiety and self-consciousness we feel are often just "bugs in the software" that can be debugged. Unlocking your natural speaking ability can positively impact your work, relationships, and overall sense of self.
