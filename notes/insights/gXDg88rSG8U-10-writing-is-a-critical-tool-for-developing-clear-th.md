The act of writing forces clarity of thought and is essential for developing strong [[concepts/critical-thinking|critical thinking]] skills. As the quote goes, "If you're thinking without writing, you just think you're thinking." This practice helps product managers crystallize strategies, articulate value, and communicate complex ideas effectively.
