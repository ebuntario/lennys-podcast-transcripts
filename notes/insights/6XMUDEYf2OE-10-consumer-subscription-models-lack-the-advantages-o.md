B2B SaaS benefits from predictable, rational customers and net dollar retention (customers spend more over time). [[concepts/consumer-subscription|Consumer subscription]] models lack these: consumers are less predictable, retain worse, and rarely increase their spend year-over-year. This necessitates extremely high annual retention rates (60-70%+) and often requires building [[concepts/network-effects|network effects]] or unique growth loops to achieve sustainable scale.
