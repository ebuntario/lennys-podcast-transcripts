Given equal skill and resources, a PM with a strong innate ability to [[concepts/prioritization|prioritize]] can generate 5x the impact of someone without it. Prioritization isn’t just about choosing projects; it includes sequencing, scope management, and even time allocation. This skill allows you to marshal your team’s resources toward the highest-leverage opportunities.
