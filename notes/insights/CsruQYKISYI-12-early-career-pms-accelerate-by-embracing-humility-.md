The most important trait for early-career product managers is humility. Acknowledge that you're operating in ambiguity where no one has the right answer, and be open to learning from everyone. This [[concepts/mindset|mindset]] of curiosity and elasticity of thought is foundational for synthesizing new information and discovering innovative solutions, far more so than dogmatic confidence.
