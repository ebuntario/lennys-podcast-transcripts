Most product leaders mistake recapping and eye contact for effective listening, missing a deeper level of understanding that enables world-class leadership. True [[concepts/active-listening|active listening]] involves perceiving unspoken context and underlying motivations, not just processing verbal content. Developing this skill transforms how you lead teams and make product decisions.
