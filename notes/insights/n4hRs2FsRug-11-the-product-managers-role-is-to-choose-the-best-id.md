In a collaborative environment, the product manager doesn't need to have all the best ideas, but they are accountable for selecting the best ones from the team. After synthesizing diverse inputs and data, the PM must make the final call, own the outcome, and decide what to do next—whether the initiative succeeds or fails. This balances [[concepts/cross-functional-collaboration|collaborative input]] with clear decision-making accountability.
