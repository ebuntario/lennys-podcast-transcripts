A critical sales skill is the ability to quickly build rapport with a new person, similar to how a bartender or barista connects with customers. Practice this in daily life by striking up genuine conversations. This "turbo rapport" allows you to ask candid discovery questions sooner, establishing trust and enabling a more effective [[concepts/discovery-call|discovery call]].
