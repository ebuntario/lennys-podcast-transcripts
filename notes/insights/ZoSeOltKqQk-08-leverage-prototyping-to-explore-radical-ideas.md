The power of [[concepts/prototyping|prototyping]] lies in exploring radical ideas to understand what users truly need, not just incremental feature development. Build a tech stack and engineering culture that supports rapid prototyping with the mindset that code can be thrown away. This process often leads to the final product goal faster than traditional development.
