The key is being able to point to specific, meaningful impact that cross-functional peers can vouch for, rather than just tenure length. Short stints are acceptable if you can articulate what you accomplished and why you moved on. Your [[concepts/career-narrative|career narrative]] should focus on leaving each place better than you found it.
