Draw a diamond and label the four points: (1) How do we define winning? (2) What do we have to win already? (3) What do we need to win? (4) What do we need to say to win? This [[concepts/winning-diamond|winning diamond framework]] shifts focus from finding a word to creating an experience, helping teams clarify their strategic objectives before beginning creative work.
