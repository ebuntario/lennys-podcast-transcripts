Choose content topics and formats based on genuine interest rather than perceived monetization potential. If you succeed with content you dislike, you'll be trapped maintaining a "job" you hate when it becomes financially significant. Prioritizing [[concepts/personal-passion|personal passion]] ensures long-term [[concepts/creator-sustainability|sustainability]] even during the unpaid early stages of audience building.
