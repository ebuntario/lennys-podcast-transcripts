Effective product team structure at a high-growth company should be oriented around timeless customer problems rather than ephemeral product surfaces. Teams focused on serving writers, readers, and growth create a stable foundation because these missions are never "done," unlike teams built around a specific feature or dashboard that may become obsolete.
