Different publications are suited for different stories. TechCrunch is strong for funding announcements, Axios for deals and FinTech, Business Insider for entrepreneurial journey pieces and pitch deck stories, and VentureBeat for AI news. Tailor your [[concepts/media-strategy|outreach]] based on the publication's beat and the nature of your announcement.
