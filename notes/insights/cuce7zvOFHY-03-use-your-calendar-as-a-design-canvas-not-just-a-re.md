Treat your calendar as a tool to design your ideal day, not just a passive log of meetings imposed by others. Block time for your highlight, exercise, meals, and even transitions. This [[concepts/proactive-planning|proactive design]] reclaims control, protects your focus time from others' requests, and helps you visualize and adjust how you actually spend your energy.
