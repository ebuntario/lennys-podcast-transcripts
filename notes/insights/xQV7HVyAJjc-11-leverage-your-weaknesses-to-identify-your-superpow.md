Your core weaknesses often create compensating superpowers; for example, dyslexia can foster exceptional questioning and pattern-recognition skills. Understanding what you "suck at" reveals the opposite strength that defines your unique value. This reframing turns perceived disabilities into professional [[concepts/strengths-weaknesses|super abilities]].
