For a horizontal tool, start with a massive, everyday use case (like note-taking) to build a broad top-of-funnel. Personal users discover the tool's deeper capabilities and later bring it into their workplaces. This [[concepts/b2c2b-motion|B2C2B motion]] provides natural, low-friction adoption for more complex enterprise use cases.
