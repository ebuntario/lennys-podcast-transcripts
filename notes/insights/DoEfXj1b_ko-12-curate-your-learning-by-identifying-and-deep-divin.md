To avoid burnout from information overload, product managers should focus [[concepts/learning|continuous learning]] on execution and specific skill gaps. Analyze your job to identify what's not working, then do a deep dive on that particular topic (e.g., user research, analytics) to build your personal toolbox of effective practices.
