A critical distinction is that product operations informs product managers but does not take away their decision-making authority. The PM remains accountable for product strategy, vision, prioritization, and tough stakeholder conversations about trade-offs. The product ops function acts as an enabler, providing systems and infrastructure, not as a substitute for [[concepts/product-management|product management]] judgment.
