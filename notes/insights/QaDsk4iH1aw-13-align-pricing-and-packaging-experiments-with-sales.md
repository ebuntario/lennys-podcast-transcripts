Pricing changes, like making a gated feature self-serve, should be guided by customer need and sales efficiency. For example, Retool opened self-hosted access to all users after realizing demand came from various company sizes, not just large enterprises. This allowed sales to focus on higher-value deals, aligning the [[concepts/pricing-strategy|pricing model]] with the ideal customer journey.
