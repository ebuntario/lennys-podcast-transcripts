To-do lists fail because they lack constraints and don't account for how long tasks actually take. Instead, schedule specific time blocks for each activity on your calendar, turning your values into time. This method, called [[concepts/timeboxing|timeboxing]], provides a realistic feedback loop for your productivity and forces you to prioritize within limited hours.
