Leaders who complain their teams can't make decisions must examine their own tolerance for disagreement. To build a scaled leadership team, you must be willing to let team members make boneheaded decisions sometimes. This [[concepts/delegation|delegation]] and trust is a critical growth edge for founders accustomed to controlling all outcomes.
