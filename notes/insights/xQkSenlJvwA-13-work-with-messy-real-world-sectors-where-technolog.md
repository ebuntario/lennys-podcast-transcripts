Founders should consider working with messy, non-tech sectors like healthcare, government, or heavy industry where technology leverage is now high and problems are undersolved. Post-ChatGPT, these sectors are more open to startups, and AI has reduced the cost of forward-deployed work. Building a [[concepts/competitive-advantage|competitive advantage]] here comes from deep domain experience and networks in these complex environments.
