When introducing new ideas, they often go from being dismissed as controversial to becoming the default way of thinking. Victory in thought leadership is not about defeating old ideas in battle, but about the concept becoming so integrated into the culture that new entrants find it obvious. This transition from insurgent revival to foundational knowledge is a sign of true impact.
