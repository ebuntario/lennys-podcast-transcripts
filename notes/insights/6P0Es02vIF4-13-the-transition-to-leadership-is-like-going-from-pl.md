Early in your career, you learn the rules and physics of the "game" to run up the score. Upon moving into leadership, you realize the rules are ambiguous, feedback is delayed, and everyone now sees you as the game's designer. This requires a fundamental [[concepts/mindset-shift|mindset shift]] from player to creator, where your internal complexity must expand to meet new demands.
