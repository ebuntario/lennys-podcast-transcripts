When the goal is to reduce a behavior (like sharing misinformation), introducing [[concepts/friction|friction]]—such as a confirmation step or a delay—slows users down and prompts reconsideration. This leverages the principle that increasing effort for undesired actions can effectively decrease their frequency, as seen in interventions on social platforms.
