Most leaders operate from a reactive mindset, driven by fear, a need to be right, or a desire to be liked. Research shows that shifting to a [[concepts/creative-leadership|creative leadership]] mindset—characterized by openness, curiosity, and purpose—is positively correlated with all dimensions of success. This shift requires redesigning your internal "operating system" and confronting underlying beliefs that hold you back.
