A clear, shared vision allows teams to operate with remarkable speed and efficiency, often with much smaller staff. When everyone understands "the Apple way" or "the Disney way," they don't waste time exploring endless permutations. This [[concepts/vision-clarity|clarity of vision]] fosters [[concepts/scenius|scenius]]—the collective genius of a small, aligned group—which is how foundational products like the original Mac (built by about 20 people) are created.
