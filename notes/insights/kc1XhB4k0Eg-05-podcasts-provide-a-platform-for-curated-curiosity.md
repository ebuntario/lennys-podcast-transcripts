A podcast creates a legitimate reason to engage with experts you admire, transforming a cold outreach into a mutually beneficial invitation. This structured format allows you to deeply explore niche topics while providing value to guests through amplification. It turns [[concepts/networking|networking]] into a content creation process that builds authority and relationships simultaneously.
