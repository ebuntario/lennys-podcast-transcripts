In a world where AI generates more software, the differentiating factors become unique design, superior craft, and high quality. As AI handles more of the obvious and repetitive tasks, human creativity and attention to detail become the new [[concepts/competitive-advantage|competitive moat]]. This shifts the conversation towards how design can be a core differentiator for products and startups.
