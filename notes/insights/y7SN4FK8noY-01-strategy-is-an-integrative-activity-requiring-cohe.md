Strategy is an integrated set of choices that positions a company to compel desired customer action. It is intellectually challenging because it requires fitting multiple elements together coherently, and emotionally intimidating because it involves making definitive choices to do some things and not others. This [[concepts/strategy-definition|integrative nature]] distinguishes it from simpler operational decisions.
