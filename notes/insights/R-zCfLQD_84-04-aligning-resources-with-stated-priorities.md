A common organizational failure is declaring a top priority but not manifesting it in resource allocation. If your top talent and engineering resources are working on migrations or moonshots not aligned with the stated number one goal, then your true priority is something else. [[concepts/strategy-execution|Executional clarity]] requires that resourcing directly reflects strategic priorities.
