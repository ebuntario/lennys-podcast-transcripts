Working in [[concepts/fintech|FinTech]] uniquely prepares product managers because they must serve two customers: the end-user and the regulator. These interests are often in direct conflict, forcing PMs to constantly navigate [[concepts/stakeholder-management|existential trade-offs]]. This high-stakes environment conditions them for superior stakeholder management and complex decision-making under pressure.
