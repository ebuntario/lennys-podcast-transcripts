When evaluating a feature or product idea, consciously push the thinking to its logical extreme: could this become a foundational service or platform for multiple products? A failure to ask this question can lead to building a point solution that misses a much larger, company-wide opportunity. This [[concepts/systems-thinking|forward-looking strategic question]] helps identify potential 10X opportunities.
