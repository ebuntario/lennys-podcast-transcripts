AI tools like Claude can act as a patient editor, helping to identify missing points or unsustained claims in your writing. You can upload content and ask what was missed or where arguments weaken. This process helps refine work before it's shared, acting as a [[concepts/artificial-intelligence|thought partner]] to improve clarity and completeness.
