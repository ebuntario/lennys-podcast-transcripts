Getting promoted is significantly harder if you're not skilled at telling stories and rallying a team behind a shared goal. While a team can sometimes compensate for a product person's weaker storytelling, lacking this skill can be a [[concepts/professional-growth|career staller]]. Reaching a decent level of [[concepts/narrative|storytelling]] and public speaking is crucial for advancement in product leadership.
