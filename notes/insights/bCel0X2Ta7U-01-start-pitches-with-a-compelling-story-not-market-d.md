Great founders manage the narrative and capture attention from the start. Instead of beginning with market size, open with your unique insight or a dramatic moment, similar to how a thriller movie starts in the middle of the action. This approach helps [[concepts/founder-presentation|founder presentation]] stand out and makes investors more receptive to the full story.
