Foundation model companies cannot and do not want to build every AI application, especially those requiring industry-specific or proprietary data. This creates immense opportunities for startups to build tailored AI products that solve deep vertical problems. The real moat often lies in the unique [[concepts/data-moats|data and processes]] behind company walls, which can be used to train specialized models.
