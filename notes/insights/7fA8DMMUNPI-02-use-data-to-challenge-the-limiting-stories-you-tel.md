The stories we tell ourselves about our capabilities are often untrue. Treat these [[concepts/self-narrative|self-narratives]] as hypotheses and gather data by seeking feedback from colleagues, similar to conducting user research. This approach allows you to validate or rewrite your story based on evidence rather than assumption.
