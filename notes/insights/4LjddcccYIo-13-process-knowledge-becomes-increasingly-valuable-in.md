Understanding how to navigate [[concepts/organizational-dynamics|organizational dynamics]] and get things done in complex companies represents a valuable form of institutional wisdom. This "process knowledge"—knowing who to talk to, how decisions get made, and what motivates different groups—often develops over time and becomes more valuable as organizations grow.
