Thriving in Web3 demands the ability to see the ideal decentralized future while navigating the centralized realities of today (Web 2.5). Successful builders understand the core principles and are willing to experiment in ambiguity, ignoring noise from critics focused on current limitations. This requires a conviction in the journey and patience to morph today's prototypes into tomorrow's ideals.
