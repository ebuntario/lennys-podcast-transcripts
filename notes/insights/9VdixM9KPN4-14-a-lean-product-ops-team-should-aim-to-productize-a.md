An effective product ops team thinks like product managers for the product organization, seeking to leverage tools and build systems that automate repetitive tasks. The goal is not to create a large human-powered service but to establish a lean team that builds and maintains "products" (like dashboards, templates, or databases) that enable the broader product org to self-serve, increasing overall [[concepts/operational-efficiency|operational efficiency]].
