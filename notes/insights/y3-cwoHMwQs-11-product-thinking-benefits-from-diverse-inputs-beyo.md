Balancing pragmatic business books with fiction and humanities creates more creative [[concepts/product-sense|product sense]]. Fiction provides insights into human experience that translate into better storytelling and user understanding. This interdisciplinary approach sparks ideas that wouldn't emerge from purely business-focused reading, particularly for products centered on human connection and narrative.
