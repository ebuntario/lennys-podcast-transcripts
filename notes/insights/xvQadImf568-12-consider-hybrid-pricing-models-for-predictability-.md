Pure usage-based models can create budgeting challenges for buyers. Most successful SaaS companies use a hybrid approach: a "good, better, best" subscription tier with built-in consumption quotas. This provides predictability for the buyer while maintaining a [[concepts/usage-based-pricing|usage-based escalator]] that drives revenue growth as usage scales.
