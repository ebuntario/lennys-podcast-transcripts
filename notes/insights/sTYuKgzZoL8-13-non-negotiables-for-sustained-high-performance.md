Core personal needs—like adequate sleep, regular exercise, proper nutrition, and alone time for introverts—are non-negotiable for maintaining performance. Treating these as table stakes, not luxuries, ensures you have the physical and mental energy required for demanding work. This [[concepts/energy-management|energy management]] is foundational, not ancillary, to professional success.
