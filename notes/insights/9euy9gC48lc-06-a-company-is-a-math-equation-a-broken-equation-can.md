Even exceptional product execution cannot salvage a fundamentally broken business model or unit economics. Companies are mathematical equations where investment must yield returns on a viable timeline. If the core assumptions—like the cost to create a sufficient content library or achieve scale—are wrong, no amount of iteration on the product itself will fix the underlying [[concepts/unit-economics|unit economics]]. Product excellence must be paired with a sound economic foundation.
