Building a [[concepts/platform-strategy|platform]] requires navigating complex economic tensions between multiple constituencies like developers, merchants, and end consumers. Platform PMs must establish clear principles about which stakeholder gets priority in trade-offs, as these decisions create second-order effects that shape the entire ecosystem.
