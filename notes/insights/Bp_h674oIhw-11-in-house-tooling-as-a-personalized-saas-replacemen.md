Democratized development enables professionals in sales, marketing, HR, and operations to build their own [[concepts/internal-tools|internal tools]] tailored to exact needs, acting as a replacement for off-the-shelf SaaS. This solves the "one-size-fits-all" problem of commercial software, allowing for perfect [[concepts/customization|customization]] and eliminating the friction of procurement and vendor management for simple workflows.
