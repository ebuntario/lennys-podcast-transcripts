Investing in user interviews, surveys, and customer panels consistently delivers value. For early-stage companies, proper [[concepts/customer-research|customer research]] can galvanize the team, provide clarity, and create momentum by offering focus and purpose. The insights gained directly inform product development and growth strategy, making it a foundational activity.
