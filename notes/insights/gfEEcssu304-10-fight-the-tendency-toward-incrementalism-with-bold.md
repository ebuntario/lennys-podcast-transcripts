Counter the fear of bold ideas by "reaching for the stars to land on the moon." Create a comprehensive [[concepts/vision|vision]] for an ideal user journey (e.g., an 11-star experience) to serve as a North Star. This enables coordinated, ambitious steps forward rather than incremental changes that may never coalesce into a great whole.
