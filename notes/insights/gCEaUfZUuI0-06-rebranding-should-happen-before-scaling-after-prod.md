The optimal time for a [[concepts/rebranding|rebrand]] is after achieving [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] but before scaling the company significantly. This allows the brand to evolve to match the company's expanded vision and potential without the burden of changing an identity deeply entrenched in a large user base.
