The most effective researchers possess five core tools: formative/generative research, evaluative/usability testing, rigorous survey design, applied statistics, and technical data skills (like SQL or prompt engineering). Being [[concepts/multi-method-research|multi-method]] prevents over-reliance on one approach and allows researchers to match the right method to the business question, moving beyond a purely qualitative tradition.
