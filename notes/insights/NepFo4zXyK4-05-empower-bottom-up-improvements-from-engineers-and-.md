Bottom-up energy from engineers and designers noticing and fixing problems is a powerful way to improve product quality. When a team member is personally vested in a problem, their motivation and creativity spike, often leading to faster and better solutions. A product manager should generally not get in the way of this organic [[concepts/product-iteration|improvement process]].
