For most startups, a well-crafted [[concepts/thought-leadership|blog post]] is more effective than a formal press release. Blogs are more shareable on social media, allow for a flexible and human tone, and can host all the same news details. Press releases are often formulaic, costly to distribute, and rarely checked by reporters directly anymore.
