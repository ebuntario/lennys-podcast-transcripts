Developers are responsible for the performance and reliability of the tools they integrate. If a service fails, it's their reputation and job on the line. This high-stakes psyche makes them deeply averse to sales pitches and trust marketing claims. They must validate a tool themselves through a proof of concept. Therefore, selling to developers requires investing deeply in [[concepts/self-serve-experience|self-serve experiences]] that allow them to build and prove value independently.
