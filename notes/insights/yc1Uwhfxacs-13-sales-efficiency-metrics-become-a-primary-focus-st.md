While early levels tolerate inefficiency, strong product-market fit (level three) requires optimizing for financial sustainability. Key benchmarks include gross margin above 60-70%, burn multiple between 1-3, net revenue retention >110%, and churn below 10%. Mastering these [[concepts/saas-metrics|efficiency metrics]] is essential for building a durable, scalable business.
