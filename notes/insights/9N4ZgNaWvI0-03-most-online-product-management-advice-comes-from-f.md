Approximately 90% of product management content online, including articles, books, and community advice, propagates practices from feature team or delivery team environments. This creates a self-perpetuating cycle where well-meaning advice reinforces suboptimal models. Aspiring product managers must cultivate [[concepts/critical-thinking|critical thinking]] to evaluate advice and seek out resources from proven, outcome-focused companies.
