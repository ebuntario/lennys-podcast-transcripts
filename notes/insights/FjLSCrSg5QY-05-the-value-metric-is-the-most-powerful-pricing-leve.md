The pricing metric (e.g., per user, per thousand visits) is the single most impactful element of a pricing model. A well-chosen [[concepts/value-based-pricing|value metric]] automatically aligns price with customer value, enabling Disney and a startup to pay appropriately different amounts. It also reduces churn by 20-25% and can double expansion revenue through automatic plan upgrades based on usage.
