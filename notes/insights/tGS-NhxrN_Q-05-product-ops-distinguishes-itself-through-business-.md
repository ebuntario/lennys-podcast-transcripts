What distinguishes [[concepts/product-operations|product operations]] from program or project management is deep understanding of the product, customer, and business inner workings. While program managers focus on execution frameworks, product ops professionals connect operational processes to customer outcomes and business metrics. This business acumen enables them to advise product leadership strategically rather than just facilitating processes.
