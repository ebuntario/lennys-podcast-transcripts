People use products in unexpected ways; for example, some Waze users watch the screen while others only listen to audio prompts. If you only design for your own usage pattern, you'll miss serving other segments. [[concepts/user-behavior|Observing real users]] reveals these differences and prevents building the wrong product for your audience.
