The biggest mistake teams make is becoming passionate about a solution before deeply understanding the customer's problem. [[concepts/product-sense|Product sense]] is built by developing [[concepts/empathy|empathy]] for the user and understanding the psychology of their primal moments. In the first 30 seconds of using a product, users are lazy, vain, and selfish, wanting to look good and feel successful quickly without a learning curve.
