Product teams should be accountable for driving accounts to [[concepts/product-qualified-account]] status, measured as a conversion rate from engaged ideal customer profile teams. This represents [[concepts/product-accountability]] for pipeline creation in product-led sales. While individual product managers might not have revenue targets, product leadership should share revenue responsibility through ownership of KPIs like PQA conversion, package mix, and free-to-paid conversion rates.
