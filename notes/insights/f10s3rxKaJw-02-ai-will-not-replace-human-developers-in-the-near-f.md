Generative AI is overhyped as a replacement for humans; it cannot replicate the creative spark and innovation central to humanity. AI tools require human-generated data and a [[concepts/human-in-the-loop|human in the loop]] to be effective, positioning them as assistants rather than autonomous pilots.
