To reduce stress and improve [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]], evaluate whether anyone will remember or care about a particular decision in six months. Most decisions people agonize over are not that consequential. This filter helps teams focus energy on truly important choices and move quickly on reasonable, good-enough options for the rest.
