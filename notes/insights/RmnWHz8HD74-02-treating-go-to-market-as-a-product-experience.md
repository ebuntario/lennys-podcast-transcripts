When products become commoditized and technical differentiation narrows, the [[concepts/customer-experience|customer experience]] of being sold to becomes a key differentiator. Companies should design the entire customer buying journey—from first awareness to long-term retention—as a series of unique, human, and valuable experiences, rather than a transactional process. This approach transforms go-to-market from a function into a core product offering.
