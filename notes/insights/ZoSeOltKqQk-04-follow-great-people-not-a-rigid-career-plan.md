Instead of a rigid five-year plan, follow people who are the best at what they do. You learn the most from great mentors and colleagues, and the skills you acquire are permanent assets. Focus on joining environments with a nexus of talented people, as this network and learning will propel your [[concepts/career-development|career development]] forward.
