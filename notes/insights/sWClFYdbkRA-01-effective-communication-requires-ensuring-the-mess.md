Effective communication isn't just about conveying a message—it's about delivering it in a way that the listener receives, understands, and remembers it. This is especially critical for [[concepts/brand-voice|brand voice]] and product messaging, where creating an emotional connection can make content more memorable and shareable. A unique, quirky tone that doesn't take itself too seriously can make users feel something, increasing engagement and word-of-mouth.
