Instead of rigid annual plans, plan for a rolling 12-month horizon and refresh that plan every six months. This creates higher confidence in the immediate six months and lower, but directional, confidence in the following six. This [[concepts/agility|agility]] allows the business to pivot based on new opportunities or faster-than-expected progress, reduces team churn, and better aligns product and go-to-market efforts.
