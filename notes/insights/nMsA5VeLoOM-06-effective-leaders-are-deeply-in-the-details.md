The best leaders demonstrate that no task is beneath them and maintain a deep connection to the craft. They audit details, run product reviews directly, and understand processes at a granular level. This combination of humility and [[concepts/strategy-execution|execution]] excellence drives quality and fosters a culture of responsibility throughout the organization.
