Build a data team with diverse backgrounds—including people from operations, finance, startups, and large companies—not just career data scientists. This [[concepts/skill-diversity|diversity of experience]] creates a rich learning environment where team members with different expertise can teach each other and provide unique perspectives on scaling challenges.
