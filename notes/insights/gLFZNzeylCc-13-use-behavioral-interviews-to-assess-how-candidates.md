A valuable interview approach is to ask behavioral questions about how candidates have handled ambiguous or challenging situations. Look for answers that demonstrate they can impose structure, chart a path forward, and—critically—seek input and help to validate their direction. This reveals their ability to navigate the inherent uncertainty of product management.
