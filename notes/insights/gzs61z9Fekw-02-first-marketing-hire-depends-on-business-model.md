When hiring your first marketer, prioritize [[concepts/business-model|business model]] experience over industry or audience experience. The required marketing activities differ drastically between top-down sales, bottom-up product-led growth, or enterprise models. Assess your current constraints: if you have distribution (engine) but weak messaging, hire for fuel; if you have great content but poor reach, hire for engine.
