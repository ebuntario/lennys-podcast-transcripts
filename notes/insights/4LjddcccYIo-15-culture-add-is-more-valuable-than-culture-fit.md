While "culture fit" can reinforce homogeneity, "culture add" focuses on what unique perspectives someone brings to a team. This shift in [[concepts/hiring|hiring]] philosophy values [[concepts/diversity-inclusion|diversity]] of experience and thought while still ensuring candidates can collaborate effectively within the existing organizational context.
