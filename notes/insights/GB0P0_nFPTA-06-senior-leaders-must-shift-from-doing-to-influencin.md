To progress beyond senior management, you must transition from being a functional expert to a strategic influencer. This requires letting go of direct execution, thinking long-term, and mastering [[concepts/systems-thinking|coordination across teams]]. The skills that got you to your current level won't suffice for the next; you must develop new leadership behaviors.
