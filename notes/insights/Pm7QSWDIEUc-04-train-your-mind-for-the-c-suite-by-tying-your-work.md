To prepare for an executive role, you must learn to think and speak in the terms of the CEO and board, which requires a strong handle on finance and how the business generates revenue. Understanding how the entire company system works together is critical, as an executive's job is about the system. Taking "tours of duty" in different departments can build this essential [[concepts/business-acumen|business acumen]].
