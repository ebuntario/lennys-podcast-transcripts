Viewing startup founding as a resume builder is a poor reason to begin. If successful, it will consume a decade or more of your life; if unsuccessful, it won't be viewed as a positive career milestone. This mindset misunderstands the all-consuming nature of [[concepts/startup-journey|the startup journey]] and often leads to quitting when challenges arise.
