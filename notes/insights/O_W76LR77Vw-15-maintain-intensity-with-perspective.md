While intensity and extraordinary effort are required for business success, it's vital to maintain perspective: none of this ultimately matters in the grand scheme. Recognizing the insignificance of our work against the backdrop of existence brings levity and allows you to play the "sport" of business with full commitment but without soul-crushing gravity. This [[concepts/work-life-perspective|balanced perspective]] fuels sustainable high performance.
