As many creators shift to video-first platforms, there remains a strategic opportunity in deep, written analysis for technical audiences. In-depth writing serves a need for scannable, referenceable content that video often cannot. Building a [[concepts/niche-expertise|reputation for depth]] in a specific field creates a durable moat that is harder for competitors to replicate quickly.
