Skills such as prioritization, communication, and [[concepts/emotional-intelligence|empathy]] are difficult to teach AI models effectively. AI research progress itself can be bottlenecked by management decisions on compute allocation and research direction. Human collaboration and understanding team dynamics remain essential for launching successful products and scaling organizations.
