Treat [[concepts/internal-communication|internal communication]] with the same strategic intent as external marketing, as it is critical for keeping a scaling company aligned. This requires dedicated resources to manage channels like intranets, newsletters, and leadership videos, creating a predictable [[concepts/operational-cadence|cadence]] of information. Learn from sales teams, who are experts at keeping distributed teams updated on product changes and messaging.
