You can create a critical advantage by leaning into craftsmanship and empathy where others fail. The "tilt your umbrella" metaphor illustrates that when competitors are oblivious to their impact on users, their failure to be considerate is your opportunity. Small, considerate conveniences foster an emotional connection that drives user advocacy and organic growth.
