For a product executive, customer escalations are not an inconvenience but a gift. They provide direct insight into problems and are the raw material for improving both the product and the systems that build it. A dedicated escalations team should pistol-whip issues to find the true [[concepts/operational-excellence|root cause]], fixing not just the symptom but the underlying software and processes.
