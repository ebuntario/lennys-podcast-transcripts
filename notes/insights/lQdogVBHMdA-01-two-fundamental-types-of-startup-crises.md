Founders face two abstract types of existential crises. The first is a [[concepts/cash-flow|cash crisis]], where your funding or revenue plan is jeopardized. The second is a loss of [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]], where your value proposition becomes irrelevant, forcing you back to square one.
