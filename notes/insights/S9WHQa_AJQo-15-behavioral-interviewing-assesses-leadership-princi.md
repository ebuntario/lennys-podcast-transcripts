Amazon's hiring process uses [[concepts/behavioral-interviewing|behavioral interviewing]] against specific leadership principles as objective criteria. This method evaluates how candidates have demonstrated principles in past situations rather than hypothetical responses. Bar Raisers ensure interviews focus on these standardized criteria rather than subjective impressions, maintaining consistency across hiring decisions.
