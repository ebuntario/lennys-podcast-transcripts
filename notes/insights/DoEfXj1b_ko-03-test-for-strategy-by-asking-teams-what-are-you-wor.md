A simple test for the presence of a coherent strategy is to ask teams what they're working on and why. If their answers don't ladder up to a common story about business goals and customer value, there is a [[concepts/strategy-deployment|strategy deployment]] problem. Teams should be able to connect their tactical work to business outcomes.
