When building teams, prioritize candidates with high agency, fast [[concepts/learning-speed|clock speed]], and energy over deep domain experience. In fast-moving fields like AI, learned habits can be a crutch, and a beginner's mind is valuable. Look for signals of these traits in how candidates engage with your product and the questions they ask during the [[concepts/hiring-practices|hiring process]].
