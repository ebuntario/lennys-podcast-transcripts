The number one predictor of successful [[concepts/ai-adoption|AI adoption]] across a company is whether the CEO uses tools like ChatGPT daily. A hands-on CEO can drive excitement, set realistic expectations, and lead by example (e.g., stating an important memo was written with AI). This top-down [[concepts/team-leadership|leadership]] creates permission and momentum for the [[concepts/organizational-change|organizational change]] required to integrate AI deeply.
