As a product organization scales, structure it into areas focused on specific target customers and problem spaces, not just features or projects. Each area should have clear, nested metrics (company, pillar, area, team) that align with business growth. This creates agency and [[concepts/accountability|accountability]] close to the teams and ensures roadmaps are driven by what's most meaningful for customers, not just a list of features.
