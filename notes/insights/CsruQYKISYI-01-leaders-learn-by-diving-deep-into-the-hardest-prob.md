Effective leaders avoid the temptation to delegate from a distance. Instead, they learn the most about challenges, problems, and successes by being in the trenches with their teams on the hardest or most complicated tasks. This hands-on approach, often called [[concepts/go-and-see|go and see]], provides critical context that high-level communication misses and builds a foundation for better [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]].
