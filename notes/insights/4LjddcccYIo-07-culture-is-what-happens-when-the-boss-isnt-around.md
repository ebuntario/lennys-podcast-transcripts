[[concepts/organizational-culture|Company culture]] becomes increasingly important in distributed organizations where employees make decisions without direct supervision. It serves as both a decision-making guide and a talent magnet. When evaluating a company's culture, ask for specific adjectives that describe it and whether different departments experience it consistently.
