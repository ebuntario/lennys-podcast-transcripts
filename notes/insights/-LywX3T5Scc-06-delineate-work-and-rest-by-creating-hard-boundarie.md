To sustain performance over decades, establish clear boundaries between work and personal time. A practical tactic is to not have email on your phone, allowing you to be "all in" when working and "all out" when not. This [[concepts/mental-clarity|mental clarity]] and space prevents burnout and often provides the perspective needed to see if you're working on the right things, not just working harder.
