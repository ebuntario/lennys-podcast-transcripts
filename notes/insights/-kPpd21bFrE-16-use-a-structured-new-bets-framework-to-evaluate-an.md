Revolut's [[concepts/new-bets-framework|new bets framework]] allows anyone to propose ideas that are evaluated like startups: market size, business case, competitive advantage, and customer problem. Approved ideas get small teams to build polished MVPs, validate metrics, then scale across Revolut's existing customer base for rapid traction.
