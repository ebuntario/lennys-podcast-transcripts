Modern data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery) act as a scalable, SQL-speaking hub for all company data. This makes the warehouse the [[concepts/single-source-of-truth|single source of truth]], enabling tools to easily pull in trusted, joined data from product, marketing, and sales. The future lies in connecting this rich warehouse data to interfaces optimized for analyzing event sequences and user behavior.
