Modern [[concepts/product-management|product management]] in India faced a unique challenge: its talent pool initially built products as a back-office service for Western markets, not for local consumers. This created a [[concepts/software-development-culture|software development culture]] accustomed to predictable, request-response models, unlike the iterative, consumer-centric approach needed for the [[concepts/india-market|Indian market]]. The field has matured as leaders experienced more product cycles and understood software's inherent unpredictability.
