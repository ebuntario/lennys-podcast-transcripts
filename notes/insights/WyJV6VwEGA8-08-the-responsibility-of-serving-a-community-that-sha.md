Building a product for a community that shapes the world's technology is both a privilege and a profound responsibility. This duty involves championing design, advancing the craft, and solving real user needs in a responsible way. Leaders should carry this not as a weight, but as motivation to build better and simplify relentlessly for their [[concepts/community-stewardship|community]].
