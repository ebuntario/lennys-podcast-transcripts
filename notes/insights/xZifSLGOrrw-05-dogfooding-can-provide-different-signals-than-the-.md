While [[concepts/dogfooding|dogfooding]] your own product is critical, the internal team's usage can provide a different signal than the general market. Teams deeply familiar with the technology's paradigms may use it in advanced, non-standard ways. It's essential to also gather empirical signal from external users to ensure the product lands intuitively and provides trivial initial value for broader [[concepts/product-market-fit|adoption]].
