Burnout progresses in stages: a "feather" is a subtle early sign like waking up tired. If ignored, it becomes a "brick" like irritability or arguments weeks later. Finally, the "dump truck" is a major health or career crisis. The goal is to cultivate [[concepts/interoception|interoception]] to notice the feather stage and adjust before catastrophic consequences.
