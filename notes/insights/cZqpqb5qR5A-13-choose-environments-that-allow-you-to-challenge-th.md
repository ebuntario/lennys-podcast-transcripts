Your ability to drive change depends heavily on the surrounding environment. If you constantly feel suffocated by processes and cynicism, it will eventually change you. Seek out or cultivate a [[concepts/innovation-culture|culture]] where autonomous leaders at all levels are empowered to push for the right things, as this is essential for doing your best work and maintaining personal drive.
