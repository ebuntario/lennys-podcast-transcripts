For long-term [[concepts/writing|writing]] success, focus on consistent publication over perfect quality. The biggest risk is quitting early due to burnout or fear of imperfection. An audience builds over years by sharing your evolving thinking, not by polishing a few pieces indefinitely. Most critical feedback is not worth internalizing.
