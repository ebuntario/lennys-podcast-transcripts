While novel experiences can be initially appealing, most people derive deeper satisfaction from meaningful collaboration and relationships. Since people spend their weekdays working, being engaged with a team provides more consistent fulfillment than constantly seeking new experiences. This understanding explains why many successful people return to building things even after achieving financial independence.
