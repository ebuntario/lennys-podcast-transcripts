Traditional [[concepts/funnel-models|funnel models]] and metrics like MQLs/SQLs are problematic because they treat all customers and products as identical, placing the business at the center instead of the customer. They often ignore the critical context of a customer's life before discovering your product and fail to account for post-acquisition metrics like retention and expansion, which are essential for [[concepts/subscription-models|recurring revenue businesses]].
