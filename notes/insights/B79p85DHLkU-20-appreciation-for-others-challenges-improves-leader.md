Understanding the personal and professional challenges people face creates more empathetic leadership and better business decisions. This [[concepts/perspective-taking]] helps leaders appreciate team members' circumstances and make more thoughtful choices. In marketing technology, where decisions impact multiple stakeholders, this awareness fosters better collaboration and more sustainable system designs.
