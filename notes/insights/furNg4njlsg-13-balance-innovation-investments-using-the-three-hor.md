Allocate resources across three horizons: 70% to core business (horizon one), 20% to adjacent opportunities (horizon two, 12-36 months), and 10% to next-generation bets (horizon three, 3-5 years). This [[concepts/innovation-portfolio|portfolio approach]] ensures sustained execution while funding future growth, with exact percentages adjusted based on team maturity and strategic focus.
