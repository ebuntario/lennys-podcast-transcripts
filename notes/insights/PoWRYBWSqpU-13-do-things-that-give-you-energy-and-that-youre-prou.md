A guiding life philosophy is to pursue activities that generate [[concepts/personal-energy|personal energy]] and that you can look back on with pride. This [[concepts/work-alignment|alignment]] creates a virtuous cycle where the work itself is rewarding, leading to greater productivity and impact. Evaluate decisions through a [[concepts/long-term-perspective|long-term perspective]]: "Will I be proud of this in 10 years?"
