Maintaining [[concepts/independence|independence]] by not taking external funding allows a company to be an "all-terrain vehicle" that can go anywhere without asking for permission. This freedom enables you to follow your gut, experiment without obligation, and turn the business into anything you want. It creates optionality and prevents being locked into a single, high-pressure path dictated by investors.
