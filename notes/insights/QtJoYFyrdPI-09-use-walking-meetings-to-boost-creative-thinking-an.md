Distributed "walk and talk" meetings, where both participants walk in their own locations with AirPods, are highly effective for [[concepts/creativity|creative]] and strategic conversations. Walking oxygenates the brain and the informal format encourages deeper thinking. This practice, adopted during remote work, can lead to more fruitful discussions than traditional sit-down meetings.
