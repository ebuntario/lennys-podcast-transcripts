AI tools like ChatGPT or Claude can act as thinking partners, analyzing journals or transcripts to surface patterns and propose deeper questions. They don't replace human connection but can augment [[concepts/radical-self-inquiry|radical self-inquiry]] by helping burn away trivial tasks and elevate focus on what matters: presence and strategic thinking.
