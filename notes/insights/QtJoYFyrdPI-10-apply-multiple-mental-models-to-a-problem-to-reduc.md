Any single [[concepts/mental-models|mental model]] simplifies reality and risks eliminating a critical dimension. To increase confidence in a decision, apply three different models to the same problem. If they converge on the same conclusion, you have significantly reduced the chance of a blind spot, making your decision more robust.
