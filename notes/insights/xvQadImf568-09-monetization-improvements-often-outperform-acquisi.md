Improving monetization is a highly efficient growth lever. Data shows a 1% improvement in monetization can have 4x the impact on the bottom line compared to a 1% improvement in acquisition. Companies that revisit pricing often see significant ARR lifts, making [[concepts/monetization|monetization strategy]] a critical and underappreciated area for optimization.
