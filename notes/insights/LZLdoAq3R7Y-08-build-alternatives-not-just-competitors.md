The goal shouldn't be to outdo or destroy the competition, but to provide a genuine [[concepts/differentiation|alternative]] with a different perspective, pricing model, or approach. If a product isn't offering a distinct alternative, it doesn't need to exist. This mindset focuses on creation and pride in unique work rather than engaging in a defensive, expensive battle.
