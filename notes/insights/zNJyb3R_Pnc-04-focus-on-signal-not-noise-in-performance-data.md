Platforms provide an overwhelming amount of data; the key is to identify the signal that aligns with your campaign goal. If the goal is conversions, focus on conversion rate and cost per conversion, not just impressions or reach. Hiring for this skill means finding people who can cut through the [[concepts/performance-metrics|noise]] to find the actionable signal.
