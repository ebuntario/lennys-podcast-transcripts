A healthy product ops function is lean and leverages tools and systems to scale its impact. A one-to-one ratio with product managers indicates inefficiency, while a one-to-hundred ratio suggests the team is under-resourced. The goal is to build a [[concepts/shared-services|shared services model]] where a small, skilled team oversees programs and infrastructure that enable many product teams to work effectively.
