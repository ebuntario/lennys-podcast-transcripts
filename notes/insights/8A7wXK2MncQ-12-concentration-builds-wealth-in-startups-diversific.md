Startups are in the business of building, not preserving. This requires making concentrated, high-stakes bets rather than hedging or diversifying efforts. A clear, focused [[concepts/strategy|strategy]] allows you to apply limited resources with maximum leverage against a specific problem. Successful companies often start by dominating a narrow wedge before expanding.
