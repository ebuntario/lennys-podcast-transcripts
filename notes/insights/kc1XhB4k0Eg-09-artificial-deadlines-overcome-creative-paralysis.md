Create external pressure to force decisions by committing to public announcements or partner collaborations. This [[concepts/constraints|constraints]] approach prevents endless deliberation about naming, formatting, or positioning. You can always iterate after launching, but getting started requires overcoming the initial [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]] hurdle that stalls many projects.
