High [[concepts/productivity|productivity]] comes from accomplishing more per minute, not from working more hours. Exceptional performers can produce high-quality output quickly by maintaining intense focus during work time. This approach enables you to achieve significant results while preserving time for life outside work, creating sustainable high-performance habits.
