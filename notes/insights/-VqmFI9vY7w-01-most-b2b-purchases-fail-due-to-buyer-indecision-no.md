40-60% of B2B purchase processes end in no decision, and most of those failures occur because buyers can't confidently choose between options. Buyers often delay or abandon purchases because making the wrong choice feels riskier than maintaining the status quo. This [[concepts/risk-aversion|risk aversion]] creates a major hurdle that sales pitches must overcome by reducing perceived decision risk.
