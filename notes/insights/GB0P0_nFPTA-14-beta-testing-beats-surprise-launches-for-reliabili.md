While surprise launches create buzz, they often reveal unexpected failures. Prioritizing [[concepts/risk-management|beta testing]] with real users—even if some details leak—provides valuable feedback and prevents public failures. The trade-off of some secrecy for greater reliability is worth it, especially at scale.
