The midlife period (35-75) represents a transformative chrysalis phase rather than a crisis. This [[concepts/life-transitions|transition]] involves unraveling outdated expectations and definitions of success, leading to greater freedom and happiness in later decades. Reframing aging as growth rather than decline can add years to your life according to research.
