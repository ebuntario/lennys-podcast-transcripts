When you feel the urge to get distracted, set a timer for 10 minutes and commit to either returning to your task or simply "surfing the urge." This technique acknowledges that emotional discomfort crests and subsides like a wave. By telling yourself "not yet" instead of "no," you build [[concepts/impulse-control|impulse control]] and prove to yourself that you can postpone distraction.
