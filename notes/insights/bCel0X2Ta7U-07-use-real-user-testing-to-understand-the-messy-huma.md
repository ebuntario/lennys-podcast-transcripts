Metrics alone can anonymize and sanitize the user experience. Schedule regular sessions to watch real people sign up and use your product, noting their tone of voice and facial expressions. This [[concepts/user-research|user research]] is often embarrassing but reveals critical insights that data dashboards cannot capture.
