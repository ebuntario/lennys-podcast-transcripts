Create and maintain a living document listing the top 10 problems your product or users face, updated quarterly. This [[concepts/problem-framing|problem-framing]] tool serves as a shared source of truth for your team and stakeholders, ensuring everyone is aligned on what matters most. It becomes the foundational insight layer for all strategic and vision work.
