Moving from monolithic codebases to service-based architectures with well-defined APIs allows teams to own their code independently. This technical foundation enables [[concepts/team-autonomy|team autonomy]] by reducing interdependencies between groups. Teams can then operate with dedicated resources focused on improving specific customer experiences without constant coordination overhead.
