The value of working at the definitive leader in a space outweighs a higher title at a lesser company. The [[concepts/brand-value|brand halo]], quality of talent, network effects, and learning environment at a market leader provide unfair career advantages. It's better to be a mid-level contributor at a winner than the top person at a tier-two company.
