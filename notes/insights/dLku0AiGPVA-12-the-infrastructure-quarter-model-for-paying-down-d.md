Dedicate specific, regular time periods—like a full quarter—solely to infrastructure and paying down [[concepts/technical-debt|technical debt]]. This allows the team to focus on building new value and shipping features during other periods without infrastructure concerns constantly interrupting flow. It creates a sustainable cycle of innovation and consolidation.
