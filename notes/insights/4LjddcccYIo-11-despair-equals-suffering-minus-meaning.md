If suffering is constant (as in Buddhist philosophy), then increasing meaning directly reduces despair. This equation highlights that finding purpose in difficult circumstances can transform your experience of hardship. It's a practical [[concepts/mindset|mindset]] tool for navigating challenging periods in work and life.
