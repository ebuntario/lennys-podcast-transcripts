For large organizations, the lack of robust [[concepts/design-systems|design systems]] was Figma's biggest adoption blocker. Instead of avoiding it, they leaned in by hosting meetups, creating advanced content, and building superior features for design systems. This transformed the blocker into the primary reason for [[concepts/enterprise-adoption|enterprise adoption]] and upgrading from pro to org tiers.
