The worst outcome for a startup isn't that people hate the product, but that they are completely indifferent to it. Most people are not [[concepts/early-adopters|early adopters]] and are too busy to try new things, so their lack of response is not a personal rejection. Founders must internalize that indifference is common and that they can always reach out again later after making improvements.
