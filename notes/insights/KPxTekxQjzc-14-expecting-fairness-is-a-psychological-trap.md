The expectation that life or business should be fair is a major source of psychological defeat. Accepting that unfairness is inherent allows you to focus on actionable responses rather than dwelling on perceived injustices. This [[concepts/resilience|resilient mindset]] enables forward progress despite inevitable inequities and setbacks.
