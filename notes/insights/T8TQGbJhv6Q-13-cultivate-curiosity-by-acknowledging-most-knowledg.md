A guiding motto for effective product work is "there is more knowledge outside my head than inside it." This fosters [[concepts/curiosity|curiosity]], humility, and active listening. Even if you know 90% of a topic, listening to others can provide the crucial remaining 10%. This mindset is essential for continuous learning and avoiding the trap of overconfidence in a fast-changing field.
