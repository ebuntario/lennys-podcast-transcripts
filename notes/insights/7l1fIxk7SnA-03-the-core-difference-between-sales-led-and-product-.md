In a sales-led funnel, interest is gauged by interactions with marketing campaigns (e.g., opening emails). In a [[concepts/product-led-funnel|product-led funnel]], product usage becomes the leading indicator of success. High usage can lead directly to self-serve checkout or create [[concepts/product-qualified-leads|product-qualified leads]] for sales outreach.
