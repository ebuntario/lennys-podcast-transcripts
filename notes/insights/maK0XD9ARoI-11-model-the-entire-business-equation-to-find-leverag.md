Building a comprehensive model that describes your entire business equation helps identify the highest leverage points for optimization. This approach moves beyond optimizing individual funnel metrics to understanding how changes affect the overall [[concepts/business-model-math|business model math]]. Product leaders who understand this higher-order thinking can make more strategic decisions about where to focus their efforts.
