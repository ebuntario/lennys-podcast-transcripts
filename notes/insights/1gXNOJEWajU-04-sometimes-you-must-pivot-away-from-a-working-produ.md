Even when a product has passionate users and good metrics, it might not fulfill the company's larger mission. Anchor moved from a successful voice-messaging app to a podcast creation platform because the original product, while loved, wouldn't achieve their goal of democratizing audio. This requires the courage to [[concepts/kill-your-darlings|kill your darlings]] and build for the 80% of potential users, not just the vocal 20%.
