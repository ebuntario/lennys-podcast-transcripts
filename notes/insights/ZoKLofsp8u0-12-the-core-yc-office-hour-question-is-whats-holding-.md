In one-on-one sessions, YC partners avoid status updates and strategy debates. Instead, they ask founders to identify the single biggest bottleneck preventing faster progress. This question forces [[concepts/startup-prioritization|prioritization]] and often leads to discovering the real obstacle through probing, as founders frequently don't know what's actually slowing them down.
