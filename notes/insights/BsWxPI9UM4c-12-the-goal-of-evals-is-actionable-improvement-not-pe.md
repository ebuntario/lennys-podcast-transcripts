Don't get paralyzed trying to create a perfect [[concepts/ai-evaluation|eval]] system. The purpose is to find ways to actionably improve your product. Even a basic process of sampling traces and fixing obvious issues will yield significant gains. Iterate and refine your evals over time as you learn more about your application's behavior.
