Momentum in a startup is reflexive: positive momentum (shipping, growth) generates more energy and motivation, while stagnation can demoralize a team. Cultivating this [[concepts/reflexive-momentum|positive flywheel]] is one of the most important tasks in an early-stage company, as it affects morale, productivity, and external perception.
