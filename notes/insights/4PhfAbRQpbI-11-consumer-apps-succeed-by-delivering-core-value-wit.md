In 2024, attention spans are approximately three seconds. You must invert the [[concepts/time-to-value|time to value]] so the user experiences the aha moment almost immediately upon opening the app. This requires ruthless prioritization, creative use of platform APIs, and designing every step of onboarding to eliminate friction and deliver core value instantly.
