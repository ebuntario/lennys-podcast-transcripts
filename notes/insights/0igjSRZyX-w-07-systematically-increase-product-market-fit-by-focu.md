Measure [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] by asking users how disappointed they'd be if they could no longer use your product. To increase this score, focus on the "somewhat disappointed" segment for whom your core benefit resonates. Systematically address their objections while doubling down on what your most passionate users love. This creates an algorithmically-driven roadmap guaranteed to improve fit.
