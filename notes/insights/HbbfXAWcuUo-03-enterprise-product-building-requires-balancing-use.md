Building for the enterprise involves simultaneously solving for two use cases: making features work well and delight users, while ensuring security, safety, and auditability. The trap is either disregarding governance or overly crippling the [[concepts/customer-experience|user experience]] in its name. Success requires developing a nuanced playbook that balances both sides effectively.
