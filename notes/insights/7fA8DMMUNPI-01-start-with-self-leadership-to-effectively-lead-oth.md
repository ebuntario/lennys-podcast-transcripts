Effective leadership begins from the inside out, not the other way around. You must first lead yourself by connecting with your own [[concepts/personal-purpose|purpose]] and mission before you can authentically lead others. This internal foundation provides sustainable fuel, whereas relying solely on external validation creates a fragile leadership shell.
