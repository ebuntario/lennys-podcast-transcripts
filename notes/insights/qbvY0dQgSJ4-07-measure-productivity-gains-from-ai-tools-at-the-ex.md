Individual managers often prefer an extra headcount over expensive AI tool subscriptions, as headcount increases their team size. Executives, focused on broader [[concepts/business-metrics|business metrics]], are more likely to see the value in AI tools that drive productivity, highlighting a disconnect in how productivity gains are measured and valued.
