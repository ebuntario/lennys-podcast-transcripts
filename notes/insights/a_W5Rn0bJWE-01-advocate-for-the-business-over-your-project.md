In a healthy organization, advocating for what is best for the overall business, even if it means your own project gets deprioritized or canceled, is a powerful [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] move. This demonstrates strategic thinking and can build trust with leadership, often leading to better opportunities. If the culture punishes this behavior, it's a clear signal about the organization's health.
