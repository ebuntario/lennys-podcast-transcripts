Revolut has three product owner specializations (UX, technical, and data science) that share 85-90% core competencies. The [[concepts/specialization-overlap|specialization represents only 5-15%]] of the role, focusing on domain expertise while maintaining common skills in problem-solving, customer empathy, business acumen, and technical understanding.
