Rigorously analyze whether the effort to measure and optimize a tiny product detail outweighs its potential impact. Investing thousands of person-hours in A/B testing, instrumentation, and analysis for a feature with minuscule expected utility is a "guaranteed loser." Focus energy on changes that can meaningfully move the needle on user value.
