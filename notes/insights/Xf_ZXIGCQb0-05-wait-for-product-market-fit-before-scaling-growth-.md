Avoid investing heavily in growth before achieving [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]]. Growing a product that doesn't satisfy a core need can do more harm than good, as users who have a poor experience are unlikely to return. If you need user feedback at scale pre-fit, consider targeting smaller, off-the-grid markets to contain growth while gathering the necessary signal.
