Key signals that a team may need to split include meetings taking longer, difficulty making decisions, and divergent work streams where members are no longer engaged in each other's updates. When a team grows too large, facilitation and [[concepts/team-communication|communication]] break down, indicating it's time to consider a grow-and-split pattern for greater effectiveness.
