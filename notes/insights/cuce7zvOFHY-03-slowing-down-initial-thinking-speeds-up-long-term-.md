Investing time in foundational thinking and hypothesis formation prevents teams from building quickly in the wrong direction. While AI tools enable rapid prototyping, rushing to build can lead to generic, undifferentiated products. A deliberate [[concepts/scenario-planning|strategic planning]] phase actually accelerates the path to product-market fit by ensuring subsequent experiments test a well-considered premise.
