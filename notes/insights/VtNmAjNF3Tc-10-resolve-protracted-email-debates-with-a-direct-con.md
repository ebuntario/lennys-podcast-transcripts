Implement a simple rule to improve team efficiency: if an email thread reaches a certain number of exchanges (e.g., 7 or 10) without resolution, mandate a direct conversation. Long, meandering email chains that add more people are a waste of time. Getting on a call or in a room forces faster [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]] and clearer communication.
