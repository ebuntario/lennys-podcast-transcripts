Before investing in sales tools, manually find 30 people you'd be excited to learn from and spend 15-20 minutes crafting a thoughtful note to each. This exercise forces you to identify common parameters (like team size, industry, role tenure) and test messaging, providing crucial learning before scaling with automation tools.
