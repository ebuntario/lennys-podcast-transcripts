Your first marketing hire for a technical product should be a practitioner from your target audience, not a traditional marketer. Figma's first hire after launch was a [[concepts/designer-advocates|designer advocate]] who gut-checked messaging, gathered feedback, and built authentic relationships. This role acts as "magic dust" for [[concepts/go-to-market|go-to-market strategy]], ensuring credibility and user-centric communication.
