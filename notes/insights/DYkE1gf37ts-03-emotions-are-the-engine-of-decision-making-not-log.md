Neurologically, we make decisions in the emotional centers of our brain; logic is used to predict how we will feel. Therefore, falling in love with all your emotions unlocks new solution sets and better [[decision-making|decision-making]], as you're no longer avoiding risks or truths to sidestep uncomfortable feelings.
