Effective business storytelling starts with a hook (mystery, secret, or surprising conclusion), delivers the core message in the middle, and ends with success and celebration. This structure leverages how human brains naturally process and retain information. Asking "What could make this story better?" after presentations develops [[concepts/communication-skills|communication skills]] more effectively than theoretical frameworks alone.
