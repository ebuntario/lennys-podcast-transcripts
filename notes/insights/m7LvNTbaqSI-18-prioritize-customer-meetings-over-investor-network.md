Founders who genuinely care about their product will naturally prioritize [[concepts/customer-development|customer development]] over investor relations. Spending excessive time networking with investors typically yields less value than direct customer engagement in the earliest stages. Intuitive time allocation toward product obsession and user conversations generally leads to better startup outcomes than polished investor pitches.
