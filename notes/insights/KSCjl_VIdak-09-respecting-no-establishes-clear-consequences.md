When someone says "no" to a request or expectation, genuinely accepting their refusal allows natural consequences to follow. This is not punitive, but respectful; it treats them as an adult capable of handling the outcomes of their choices. This approach reinforces [[concepts/accountability|healthy boundaries and accountability]].
