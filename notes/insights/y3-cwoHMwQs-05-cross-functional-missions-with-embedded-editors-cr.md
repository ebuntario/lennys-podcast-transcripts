Embedding editors within product teams creates a powerful fusion of editorial expertise and product thinking. This structure allows organizations to scale editorial judgment through algorithms trained on expert signals rather than just engagement metrics. The [[concepts/editorial-product-integration|editorial-product integration]] enables unique storytelling formats that wouldn't emerge from purely data-driven approaches.
