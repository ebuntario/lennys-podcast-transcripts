A leader's primary job in a changing environment is not to have all the answers but to be skilled at finding them. This requires embracing "I don't know" and convening the right people to explore solutions. The most experienced leaders are comfortable with uncertainty and focus on [[concepts/creative-problem-solving|problem-solving]] processes rather than projecting omniscience, which is both unrealistic and unhelpful during rapid scale.
