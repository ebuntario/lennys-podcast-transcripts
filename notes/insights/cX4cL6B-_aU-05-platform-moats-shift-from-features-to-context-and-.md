In AI-driven platforms, the [[concepts/platform-moats|primary moat]] is shifting from raw model capabilities to [[concepts/ai-context|context and memory]]. The platform that accumulates the most personalized user context creates a feedback loop where better outputs drive more usage, which in turn gathers more context, creating a powerful defensible position.
