A common pitfall is becoming obsessed with the complexities of AI solutions and forgetting the core problem you're trying to solve. A [[concepts/problem-first-approach|problem-first approach]] forces you to deconstruct workflows and identify which parts are truly ripe for AI augmentation, ensuring you build for user needs rather than technological novelty.
