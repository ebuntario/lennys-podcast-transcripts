A growing trend sees senior leaders moving from single-company roles to portfolio careers involving advising, fractional leadership, content creation, and investing. This is driven by a desire for flexibility, the ability to apply [[concepts/specific-knowledge|specific knowledge]] across multiple companies, and the high demand for proven operators. It represents a shift from concentrated equity risk to a diversified impact model.
