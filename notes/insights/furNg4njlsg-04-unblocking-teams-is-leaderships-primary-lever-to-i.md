Product teams inherently want to move fast; slowdowns usually stem from organizational or technical roadblocks. Leadership's core role is to create systems where teams can instantly flag obstacles and get them resolved quickly. Removing these [[concepts/team-blockers|blockers]] starts a virtuous cycle of wins, building organizational courage and competency for faster delivery.
