Historical patterns show interfaces moving from graphical to programmatic, as seen with databases moving to SQL and cloud to Terraform. [[concepts/code-native-interface|Code-native interfaces]] better compose with LLMs and enable infinite scale for agent collaboration. Product makers must rewire their mindset from pixel-perfect UI to how systems and agents will read, compose, and interact with their product.
