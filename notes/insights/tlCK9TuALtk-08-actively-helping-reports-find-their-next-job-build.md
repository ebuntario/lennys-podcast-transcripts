Committing to help team members find their next role—even outside the company—fosters radical transparency, reduces surprise departures, and builds a powerful long-term reputation as a manager who puts people first. This investment in [[concepts/talent-development|career development]] often leads to boomerang employees and positive blind references that attract future talent.
