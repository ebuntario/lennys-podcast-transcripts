Founders often struggle with delegation due to a desire for control and high standards, but this becomes a bottleneck to scaling. Learning to [[concepts/psychological-safety|trust the team]] and delegate fully is essential, even if it means accepting that some outputs won't match personal perfection, as it's the only way to scale the company effectively.
