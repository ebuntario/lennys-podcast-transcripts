The most durable products combine [[concepts/network-effects|network effects]] with genuine core product differentiation. Twitter's longevity stems from this combination: its unique product (public, real-time, text-based feed) created differentiation, while network effects made it increasingly valuable as more people joined. This dual engine creates significant staying power that neither element alone can provide.
