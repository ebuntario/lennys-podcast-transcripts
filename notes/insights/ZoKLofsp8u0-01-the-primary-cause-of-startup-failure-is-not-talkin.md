If a startup doesn't talk to users, it won't find [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]], and nothing else matters. The most common mistake founders make is avoiding customer conversations due to fear of rejection. Y Combinator's core principle, "make things people want," remains eternally true and hinges on this direct user engagement.
