Simplify marketing by viewing it as needing both [[concepts/marketing-framework|fuel and an engine]]. Fuel is the valuable content, copy, and design you create, while the engine is the system for distributing that fuel to the right people and tracking its performance. The key question for growth is identifying whether your biggest constraint or opportunity lies in creating more fuel or improving your distribution engine.
