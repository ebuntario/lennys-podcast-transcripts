When giving constructive feedback, focus solely on motivating the desired behavior change. Trim 90% of the venting or "justice-seeking" language that doesn't serve that goal. Vent elsewhere first, then enter the conversation calm and focused on the [[concepts/outcome-orientation|future outcome]].
