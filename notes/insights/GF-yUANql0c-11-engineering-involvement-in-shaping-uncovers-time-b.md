Including a senior engineer in shaping sessions acts like a "grumpy plumber" who insists on checking the pipes. They can quickly spot hidden complexities—like discovering an onboarding flow has three different code branches—before the project starts. This early discovery allows for informed trade-offs and realistic scoping, preventing mid-project surprises.
