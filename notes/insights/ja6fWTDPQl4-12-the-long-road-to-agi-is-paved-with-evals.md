The path to advanced AI is longer than some predict and will be driven not by 10x more pre-training data but by thoughtful, expert-created [[concepts/ai-evaluation|evals]] and post-training datasets. Automating a majority of knowledge work is likely within a decade, but achieving superintelligence requires solving the eval problem for every domain humans currently outperform AI.
