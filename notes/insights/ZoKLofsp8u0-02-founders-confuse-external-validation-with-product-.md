Founders often mistake acceptance into programs like YC or investment from angels as signs they have achieved [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]]. This external validation is misleading, as true fit comes only from users wanting and using the product. It's critical to remind early-stage teams that they likely do not have product-market fit and must focus on direct customer learning.
