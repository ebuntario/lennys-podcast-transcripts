A single founder-type engineer with a compelling idea, paired with the right design and product support, can create more impact than large teams. These technical leaders who operate at the intersection of vision and execution are 10x more valuable than additional headcount. Organizations should identify and empower these [[concepts/technical-leadership|visionary builders]] rather than assuming scaling requires more people.
