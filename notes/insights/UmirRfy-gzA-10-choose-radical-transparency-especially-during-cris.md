When trust is at risk, don't hide behind the corporate brand. After announcing their acquisition, Figma held a public Twitter Space with founders and advocates to answer tough questions directly. This [[concepts/crisis-communication|crisis communication]] tactic of [[concepts/brand-transparency|brand transparency]] helps maintain community connection and gives users a chance to be heard, which can begin to rebuild trust.
