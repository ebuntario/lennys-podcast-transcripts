The disciplines within product development are becoming more interdisciplinary. Product managers are expected to be more technical, designers more business-oriented, and engineers more user-focused. This convergence fosters deeper [[concepts/collaboration|collaboration]] and enables great ideas to come from anywhere, moving teams closer to a model of true shared ownership.
