Use a simple, low-maintenance system like a shared slide deck where teams post screenshots or prototypes of work-in-progress. This visual [[concepts/communication|communication]] increases awareness across the organization, prevents redundant or colliding projects, and fosters serendipitous collaboration, saving time and improving outcomes.
