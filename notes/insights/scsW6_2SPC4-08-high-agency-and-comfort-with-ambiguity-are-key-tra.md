Successful product managers in dynamic AI environments don't wait for permission; they see problems and act. They must be extremely comfortable with ambiguity, as problems are often ill-formed and solutions are discovered through execution. A key part of the role is knowing when to be [[concepts/leadership-traits|decisive]] and make a call to unblock progress, even without perfect information.
