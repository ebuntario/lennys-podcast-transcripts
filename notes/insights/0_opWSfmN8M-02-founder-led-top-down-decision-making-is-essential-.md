Greatness is created when a [[concepts/founder-leadership|founder-led CEO]] makes brave, hard decisions unilaterally and owns the results. During a major pivot, democratic or committee-based decision-making often fails to create necessary momentum. A strong, top-down approach is required to cut through organizational inertia and execute a high-stakes strategic shift.
