Many people carry outdated [[concepts/mental-models|mental models]] about what AI can do based on experiences from months ago, creating an "arbitrage opportunity" for those who update their expectations. Since AI capabilities have advanced dramatically in compressed timeframes, consciously demanding more from current tools can unlock capabilities you might otherwise overlook.
