Strategy often exists only in people's heads, leading to misalignment. A powerful exercise is to write concise memos (1-2 pages) at each level—executive, product leadership, and team—that articulate the vision, priorities, and context. This written [[concepts/communication|communication]] creates a shared understanding and a clear line of sight from tactics to goals.
