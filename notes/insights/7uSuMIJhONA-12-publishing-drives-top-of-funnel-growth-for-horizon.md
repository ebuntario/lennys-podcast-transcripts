For horizontal tools, many users are "problem solvers" who start with a need, not a blank canvas. A publishing loop lets users share solutions publicly, attracting others with the same problem. This turns your product into a hybrid between a content platform and an app store, driving discovery and [[concepts/user-acquisition|acquisition]] through utility, not just features.
