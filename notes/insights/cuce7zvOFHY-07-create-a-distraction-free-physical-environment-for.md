Radically alter your environment to support [[concepts/deep-work|deep work]]. This can mean canceling the internet in your office, using a timer to shut off your router, or keeping the TV/projector out of sight so using it requires deliberate setup. When distractions are physically harder to access than your highlight, you naturally gravitate toward the meaningful work.
