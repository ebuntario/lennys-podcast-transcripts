After receiving advice, close the [[concepts/curiosity-loop|curiosity loop]] by processing the information and thanking the contributors. Sharing how their input affected your decision makes people feel heard and valued, reinforcing the helper's high. This transforms a one-sided ask into a mutually beneficial exchange and strengthens professional relationships.
