While customer interviews are ideal, well-designed [[concepts/surveys|surveys]] targeting your best recent customers can yield clear, actionable patterns in just a few weeks. This approach can prevent the common pitfall of lengthy research leading to [[concepts/decision-making|analysis paralysis]], allowing teams to move forward decisively with new messaging and strategy.
