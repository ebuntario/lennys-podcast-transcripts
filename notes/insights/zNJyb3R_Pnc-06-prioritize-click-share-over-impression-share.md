Instead of obsessing over being at the top of the page (impression share), focus on showing your ad to the right person, measured by click share. Optimizing for [[concepts/ad-relevance|ad relevance]] and user intent ensures your budget is spent efficiently on users likely to engage, which platforms will then reward with better placement.
