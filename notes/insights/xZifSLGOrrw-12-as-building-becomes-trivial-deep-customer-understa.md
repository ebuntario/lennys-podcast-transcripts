When AI makes building software dramatically faster and easier, the competitive advantage shifts from execution speed to deep, intimate understanding of a specific customer's problems. A founder with a strong network and insight into an underserved customer segment is better positioned than a great builder with no specific customer in mind. This trend is bullish for [[concepts/vertical-software|vertical AI startups]] that solve deep, workflow-specific problems.
