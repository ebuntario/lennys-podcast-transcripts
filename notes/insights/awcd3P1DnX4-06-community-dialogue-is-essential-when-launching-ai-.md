Launching an AI product trained on public code requires extensive dialogue with the developer community to address skepticism and ethical concerns. This involves transparent conversations about model training, security, and the tool's intended role as an augmenter, not a replacer. This [[concepts/community-dialogue|community dialogue]] often requires scaling the product management function more than engineering in the early stages.
