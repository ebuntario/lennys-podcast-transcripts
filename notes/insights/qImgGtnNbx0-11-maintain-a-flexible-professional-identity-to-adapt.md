To be a great founder or leader, avoid an ossified view of your professional identity (e.g., "I am an engineer"). Instead, cultivate a flexible identity as a "builder" and transform into what the company needs at each stage—whether that involves selling, recruiting, designing, or engineering. This [[concepts/career-adaptability|adaptability]] is essential because building a significant company requires wearing many different hats effectively.
