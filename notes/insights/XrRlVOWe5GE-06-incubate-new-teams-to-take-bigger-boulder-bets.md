Avoid the trap of constant incrementalism by strategically incubating new, focused teams to explore emerging opportunity areas. Give these teams space to run with a "learn-fast" mandate, freeing them from normal quarterly planning cycles. This approach, used for areas like AI and native audiovisual features, allows for rapid prototyping and learning before a new capability is integrated into the core [[concepts/product-roadmap|product roadmap]].
