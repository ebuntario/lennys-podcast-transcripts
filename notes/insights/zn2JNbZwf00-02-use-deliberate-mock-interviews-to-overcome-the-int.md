Interviewing is a critical skill for career advancement, yet companies rarely give feedback on performance. To improve, conduct dozens of [[concepts/deliberate-practice|mock interviews]] with peers or mentors, especially those skilled at interviewing. Practice until you can perform well even under high-stress conditions, as this is often the barrier to landing roles at top companies.
