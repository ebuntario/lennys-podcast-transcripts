Deep [[concepts/relationship-building|relationship building]] with engineering managers and other partners involves knowing their personal motivations, birthday, and career aspirations. Understanding the human behind the role transforms a transactional work relationship into a genuine partnership. This connection is crucial for navigating challenges and having fun while doing hard work together.
