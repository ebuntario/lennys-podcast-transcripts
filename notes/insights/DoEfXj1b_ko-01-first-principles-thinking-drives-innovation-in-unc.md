Hiring smart people with no prior experience in a domain can lead to significant innovation, as they approach problems without preconceived frameworks. At Dropbox, hiring individuals who had never done sales for sales roles fostered unconventional [[concepts/go-to-market|go-to-market]] motions. This approach leverages [[concepts/first-principles-thinking|first principles thinking]], where people start from "I don't know anything" and build their own mental models based on fresh context.
