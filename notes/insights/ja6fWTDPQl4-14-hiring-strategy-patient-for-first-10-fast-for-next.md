A nuanced [[concepts/hiring-strategy|hiring strategy]] involves extreme patience and high standards for the first ~10 hires to establish exceptional [[concepts/talent-density|talent density]]. Once product-market fit is proven and demand outstrips capacity, the focus should shift to speed when scaling from 10 to 100 people. Moving too slowly at this growth stage can limit potential velocity.
