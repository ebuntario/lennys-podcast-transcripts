The transition from synthetic data to expert-annotated real interactions can dramatically improve model performance. In healthcare applications, annotating hundreds of thousands of patient-physician interactions boosted acceptance rates from 30-60% to 83%. This creates a [[concepts/data-advantage|data advantage]] that becomes core IP, as models tuned on proprietary behavioral data deliver superior outcomes.
