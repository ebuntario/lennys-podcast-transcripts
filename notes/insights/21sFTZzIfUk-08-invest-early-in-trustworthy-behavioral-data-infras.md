Growth teams cannot function without high-quality, trustworthy behavioral data. Invest early in the infrastructure, tooling, and processes for collecting intentional data, such as event tracking plans and schema conformance testing in CI/CD pipelines. Having confidence in your data is paramount, as it will inform critical decisions around retention, activation metrics, and [[concepts/experimentation-platform|experimentation]]. Starting early is key because it takes time to accrue enough data to run meaningful analyses and inform your growth model.
