Customer and sales escalations for unplanned features should be evaluated against a clear product strategy rather than immediately accommodated. A [[concepts/strategy|strategic framework]] provides rigor for determining whether escalations align with core objectives or represent distractions. This prevents reactive roadmap changes that accumulate technical and product debt.
