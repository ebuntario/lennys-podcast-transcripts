Beginning a new venture as an "experiment" rather than a "startup" reduces pressure and shifts the primary goal from success to learning. This [[concepts/risk-mindset|experimental mindset]] encourages curiosity, tinkering, and adaptation based on user feedback, which is a more sustainable and insightful way to explore an idea's potential.
