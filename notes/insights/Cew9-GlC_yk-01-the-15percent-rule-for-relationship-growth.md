To deepen a relationship, disclose about 15% more than feels comfortable—this small step expands your [[concepts/comfort-zone|comfort zone]] without causing alarm. This incremental [[concepts/vulnerability|vulnerability]] invites reciprocity and builds trust gradually, creating a new baseline for connection. The key is to experiment and observe the other person's response, adjusting your openness accordingly.
