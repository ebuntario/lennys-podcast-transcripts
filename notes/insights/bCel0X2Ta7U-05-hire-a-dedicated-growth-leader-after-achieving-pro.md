The time to build a growth team is when you have evidence of [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]], even if it's not perfect. The first person to lead growth should be someone with deep trust within the company and a strong understanding of the customer, as they will need to invent the specific tactics that work for your unique product and audience.
