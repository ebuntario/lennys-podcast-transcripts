As product leaders become more senior, the "art" of the job—communication, collaboration, and people skills—becomes more critical than the "science" of frameworks and tactics. Investing in skills like storytelling and having difficult conversations is as important as technical product analysis. Senior challenges are predominantly about people, persuasion, and creating environments where teams can thrive.
