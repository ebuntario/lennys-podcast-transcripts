When data indicates failure, leaders must pivot from data obsession to belief obsession. Founders like Brian Chesky used storytelling to paint a vivid, believable future that re-energized teams. This involves connecting employees to the core mission and demonstrating unwavering frontline commitment, which is more effective than compensation alone in retaining talent during downturns.
