AI tools like ChatGPT can enhance a PM's work by improving outputs like mission statements and user personas. They help generate clearer, more universally understandable language and uncover [[concepts/segmentation|user segments]] and motivations you might not have considered. The goal is to augment your thinking, not automate your job away.
