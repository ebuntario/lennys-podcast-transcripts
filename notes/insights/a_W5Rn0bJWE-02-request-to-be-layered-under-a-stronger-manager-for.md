If you're struggling in a role, proactively requesting to report to a different manager who can provide better support is an unconventional but effective [[concepts/professional-growth|career advancement]] tactic. This requires self-awareness and confidence that the change will lead to better work and results for the team. In a healthy culture, leadership will respect this honest assessment of what's needed for success.
