The [[concepts/pathless-path|pathless path]] is a shift from seeing uncertainty as a problem to be solved toward embracing discomfort and not knowing what comes next. It involves a personal journey from operating from a place of scarcity to having faith in abundance and possibility. This narrative allows people to thrive outside predefined tracks by accepting that reinvention is a constant part of modern work life.
