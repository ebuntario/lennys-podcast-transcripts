When building a new [[concepts/category-creation|category]], validation from analysts and directory sites is crucial for establishing credibility. Simultaneously, extensive thought leadership content is needed to educate buyers about the category's unique value propositions and the pain points it solves. This dual approach helps create budget allocation for a previously unrecognized need.
