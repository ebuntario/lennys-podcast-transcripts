Founders and innovators often fall in love with their solution, but legendary category designers stay obsessed with the problem. Spend more time understanding and articulating the problem from the customer's perspective than perfecting your product. This deep focus on the problem allows you to reframe it in a novel way, which is the foundation for creating a new category that customers will care about and adopt.
