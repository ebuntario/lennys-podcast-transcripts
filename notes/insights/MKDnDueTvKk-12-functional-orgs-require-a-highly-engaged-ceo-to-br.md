A functional organizational model (with separate heads for product, engineering, design) can lead to consensus-driven deadlock or conflict if the CEO or GM is not deeply engaged. This structure requires a leader who is willing to lean in, [[concepts/tiebreak-decisions|tiebreak decisions]], and resolve conflicts swiftly to maintain momentum and clear direction.
