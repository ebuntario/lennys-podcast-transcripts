Explore industries you are passionate about, then "sniff around" within them to find the most profitable and defensible niches. For example, a love for film led to investing in the social network Letterboxd, and being a former barista led to buying the AeroPress company. Your passion provides the domain knowledge needed to identify [[concepts/competitive-advantage|unfair advantages]] and sustainable business models others might overlook.
