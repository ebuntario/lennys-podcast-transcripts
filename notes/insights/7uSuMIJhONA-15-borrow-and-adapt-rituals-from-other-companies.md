Studying how other companies run meetings, make decisions, or onboard people is a cheap form of R&D for improving your own operations. For example, adopting Coinbase's "rapids" role clarification improved Coda's decision-making rituals. Be open to integrating and tweaking external [[concepts/team-rituals|rituals]] to solve specific internal challenges.
