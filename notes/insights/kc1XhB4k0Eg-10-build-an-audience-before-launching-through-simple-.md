Start collecting interested contacts long before launching through a simple "friends newsletter" sharing life updates or interesting findings. This builds an [[concepts/audience-engagement|audience foundation]] of people who already know you, creating immediate listeners upon launch. Even irregular, casual updates maintain connections that can be activated when you have substantive content to share.
