Creating agents trained on a company's unique internal knowledge—like trust vectors, growth loops, or member research—yields far better results than generic AI. These [[concepts/domain-specific-agents|domain-specific agents]] can critique ideas and provide insights grounded in institutional history. The future involves orchestrating these specialized agents to work together seamlessly.
