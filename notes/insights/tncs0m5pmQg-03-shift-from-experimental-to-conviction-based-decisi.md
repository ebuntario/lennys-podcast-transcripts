At a startup, you often lack the user base for statistically significant [[concepts/testing|experimentation]]. The mindset should shift from an experimental approach to a conviction-oriented one. The goal is to gather enough data to have "informed conviction," make a decision, execute, and then validate whether it was correct, rather than getting stuck in analysis paralysis.
