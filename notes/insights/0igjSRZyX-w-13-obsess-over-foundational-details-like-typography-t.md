For core interaction elements, go beyond standard options. Selecting a font involves evaluating aesthetics, emotional tone, reading speed, and edge cases (like how email addresses render). This level of [[concepts/attention-to-detail|attention to detail]] in foundational choices creates a cohesive, high-quality experience that feels intentionally crafted and supports the product's premium positioning.
