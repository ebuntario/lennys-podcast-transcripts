Many companies provide mental health benefits that cover therapy sessions, counseling, or digital wellness platforms, but employees often overlook these offerings. Review your healthcare plan or employee assistance program to access subsidized or free [[concepts/mental-health-resources|mental health resources]]. Utilizing these benefits reduces financial barriers to seeking help and normalizes proactive mental care as part of overall well-being.
