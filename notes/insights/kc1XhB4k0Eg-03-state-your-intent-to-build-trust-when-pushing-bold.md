When advocating for ambitious or controversial ideas, explicitly state your intent to prevent others from assuming self-interest. A simple preface like "I only care about the company's success, and I believe this idea will achieve that" disarms skepticism. This [[concepts/team-communication|communication]] tactic builds trust and makes colleagues more receptive to bold proposals.
