PhD-level experts expect a different experience than low-cost generalist labor. Building a community with cohort-based training, clear project pathways, and respectful treatment increases retention and data quality. This approach contrasts with transactional platforms that treat experts like commodity labor, leading to high drop-off rates and lower lifetime value per contributor.
