An authentic [[concepts/brand-building|brand]] emerges when visual aesthetics, voice, and customer interactions are deeply intertwined with the company's core values. When values like candor or agility are embedded in the brand system, every customer touchpoint naturally reflects the company's DNA without requiring extensive training.
