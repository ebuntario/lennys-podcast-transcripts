A key to user adoption is creating a product that delivers value with minimal effort and without disrupting existing habits. The ideal [[concepts/product-design|product design]] is one you install and it immediately makes you better across all your applications, requiring no configuration or change in behavior. This low-friction, high-value experience is a powerful driver of sustained usage.
