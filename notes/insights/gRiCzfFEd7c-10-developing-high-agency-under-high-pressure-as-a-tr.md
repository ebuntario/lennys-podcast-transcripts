Working in a fast-paced, unpredictable environment like news develops a unique skill: high agency under high pressure. This combination of decisive action and adaptability in the face of chaos is a transferable [[concepts/leadership-asset|leadership asset]] for any product development team. It enables rapid hypothesis testing and risk reduction when time is critical.
