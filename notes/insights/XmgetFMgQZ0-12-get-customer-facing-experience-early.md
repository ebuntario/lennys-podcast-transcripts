Seek roles that put you directly in front of customers early in your career, as you'll be serving customers your entire professional life. Handling diverse customer scenarios builds deep empathy and sharpens your intuition about what they truly need. This foundational [[concepts/empathy|customer understanding]] becomes a superpower in any product role.
