Bringing engineers directly into customer meetings has a transformative impact on [[concepts/product-development|product development]] by creating firsthand understanding of user pain points and delight. This practice increases engineering team confidence in customer conversations and gives their feedback greater weight in development decisions. The resulting alignment between engineering insights and customer needs leads to more effective prioritization and product decisions.
