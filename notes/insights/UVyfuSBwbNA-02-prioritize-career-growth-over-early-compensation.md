Do not optimize for [[concepts/compensation|compensation]] early in your career. If you are on a track to become an executive or founder, you will make the vast majority of your earnings in the later years. Focus instead on roles that offer high learning [[concepts/professional-growth|compounding]], align with your strengths, and are enjoyable, as this creates a virtuous cycle for long-term success.
