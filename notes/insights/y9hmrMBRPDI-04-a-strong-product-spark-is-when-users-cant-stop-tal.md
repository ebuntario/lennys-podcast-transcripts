A key sign of [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] is when users talk about the product constantly and proactively share it with teammates and others at different companies. This top-of-mind enthusiasm and desire to evangelize creates a powerful organic growth engine, as your most passionate users become your best distribution channel.
