Optimizing [[concepts/user-onboarding|onboarding]] primarily impacts [[concepts/retention|retention]] by facilitating early [[concepts/habit-formation|habit formation]] with the product. Since churn is most likely during initial product usage, improving onboarding can shift retention curves outward by 10-20 percentage points. Users are most motivated during onboarding, making it the ideal time to connect them with core product value.
