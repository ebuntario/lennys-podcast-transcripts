The most valuable team members are "disagreeable givers"—people motivated by what's best for the company who aren't afraid to push back or deliver tough feedback. This contrasts with "agreeable takers," who are pleasant but self-interested. Cultivating a culture of [[concepts/radical-candor|radical candor]] where direct, honest feedback is expected helps maintain an accurate feedback loop, especially for leadership.
