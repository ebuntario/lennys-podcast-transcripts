You can make product decisions based on small sample sizes because you're in the business of changing behavior, not discovering universal truths. One customer interview provides more evidence than the zero data typically used for business decisions. Continuous feedback loops and subsequent testing provide validation, making [[concepts/small-data|small data]] a practical starting point.
