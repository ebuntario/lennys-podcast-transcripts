The AI training data market has shifted from a crowdsourcing problem—finding low-skilled people for basic tasks—to a sourcing and vetting problem for high-caliber experts. Labs now need experienced professionals like software engineers, lawyers, and doctors to evaluate and interpret complex capabilities. This creates a new, high-value category of [[concepts/labor-marketplaces|labor marketplaces]] focused on quality over volume.
