As AI handles more execution tasks, human value shifts to generating and filtering novel ideas. [[concepts/creative-thinking|Creative thinking]] and listening to user needs become differentiators, since general models may not replace deep user understanding. The abundance of possible ideas means success depends on connecting dots across fields to invent new interaction paradigms.
