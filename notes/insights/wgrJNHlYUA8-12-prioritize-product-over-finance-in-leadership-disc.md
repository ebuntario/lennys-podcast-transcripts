Canva's board meetings dedicate most of the time to product updates, with financials condensed to a single slide. This reflects a core belief that [[concepts/product-obsession|product excellence]] is the primary driver of long-term company success. This focus is sustainable when financial performance is strong and investors are aligned with the product-led vision.
