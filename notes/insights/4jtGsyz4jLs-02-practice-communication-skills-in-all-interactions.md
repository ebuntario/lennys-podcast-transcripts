Treat every stakeholder interaction as important practice for executive communication. You won't get enough reps if you only try to shine during formal presentations to senior leaders. By applying [[concepts/deliberate-practice|deliberate practice]] in daily conversations, you build the muscle memory needed for high-stakes situations.
