Common indicators of excessive friction include constantly breaking builds, flaky tests, overly long processes, and high switching costs between projects. These [[concepts/developer-experience|developer experience]] smells signal there is significant [[concepts/process-improvement|low-hanging fruit]] for improving team velocity.
