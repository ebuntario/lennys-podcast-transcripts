Consistently pushing to ship a product or feature even one week earlier creates a compounding advantage. Starting the next initiative sooner builds a lead that grows over time, making it difficult for competitors to catch up. Founders who maintain this [[concepts/urgency|sense of urgency]] prevent complacency after big wins.
