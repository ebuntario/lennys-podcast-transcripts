Be highly skeptical of entering [[concepts/business-model|business models]] where many others have repeatedly failed, thinking you can do it better. Industries like local news, bars, or pizzerias often have fundamental structural challenges that even brilliant management can't overcome. If there are "dead bodies" in a space, there is likely a hidden, difficult problem that keeps killing ventures.
