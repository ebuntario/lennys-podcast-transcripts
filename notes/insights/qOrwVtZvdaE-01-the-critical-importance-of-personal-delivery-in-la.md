The biggest marker between a botched layoff and a successful one is whether an employee hears the news from their manager in a [[concepts/one-on-one-meetings|one-on-one]]. If they hear it via email, group chat, or alongside others, it feels dehumanizing and often leads to public anger. A personal, private conversation allows the individual to express their emotions and preserves dignity, which is crucial for a humane process.
