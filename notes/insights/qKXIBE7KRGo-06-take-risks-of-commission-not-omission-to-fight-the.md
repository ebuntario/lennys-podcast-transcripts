For a startup, the enemy is the status quo. Minimizing risk by doing nothing allows the status quo to win by default. It is better to make mistakes of commission—where you act, observe, learn, and adapt quickly—than mistakes of omission, where you let opportunities slip by unnoticed. This [[concepts/risk-taking|proactive risk-taking]] is an investment in long-term growth over short-term safety.
