Offsites are powerful because they yank people out of their daily routine, activating new ways of thinking and creating a shared group experience. The act of collectively brainstorming and working through problems in a dedicated space cements [[concepts/alignment|alignment]] and plans more effectively than a leader announcing a pre-baked strategy. It is an irreplaceable tool for [[concepts/team-building|team building]] and forging a common belief system.
