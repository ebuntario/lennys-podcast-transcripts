Former product managers often excel in [[concepts/product-operations|product operations]] leadership roles because they intimately understand the pain points and inefficiencies they're trying to solve. This firsthand experience helps them prioritize efforts that will most effectively support product teams and drive customer outcomes. Their product background also builds credibility with both product managers and business stakeholders when advocating for process changes.
