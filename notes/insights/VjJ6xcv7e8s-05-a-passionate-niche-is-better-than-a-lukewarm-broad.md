When analyzing survey data, you may find a small group with intense loyalty (high "very disappointed" score) and a larger group with mild interest. Strategic preference should lean towards the intensely passionate niche. The biggest early competition is irrelevance, and being deeply relevant to a specific group provides a stronger foundation for [[concepts/product-strategy|long-term success]] than a lukewarm broad appeal.
