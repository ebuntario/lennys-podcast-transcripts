Journalists and audiences find problem-solving journeys more compelling than pure success stories. Share the specific challenges you faced and the creative, counterintuitive solutions you implemented. This [[concepts/storytelling|storytelling]] approach provides actionable insights for readers and makes your narrative more relatable and valuable than a simple victory lap.
