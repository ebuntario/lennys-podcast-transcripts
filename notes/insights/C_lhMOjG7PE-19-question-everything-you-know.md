Adopt the mindset that "everything you know is wrong" to maintain intellectual humility and curiosity. This [[concepts/first-principles-thinking|first-principles approach]] encourages experimentation and prevents assumptions from limiting innovation. By regularly testing even basic assumptions (like whether a Tesla can switch gears while moving), you discover possibilities that conventional wisdom overlooks.
