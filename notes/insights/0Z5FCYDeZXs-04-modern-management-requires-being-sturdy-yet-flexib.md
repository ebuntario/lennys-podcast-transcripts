In an era of accelerating change, a manager's key role is to manage uncertainty and fear while driving adaptation. The ideal mindset is to be like a willow tree: sturdy enough to withstand storms and provide stability, yet flexible enough to bend without breaking. This balance allows leaders to guide teams through [[concepts/change-management|continuous change]] without creating chaos.
