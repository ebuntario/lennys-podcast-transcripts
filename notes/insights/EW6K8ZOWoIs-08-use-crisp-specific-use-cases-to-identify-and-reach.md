When selling into complex organizations, a vague value proposition makes it hard for contacts to spend their social capital on your behalf. By defining a [[concepts/problem-framing|crisp, specific use case]], you make it easier for allies to know exactly which person in the organization has "their finger on the button." This precision accelerates the path to the real decision-maker.
