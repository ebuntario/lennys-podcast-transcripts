For any significant decision—hiring, relationships, career paths—ask yourself: "Knowing what I know today, would I make the same choice?" If the answer is no, change course immediately. This [[concepts/decision-making|decision framework]] applies a clear timeline for re-evaluation and prevents staying in suboptimal situations due to inertia.
