Great companies have a small set of named, templated rituals that every employee knows by their first Friday. These [[concepts/team-rituals|rituals]] act as a mirror of culture, making abstract values concrete through repeated practice. Examples include Dory/Pulse for meetings or Gusto's group offer calls, which signal what the organization prioritizes and how work gets done.
