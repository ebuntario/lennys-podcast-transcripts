In a world of infinite content, audiences rely on trusted curators—individuals or brands with a specific vibe—to filter and recommend what’s worth consuming. Aligning with these [[concepts/trusted-recommendations|curators]] can provide creators with access to a receptive audience. This dynamic mirrors the traditional roles of record labels or publishers in validating quality.
