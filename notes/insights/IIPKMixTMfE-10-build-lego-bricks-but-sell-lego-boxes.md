While your core value is creating modular, reusable components (Lego bricks), most users want complete, ready-made solutions (Lego boxes). For enterprise sales, you must package your bricks into specific solutions that sit on a customer's P&L. This requires shifting mindset without compromising the underlying [[concepts/modular-architecture|modular architecture]].
