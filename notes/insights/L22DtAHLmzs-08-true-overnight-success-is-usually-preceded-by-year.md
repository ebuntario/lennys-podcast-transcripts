Extreme, rapid growth after a launch often rests on a multi-year foundation of building core technology and team cohesion. The public narrative focuses on the explosive growth curve, but the [[concepts/founder-journey|real work]] was the years of persistence, failed experiments, and technological development that created the unique platform enabling that sudden success.
