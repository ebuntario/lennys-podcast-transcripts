Startups frequently claim [[concepts/scale-economies|scale economies]] derived from data, but this is a rare source of power. Often, the cost-per-unit advantage flattens out at a certain scale, meaning larger competitors are already in "shouting distance" of each other. The marginal benefit of additional data may not create a material, defensible cost advantage.
