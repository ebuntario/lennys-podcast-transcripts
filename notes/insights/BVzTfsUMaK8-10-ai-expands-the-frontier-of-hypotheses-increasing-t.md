Generative AI doesn't automate data science away; it explosively expands the number of possible explanations, hypotheses, and creative tests. This makes the human's role in the loop—funneling, prioritizing, and applying judgment—more critical than ever. The [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]] bottleneck shifts from generating options to discerning which ones are meaningful and actionable.
