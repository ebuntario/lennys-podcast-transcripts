After an R&D team hands off a proven concept, the new operational product team must feel full ownership of the roadmap. They have the closest feedback loop with customers and are responsible for the product's evolution. Outsourcing innovation solely to R&D stifles the product team's ability to iterate and respond to market needs effectively.
