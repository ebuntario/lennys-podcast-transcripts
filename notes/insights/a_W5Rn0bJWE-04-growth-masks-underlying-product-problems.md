High growth can conceal fundamental issues like poor liquidity in a marketplace or a high-friction user experience. When growth slows or turns negative, these problems become urgent priorities. Experiencing a downturn provides invaluable perspective for a product manager, fostering a healthier paranoia and a more urgent, clear-eyed approach to [[concepts/product-health|product health]].
