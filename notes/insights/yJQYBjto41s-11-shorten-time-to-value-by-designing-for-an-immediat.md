A product's first experience should quickly reveal its unique value, like collaborative multiplayer in Figma or a compelling prototype from a prompt in Figma Make. Designing for this rapid "aha" moment is crucial for [[concepts/onboarding|user onboarding]] and conversion, beyond just providing a minimal viable product.
