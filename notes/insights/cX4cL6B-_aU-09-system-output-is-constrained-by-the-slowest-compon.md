In [[concepts/ai-adoption|AI transformation]], overall output is constrained by the slowest part of the system, whether it's IT approval processes, legal constraints, or uneven skill development across functions. Companies must identify and ruthlessly attack these [[concepts/bottlenecks|bottlenecks]] rather than just accelerating the most visible parts of their workflow.
