Valuable product lessons can be found outside of tech, such as in architecture and urban planning. Books like *The Timeless Way of Building* highlight how spaces fail when the builders (developers) are disconnected from the inhabitants (residents), a direct parallel to digital platforms where misaligned [[concepts/incentive-design|incentives]] (like advertising) can create suboptimal user experiences.
