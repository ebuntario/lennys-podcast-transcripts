The failure of Google Local (a digital Yellow Pages) and the success of Google Maps illustrate a core product principle. Winning products don't just digitize existing workflows; they use new technology to create [[concepts/product-innovation|fundamentally new experiences]] native to the platform. This answers the critical user question, "Why should I use this?" by offering a differentiated and compelling value proposition that wasn't previously possible.
