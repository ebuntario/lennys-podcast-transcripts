If you cannot see almost limitless opportunities to improve your product, you should not be designing it. A healthy [[concepts/product-improvement|product improvement]] mindset involves feeling a degree of embarrassment about the current offering, which fuels a continuous desire to make it better. This critical perspective should be openly acknowledged and cultivated within the organization to drive excellence.
