AI's leverage allows for a new, flexible [[concepts/startup-funding|fundraising model]] like a "sip seed," where investors commit capital that the startup can draw down as needed. This aligns with extreme [[concepts/capital-efficiency|capital efficiency]]—building a product like Cora for roughly $300k including salaries—and gives founders psychological safety without the pressure of a large, burning bank balance. It reflects how [[concepts/ai-leverage|AI leverage]] changes traditional startup resource requirements.
