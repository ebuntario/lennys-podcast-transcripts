40-60% of B2B purchase processes end in no decision, primarily because buyers can't confidently choose between options. The buyer's primary job is often "how do I make a decision without getting fired?" rather than just evaluating features. This [[concepts/risk-aversion|risk aversion]] leads to delaying decisions as the safest option when overwhelmed.
