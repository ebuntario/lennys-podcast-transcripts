A common early-stage tactic is to "jumpstart" or "hack" one side of the marketplace by tapping into existing channels where that side is already latent. For example, Thumbtack and others used Craigslist to find initial suppliers. This "play one player mode" allows you to focus on validating the core value proposition and [[concepts/strategic-growth-management|growth strategy]] for the harder side before building the full two-sided flywheel.
