AI-native companies require collapsing traditional role silos across all functions, not just product development. Marketers should be able to execute campaigns end-to-end, and salespeople need to be fluent in product demos like sales engineers. This "full-stack" mentality reduces dependencies and increases velocity, echoing the versatile approach of early-stage startups.
