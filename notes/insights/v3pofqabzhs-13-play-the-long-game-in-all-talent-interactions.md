Success in hiring, recruiting, and career development requires playing the long game—building relationships and trust over time, not just during transactional moments. This applies to founders nurturing candidate relationships, PMs networking, and recruiters respecting career trajectories. A [[concepts/long-term-strategy|long-term strategy]] yields better outcomes than short-term, quota-driven tactics.
