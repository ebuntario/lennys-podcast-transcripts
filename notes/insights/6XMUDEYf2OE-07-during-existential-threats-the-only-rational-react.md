When facing a disruptive competitor, assume they are playing an optimal game and that their model will find a way to become profitable if the market rewards it. The mistake is to assume the disruptor is wrong and will fail. Base your strategy on the threat being real, which may mean copying the approach, acquiring the disruptor, or making a major [[concepts/strategic-pivots|strategic pivot]].
