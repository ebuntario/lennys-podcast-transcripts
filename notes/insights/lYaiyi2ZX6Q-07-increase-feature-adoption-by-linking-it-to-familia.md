When introducing a novel product or feature, tie it to a familiar concept to accelerate [[concepts/user-onboarding|user understanding]] and adoption. Presenting a digital wallet's account number on an image of a credit card helped users grasp its function, as they already understood how a card works. This bridges the gap between an unfamiliar solution and the user's existing mental model.
