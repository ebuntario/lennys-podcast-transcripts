A successful professional feed must serve two core member needs: strengthening connections within one's existing network and providing valuable knowledge and advice from outside it. LinkedIn's [[concepts/content-strategy|content strategy]] and algorithmic tuning prioritize delivering both relationship-based updates and expert insights, as these are the primary ways people discover economic opportunity through content.
