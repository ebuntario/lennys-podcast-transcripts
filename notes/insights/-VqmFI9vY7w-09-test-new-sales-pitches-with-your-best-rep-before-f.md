When implementing a new sales pitch, start by training your top-performing sales representative and having them test it with qualified prospects. Gather feedback after each pitch to refine messaging and structure. Once the top rep confirms it outperforms the old approach, use their success to convince the rest of the sales team to adopt the new [[concepts/sales-enablement|process]].
