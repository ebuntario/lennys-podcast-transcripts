Adopt "indistractable" as a personal identity, similar to how a vegetarian doesn't debate eating meat. When you internalize this [[concepts/identity|identity]], behaviors like putting phones away during meals or declaring focus times become natural expressions of who you are. This shift moves you from debating actions to embodying principles.
