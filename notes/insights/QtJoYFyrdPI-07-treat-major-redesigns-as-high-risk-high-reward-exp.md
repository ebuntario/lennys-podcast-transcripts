Unlike new features, a [[concepts/product-redesign|redesign]] is not voluntary for users and carries a high cost if it fails. To test a new paradigm effectively, you often need a substantial MVP to avoid false negatives from poor execution. Be prepared for significant internal investment and external criticism, and use data ruthlessly to determine if the core hypothesis is valid.
