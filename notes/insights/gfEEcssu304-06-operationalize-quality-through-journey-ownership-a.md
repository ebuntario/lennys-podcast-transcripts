Assign cross-functional leaders to own critical user journeys end-to-end. Have them regularly "walk the store"—experiencing the product as a user—and friction log issues. Calibrate quality scores in multidisciplinary reviews to build shared understanding and accountability for the [[concepts/product-quality|quality bar]] across the company.
