After identifying "very disappointed" users, ask an open-ended question: "What is the primary benefit you get?" Use the responses to create a multiple-choice survey for a new cohort. Then ask, "Why is that benefit important to you?" This uncovers the deeper context and [[concepts/customer-research|customer pain points]], providing powerful ingredients for messaging and [[concepts/startup-growth|growth strategy]].
