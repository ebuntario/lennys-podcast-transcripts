The concept of a [[concepts/mvp|Minimum Viable Product]] has a different meaning in safety-critical domains like autonomous vehicles. While the core philosophy of launching and iterating still applies, the MVP bar for safety is extremely high and cannot involve cutting corners. This requires a product management approach that balances rapid iteration with an uncompromising commitment to safety from the outset.
