The primary benefit of press coverage is often the validation it provides, not the direct audience reach. You can use a feature to say "As seen in" on your website, share it on social media with paid promotion, or include it in pitches to partners and investors. This [[concepts/social-proof|social proof]] builds credibility and can be more valuable than the immediate traffic from the article itself.
