For a narrative to become a true north star across the company, the [[concepts/ceo-role|CEO]] must personally lead its development and champion it. This provides essential "air cover" for product, marketing, and sales teams. When the narrative is driven from the top, it ensures company-wide [[concepts/leadership-alignment|leadership alignment]] and consistent messaging.
