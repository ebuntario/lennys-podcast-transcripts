Lagging indicators like closed-won deals come too late to correct course. Instead, track leading indicators such as the number of first meetings, conversion to second meetings, and progression through pipeline stages. Focusing on these [[concepts/leading-indicators|leading indicators]] allows for faster coaching and intervention, preventing months of wasted effort.
