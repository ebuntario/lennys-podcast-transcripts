When you instinctively recognize a superior product experience—like the iPhone's launch—trust that instinct even if industry veterans dismiss it due to entrenched beliefs (e.g., "carriers control everything"). Market shifts often invalidate conventional wisdom, and early [[concepts/product-intuition|product intuition]] about user experience can be a leading indicator of disruption.
