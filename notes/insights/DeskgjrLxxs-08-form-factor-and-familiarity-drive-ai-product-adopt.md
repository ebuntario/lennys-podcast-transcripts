Products like document editing or task reminders succeed by combining familiar interfaces (e.g., Google Docs, notifications) with "magical" AI moments. [[concepts/form-factor|Form factor]] follows function, as seen with file uploads enabling natural interaction with long contexts. Choosing the right interface reduces cognitive load and leverages existing user mental models.
