The foundation of effective product communication is deep listening, not just articulate speaking. When a user is venting about a problem, pause and listen fully to extract the root cause before reacting. This [[concepts/mindful-communication|mindful communication]] builds trust and ensures you are solving the right problem, not just defending your product.
