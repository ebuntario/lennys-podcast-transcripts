Forget about politics, promotions, or growing your org. If you wake up every day focused on having the biggest [[concepts/impact-driven-work|impact]] you can—or using your team to create maximum impact for the company—that becomes a powerful guiding light for all your decisions. This mindset naturally correlates with career growth, as leaders notice and reward those who drive significant business results.
