During reference checks, ask direct questions like "Why would I *not* hire this person?" and "What are their actual weaknesses?" to move beyond generic praise. Pay attention to pauses or hesitations, and prioritize back-channel references over provided ones for a more authentic view. This [[concepts/hiring-due-diligence|due diligence]] reveals a fuller picture of the candidate.
