For developer-focused products like Stripe, marketing artifacts are the first impression and must meet the same high bar as the product itself. Technical audiences are trained to spot inconsistencies and bugs, so marketing must be substantive, accurate, and crafted with the same care. This deep integration means the [[concepts/brand-as-product|brand experience]] is inseparable from the product experience.
