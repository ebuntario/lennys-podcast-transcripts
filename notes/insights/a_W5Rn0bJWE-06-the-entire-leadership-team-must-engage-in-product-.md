During tough times, product strategy cannot be owned solely by the product team. The CFO, head of sales, and other leaders must have a seat at the table and feel ownership over the strategic direction. As a product leader, it's your job to bring [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] to the group in a way that allows everyone to engage, internalize, and understand its implications for their domain.
