As AI makes code generation easier, competing on functionality becomes less effective. Winning requires exceptional [[concepts/design-principles|design craft]] and user experience, moving from "good enough" to "excellent." This shift elevates the strategic importance of designers and demands that other roles engage more deeply with design.
