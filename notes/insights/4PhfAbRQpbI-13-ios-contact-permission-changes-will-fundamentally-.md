Upcoming iOS changes that require users to manually select individual contacts for access will cripple traditional friend-finding and invitation mechanics. This will likely entrench incumbents and force a fundamental reinvention of how new apps build [[concepts/social-graphs|social graphs]]. Founders relying on contact sync need an immediate Plan B.
