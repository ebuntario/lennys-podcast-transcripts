Create a physical or visual space dedicated to imagining the world you want to live in decades from now. By keeping this [[concepts/long-term-thinking|long-term vision]] present in your daily environment, you can evaluate small decisions against whether they angle you toward that desired future. This practice shifts focus from reacting to immediate pressures to proactively shaping outcomes aligned with your deepest values.
