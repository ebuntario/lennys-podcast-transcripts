Relying on paid acquisition too early can create dependency and obscure whether the product truly delivers value. Focusing on [[concepts/organic-growth|organic growth]] forces teams to prioritize retention and prove real product-market fit. Paid ads are best used for testing messaging and value propositions rather than as a primary growth channel for early-stage startups.
