Consider adopting Shape Up when the current process consistently results in projects dragging, teams feeling stuck in the weeds, and leadership frustrated by a lack of visible progress. The discomfort of not shipping and the exhaustion of constant rework create the necessary "activation energy" to overcome inertia and try a different approach.
