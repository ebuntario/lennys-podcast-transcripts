Computer science education is fundamentally about [[concepts/creative-problem-solving|system thinking]] and problem-solving, not just coding. This holistic skill—understanding how components interact and diagnosing issues across a system—is difficult for AI to automate and will remain a critical, durable advantage for engineers.
