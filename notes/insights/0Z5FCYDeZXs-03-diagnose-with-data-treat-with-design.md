Data's primary role is to help diagnose problems and understand reality, not to prescribe specific solutions. You should use data to identify opportunities and user pain points, but the creative work of designing the solution—the "treatment"—still requires human [[concepts/product-design|design intuition and invention]]. This framework resolves the tension between data-driven and intuition-driven approaches.
