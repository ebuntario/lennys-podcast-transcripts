Reaching level four (extreme product-market fit) means having a valuable, efficient company. The next challenge is sustaining growth by expanding your total addressable market. This is typically done by launching new products for existing customers (like Vanta adding modules) or entering new markets, which requires finding [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] anew for each initiative.
