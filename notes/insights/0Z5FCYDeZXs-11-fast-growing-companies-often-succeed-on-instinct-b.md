Many hyper-growth startups initially thrive on good instincts and "vibes" without robust data logging or analysis systems because they move faster than traditional infrastructure allows. However, when growth inevitably slows, the lack of [[concepts/business-observability|business observability]] becomes a critical weakness, making it difficult to diagnose root causes and predict trends.
