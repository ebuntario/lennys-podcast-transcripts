Tinder's successful SEO strategy focused on solving "loneliness problems" in specific cities rather than ranking for generic "dating" keywords. Identify the core emotional or practical problems your product solves, then create content addressing those needs in specific contexts. This approach works even with AI Overviews because it targets mid-funnel intent.
