In high-performing B2B companies, the head of product should be deeply involved in sales, especially for large deals. They possess the deep product knowledge and authority to make roadmap commitments that can win or save complex deals. This turns them into a strategic weapon, acting as a "mini-CEO" in customer conversations and providing scale beyond what the sales team alone can offer.
