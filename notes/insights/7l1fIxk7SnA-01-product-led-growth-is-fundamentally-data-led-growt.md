When you offer a free product, you exchange it for two key benefits: broader reach and deep insights into [[concepts/user-behavior-analytics|user behavior analytics]]. Understanding which features correlate with higher conversion and retention is essential; without a solid [[concepts/data-foundation|data foundation]], you're giving away value without gaining actionable intelligence.
