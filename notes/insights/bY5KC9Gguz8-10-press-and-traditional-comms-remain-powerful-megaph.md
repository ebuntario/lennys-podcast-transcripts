Despite the rise of owned media and direct channels, traditional [[concepts/public-relations|press and PR]] offer a unique stamp of credibility and a megaphone to reach broad, new audiences. A major feature in a reputable publication can be a company's "big break," driving significant awareness, candidate interest, and customer discovery.
