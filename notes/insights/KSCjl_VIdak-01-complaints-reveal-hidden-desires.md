Complaints are a powerful tool for uncovering what you truly want. Every complaint implies a [[concepts/desire-articulation|dream or a better future]] where that irritation is resolved. By examining your frustrations, you can articulate a vision that moves you from passive annoyance to active pursuit of meaningful goals.
