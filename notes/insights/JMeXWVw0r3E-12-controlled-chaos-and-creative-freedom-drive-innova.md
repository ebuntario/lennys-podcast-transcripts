Successful, fast-growing products often look chaotic from the inside. Block's philosophy is to create a foundation that prevents major liabilities but otherwise allows engineers significant [[concepts/autonomy|autonomy]] and creative freedom. This "controlled chaos" breeds innovation, as brilliant engineers deliver amazing results when not overly constrained by strict processes, even if they occasionally go down rabbit holes.
