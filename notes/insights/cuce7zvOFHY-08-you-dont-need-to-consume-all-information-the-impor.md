The feeling that you must stay on top of all news, social media, and industry updates is a major source of distraction and anxiety. In reality, critically important information will reach you through other people or major channels. Curate a lean [[concepts/information-diet|information diet]] (e.g., a weekly magazine) and trust that you can let go of the constant stream without missing what truly matters.
