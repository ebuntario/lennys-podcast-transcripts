Specific [[concepts/behavioral-signals]] within product usage can indicate enterprise evaluation processes. Admin switches in an account or visits to terms of use/privacy policy pages are strong indicators of [[concepts/buying-intent]]. These deeper engagement signals are more meaningful than surface-level actions like landing on enterprise pages, which might be accidental and lead to ineffective sales outreach.
