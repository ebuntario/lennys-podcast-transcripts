The fundamental job in [[concepts/b2b-sales|B2B sales]] is not to sell a commodity but to solve a customer's problem. A great sales rep acts as an expert guide, honestly explaining a product's strengths and weaknesses while helping the prospect understand why it's the best solution for their specific situation. This approach builds trust and is more effective than adversarial or transactional tactics.
