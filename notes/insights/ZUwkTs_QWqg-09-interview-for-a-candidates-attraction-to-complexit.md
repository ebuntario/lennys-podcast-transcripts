A revealing interview question is to ask about the most complex system a candidate has ever built. Look for two things: first, a genuine gravitation toward difficult, interconnected problems (a key trait for solving hard challenges), and second, their ability to [[concepts/systems-thinking|simplify and explain]] that system's cause-and-effect relationships. This reveals both passion for hard problems and analytical clarity.
