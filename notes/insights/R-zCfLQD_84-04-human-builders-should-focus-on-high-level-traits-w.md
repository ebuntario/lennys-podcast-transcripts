The key traits for builders in an AI-augmented world are vision, empathy, communication, creativity, and, most critically, [[concepts/human-judgment|human judgment]] for decision-making in ambiguous situations. The strategy is to automate everything outside these core areas aggressively. This allows great builders to amplify their impact rather than getting bogged down in executional tasks.
