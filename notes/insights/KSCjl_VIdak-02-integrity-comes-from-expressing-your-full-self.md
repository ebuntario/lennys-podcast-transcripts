Personal integrity is maintained by fully expressing your thoughts, feelings, and desires. When you withhold what you want to say or ignore your subtle emotions, you create internal conflict and [[concepts/integrity|dishonesty with yourself]]. Regularly checking if you've expressed yourself completely is key to living authentically.
