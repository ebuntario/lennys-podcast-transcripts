As the cost of software development decreases, the competitive differentiator shifts from utility to emotional experience. The new standard is a [[concepts/minimum-lovable-product|Minimum Lovable Product (MLP)]], where the feedback cycle from idea to user testing can collapse to a day or weeks, not months.
