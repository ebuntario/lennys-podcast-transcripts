To define your purpose, first list cultural tensions in the world relevant to your business and ways to describe your brand's best self. Then, select the strongest item from each list and use them as context to brainstorm purpose statements. Starting with "The world would be a better place if..." can help frame the exercise before finalizing with "We exist to..."
