Building a [[concepts/startup-ecosystem|startup ecosystem]] platform that addresses multiple pain points (funding, incorporation, banking, knowledge sharing) can dramatically accelerate startup creation in underserved regions. By solving infrastructure problems collectively, these platforms reduce friction for individual entrepreneurs and create network effects that benefit the entire ecosystem.
