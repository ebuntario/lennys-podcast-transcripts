Marketing technology starts as a shared responsibility across growth, product, and engineering teams in early-stage startups. As companies grow beyond 100-150 people, this [[concepts/village-approach|village approach]] becomes unsustainable due to complexity in data flows, schema management, and contract liability. At this stage, organizations need a dedicated [[concepts/marketing-technology]] function to centrally own systems and tools, similar to how IT manages SSO and security infrastructure.
