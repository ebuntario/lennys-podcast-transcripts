A key lesson from investing is to never write off a portfolio company prematurely. Resilient founders can create [[concepts/founder-resilience|momentum out of seemingly nothing]] through a pivot, a key partnership, or a market shift, turning a struggling venture into a success long after an investor might have lost faith.
