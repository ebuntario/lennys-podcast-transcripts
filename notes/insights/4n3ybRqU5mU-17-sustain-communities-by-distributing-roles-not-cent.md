A community is not sustainable if it rests on the shoulders of one or two people. Distribute the workload and responsibilities by creating circles of interest around different topics and building bridges between them. This requires dedicated people (not necessarily full-time) to manage curation, moderation, and rituals, preventing a pyramid-like structure and ensuring long-term [[concepts/sustainability|sustainability]].
