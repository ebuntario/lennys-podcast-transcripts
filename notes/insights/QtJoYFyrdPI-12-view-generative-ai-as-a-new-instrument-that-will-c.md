Generative AI for music should be viewed as a powerful new [[concepts/instrument|instrument]], similar to how digital audio workstations (DAWs) enabled entirely new genres like EDM. It will empower a new class of creators who couldn't previously express their musical ideas. While it makes generic creation easier, true originality will become even more valuable and difficult to achieve.
