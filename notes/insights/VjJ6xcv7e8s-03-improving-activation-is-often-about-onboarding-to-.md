Moving retention is difficult, but significant improvements often come from optimizing [[concepts/onboarding|onboarding]] rather than tactical retention hacks. The key is guiding new users to the specific "must-have" experience quickly. This focus on [[concepts/activation|activation]] and speed-to-value is a higher-leverage activity for improving long-term retention.
