Founders should explore personal interests and experiences to discover [[concepts/startup-ideas|startup ideas]] that others might overlook. When an area becomes fashionable (like trucking startups after initial successes), competition increases and opportunities diminish. Mining your unique expertise—as Zip did with procurement software or Whatnot did with collectibles—creates defensible advantages in less crowded markets.
