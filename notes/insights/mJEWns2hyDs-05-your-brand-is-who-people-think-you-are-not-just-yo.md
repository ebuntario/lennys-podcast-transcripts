A brand is not merely a logo, font, or color palette; it is the perception held by your audience. [[concepts/brand-strategy|Brand strategy]] involves defining what you want people to think about your company and then taking actions to shape that perception. A strong brand aligns internal culture with external messaging to create a cohesive and compelling identity.
