In growth-stage companies, organizational structures are fluid and can be designed around talented, motivated individuals. You should not feel constrained by the existing org chart; instead, proactively propose new structures that solve business problems and align with your career goals. This mindset of [[concepts/agency|agency]] allows you to shape your role and the organization's direction.
