Many new leaders mistakenly believe their role is to keep employees happy, which can lead to avoiding necessary [[concepts/difficult-conversations|difficult conversations]] and accountability. True [[concepts/leadership|leadership]] involves driving toward results, setting clear expectations, and creating a structure for success. A winning culture, where people understand their impact and celebrate milestones, naturally fosters engagement more effectively than prioritizing comfort over performance.
