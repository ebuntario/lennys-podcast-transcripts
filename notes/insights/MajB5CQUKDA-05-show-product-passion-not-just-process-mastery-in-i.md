During interviews, demonstrate deep [[concepts/product-passion|product passion]] and customer understanding rather than just process knowledge. Rich feedback on existing products and ideas for improvement can compensate for lack of formal experience. The key is falling in love with the problem space, which shows genuine interest and potential for contribution.
