Onboarding should not be an afterthought or a plug-and-play overlay. The most effective onboarding is deeply intertwined with the core product experience, using the product itself to teach the user. This requires thinking about the user's first steps and the path to value from the earliest stages of [[concepts/product-design|product design]].
