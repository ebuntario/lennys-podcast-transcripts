A simple, authentic mission can rapidly bring people together by giving them agency and a clear way to help. For example, VaccinateCA's concise premise of "call pharmacies and map vaccine availability" allowed volunteers to immediately understand their impact. This [[concepts/community-building|community-building]] is powerful for both societal good and internal morale, and can be a significant advantage in hiring.
