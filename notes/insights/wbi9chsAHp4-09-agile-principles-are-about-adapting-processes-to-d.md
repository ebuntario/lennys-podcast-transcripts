The core of agile is moving quickly, getting feedback, and adapting based on what works. Teams should regularly ask if their meetings and ceremonies actually serve customer needs or just fulfill process requirements. Embracing [[concepts/inspect-and-adapt|inspect and adapt]] means being willing to change or discard practices that don't add value.
