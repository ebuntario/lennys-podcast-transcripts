The most exciting application of AI is enabling organizations to tackle more ambitious problems and expand their scope, not simply cut costs. This growth mindset focuses on how AI can augment human capabilities and create new opportunities. Adopting this [[concepts/business-strategy|business strategy]] leads to more dynamic hiring and investment in new areas.
