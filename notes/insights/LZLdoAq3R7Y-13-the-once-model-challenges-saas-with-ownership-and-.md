The [[concepts/once-model|"Once" model]] offers an alternative to SaaS by selling software for a single price, allowing customers to download and host it themselves. This addresses subscription fatigue and provides ownership, air-gapped security, and access to the source code. It targets commodity software categories where many alternatives charge luxury monthly prices, offering a high-quality, essential generic at a one-time fee.
