Founders should engage with recruiters or talent advisors in a consultative way early on, even before an active search. Leaning on venture firm resources or informal advisors helps build market knowledge and pipelines. This [[concepts/proactive-hiring|proactive engagement]] positions you to move quickly when hiring needs arise.
