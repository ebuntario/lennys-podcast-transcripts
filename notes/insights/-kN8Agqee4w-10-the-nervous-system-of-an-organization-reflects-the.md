A leader's emotional state has a disproportionate impact on their team due to emotional contagion. Burnout in leaders carries high hidden costs—estimated at a median of $100k+ per incident—from talent attrition, poor decisions, and lost productivity. Investing in [[concepts/nervous-system-regulation|nervous system mastery]] is a strategic advantage that protects the bottom line.
