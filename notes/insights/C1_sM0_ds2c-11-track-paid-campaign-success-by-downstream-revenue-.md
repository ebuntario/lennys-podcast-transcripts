Evaluate paid channels based on the full customer journey and lifetime value, not just lead generation. Implement dashboards that track conversion rates from lead to qualified opportunity to closed deal, and calculate the revenue generated per customer over time. This [[concepts/data-driven-decisions|data-driven approach]] ensures you invest in channels that deliver profitable growth, not just top-of-funnel activity.
