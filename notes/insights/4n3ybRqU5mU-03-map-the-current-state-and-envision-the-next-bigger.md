Effective coaching requires knowing where each product manager is today and having a vision for their future that extends beyond their current role. A key practice is to quarterly identify the "next bigger challenge" you would assign them to catalyze growth. Writing this down helps you recognize and seize the right [[concepts/opportunity-identification|opportunity]] when it arises, enabling substantial development over time.
