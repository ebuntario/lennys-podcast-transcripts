Effective [[concepts/parenting-philosophy|parenting]] involves gradually transferring decision-making authority to children, allowing them to make increasingly significant choices with appropriate support. The goal is to reach complete [[concepts/independence|independence]] by adulthood, which requires consciously creating opportunities for children to practice decision-making and experience natural consequences.
