High-leverage meetings, such as executive reviews or board presentations, require extensive preparation to anticipate all possible questions and concerns. A key tactic is to role-play the meeting with a leader who can impersonate different executives, focusing on their unique styles and priorities. This [[concepts/meeting-preparation|preparation]] de-risks the outcome and transforms the meeting from a "big reveal" into a confident, collaborative discussion.
