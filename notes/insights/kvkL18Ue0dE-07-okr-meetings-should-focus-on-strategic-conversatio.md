When OKR meetings become boring task reviews, it indicates the process has drifted from strategic alignment to micromanagement. Effective reviews focus on whether current strategies are working and what adjustments might be needed. This requires [[concepts/leadership-trust|trusting teams]] to handle execution details while leadership focuses on higher-level direction and removing obstacles.
