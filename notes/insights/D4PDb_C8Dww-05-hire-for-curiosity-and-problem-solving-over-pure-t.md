While technical skills are table stakes, the top differentiator in data talent is innate curiosity and self-motivation to pull on threads. Look for candidates who notice inconsistencies in case studies and proactively investigate. Also, assess how they handle being wrong and make decisions with imperfect information, as these [[concepts/creative-problem-solving|soft skills]] are critical for real-world impact.
