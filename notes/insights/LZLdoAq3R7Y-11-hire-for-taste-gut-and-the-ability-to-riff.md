When hiring for product and design roles, look beyond execution to a candidate's taste, what they admire, and their [[concepts/gut-instinct|gut instincts]]. A key interview technique is to give a project and then ask, "If you had more time, what would you do?" This reveals their ability to think on the spot and riff creatively, which is essential for a dynamic, intuition-driven culture.
