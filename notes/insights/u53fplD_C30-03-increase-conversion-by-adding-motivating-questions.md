While reducing friction typically improves [[concepts/conversion-optimization|conversion]], strategically adding easy questions (like multiple-choice selections about desired benefits) can increase motivation by making benefits salient. This works because questions actively engage users with the value proposition, making them more willing to overcome subsequent friction in the flow.
