In big tech functional structures, [[concepts/product-management|product managers]] often become team secretaries focused on documents and approvals, detached from design and pixels. Products live and die in the pixels, especially for consumer apps, so this separation can atrophy core product skills. Some companies like Snapchat and Apple give designers more control, leading to more novel outcomes.
