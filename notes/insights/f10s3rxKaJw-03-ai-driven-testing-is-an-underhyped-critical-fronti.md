As AI generates more code, comprehensive testing becomes paramount. [[concepts/ai-testing|AI-driven testing]]—including unit, integration, load, and security testing—is an underhyped area that can ensure quality at scale. Automating test suite generation allows developers to focus on higher-value tasks while maintaining software reliability.
