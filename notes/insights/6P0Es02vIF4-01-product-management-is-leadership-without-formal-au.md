A core part of product management is that you are a leader from day one, even without any formal authority. This forces you to practice the hardest parts of [[concepts/team-leadership|leadership]]—like influence and persuasion—early in your career, which becomes a significant advantage when you later gain managerial responsibilities. The role inherently develops your ability to lead through collaboration and vision rather than command.
