Dramatically improve [[concepts/product-activation|activation]] and trial-to-paid conversion by immediately guiding users to the features most critical to their primary [[concepts/jobs-to-be-done|job to be done]]. Research reveals which features deliver the core value; redesign the signup and [[concepts/product-onboarding|onboarding]] flow to make those features central and easy to discover first.
