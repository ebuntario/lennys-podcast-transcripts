The customer and market insights pillar focuses on operationalizing qualitative data. This includes creating research participant databases, aggregating findings into queryable repositories, and establishing feedback loops with sales and support. This function helps break down silos by systematically gathering [[concepts/customer-insights|customer insights]] from across the organization and making them accessible to product teams.
