Established company processes often crush innovative initiatives through approval requirements and resource constraints. Successful [[concepts/innovation-structures|innovation structures]] involve senior leadership sponsorship and separate decision-making pathways. These protected environments allow experimental teams to move quickly without conforming to standard organizational patterns that favor incremental improvements.
