A combined PLG and sales motion builds a resilient business by achieving both a large number of customers and a large amount of revenue. This dual strength makes a company very hard to assail, as competitors would need to displace a massive customer base. PLG ensures someone in the organization is incentivized to focus on end-user success and enjoyment, which is a natural [[concepts/competitive-advantage|competitive advantage]] in markets where buyers and users are different.
