When building a product-led growth motion, don't just chop up your enterprise product and offer a cheaper plan. Start fresh by understanding the unique problems of the self-serve audience. For example, startups using analytics tools often lack dedicated analysts, so their core need is guidance on best practices and benchmarking. Anchor your [[concepts/product-led-growth|PLG experience]] in solving these specific problems.
