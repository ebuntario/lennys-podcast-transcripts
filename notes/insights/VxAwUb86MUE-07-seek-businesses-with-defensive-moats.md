When evaluating a business, prioritize those with a strong defensive "moat" that makes them hard to compete with. The most valuable moats are powerful brands (like Coca-Cola) or [[concepts/network-effects|network effects]] (like a social platform where users won't leave because their friends are there). These characteristics provide pricing power and long-term sustainability, making the business resilient even with minimal intervention.
