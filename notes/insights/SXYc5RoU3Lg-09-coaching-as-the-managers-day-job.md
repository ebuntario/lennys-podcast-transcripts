Doing the work is an individual contributor's job, but getting better at the work is the manager's job through [[concepts/coaching|coaching]]. Like a sports coach observing from the sidelines, a manager's core responsibility is to create space for practice, give feedback, and design specific playbooks to help their reports achieve outcomes and reach their potential.
