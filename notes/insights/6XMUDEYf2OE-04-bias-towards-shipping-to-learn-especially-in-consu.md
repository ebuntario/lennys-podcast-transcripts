In consumer products with less sophisticated customers, the fastest way to learn is often to ship and measure impact, not to conduct extensive upfront research. Reserve scarce [[concepts/customer-research|user research]] resources for areas of high uncertainty and high leverage. For problems with low uncertainty, like redesigning a login page, look at what successful companies have done instead.
