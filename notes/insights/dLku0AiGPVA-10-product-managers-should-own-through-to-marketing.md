Product Managers should own the entire journey, including marketing. Channels like search or social ads are essentially placing a button to your product in another environment; the funnel starts there. Owning this end-to-end allows for optimizing the full experience from first touch to core usage. This requires PMs to develop instincts for [[concepts/growth-marketing|growth marketing]], messaging, and positioning, not just building features.
