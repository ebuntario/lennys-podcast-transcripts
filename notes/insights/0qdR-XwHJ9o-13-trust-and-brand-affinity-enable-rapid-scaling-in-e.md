A decade of trust with an audience eliminates the need for costly acquisition campaigns when launching a new service. Experts are more likely to participate, convert at higher rates, and remain engaged because of existing brand affinity. This allows a platform to scale a new business line rapidly without the customer acquisition costs that burden competitors.
