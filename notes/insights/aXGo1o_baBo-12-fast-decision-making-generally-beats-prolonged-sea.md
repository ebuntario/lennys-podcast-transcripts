A guiding principle for execution is that "fast beats right." Momentum and learning from action are often more valuable than waiting for a perfect, fully-baked solution. This bias for action and [[concepts/decision-making|decisiveness]] accelerates progress and allows teams to iterate based on real-world feedback.
