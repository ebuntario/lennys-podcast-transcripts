Writing forces clarity of thought and exposes gaps in reasoning. The process of articulating an idea—like bundling theory or eigenquestions—for an audience sharpens the concept and makes it shareable. This [[concepts/team-communication|disciplined writing]] can lead to unexpected opportunities, such as board roles or industry recognition, by demonstrating deep, structured thinking.
