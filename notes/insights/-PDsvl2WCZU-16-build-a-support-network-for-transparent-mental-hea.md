Cultivate a circle of trusted friends with whom you can openly discuss mental health struggles, as sharing often encourages reciprocal vulnerability and creates a safety net. Start by confiding in one or two people about your own challenges; you'll likely find they have similar experiences. This [[concepts/support-network|support network]] helps differentiate between temporary stress and deeper issues, providing perspective during periods of personal or professional turmoil.
