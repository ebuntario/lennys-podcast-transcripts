In fast-moving AI environments, planning requires identifying what [[concepts/technology-season|season]] you're in based on industry shifts, like the advent of agents. Teams set loose quarterly OKRs and short-term squad goals while leaving slack for unforeseen disruptions. This approach grounds everyone in current secular changes while maintaining flexibility for the slope of innovation, not just the snapshot.
