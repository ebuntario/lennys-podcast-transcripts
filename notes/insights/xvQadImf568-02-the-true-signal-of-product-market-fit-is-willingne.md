Waiting too long to monetize can be a major misstep. While early free usage provides valuable R&D feedback, the ultimate signal of [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] is customers opening their wallets. Delaying monetization cheapens your product's perceived value and misses critical feedback on what customers are actually willing to pay for.
