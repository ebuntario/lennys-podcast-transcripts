A common anti-pattern is splitting up a high-performing team to "spread the magic" across other groups. This often destroys the unique chemistry and dynamics that made the team successful in the first place. Team performance is more than the sum of its parts; it relies on specific [[concepts/team-dynamics|team dynamics]] and relationships that are not easily transferable.
