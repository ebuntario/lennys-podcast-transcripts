LinkedIn Learning is not an adjacent business but a core, strategic component of the jobs marketplace. If a job seeker lacks a required skill, the platform can provide a pathway to learn it, thereby completing the [[concepts/ecosystem-thinking|ecosystem loop]] of "connect to economic opportunity." This turns a potential dead-end into a viable pathway and strengthens the core value proposition.
