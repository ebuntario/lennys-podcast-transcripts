Consider creative, low-cost tactics to signal legitimacy to target customers. Airtable used remnant billboard inventory in specific geographic clusters (like media offices in NYC) to make large companies perceive them as an established player. This form of [[concepts/brand-signaling|brand signaling]] can reduce perceived risk and aid in closing large deals.
