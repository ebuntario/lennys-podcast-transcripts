A Growth CMO operates with an agile, iterative mindset, treating brand investments and marketing initiatives like product features that can be rapidly tested and refined. This contrasts with traditional CMOs who often plan campaigns far in advance and view brand as a fixed asset. By adopting [[concepts/agile-marketing|agile marketing]] practices and working closely with product teams, Growth CMOs ensure marketing efforts compound and adapt in real-time to market feedback.
