To get buy-in for an AI investment, point to successful adjacent products within or outside your company as proof of concept. Present a contingency or rollback plan to de-risk the proposal. Building [[concepts/stakeholder-buy-in|stakeholder buy-in]] relies on analogies and demonstrating manageable downside.
