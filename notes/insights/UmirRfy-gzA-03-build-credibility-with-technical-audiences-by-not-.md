Designers and engineers have a high "bullshit meter" and reject traditional marketing buzzwords. To build [[concepts/audience-credibility|audience credibility]], create deep, technical content about product decisions and craft—if a non-expert can write it, it's not specialized enough. This approach fosters authenticity and attracts practitioners who care deeply about their tools.
