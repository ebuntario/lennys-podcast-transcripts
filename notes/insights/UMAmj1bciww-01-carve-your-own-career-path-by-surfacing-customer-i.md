Your executive team often lacks direct access to customer insights as a company grows. Your superpower is staying close to customers, identifying their pain points, and bringing those valuable insights to life for leadership. This builds your internal brand as a subject matter expert and creates opportunities outside your defined role, reducing reliance on a single manager for [[concepts/professional-growth|career growth]].
