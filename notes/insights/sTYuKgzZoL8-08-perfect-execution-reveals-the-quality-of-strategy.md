A project failing due to poor execution leaves teams unsure if the strategy was sound. The ideal is perfect execution on the right strategy, but the next best scenario is perfect execution on a wrong strategy, as it provides a clear lesson. This mindset prioritizes fixing people and process issues first to enable honest [[concepts/strategy-validation|strategy validation]].
