A company's health can be diagnosed by examining its meetings and decision-making processes. Aim to make every meeting a "five-star meeting" where participants leave feeling it was great. Problems in specific meetings directly point to broader [[organizational-culture|cultural issues]] that need addressing, often leading to fewer but more effective meetings.
