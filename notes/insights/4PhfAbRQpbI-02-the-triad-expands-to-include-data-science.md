The traditional product triad of product, design, and engineering is evolving to include data science as a core function. Embedding a data scientist within a [[concepts/cross-functional-teams|cross-functional team]] allows for deeper focus, faster problem-solving, and better pattern recognition. This dedicated expertise removes the blocker of negotiating for centralized resources and accelerates data-informed decisions.
