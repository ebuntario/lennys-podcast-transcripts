Rigorous [[concepts/creative-testing|creative testing]] isolates variables like copy or imagery to determine what resonates at each funnel stage. Testing unpolished, authentic content often outperforms highly produced brand assets. Implementing a structured testing process yields continuous improvements in click-through rates and conversion efficiency.
