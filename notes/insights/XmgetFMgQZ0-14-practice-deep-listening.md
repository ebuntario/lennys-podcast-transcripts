Inspired by Rick Rubin, practice listening to absorb every fragment of what someone is saying—including body language—while consciously turning off the part of your brain crafting a response. This deep [[concepts/listening-skills|listening]] builds better understanding and trust, and is a critical skill for product managers who must synthesize diverse inputs.
