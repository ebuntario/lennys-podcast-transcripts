A global contraction in paid media budgets is happening due to stricter payback windows (now 6 months or less) and less tolerance for attribution ambiguity. However, this opens opportunities for companies with strong unit economics and better [[concepts/attribution-modeling|attribution stacks]] to benefit from decreased competition and lower CPMs. New tools focusing on media mix modeling and incrementality testing are emerging to address attribution challenges.
