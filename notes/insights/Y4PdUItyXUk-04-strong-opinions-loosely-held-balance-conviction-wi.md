Successful teams maintain stubborn beliefs about their core [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] or customer value, even when short-term data suggests otherwise. This conviction is balanced with the ability to adapt when evidence warrants change. This approach recognizes that product success often requires faith in long-term investments that immediate metrics can't justify.
