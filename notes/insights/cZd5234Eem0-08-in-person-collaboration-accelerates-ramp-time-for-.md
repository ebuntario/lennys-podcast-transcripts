For early-stage startups, having junior sellers work remotely significantly slows their learning and correction cycles. Sitting side-by-side allows for immediate feedback after calls, rapidly updating their "software." Fast [[concepts/coaching|coaching]] loops are a race against time for startups and are harder to achieve asynchronously.
