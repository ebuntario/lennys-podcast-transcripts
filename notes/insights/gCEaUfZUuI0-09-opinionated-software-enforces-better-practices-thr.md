[[concepts/opinionated-software|Opinionated software]] builds specific workflows or rules into the platform to guide users toward better outcomes, such as reducing bias or increasing transparency. This approach is valuable when existing processes are flawed or when a new, principled method needs technological enforcement to be adopted effectively.
