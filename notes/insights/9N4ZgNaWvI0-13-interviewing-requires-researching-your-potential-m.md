The single most important factor in a product manager's early career growth is their direct manager. Before accepting a role, thoroughly research the manager's background on LinkedIn. Look at the companies they've worked for and the products they've built to assess whether they have experience in an empowered, outcome-driven model. This due diligence is crucial for [[concepts/professional-growth|career development]].
