Product managers accelerate their careers by excelling in three areas: what you produce (outputs and outcomes), what you bring to the table (skills and artifacts), and what's your operating model (how you work with others). Strong PMs typically perform well in two of these areas, while those experiencing rapid growth excel in all three. This framework provides a structured approach to [[concepts/career-development|career development]] beyond just working on high-impact products.
