Your manager's primary goal is for you to succeed, as it makes their job easier. Instead of struggling in isolation, proactively [[concepts/manager-leverage|ask for help]] by framing updates clearly, such as providing a written status with specific blockers. You can even draft the email you need them to send, making it easy for them to assist and clear your path.
