Shopify maintains 8-20 direct reports per manager to keep the organization flat and alignment strong. They've observed that alignment degrades as distance from leadership increases. This [[concepts/flat-structure|flat structure]] reduces bureaucracy and ensures strategic clarity flows quickly throughout the organization without distortion through multiple layers.
