For B2B or lead generation, shift focus from cost per lead to customer lifetime value. Use data connectors to join CRM revenue with ad platform data, enabling predictive [[concepts/lead-scoring|lead scoring]]. This allows real-time bidding on audiences with higher conversion potential, maximizing long-term revenue over short-term lead volume.
