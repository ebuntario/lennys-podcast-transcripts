The belief that "execution is everything" and strategy is unnecessary represents poor product leadership taste. Companies with strong assets but unclear [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] consistently fail to reach their full potential, regardless of execution quality. Recognizing the fundamental role of strategy separates competent product leaders from exceptional ones.
