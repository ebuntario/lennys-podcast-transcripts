Frameworks like Jobs-to-be-Done or OKRs become counterproductive when followed religiously without nuance. They should facilitate customer-centric thinking and alignment, not replace judgment. The sign a framework is failing is when it leads to subjectively bad product decisions or prevents teams from pursuing bold bets that don't neatly fit the [[concepts/metrics-framework|metrics framework]].
