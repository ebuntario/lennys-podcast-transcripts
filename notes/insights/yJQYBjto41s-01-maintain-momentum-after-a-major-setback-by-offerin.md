When a major company event like a failed acquisition creates uncertainty, offer a structured off-ramp to reset focus. Figma's "Detach" program allowed employees to take severance and reapply later, which filtered for renewed commitment. This [[concepts/leadership|leadership]] tactic provides clarity and helps the organization move forward with a motivated team.
