In the digital economy, [[concepts/network-effects|network effects]]—where a product or service becomes more valuable as more people use it—are a primary source of strategic power. This dynamic underpins many modern [[concepts/business-models|business models]], from social media to marketplaces. The race is often to build a critical mass in the [[concepts/user-base|user base]] as fast as possible to create a self-reinforcing advantage.
