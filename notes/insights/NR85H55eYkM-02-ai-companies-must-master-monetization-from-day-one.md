AI founders must tackle [[concepts/monetization-strategy|monetization]] from the earliest stages because of underlying cost dynamics and the risk of training customers to expect more for less. AI products often tap into labor budgets, which are 10x larger than software budgets, making early value capture critical to avoid under-monetization.
