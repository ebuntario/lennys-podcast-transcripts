The dominant "resource-based view of the firm" taught in many business schools focuses on building resources but offers little practical guidance on *which* resources to build and why. This theoretical bent leaves students unprepared for the integrative, choice-driven reality of [[concepts/strategy-definition|real-world strategy]].
