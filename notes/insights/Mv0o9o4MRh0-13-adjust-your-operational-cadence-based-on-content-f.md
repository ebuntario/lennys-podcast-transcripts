Your review cadence (e.g., quarterly vs. six-week) is off if the content feels stale or if no meaningful progress is made between sessions. For fast-moving teams, a shorter cadence may be necessary to maintain [[concepts/company-velocity|velocity]]. Avoid creating overhead by using live dashboards instead of pre-prepared presentations for [[concepts/success-metrics|metrics]] reviews, ensuring the process accelerates rather than slows work.
