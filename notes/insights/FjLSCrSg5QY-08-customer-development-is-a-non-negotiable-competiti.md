Despite widespread acknowledgment, only 1 in 5 companies have defined buyer personas and only 1 in 10 conduct quarterly customer research. Companies that systematically do [[concepts/customer-research|customer development]] see 15-20% higher growth rates, better retention, and higher willingness to pay. In today's dense markets, brute-force growth is unsustainable; deep customer understanding is the true differentiator.
