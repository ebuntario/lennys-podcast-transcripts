The most effective way to drive [[concepts/ai-adoption|AI adoption]] is for leaders to use the tools themselves daily. At Block, the CEO, CTO, and executives use Goose regularly, which informs how they change organizational workflows. This [[concepts/hands-on-experimentation|hands-on experimentation]] provides deeper insight than reading think pieces and is crucial for understanding how to apply AI to team processes.
