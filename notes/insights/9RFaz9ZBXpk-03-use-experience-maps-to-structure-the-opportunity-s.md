Structure your opportunity space by mapping the customer's end-to-end experience, breaking it into distinct chronological moments. For a streaming service, this might include: deciding to watch something, choosing what to watch, evaluating quality, and the viewing experience itself. This [[concepts/experience-mapping|experience map]] reveals needs and pain points at each step, creating a shared understanding of where to focus discovery efforts.
