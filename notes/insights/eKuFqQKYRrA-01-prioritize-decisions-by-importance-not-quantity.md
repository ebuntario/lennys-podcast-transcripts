The most critical [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]] skill is identifying which decisions truly matter. Since PMs face hundreds of choices, they must prioritize the few that are irreversible or materially impact many users. For less important decisions, rely on gut instinct or delegate to maintain team velocity and focus energy where it counts most.
