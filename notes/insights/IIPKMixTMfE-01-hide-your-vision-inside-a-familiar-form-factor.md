When your core vision is too abstract for most users, you must package it inside a familiar, desired product. Notion learned to hide its "Lego for software" vision within a [[concepts/productivity-tool|productivity tool]] people already understood and used daily. This "sugar-coated broccoli" approach allows you to deliver deep value through a palatable entry point.
