Reverse engineering is a powerful exercise, especially for those low in openness or high in conscientiousness. It involves deconstructing a desired outcome to understand its necessary inputs or analyzing the current reality to see if your decisions are disconnected from it. This builds crucial [[concepts/situational-awareness|situational awareness]], ensuring your strategies are grounded and effective, rather than based on untested assumptions.
