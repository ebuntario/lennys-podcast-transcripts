Visualizing a future team structure on whiteboards and inviting team members to give feedback increases transparency and reduces anxiety during reorganizations. This method, called "whiteboard reteaming," shows team missions, open roles, and proposed placements, allowing people to identify opportunities and correct design mistakes. It transforms [[concepts/change-management|change management]] from a secretive process into a collaborative one.
