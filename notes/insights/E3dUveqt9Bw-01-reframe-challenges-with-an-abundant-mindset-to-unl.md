When faced with daunting professional challenges, consciously shifting from a [[concepts/mindset-shifts|scarcity mindset]] to an abundant one is transformative. Asking "How can I have fun with this?" or "What can I learn?" opens creative pathways and increases your emotional and mental energy for problem-solving. This reframing is a critical [[concepts/leadership|leadership]] skill that also transfers a positive mindset to your team.
