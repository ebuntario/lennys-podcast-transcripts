While user count and usage volume are important [[concepts/behavioral-signals]] for [[concepts/product-qualified-account]] identification, velocity changes are particularly powerful for timing sales engagement. Sudden increases in users added, events sent, or storage utilized indicate the right moment for sales involvement. Tracking these velocity metrics alongside volume thresholds provides a more complete picture of when an account is ready for enterprise conversations.
