It's acceptable and often smart to take inspiration from other successful products, but execution must be tailored to your own platform's strengths and user context. Copying the core mechanic that works is a shortcut, but you must then [[concepts/contextual-innovation|innovate contextually]] by integrating it seamlessly with your existing product mechanics and community.
