In enterprise sales, it's acceptable to lose some deals (20-30%) due to price to find the upper bound. Continuously ask for more to understand the true price ceiling, as founders are often surprised by how much they can charge. This process provides critical feedback on the maximum [[concepts/value-based-pricing|value-based price]] the market will bear.
