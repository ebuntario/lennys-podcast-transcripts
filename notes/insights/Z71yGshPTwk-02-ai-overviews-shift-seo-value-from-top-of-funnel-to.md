[[concepts/ai-search|AI Overviews]] are absorbing top-of-funnel informational queries, making traditional "best of" content less valuable. The real SEO opportunity now lies in mid-funnel queries where users have specific intent and are ready to take action. Companies should focus on creating assets that solve problems users discover after AI gives them initial direction.
