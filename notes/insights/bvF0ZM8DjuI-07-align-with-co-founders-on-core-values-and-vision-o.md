Before starting a company, co-founders should conduct a "prenup" by clarifying their individual core [[concepts/values-clarification|values]] and their shared vision for the company's success. Discussing values like "excellence" versus "work-life balance" reveals potential tensions early. Explicitly defining what long-term success looks like (e.g., independence vs. a large exit) prevents major [[concepts/co-founder-alignment|conflict]] years later when strategic choices must be made.
