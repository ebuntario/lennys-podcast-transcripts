To build a diverse team, you must actively seek out candidates from underrepresented groups, especially in the early stages, as passive pipelines often fail. Early hires from diverse backgrounds will refer others, raising the baseline of respect and making the company more attractive to a wider range of talent, creating a positive [[concepts/team-building|team-building]] flywheel.
