Continuously evaluate your career through the lens of what you enjoy doing versus what you excel at, seeking roles that sit at the intersection. For example, if you love mentoring entrepreneurs and are skilled at connecting cross-context insights, advising or investing might be fulfilling paths. This [[concepts/career-alignment|alignment matrix]] helps guide professional transitions, ensuring you leverage your strengths while pursuing work that brings genuine satisfaction and impact.
