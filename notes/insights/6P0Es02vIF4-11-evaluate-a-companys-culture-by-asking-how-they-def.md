A powerful question for candidates to ask potential employers is, "How does your company define a product team?" The answer reveals volumes about collaboration, decision-making, and the role of product management within the [[concepts/organizational-culture|company culture]]. Similarly, asking how a recent feature "became a law" shows whether the environment is driven by stakeholder shouts or customer discovery.
