When asked "Why are you doing this?", a niche market answer is insufficient. The real motivation is what will drive you to work late after four years when money is gone and things are difficult. This internal drive varies—solving a technical problem, proving oneself, or changing the world—and is crucial for [[concepts/resilience|resilience]] and conflict resolution within the founding team.
