Major [[concepts/innovation|innovation]] often requires leaving a local optimum (your current successful hill) to reach a higher global optimum (a distant mountain). This means accepting a temporary descent into a valley of uncertainty and hard work. The vision of the future summit is what sustains you through the difficult transition.
