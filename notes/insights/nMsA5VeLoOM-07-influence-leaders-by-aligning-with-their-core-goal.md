To effectively influence founders or CEOs, avoid debating details and instead align your proposal with their primary objectives. Reframe a scope or hiring debate around the shared goal, such as "winning" or delivering a "magical customer experience." This shifts the conversation from pushback to collaborative [[concepts/stakeholder-management|stakeholder management]].
