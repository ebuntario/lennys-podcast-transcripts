Many companies, especially outside Silicon Valley, have created excessive roles like agile coaches, product owners, and product ops, leading to bloated teams. This [[concepts/organizational-bloat|organizational bloat]] often results in high cost, low velocity, and poor innovation. The best companies achieve more with leaner, cross-functional teams focused on outcomes rather than process and output.
