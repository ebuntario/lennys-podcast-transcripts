Identify the "marginal user"—someone on the cusp of taking a key action but struggling—to reveal systemic product friction. Look at regions with high traffic but low conversion, then observe those users directly. While data provides clues, [[concepts/customer-research|direct observation and conversation]] are essential to uncover problems orthogonal to funnel analytics, such as users entering names nobody recognizes.
