The brain can be understood as having distinct systems or "departments" like a college campus. Most people over-rely on the "history department," which retrieves known information, because it's the lowest energy form of thinking. To get better answers, especially for innovation and product development, you should intentionally route problems to the more experimental "science department" or the creative "art department" of your [[concepts/brain-systems|brain systems]].
