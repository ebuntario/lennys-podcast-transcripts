Averages can hide catastrophic but rare failures (e.g., "Never Delivered" orders). These edge cases often drive disproportionate churn and cost. Actively look for these fail states, quantify their full impact (including lost future orders), and set concrete goals to eliminate them. Improving the worst [[concepts/customer-experience|customer experiences]] can have an outsized business impact.
