The fundamental advice for [[concepts/bootstrapping|bootstrapping]] is to stay as small as possible for as long as possible and keep costs incredibly low. Do as much work yourself as you can before hiring, and avoid spending on non-essentials like fancy branding or offices. This [[concepts/scrappiness|scrappy]] discipline is the primary lever you can control when starting out.
