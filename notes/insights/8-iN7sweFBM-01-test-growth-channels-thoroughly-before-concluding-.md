The only thing worse than a channel or tactic failing is prematurely concluding it doesn't work because you didn't give it an appropriate shot. Many companies claim a channel like [[concepts/youtube-marketing|YouTube marketing]] doesn't work, but upon inspection, their initial attempt wasn't designed to succeed from the start. Proper experimentation requires sufficient time and a well-structured test to yield valid results.
