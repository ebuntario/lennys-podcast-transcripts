[[concepts/tiktok-marketing|TikTok ads]] perform best with unpolished, user-generated style content and founder-led storytelling. The platform currently offers lower CPCs due to less competition, but requires high creative output. Brands that partner with influencers or showcase authentic product experiences see stronger engagement and conversion.
