When a founder insists on a specific direction, first seek to understand the core objective behind their request. If their proposed mechanism seems wrong, having identified the true goal allows you to [[concepts/communication|propose alternative options]] that better achieve it. This moves the conversation from a battle over a "pet project" to a collaborative discussion on [[concepts/strategic-alignment|strategic alignment]].
