A mature PLG motion involves a head of growth product (focused on activation), a head of growth marketing (focused on quality signups), and a head of product-led sales (focused on converting PQLs). Each owns distinct [[concepts/growth-metrics|growth metrics]] aligned to their part of the funnel.
