At its core, growth is not overly complex; it involves identifying where your target customers experience pain points and providing valuable solutions. The discipline lies in consistently executing on this [[concepts/first-principles-thinking|first-principles approach]]—researching customer questions, creating helpful content, and engaging authentically in communities—rather than seeking shortcuts or overly sophisticated tactics.
