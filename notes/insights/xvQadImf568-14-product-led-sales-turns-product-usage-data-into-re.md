Product-led sales harnesses product usage data to identify which free or low-tier accounts are most ready to upgrade. By shining a light on high-potential accounts, sales teams can drive large expansion deals, effectively creating "free money" from existing users. This bridges [[concepts/product-led-growth|product-led growth]] with a proactive sales motion.
