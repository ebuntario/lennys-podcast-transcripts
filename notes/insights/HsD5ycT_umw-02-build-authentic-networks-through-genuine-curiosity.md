Building a valuable network stems from authentic curiosity about people's stories, not transactional "networking." Schedule regular coffees with peers and leaders with no agenda, focusing on learning about them and offering help. This compounds over time, creating a web of relationships that enables serendipity and support throughout your [[concepts/career-development|career]].
