To become proficient with [[concepts/ai-tools|AI tools]], dedicate a full week to achieving a specific outcome using them. Choose a real problem, work through it end-to-end, and constantly ask the AI for help when stuck. This immersive, goal-oriented practice will put you in the top 1% of users globally and provide practical understanding far beyond theoretical knowledge.
