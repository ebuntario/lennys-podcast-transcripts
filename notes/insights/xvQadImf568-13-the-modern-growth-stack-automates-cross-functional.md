The Modern Growth Stack refers to cloud-native tools that automate workflows for product, growth, and revenue teams. These tools (e.g., for experimentation, product-led sales, billing) replace in-house infrastructure, freeing engineering resources. They unlock data, enable cross-functional collaboration, and drive hard ROI through both cost savings and [[concepts/revenue-optimization|revenue impact]].
