Instead of relying on theoretical arguments, the team used a step-by-step approach: first proving people found the *concept* helpful, then that they could write good notes, then that the algorithm could identify them. This method of letting [[concepts/experimentation|data-driven results]] build confidence was key to gaining support through multiple leadership changes.
