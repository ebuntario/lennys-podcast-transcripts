The immediate impact of AI is augmenting human capability, not replacing jobs outright. Individuals who leverage AI tools will become significantly more productive and competitive. For example, [[concepts/ai-adoption|AI adoption]] in engineering can unlock order-of-magnitude efficiency gains, transforming how work is done rather than eliminating the need for workers.
