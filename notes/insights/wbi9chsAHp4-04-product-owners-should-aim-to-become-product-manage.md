Product owners often get stuck in tactical backlog management and user story writing. To advance, they must demonstrate how their work impacted business metrics and customer value. Shifting the conversation from process to outcomes—by asking "What do we hope will happen when we release this?"—is key to evolving into a [[concepts/product-manager|product manager]].
