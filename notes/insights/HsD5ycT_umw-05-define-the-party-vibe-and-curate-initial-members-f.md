When building a community, explicitly define its "vibe"—is it a formal dinner or a casual party? As the host, curate the initial member mix to include diverse energies: influential but busy people, boisterous connectors, and thoughtful contributors. This intentional [[concepts/curation|curation]] and clear tone-setting are more critical than scale for fostering trust and engagement.
