Product managers should actively share credit with engineering teams to build trust and avoid resentment. This involves stepping back during presentations and letting engineers speak about their contributions, which reinforces [[concepts/team-dynamics|collaborative teamwork]] and acknowledges the collective effort behind a project's success.
