Generative [[concepts/artificial-intelligence|AI]] acts as a tool to explore a wider "surface area of possibility" in less time. Like a director using ChatGPT to generate scenario options they wouldn't use but that spark new ideas, product leaders can use AI to rapidly explore alternatives. This expansion of creative exploration allows professionals to consider more solutions and identify the best path forward more efficiently.
