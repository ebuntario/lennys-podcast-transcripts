If a previously healthy growth channel (like self-service) plateaus, it often signals a shift in your target audience. The solution is not to double down on optimizing old levers, but to "throw the roadmap away" for a period. Dedicate the next six months to generating and testing new hypotheses about what the next generation of customers needs, explicitly sacrificing short-term impact for long-term [[concepts/product-learning|learning]].
