Running a social network presents content moderation challenges on a different scale than traditional publishing platforms. Even with experience handling diverse content on a CMS, the real-time, interactive, and community-driven nature of social media introduces unique complexities. This requires dedicated systems and resources for [[concepts/content-moderation|community management]] that go beyond standard platform operations.
