Study your company's history of product launches and strategic decisions to become an organizational historian. Understanding *why* past decisions were made, successful or not, provides crucial [[concepts/context-building|context]] for future [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]]. This also helps you anticipate and address team members' "baggage" or objections based on previous failed attempts.
