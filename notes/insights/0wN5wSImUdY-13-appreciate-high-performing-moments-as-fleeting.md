Exceptional team experiences are precious but temporary due to inevitable growth, market shifts, and personnel changes. Leaders and team members should practice gratitude for these periods rather than clinging to them or fearing evolution. Recognizing that [[concepts/organizational-culture|organizational culture]] naturally evolves helps teams navigate change with more resilience and less resistance.
