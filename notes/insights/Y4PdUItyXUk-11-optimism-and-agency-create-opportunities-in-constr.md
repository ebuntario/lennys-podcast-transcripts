Even in seemingly rigid organizations, [[concepts/product-leadership|product managers]] can find opportunities to practice modern techniques by reframing constraints. Documenting assumptions, clarifying success metrics, or mapping customer journeys are activities possible in almost any environment. This proactive approach builds valuable experience regardless of organizational limitations.
