When your first user encounters a critical blocker, treat it as an all-hands emergency. Figma's founders drove to a user's office to physically fix his MacBook when a file wouldn't open, demonstrating that [[concepts/early-stage-growth|early-stage growth]] depends on extreme dedication to user success. This level of service builds immense loyalty and turns early adopters into powerful champions.
