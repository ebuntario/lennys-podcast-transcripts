Establish a clear list of Directly Responsible Individuals (DRIs) for all key areas, not just job titles, to improve [[concepts/cross-functional-collaboration|cross-functional collaboration]] between product and marketing. This clarifies who owns what (e.g., onboarding flow, website copy) and who to consult. Combine this with shared planning sessions and upfront briefs to align on goals and speed up execution.
