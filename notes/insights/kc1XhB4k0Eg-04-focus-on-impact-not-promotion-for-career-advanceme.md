The most effective performers prioritize maximizing their [[concepts/impact|impact]] on the company rather than focusing on promotions or pleasing their manager. This mindset leads to stronger outcomes and naturally advances [[concepts/career-development|career development]]. When your primary metric is contribution, you make decisions that benefit the organization, which leadership ultimately rewards.
