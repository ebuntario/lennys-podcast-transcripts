Contrary to the belief that managers hold people back, leaders actually benefit from promoting strong performers. Having capable direct reports who can handle more responsibility makes the manager's job easier and reflects well on their [[concepts/team-leadership|leadership abilities]]. This alignment of interests means most managers want to help you advance.
