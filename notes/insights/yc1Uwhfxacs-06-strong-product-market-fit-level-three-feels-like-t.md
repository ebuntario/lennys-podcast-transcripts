At level three, growth begins to feel organic and driven by market pull, with significant inbound from referrals and word-of-mouth. Founders describe the shift as the "boulder rolling downhill." Key benchmarks include approaching 100 customers, ARR between $5M-$25M, and the need to start seriously optimizing for [[concepts/unit-economics|efficiency metrics]] like gross margin and burn multiple while maintaining growth.
