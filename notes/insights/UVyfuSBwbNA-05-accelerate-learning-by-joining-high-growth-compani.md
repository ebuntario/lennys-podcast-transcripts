The fastest way to accelerate your [[concepts/professional-growth|career development]] is to join companies experiencing hyper-growth. These environments compound your learning at a much faster rate, similar to daily versus annual compound interest. You encounter novel problems, build a strong network through shared success, and are inspired by mission-driven founders, all of which push your boundaries.
