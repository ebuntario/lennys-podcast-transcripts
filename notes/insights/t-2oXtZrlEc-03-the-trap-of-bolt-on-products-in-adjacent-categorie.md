Expanding into adjacent categories often results in building "the sixth-best" solution in that category, which few customers want. These bolt-on products typically contribute only 5-10% to revenue without accelerating growth, while diverting resources from the core. A successful [[concepts/bundling-strategy|bundling strategy]] requires an anchor product so compelling that customers accept good-enough solutions for other needs.
