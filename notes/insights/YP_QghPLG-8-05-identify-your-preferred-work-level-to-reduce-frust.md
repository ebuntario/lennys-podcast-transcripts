Product work occurs at three levels: impact, execution, and optics. Frustration arises when you spend most of your time operating at a level that doesn't align with your [[concepts/superpowers|superpowers]] and natural preferences. Being honest about your preferred work level and making career decisions accordingly leads to greater satisfaction and better performance.
