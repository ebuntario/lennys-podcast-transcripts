[[concepts/communication-influence|Communication and influence]] skills are essential for growth practitioners who must often overcome preconceived notions about growth being at odds with product craftsmanship. This involves strategic communication of how experiments fit into larger bets, team leadership, and [[concepts/stakeholder-management|stakeholder management]]. These skills require relearning with each new company or team since different organizations trade in different currencies.
