Moments that stretch you or make you feel underqualified are the ones that provide a new foundation for growth. Instead of asking the ambiguous question "Are you growing in your role?", ask "How many 'oh shit' moments have you had recently?" If it's been a long time since you've felt meaningfully stretched, it's a signal to seek new challenges that push your [[concepts/career-growth|career growth]] forward.
