Innovation often happens at the intersection of functions. Encourage deep understanding across roles: designers understanding PM and engineering, PMs understanding marketing and design, engineers understanding product. When individuals can operate with [[concepts/cross-functional-skills|cross-functional depth]], micro-decisions throughout the company are better optimized for the entire user funnel, leading to more cohesive and effective products.
