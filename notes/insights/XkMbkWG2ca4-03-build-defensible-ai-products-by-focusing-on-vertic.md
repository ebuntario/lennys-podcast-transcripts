To avoid direct competition with general-purpose AI platforms, build products that solve specific, vertical problems. Companies that combine domain expertise with AI to create tailored solutions—like legal AI or sales agents—establish a defensible position. General-purpose AI builders will focus on broad capabilities, leaving room for deep [[concepts/vertical-specialization|vertical specialization]] on top of their models.
