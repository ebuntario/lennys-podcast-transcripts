The primary competitive advantage in human data provision is direct, trusted access to a qualified audience, not just recruitment capability. Companies spending millions on performance ads and recruiters face high acquisition costs and low conversion, while platforms with existing [[concepts/trust-and-brand|trust and brand affinity]] can engage experts efficiently. This moat is strengthened when the audience is pre-verified, as with university-affiliated networks.
