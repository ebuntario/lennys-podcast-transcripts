Your career trajectory is heavily influenced by the momentum of the companies you work for, as high-growth environments provide accelerated experiences and responsibilities. For example, a team might grow from 5 to 7 people in five years at a slow-growth company, but from 15 to 100 in four years at a hypergrowth company. Choosing the right [[concepts/company-selection|high-momentum company]] early, like a Tesla or OpenAI, can be a significant career multiplier.
