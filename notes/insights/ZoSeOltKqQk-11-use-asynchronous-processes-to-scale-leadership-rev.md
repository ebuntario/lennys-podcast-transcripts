To scale leadership oversight without excessive meetings, implement asynchronous reviews for team OKRs. Have teams post a document and a short video summary, which leaders review together in a batch. This [[concepts/asynchronous-communication|asynchronous process]] saves time and allows leaders to focus follow-up meetings only on the highest-priority or most problematic projects.
