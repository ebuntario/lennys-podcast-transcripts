Sustainable [[concepts/continuous-discovery|continuous discovery]] comes from changing daily habits, not just adopting new methodologies. Teams must shift from project-based research to weekly interviews and rapid assumption testing. This behavioral change enables the continuous learning cadence that separates successful product teams from those stuck in feature factory modes.
