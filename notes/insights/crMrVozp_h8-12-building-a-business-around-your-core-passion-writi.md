Leaning into your core passion as a founder—rather than forcing a conventional startup mold—can create a unique, defensible [[concepts/business-model|business model]]. For example, centering a company around writing (the newsletter) led to unique insights, audience trust, and a pipeline for incubated products. This [[concepts/personal-alignment|personal alignment]] reduces the "tax" of doing work you dislike and often results in a more authentic and sustainable company shape than cargo-culting standard practices.
