Leaders should investigate project timelines with curiosity to identify unnecessary padding or incorrect assumptions. Maintaining a flatter org structure and actively managing [[concepts/technical-debt|technical debt]] prevents systemic slowdowns. This approach requires balancing the need for speed with the foundational work required for sustainable growth.
