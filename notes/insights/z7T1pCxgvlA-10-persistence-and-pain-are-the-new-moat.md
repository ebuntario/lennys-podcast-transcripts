In the AI era, a significant [[concepts/competitive-advantage|competitive advantage]] comes from the persistence to go through the pain of iterative learning and implementation. The knowledge gained from understanding what doesn't work, fixing messy enterprise data, and calibrating systems to specific workflows becomes a durable moat that is hard for competitors to replicate quickly.
