Organizations succeed with AI by first ensuring everyone achieves [[concepts/ai-fluency]] through daily tool usage. Next, they apply AI to improve existing processes like customer support, measuring tangible impact. Finally, they inflect growth by creating new customer experiences or categories, avoiding the trap of pursuing AI projects without a clear blueprint for value.
