Creating space for teams to experiment and "fail forward" is essential for breakthroughs like Copilot. An [[concepts/innovation-culture|innovation culture]] values learning from mistakes and funds exploration without guaranteeing success. Structuring innovation too rigidly can stifle the organic, creative sparks that drive progress.
