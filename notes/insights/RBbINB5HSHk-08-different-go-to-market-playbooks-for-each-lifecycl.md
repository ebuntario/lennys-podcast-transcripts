Each stage of the technology adoption lifecycle requires a distinct playbook: the Early Market uses a visionary-focused project model; the Bowling Alley uses a problem-centric solution model; the Tornado uses a land-grab, horizontal coverage model; and Main Street focuses on service innovation and expansion. Using the wrong playbook for the phase will slow you down or cause failure.
