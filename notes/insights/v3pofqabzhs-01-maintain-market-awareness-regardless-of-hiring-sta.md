Both candidates and hiring managers should always keep a pulse on the market to understand what good looks like. This involves knowing which companies are thriving and which individuals are building great things and have strong reputations. This ongoing [[concepts/market-awareness|market awareness]] prevents you from being caught off guard and helps you make informed decisions when opportunities arise.
