Effective leaders couple a clear goal with a compelling "why" to drive organizational change. A consistent strategic narrative, repeated frequently, helps teams internalize the purpose behind new priorities and working methods. This combination of a clear [[concepts/north-star-metric|north star metric]] and a strong narrative is a powerful tool for guiding a company through cultural and operational transitions.
