Underperforming teams often fail in similar ways, while successful teams reach excellence through varied approaches. Some organizations thrive with rigorous, process-driven [[concepts/decision-making|decision-making]], while others succeed through collaborative, ad-hoc methods. The key is aligning your team's structure and processes with your specific context and strategy.
