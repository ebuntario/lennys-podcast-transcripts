AI is displacing tactical work in [[concepts/ai-in-marketing|performance marketing]], shifting human roles toward strategic oversight. Teams now focus more on modeling, validation, and asking the right questions rather than manual implementation. This transition mirrors broader industry trends where AI handles execution, freeing humans for higher-level problem-solving.
