[[concepts/ai-applications|AI tools]] can dramatically speed up the growth experimentation cycle. For example, AI-powered Slack bots can answer ad-hoc data questions instantly, making the company more data-informed. AI can also summarize experiment analyses and suggest new ideas, accelerating the ideation and research phases that feed the next round of testing.
