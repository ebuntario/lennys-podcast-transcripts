When stuck, ask yourself how a specific admired leader would approach the problem. For example, "What would Boz say about principles?" or "What would Rob Goldman say about looking at the dashboard?" This mental [[concepts/coaching|emulation]] provides diverse lenses and expands your [[concepts/creative-problem-solving|problem-solving]] toolkit.
