The act of writing forces clarity and exposes gaps in logic that mere thinking can obscure. As the quote goes, "If you're thinking without writing, you just think you're thinking." Developing the habit of writing out strategies, plans, and arguments is a crucial [[concepts/personal-effectiveness|personal effectiveness]] skill for product managers to refine their ideas and communicate them effectively.
