Building [[concepts/ai-product-development|AI products]] fundamentally differs from traditional software due to [[concepts/non-determinism|non-determinism]]. You cannot predict how users will phrase their inputs in natural language interfaces, nor can you guarantee the exact output from the LLM. This requires designing for a range of possible behaviors rather than a single, fixed workflow.
