Begin with the smallest possible viable product to learn what you actually need to build. VaccinateCA started with just phones, Discord, and a spreadsheet, which provided immense impact and critical learning before scaling. This [[concepts/mvp-strategy|MVP development]] approach ensures agility and prevents overbuilding based on incorrect assumptions.
