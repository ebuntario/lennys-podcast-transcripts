In B2B sales with multiple decision-makers, your primary contact (the champion) needs tools to address concerns from other departments like IT, finance, or end-users. Provide resources like security documentation, ROI calculators, or adoption plans that help champions navigate internal objections. This [[concepts/sales-enablement|support]] increases deal velocity by preparing champions for predictable pushback.
