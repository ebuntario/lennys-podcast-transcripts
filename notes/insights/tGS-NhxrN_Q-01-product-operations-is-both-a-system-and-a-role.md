[[concepts/product-operations|Product operations]] is fundamentally a system created to allow a product team to thrive, not just a person. It involves establishing processes and structures that enable product managers to focus on their core work. The role of a product ops person emerges to manage and evolve this system, acting as a strategic partner to product leadership and a support function for product managers.
