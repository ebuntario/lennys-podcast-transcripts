Host exclusive, all-expenses-paid events for current customers only. This creates a psychological incentive to remain a customer to attend, reducing churn. Use the event's social settings to gather warm introductions to new prospects from your happiest customers, effectively turning retention efforts into a [[concepts/pipeline-generation|pipeline generation]] engine.
