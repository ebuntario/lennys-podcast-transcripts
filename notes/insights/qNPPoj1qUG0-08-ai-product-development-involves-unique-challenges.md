Managing an AI product involves unique challenges: embracing uncertainty in research outcomes, creatively sourcing quality training data, and navigating a different career progression where launches are less frequent. PMs must be cheerleaders who manage [[concepts/project-uncertainty|project uncertainty]] and clarify growth metrics with leadership early on.
