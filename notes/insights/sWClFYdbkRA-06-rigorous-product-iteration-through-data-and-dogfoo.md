Simply copying successful features from other products without understanding the underlying mechanics often fails. Successful [[concepts/product-iteration|product iteration]] requires constant hypothesis testing, data analysis, and [[concepts/dogfooding|dogfooding]] (using your own product). This builds institutional knowledge about what actually drives metrics rather than just implementing surface-level patterns.
