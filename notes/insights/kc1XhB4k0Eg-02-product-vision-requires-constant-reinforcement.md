Creating a compelling [[concepts/product-vision|product vision]] is not enough; you must repeatedly communicate it to build internal buy-in. Just as founders restate their mission at every all-hands, product leaders should consistently articulate the "why" behind a project. This ensures every team member understands the narrative and can connect their work to the larger goal, fostering cohesive execution.
