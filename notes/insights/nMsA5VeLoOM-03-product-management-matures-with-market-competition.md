In nascent markets like Web3, products can achieve initial scale without traditional product management structures. However, as the market matures and competition increases, [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]] and execution become a key competitive advantage. This leads to a natural evolution where companies hire product leaders to manage growing complexity and prioritize effectively.
