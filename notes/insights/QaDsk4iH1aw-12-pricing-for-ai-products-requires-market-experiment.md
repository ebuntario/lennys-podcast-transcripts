The value created by AI doesn't neatly fit traditional SaaS, seat-based, or even usage-based pricing. Companies must [[concepts/testing|experiment]] to discover the right metric—whether it's hours saved, tasks completed, or a new unit of value. Test different models in the market to build conviction, as the optimal pricing strategy is still being discovered.
