Successful founders must architect their business to dominate both [[concepts/market-share|market share]] and [[concepts/wallet-share|wallet share]], not just one. This requires equal attention to acquisition, monetization, and retention. Focusing on a single-engine strategy—like growth at all costs or early monetization at the expense of acquisition—leads to unsustainable businesses.
