While mentorship provides day-to-day guidance, [[concepts/sponsorship|sponsorship]] is about someone with influence trusting you to solve big problems and advocating for you. You create sponsorship opportunities by doing excellent work, communicating its impact, and demonstrating how your efforts connect to the organization's most important priorities.
