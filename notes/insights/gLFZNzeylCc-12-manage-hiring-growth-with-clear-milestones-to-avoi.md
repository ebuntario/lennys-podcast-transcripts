A critical lesson is to avoid over-hiring by establishing clear gates and milestones that must be achieved before unlocking further team growth. This [[concepts/resource-management|disciplined approach]] prevents the painful scenario of having to lay off people you've recruited and ensures hiring aligns directly with proven business needs and results, not just ambitious vision.
