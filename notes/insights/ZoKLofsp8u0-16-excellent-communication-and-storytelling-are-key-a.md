The ability to communicate complex ideas clearly and inspire others is a common trait among successful founders. This [[concepts/narrative|storytelling]] skill is crucial for fundraising, hiring, and motivating teams. Founders who can articulate why their work matters in a compelling way naturally attract followers and build belief in their vision.
