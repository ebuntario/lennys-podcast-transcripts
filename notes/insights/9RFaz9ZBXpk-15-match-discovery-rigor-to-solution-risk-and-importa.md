Not all solutions require extensive discovery; focus your efforts on core product experiences and differentiators. Assess the risk involved in each bet and allocate discovery effort accordingly. Forgetting password flows might need minimal discovery, while features central to your value proposition deserve robust customer research and assumption testing.
