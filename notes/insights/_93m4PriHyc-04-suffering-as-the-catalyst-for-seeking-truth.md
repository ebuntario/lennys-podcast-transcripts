Significant personal transformation rarely occurs without significant suffering. This suffering, often reaching a [[concepts/rock-bottom|rock bottom]], provides the necessary courage to embark on the difficult journey of seeking truth. The initial drive for change is frequently fueled by the intense fear of returning to that state of profound unhappiness.
