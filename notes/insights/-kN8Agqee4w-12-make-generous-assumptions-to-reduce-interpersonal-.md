In any conflict or frustrating situation, consciously choose to tell the most generous story possible about the other person's intentions. This isn't naive fabrication, but selecting from the spectrum of plausible explanations the one that assumes good faith or mitigating circumstances. This practice reduces your own [[concepts/emotional-reactivity|reactivity]] and fosters better relationships.
