Increase execution speed by aligning sprint planning cycles across different functions, such as product, engineering, and marketing. Bring lifecycle marketers into the product team's sprint cycles so their projects are tightly integrated and move at the same pace. This [[concepts/cross-functional-collaboration|synchronized rhythm]] eliminates silos, improves coordination, and significantly accelerates the delivery of growth initiatives.
