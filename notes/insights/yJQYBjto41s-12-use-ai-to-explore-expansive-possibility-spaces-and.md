AI models excel at generating and mapping out multi-dimensional option spaces, such as character traits for fiction writing. This process helps build intuition about a domain far more efficiently than manual exploration. Leveraging AI for this [[concepts/creativity|creative]] exploration is an underutilized tactic beyond basic task automation.
