Successful AI applications build substantial proprietary technology on top of foundation models rather than creating thin wrappers. Companies like Cursor develop multiple specialized models and accumulate domain-specific data through user interactions. This deep [[concepts/product-moat|integration creates defensibility]] that simple API calls cannot achieve, especially in complex enterprise environments with unique semantics and access controls.
