Effective hiring managers approach interviews as a mutual discovery process, not an exam. The goal is to find candidates who will thrive in the specific company culture and role, which requires understanding that success at one company doesn't guarantee success at another. This mindset leads to better long-term [[concepts/hiring|talent acquisition]] and retention.
