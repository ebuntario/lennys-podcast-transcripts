Build a sales team with a mix of traditional sales professionals and those from nontraditional backgrounds like consulting or banking. The former bring core [[concepts/sales-culture|sales skills]] and process knowledge, while the latter excel at quantitative analysis, financial modeling, and consultative problem-solving. This combination creates a rich learning environment where each group elevates the other's capabilities.
