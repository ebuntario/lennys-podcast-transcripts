The current AI model you use is the worst one you'll ever use for the rest of your life, as capabilities improve dramatically every few months. This requires a fundamental shift in [[concepts/product-strategy|product strategy]], where you must build for the capabilities that are almost here, not just what exists today. Teams should keep building even if a product is on the edge of current model limits, knowing the technology will soon catch up and make it sing.
