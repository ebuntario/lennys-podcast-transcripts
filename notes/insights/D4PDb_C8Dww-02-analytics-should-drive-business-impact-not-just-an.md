Analytics should be a business impact-driving function with a seat at the table alongside product and engineering. The role is not just to answer "why" but to answer "so what" and "what do we do now." This requires a [[concepts/proactive-analysis|proactive approach]] to finding opportunities and having a point of view on business decisions.
