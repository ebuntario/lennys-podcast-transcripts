After a layoff, hold an all-hands with the remaining team to address their fears (e.g., job security, company stability). Then, have each manager conduct a one-hour [[concepts/one-on-one-meetings|one-on-one]] with every remaining report solely to listen and make them feel heard regarding their sadness, anger, and fear. This process accelerates emotional recovery and can lead to improved team performance within weeks.
