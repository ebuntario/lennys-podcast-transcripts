With AI tools, the cost of writing and deleting code plummets. The future involves describing multiple experiments in detail, having AI build them overnight, and discarding most of them. This shifts the paradigm from careful refactoring to constant, low-cost rewriting, enabling a form of [[concepts/rapid-prototyping|rapid prototyping]] at the system level that was previously impossible.
