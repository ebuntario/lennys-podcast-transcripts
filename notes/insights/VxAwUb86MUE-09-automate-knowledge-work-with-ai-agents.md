Use [[concepts/artificial-intelligence|AI agent]] platforms to automate repetitive knowledge work, such as email triage, scheduling, and CRM management. Building workflows that filter, prioritize, and even draft responses can replace the need for a full-time assistant, providing a "reliable employee" that works 24/7 for a low cost. This [[concepts/automation|automation]] frees up mental bandwidth and reduces the stress of managing an overflowing inbox.
