When gripped by [[concepts/fear|fear]], people make exaggerated predictions and their brains cannot see reality clearly. The advice from fear or anger typically points in the exact opposite direction you should go. Checking with an unbiased third party who is not emotionally involved can provide a clearer perspective and better guidance.
