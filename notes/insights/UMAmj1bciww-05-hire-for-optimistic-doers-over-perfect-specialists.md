In a startup environment, prioritize hiring curious, action-oriented generalists ("optimistic doers") over specialists who are rigid about their role. Look for people who ask "how can we get this done?" rather than "that's not my job." This mindset is critical for maintaining [[concepts/startup-momentum|momentum]] and adapting quickly.
