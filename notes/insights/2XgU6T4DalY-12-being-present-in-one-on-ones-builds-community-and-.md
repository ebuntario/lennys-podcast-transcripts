Investing in being fully [[concepts/presence|present]] during one-on-one conversations is a powerful practice for [[concepts/networking|relationship building]]. Treating these meetings as sacred, being genuinely curious, and making a positive impact on others fosters a strong professional community. This investment in people often yields reciprocal benefits and builds deep [[concepts/psychological-safety|trust]] over time.
