New categories typically emerge during major market shifts or technological innovations, not from arbitrary company decisions. For example, the rapid shift to distributed work during COVID spurred the creation of new employment platform categories. Pursuing [[concepts/category-creation|category creation]] is most viable when the market itself is demanding new solutions.
