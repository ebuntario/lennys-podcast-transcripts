No one can fully design a successful product in their head; you must put it in front of users to understand it. Embrace being wrong as a fundamental part of the process, and maintain flexibility to incorporate feedback while staying rigid about your core mission. This balance between [[concepts/vision-persistence|vision persistence]] and adaptive learning is key to product success.
