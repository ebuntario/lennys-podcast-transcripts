The primary cause of startup failure is founders resigning themselves to failure rather than actual financial depletion. Many companies shut down while still having resources because founders run out of ideas or motivation. Maintaining [[concepts/founder-resilience|founder resilience]] and continuing to experiment with new approaches—even when facing setbacks—often determines survival more than bank balances.
