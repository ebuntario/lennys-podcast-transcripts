Genuine [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]] is unmistakable when you see it; if you're unsure, you don't have it. The Silicon Valley "never quit" mindset often serves VC incentives, not founders. If you've pivoted multiple times over several years without obvious, rip-roaring growth, it is often wiser to quit and reset with a clean slate rather than persist against overwhelming odds.
