The most common and successful path into product management is often an internal transfer. Success hinges on demonstrating key characteristics: be curious, passionate about the product and solving customer problems, and proactive in expressing interest and taking on side projects. Building relationships and showing your work within the company significantly increases your chances of moving into a PM role.
