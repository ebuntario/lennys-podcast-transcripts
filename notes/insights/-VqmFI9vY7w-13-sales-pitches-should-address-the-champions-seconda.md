Beyond solving their business problem, B2B buyers have a personal objective: making a safe decision that won't jeopardize their career. Effective sales approaches address this by providing [[concepts/risk-mitigation|risk reduction]] through guarantees, implementation support, and tools to justify the purchase internally. Helping buyers feel secure in their decision increases conversion rates.
