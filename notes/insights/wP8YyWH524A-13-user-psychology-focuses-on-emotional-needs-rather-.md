Understanding [[concepts/user-psychology|user psychology]] means recognizing that people typically approach products with emotional frames rather than logical ones. Successful products address the emotional challenges users are trying to solve before appealing to rational benefits. This psychological understanding forms a crucial component of deep [[concepts/customer-knowledge|customer knowledge]] for growth practitioners.
