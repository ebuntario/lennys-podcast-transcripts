Leverage [[concepts/social-norms|social norms]] (showing what others are doing) and [[concepts/completion-bias|completion bias]] (the desire to check off items) as built-in motivators within products. These psychological drivers provide the immediate benefits needed to overcome inertia and encourage users to take actions that align with their long-term goals.
