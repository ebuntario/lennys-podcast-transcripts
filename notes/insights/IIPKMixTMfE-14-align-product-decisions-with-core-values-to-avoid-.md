Introducing features that contradict your product's core architectural philosophy (like hard-coding vs. modular building blocks) creates internal conflict. This "organ rejection" manifests in technical debt, confused users, and misaligned employees. Staying true to your [[concepts/first-principles|first principles]] is essential for long-term coherence.
