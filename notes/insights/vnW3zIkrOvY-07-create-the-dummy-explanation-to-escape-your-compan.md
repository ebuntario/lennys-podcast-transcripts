Teams living inside their product bubble must constantly create explanations assuming zero prior knowledge. Avoid internal jargon, acronyms, and assumed context, as customers don't live in your world. This "dummy explanation" test ensures your communication is accessible and resonates with people encountering your brand for the first time.
