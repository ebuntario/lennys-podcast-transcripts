At scale, the simple process of taking an idea to launch has expanded into numerous substeps like multiple research sources and review stages. This [[concepts/process-bloat|process bloat]] leads to organizational complexity and [[concepts/microspecialization|microspecialization]], where individuals handle narrow tasks. The result is slow velocity and an inability to iterate quickly, which is where real product success is found.
