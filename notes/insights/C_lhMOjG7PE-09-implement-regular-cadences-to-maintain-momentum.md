Weekly updates and six-week reviews create consistent [[concepts/accountability|accountability]] and momentum without micromanagement. These regular check-ins force teams to demonstrate progress, share learnings, and realign resources frequently. The short cycles prevent projects from drifting and enable quick course corrections based on fresh feedback.
