The shift to AI-first requires product leaders to let go of dictating every aspect of a user experience. Instead of controlling the exact "temperature of the broccoli," you provide the ingredients and cooking guidelines, and allow AI to create the experience. This requires building [[concepts/artificial-intelligence|safety guards and responsible AI]], but embraces the power of non-deterministic, learning systems.
