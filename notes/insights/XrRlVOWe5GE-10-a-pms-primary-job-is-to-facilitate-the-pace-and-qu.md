Reject the "mini-CEO" model of product management. Instead, view your core function as facilitating high-quality decision-making within the team, not being the sole decision-maker. Your role is to create the conditions—through clear principles, data, and frameworks—where engineers, designers, and others can make great calls quickly. This shift from arbiter to amplifier is key to a healthy, empowered [[concepts/product-team|team dynamic]].
