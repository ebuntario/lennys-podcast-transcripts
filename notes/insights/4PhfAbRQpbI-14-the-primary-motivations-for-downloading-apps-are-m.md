People download apps for a few core reasons: to make or save money (e.g., WhatsApp for free texting), to find a mate (e.g., Tinder), or to unplug from reality (e.g., Netflix). Understanding which fundamental [[concepts/consumer-motivation|human motivation]] your product serves is crucial for clear positioning and messaging, both internally and to users.
