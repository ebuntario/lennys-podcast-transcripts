Leaders must discern when a stalled project requires patience versus when it signals a broken process. Signs of a process problem include an unclear path to completion, uncomfortable silence from the team, or recurring missed deadlines. A leader's role is to dive deeper, [[concepts/problem-solving|unstick the blockage]], and ensure the right operational structure is in place, rather than passively hoping things will improve.
