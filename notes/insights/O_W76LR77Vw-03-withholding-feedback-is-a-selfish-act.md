Choosing not to give someone helpful feedback because it would make you uncomfortable is fundamentally selfish, as it prioritizes your own comfort over their improvement and the team's success. High-performance teams operate on a foundation of [[concepts/radical-candor|radical candor]], where useful observations are shared openly. This feedback should be open to discussion but never withheld.
