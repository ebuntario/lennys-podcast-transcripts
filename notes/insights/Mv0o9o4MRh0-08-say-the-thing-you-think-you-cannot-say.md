Tactfully voicing unspoken observations is a critical leadership skill that unlocks team progress. The method is to "detoxify" your internal commentary by asking a non-threatening question or making an observation you own (e.g., "I feel we're not discussing X"). This approach opens doors for others, builds [[concepts/psychological-safety|psychological safety]], and is often your greatest unacknowledged strength.
