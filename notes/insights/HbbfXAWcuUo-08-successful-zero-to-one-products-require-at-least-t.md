For breakthrough products, look for at least two of three inflection points: a step-function change in technology, a significant shift in consumer behavior, or a new [[concepts/market-timing|business model]] that creates advantage. Having only one inflection point rarely creates sustainable differentiation, while having multiple creates the conditions for category-defining success.
