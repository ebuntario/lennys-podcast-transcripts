Effective influence starts by intentionally choosing a "character" or mode that fits your personality, such as the compassionate caregiver, the logical challenger, or the behind-the-scenes connector. By consistently operating from this chosen style, you give yourself permission and create predictability for others. This intentionality turns your natural strengths into a reliable channel for [[concepts/influence|influence]].
