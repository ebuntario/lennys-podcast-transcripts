AI should augment human dignity and agency, not replace them, meaning every person—from artists to nurses to farmers—has a part to play. Individuals can embrace AI as a tool to enhance their unique skills and participate in community discussions about its governance. The focus should be on how [[concepts/technology-adoption|technology serves human needs]] and empowers people in their respective fields.
