The critical, repetitive voice in your head is always incorrect, even if it contains a grain of truth. It functions like an abusive boss or a child having a temper tantrum, not a helpful guide. Building a new, experimental relationship with this [[inner-critic|inner critic]]—rather than trying to silence it—is the path to freedom and productivity.
