The PMF (People, Mission, Financials) framework provides a rigorous approach to [[concepts/company-evaluation|company evaluation]] when considering job opportunities. People refers to working with those you can have productive disagreements with, mission ensures your work creates positive outcomes, and financials examines the company's fiscal discipline. Settling for two out of three criteria often leads to poor outcomes.
