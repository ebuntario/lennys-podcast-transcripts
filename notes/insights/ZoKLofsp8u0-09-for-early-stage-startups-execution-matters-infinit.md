Strategy assumes you can pursue multiple paths simultaneously, but a small startup can only do one thing at a time. Before achieving [[concepts/product-market-fit|product-market fit]], the only strategy is to get people to want your product. Good founders are execution-oriented, maintaining a single clear priority and relentlessly working on it, rather than holding abstract strategy discussions.
