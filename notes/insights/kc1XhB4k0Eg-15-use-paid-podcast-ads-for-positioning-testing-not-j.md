Run small-scale podcast advertising campaigns primarily to test positioning elements like cover art, descriptions, and trailer content. By comparing click-through and subscription rates against benchmarks, you gain data-driven insights about what resonates before investing heavily in production. This [[concepts/market-testing|market testing]] approach uses advertising as a research tool rather than just a growth channel.
