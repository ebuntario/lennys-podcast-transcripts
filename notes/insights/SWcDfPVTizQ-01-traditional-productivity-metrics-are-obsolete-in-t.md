Traditional metrics like lines of code are now actively harmful as [[concepts/ai-impact|AI tools]] can easily inflate them with verbose, low-quality output. These outdated measures fail to capture real value and can introduce technical debt, making them a poor proxy for [[concepts/productivity-measurement|developer productivity]] in the new landscape.
