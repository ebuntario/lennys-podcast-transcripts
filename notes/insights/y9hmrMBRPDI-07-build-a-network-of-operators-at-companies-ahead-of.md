An underrated way for product managers to spend time is investing in a [[concepts/professional-networking|network]] of operators at companies similar to yours but slightly ahead in growth. Ask them how they navigated challenges like open source, pricing, or acquisitions, then bring the best ideas back to apply within your own organization.
