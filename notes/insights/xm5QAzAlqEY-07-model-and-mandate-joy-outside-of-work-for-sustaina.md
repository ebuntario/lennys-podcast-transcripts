Just as athletes need recovery for peak performance, knowledge workers need active rest and joy to prevent [[concepts/burnout-prevention|burnout]] and sustain creativity. As a leader, openly model your own hobbies and creative pursuits. In one-on-ones, ask team members, "What did you do for joy today?" and treat a "no" as a problem to solve, giving them permission to prioritize well-being.
