A sales motion is the set of activities that move a prospect to a close. Founders should treat it like source code: after each customer interaction, update slides, scripts, and talk tracks to handle new objections or questions. This continuous [[concepts/iteration|iteration]] based on [[concepts/customer-feedback|customer feedback]] packages the learnings for future reps.
