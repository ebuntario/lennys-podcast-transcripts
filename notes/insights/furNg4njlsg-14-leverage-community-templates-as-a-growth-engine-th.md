User-created templates on Miroverse become acquisition channels when they rank for relevant searches (e.g., a FIFA World Cup template appearing in Google results). These [[concepts/community-content|community assets]] drive top-of-funnel traffic while demonstrating practical use cases, creating a virtuous cycle of discovery, adoption, and further template creation.
