Reaching the executive suite often requires working harder, learning more, and pushing yourself beyond your core job description. Early in her career, Carilu worked two extra hours daily and volunteered for roles in other departments, like running a product beta, to gain cross-functional insights. This willingness to take on responsibilities in the "white space" demonstrates the appetite and skill for greater leadership.
