As you advance into senior [[concepts/leadership-development|leadership roles]], the most critical skills shift from individual technical competence to [[concepts/organizational-design|organization design]] and [[concepts/empathy|empathy]]. Your role becomes building effective teams, creating the right incentives, and removing blockers. Empathy involves consciously removing your own perspective to understand others' goals and fears, enabling you to find common objectives and build alignment.
