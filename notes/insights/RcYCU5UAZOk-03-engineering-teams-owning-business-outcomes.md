Embed engineering teams directly within growth functions and give them shared ownership of business outcomes like pipeline and payback period. When engineers feel accountable for the sales quota, they proactively build technology to automate workflows, improve lead prioritization, and craft messaging. This [[concepts/cross-functional-teams|cross-functional ownership]] creates a significant competitive advantage in channel efficiency.
