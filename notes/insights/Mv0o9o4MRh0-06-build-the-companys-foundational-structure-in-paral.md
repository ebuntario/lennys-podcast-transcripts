While product-market fit is paramount, neglecting to build the company's supporting structures can cause the entire mission to fail later. Foundational elements like goals, hiring processes, and cultural documents are the "posts, beams, and mechanicals" of your [[concepts/company-building|company-building]] effort. Implementing a replicable [[concepts/operating-system|operating system]] early prevents the chaos of an organization built through 17 unplanned additions.
