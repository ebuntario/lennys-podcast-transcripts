A super small, elite team can move faster and achieve more than a large organization because the best people aren't distracted by bureaucracy and internal processes. This approach enables companies to reach massive scale, like over a billion in revenue, with fewer than 100 people by focusing on deep [[concepts/operational-efficiency|operational efficiency]] and avoiding the distractions common in large tech companies.
