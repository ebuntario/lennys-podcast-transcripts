To overcome innovation stagnation in a scaled company, create a new product team as a separate C-Corp with a founder-mentality leader. This entity should report directly to the CEO, operate with a small team, and be completely decoupled from the core product's code and brand. This structure grants the [[concepts/autonomy|autonomy]] and speed of a startup while leveraging the parent company's resources.
