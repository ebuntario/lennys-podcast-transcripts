Begin [[concepts/product-led-growth]] implementation with manual analysis using spreadsheets and dashboards to establish [[concepts/data-sales-fit]]. Partner closely with sales to identify intuitive signals that correlate with successful conversions before building automated models. This [[concepts/evolution-not-revolution]] approach ensures you understand the data thoroughly before scaling, preventing the common pitfall of implementing automation on misunderstood correlations.
