In the AI era, CEOs must become "chief taste makers" by getting hands-on with the technology. This means using AI tools like [[concepts/chatgpt|ChatGPT]] or Claude multiple times per hour, not just occasionally. To understand the solution space and make bold product decisions, you must experience the capabilities and limitations of the underlying models directly.
