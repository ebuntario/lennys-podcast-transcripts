As a product leader, you should objectively diagnose your team's and product's strengths and weaknesses, then lay this out clearly with your peers. Since product management can be confusing to non-product executives, you must explain your current state, your target destination, and the timeline to get there, such as why certain [[concepts/okrs|OKRs]] might not be fully quantitative yet.
