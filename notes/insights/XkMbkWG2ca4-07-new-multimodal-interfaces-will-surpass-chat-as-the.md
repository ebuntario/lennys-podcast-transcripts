Chat interfaces are currently dominant but represent an early, limited paradigm for human-AI interaction. Future interfaces—like infinite canvases that blend text, images, and references—will provide more intuitive and powerful ways to collaborate with AI. Building products that move beyond the chatbox will be a significant competitive advantage.
