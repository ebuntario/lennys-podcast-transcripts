Choosing a city to live in can be approached like evaluating a product, weighing costs (housing, taxes, food) against benefits (lifestyle, community, weather). This [[concepts/decision-making|framing]] helps make a rational choice based on personal ROI, allowing for flexibility to adapt as priorities change, such as splitting time between locations.
