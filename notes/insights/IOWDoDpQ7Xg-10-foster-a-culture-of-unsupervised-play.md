Modern, highly curated environments can stifle the willingness to experiment and fail. Innovation requires the freedom to "mess around" with ideas without immediate pressure for measurable output. Cultivate personal and organizational tolerance for [[concepts/exploratory-play|exploratory play]], as it's the foundation for discovering breakthrough ideas.
