LinkedIn's guiding principle of "connecting people to economic opportunity" acts as a powerful [[concepts/decision-frameworks|decision framework]] that cuts through complexity. When evaluating any product change, teams ask how it advances this North Star, which simplifies prioritization across interconnected marketplaces. This consistent, repeated vision creates an organizational "immune system" that naturally aligns efforts and helps resolve conflicts.
