The decision to shut down a product comes when you're putting in 10 units of effort for 1 unit of output, and there's no natural energy or compounding growth in the system. This often manifests as lack of organic spread, deteriorating ecosystem dependencies (like mobile web quality), or misalignment with team strengths. Recognizing this [[concepts/opportunity-cost|opportunity cost]] early frees resources for more promising ventures and is often respected by the community.
