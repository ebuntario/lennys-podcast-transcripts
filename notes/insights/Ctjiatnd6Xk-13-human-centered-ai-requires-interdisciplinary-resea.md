Developing AI responsibly necessitates collaboration across fields like medicine, law, humanities, and business to understand its broad societal impact. It also requires proactive [[concepts/ai-policy|policy engagement]] to bridge the gap between technologists and regulators, ensuring governance keeps pace with innovation. Institutes focused on [[concepts/human-centered-ai|human-centered AI]] can coordinate this [[concepts/interdisciplinary-research|cross-disciplinary work]] and advocate for beneficial frameworks.
