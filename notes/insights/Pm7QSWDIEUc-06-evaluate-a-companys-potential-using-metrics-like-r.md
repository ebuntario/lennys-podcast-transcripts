When assessing a company's potential, key metrics include the Rule of 40 (profitability + growth rate), the quality of investors, Net Promoter Score, and critically, [[concepts/product-metrics|net dollar retention]] (NDR). A high NDR, like Snowflake's historic 180%, indicates customers are significantly expanding their usage, signaling a strong, healthy business that can grow efficiently from its existing base.
