AI coding assistants enable project-based learning by allowing students to build real tools for businesses while receiving contextual guidance. The AI acts as a tutor, whispering solutions and explanations, which helps learners understand codebases and patterns faster. This makes [[concepts/ai-in-education|AI in education]] a powerful tool for both skill acquisition and practical application.
