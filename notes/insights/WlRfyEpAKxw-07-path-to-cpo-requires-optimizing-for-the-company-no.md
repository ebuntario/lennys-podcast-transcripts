Aspiring product executives must learn to optimize for the entire company's success, even at the expense of their own team's priorities. Developing an [[concepts/executive-mindset|executive mindset]] involves understanding the entire business deeply, learning to speak the language of other executives, and interpreting their questions as attempts to learn rather than tests. Refusing to overspecialize and combining diverse skills allows a leader to work on the most important company-wide problems.
