Effective buy-in involves partnering early and often with all affected stakeholders, incorporating their feedback throughout the development process. Come to final decision meetings not to "win" but to collaboratively solve a problem, openly sharing hypotheses and data. This builds advocates and ensures the solution is robust, as it incorporates diverse [[concepts/stakeholder-management|stakeholder perspectives]] from the start.
