A strong [[concepts/sales-pitch-framework|sales pitch framework]] begins with sharing your unique insight about the market to establish credibility. This "setup" phase helps buyers understand the broader landscape before discussing your solution. For example, Help Scout starts by explaining how digital businesses view customer service as a growth driver, not a cost center.
