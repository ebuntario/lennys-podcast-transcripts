Many flash-in-the-pan consumer products are clever interfaces that lack lasting power. Durable [[concepts/consumer-products|consumer products]] often succeed by building underlying [[concepts/network-effects|network effects]] or creating market tilts in their favor. Examples include leveraging excess capacity, driving traffic that incentivizes partners, or tapping into a unique psychological insight that competitors can't easily replicate.
