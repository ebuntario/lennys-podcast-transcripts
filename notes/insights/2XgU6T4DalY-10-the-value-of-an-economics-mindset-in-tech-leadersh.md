An [[concepts/economics-mindset|economics mindset]] is a valuable form of data science that brings [[concepts/systems-thinking|systems thinking]] to business problems. It's particularly useful for understanding [[concepts/incentive-alignment|incentives]] and anticipating unintended consequences, both in internal team dynamics and external product strategy. This perspective helps simplify complex problems into tractable models.
