When [[concepts/pitching|pitching]] a funding story, be direct: state the amount raised, the investors, and the space the company operates in. Offer the story as an exclusive to one reporter at a time. Follow up respectfully after a few days, potentially with new information, before moving to another relevant reporter if there's no response.
