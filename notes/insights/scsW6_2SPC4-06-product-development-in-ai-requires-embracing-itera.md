Given the rapid pace of AI change, detailed long-term roadmaps are less useful than a philosophy of [[concepts/iterative-deployment|iterative deployment]]. It's better to ship early, learn from how the product is used in the real world, and co-evolve with society. This approach values speed and learning over perfection, accepting that some launches will require rollbacks or significant changes.
