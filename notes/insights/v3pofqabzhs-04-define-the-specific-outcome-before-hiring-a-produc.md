Before starting a search, get granular on the hire's mandate and what success looks like in 90 days, one year, and beyond. Determine if you need a visionary, a hands-on builder, or a specialist in growth or monetization. This [[concepts/role-clarity|role clarity]] prevents hiring for a vague title and ensures alignment on expectations from the start.
