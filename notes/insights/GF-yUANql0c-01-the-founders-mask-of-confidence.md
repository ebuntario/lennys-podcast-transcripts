Founders and CEOs often experience significant anxiety and stress, yet they must project a [[concepts/team-leadership|mask of confidence]] externally and internally. This tension exists because teams need to trust their leader, and exposing them to the founder's full anxiety can be counterproductive, creating a difficult balance between authenticity and necessary stability.
