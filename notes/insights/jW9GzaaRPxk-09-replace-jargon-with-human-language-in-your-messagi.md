Strip out marketing jargon like "robust multiplayer experience" or "human-in-the-loop collaboration." To craft clear [[concepts/messaging|messaging]], verbally answer basic questions: What does the product do? Who is it for? Who are your customers? The way you explain it in conversation is usually closer to effective press language than formal marketing copy.
