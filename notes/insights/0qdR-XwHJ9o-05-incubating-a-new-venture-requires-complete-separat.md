To successfully incubate a high-growth venture inside an established company, create full separation in teams, office space, and operating cadence. Dedicate people 100% to the new initiative with no legacy responsibilities, and adopt a metrics-driven, fast-paced culture comfortable with ambiguity. This prevents the processes and priorities of the mature business from stifling the [[concepts/startup-within-a-company|zero-to-one effort]].
