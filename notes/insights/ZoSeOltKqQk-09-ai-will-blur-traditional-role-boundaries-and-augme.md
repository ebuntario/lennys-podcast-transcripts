Generative AI will significantly change how we work, blurring lines between product managers, engineers, and designers as tools enable each to perform tasks outside their traditional domain. To avoid being left behind, actively use AI products to augment your productivity. The people who learn to leverage [[concepts/artificial-intelligence|AI]] effectively will work faster and be far ahead of others.
