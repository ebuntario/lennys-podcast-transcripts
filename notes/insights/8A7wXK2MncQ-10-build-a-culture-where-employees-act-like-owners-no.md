A key cultural value is encouraging employees to "think and act like owners, not renters." This means doing what's right even when no one is watching and having the courage to deliver bad news early. This [[concepts/company-culture|ownership mindset]] empowers individuals at all levels to take responsibility, push back when necessary, and drive outcomes as if the company were their own.
