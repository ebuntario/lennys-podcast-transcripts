Even if a founder built a product to solve their own problem, markets, products, and customers evolve. Regular [[concepts/customer-research|customer research]] with your best customers consistently reveals new insights that can improve positioning and the product experience. Internal assumptions are not a substitute for direct, ongoing [[concepts/market-validation|validation]].
