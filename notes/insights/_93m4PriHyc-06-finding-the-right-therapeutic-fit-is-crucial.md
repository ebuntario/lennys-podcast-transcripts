The most important factor in choosing a therapist is feeling a sense of safety and comfort, akin to an animal feeling secure enough to learn. Ideally, the therapist should also match or exceed your intellectual capacity to provide insights that inspire and resonate. The therapeutic relationship is foundational for effective [[concepts/healing|healing]] and growth.
