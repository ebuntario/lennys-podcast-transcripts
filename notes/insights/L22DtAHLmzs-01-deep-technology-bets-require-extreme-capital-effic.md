When pursuing a [[concepts/technology-bets|deep technology bet]] with an uncertain timeline to product-market fit, maintaining an extremely low burn rate is critical. This allows a startup to take many "shots on goal" over years without running out of capital. The default stance should be to spend as little as possible until you see undeniable market pull, as premature scaling on assumptions can kill the company before the core technology finds its ideal application.
