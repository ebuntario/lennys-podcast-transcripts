Many leaders struggle because they try to conform to a narrow archetype of what a leader "should" be. Effective [[concepts/authentic-leadership|authentic leadership]] comes from understanding your unique strengths and values and leading from that authentic place. You can achieve outcomes through different styles; the key is to find environments where your authentic approach is valued.
