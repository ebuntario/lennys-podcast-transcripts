Traditional SaaS companies often capture 10-20% of the value they create. For AI products with high attribution and autonomy, you can justify capturing 25-50% through an [[concepts/outcome-based-pricing|outcome-based pricing model]]. This is possible because AI directly impacts business KPIs, delivers hard cost savings, and operates autonomously.
