Actively share skills and knowledge that aren't explicit to your core role. For example, if you're good at communicating with brevity, write tips for the team. This builds your reputation as a supportive, creative problem-solver across the company. When promotion discussions happen, having a broader positive brand makes it easier for your manager to advocate for you and for others to support your [[concepts/career-growth|career advancement]].
