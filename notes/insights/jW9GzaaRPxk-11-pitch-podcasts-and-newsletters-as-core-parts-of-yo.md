Newsletters and podcasts are now fundamental parts of the media landscape and should be included in any [[concepts/pr-strategy|PR program]]. To find relevant shows, search for your customer vertical, then qualify them by checking if they feature vendor executives, are actively producing, and have high-quality, aligned past guests.
