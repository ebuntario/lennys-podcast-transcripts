Stand-up comedy provides a model for [[concepts/rapid-iteration|tight iteration cycles]] with immediate, clear feedback—similar to launching minimum viable products. Developing resilience to tough feedback in this context translates directly to product building, where initial versions often need refinement despite having strong vision.
