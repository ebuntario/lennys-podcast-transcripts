The idea that sales requires a "born seller" is a myth perpetuated by those protecting their own value. Sales involves a set of learnable behaviors like rapid rapport building and asking good questions. Adopting a [[concepts/sales-mindset|sales mindset]]—shifting from deep work to frequent customer interactions—is a skill anyone can develop with practice.
