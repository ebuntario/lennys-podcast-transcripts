Take major [[concepts/career-risk|career risk]] during a 2-3 year window when you have sufficient [[concepts/skill-development|skill-building]] to recover if the bet fails. Early career should focus on core skill development in stable environments, while mid-career is ideal for "big swings" that create career-defining stories. This timing maximizes upside while preserving downside protection.
