The most effective way to start a startup is by talking to potential customers and attempting to pre-sell your concept before any development begins. If you can generate genuine excitement and secure commitments through conversations alone, that's a strong signal to proceed with building. This [[concepts/idea-validation|idea validation]] approach prevents wasted effort on products nobody wants and provides clearer direction for development.
