When companies transition from hypergrowth to complex operations, they need systematic approaches since leadership can't be in every meeting. Amazon applied scientific experimentation to develop [[concepts/process-innovation|process innovation]] during its 2003-2007 scaling period. These repeatable processes allow organizations to manage complexity while maintaining speed and ownership.
