Avoid asking "what should we do with AI?" Instead, work backwards from customer problems and identify where AI can reduce friction or manual work. This [[concepts/problem-first-thinking|problem-first approach]] ensures AI integration is purposeful and enhances existing workflows rather than being a superficial add-on.
