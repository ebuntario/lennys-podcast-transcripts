Assume executives have a "dinosaur brain" that can only hold a few key facts. Your value lies in synthesizing information and presenting a clear, opinionated recommendation. This complementary approach—where you own the recommendation and they own the broader context—enables faster, more effective [[concepts/executive-communication|executive communication]].
