Companies should adapt Amazon's processes to their existing [[concepts/cultural-adaptation|culture]] rather than attempting wholesale transformation. The goal is improving specific organizational capabilities, not becoming Amazon. Successful implementation involves selecting processes that address current pain points while respecting established cultural norms and operational realities.
