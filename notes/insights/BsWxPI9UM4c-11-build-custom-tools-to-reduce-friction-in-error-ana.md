Since manual trace inspection is so critical, invest in building or customizing simple tools to make the process effortless. Use AI-assisted coding to create lightweight interfaces that streamline viewing, annotating, and categorizing traces. Reducing friction here ensures your team will consistently engage in this high-value activity.
