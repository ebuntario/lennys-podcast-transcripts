Every time you grant decision-making autonomy to an AI system, you relinquish some control. To manage this [[concepts/agency-control-tradeoff|agency-control trade-off]], start with high human control and low agency, such as an AI that only suggests actions. This allows you to build confidence and trust in the system's reliability before gradually increasing its autonomy in subsequent versions.
