Landing a desired role often requires repeated, proactive effort—like showing up at an office multiple times or volunteering for projects outside your official scope. This persistence demonstrates commitment and allows you to build the skills and relationships needed for the job. Creating your own luck through [[concepts/persistence|persistent effort]] is more effective than waiting for the perfect opportunity.
