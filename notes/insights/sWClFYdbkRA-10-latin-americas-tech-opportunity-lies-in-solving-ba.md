Latin America represents massive [[concepts/market-opportunity|market opportunity]] because many basic services haven't been digitized yet, creating low-hanging fruit for startups. The region often skips technological generations (like moving straight to mobile), allowing for innovative solutions tailored to local contexts. Entrepreneurs who understand these [[concepts/emerging-markets|emerging market]] dynamics can build solutions that scale across similar regions.
