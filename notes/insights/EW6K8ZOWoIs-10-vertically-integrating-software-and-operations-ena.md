Building and controlling the full stack—from the patient-facing app to the provider's tools and the back-end claims processing—allows a company to create seamless experiences that consumers expect but rarely find. This [[concepts/vertical-integration|deep vertical integration]] is what enables features like native integration of continuous glucose monitor data into an electronic medical record, creating a cohesive and modern user journey.
