In a rapidly changing environment, focus on the journey of continuous improvement rather than a fixed end state. This [[concepts/mindset|growth mindset]] principle—"becoming is better than being"—applies to both individuals and organizations. Measure progress by how much you've learned and grown over a period, embracing [[concepts/continuous-improvement|continuous evolution]] as the goal itself.
