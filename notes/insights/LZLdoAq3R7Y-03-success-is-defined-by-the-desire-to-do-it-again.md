A business is successful if, at the end of the day, you want to do it again because it was enjoyable and worthwhile. This [[concepts/business-philosophy|philosophy]] prioritizes personal fulfillment and pride in the work over external metrics like revenue targets or growth. It shifts the focus from chasing outcomes to creating a sustainable and enjoyable process that you'd willingly repeat.
