If other marketing channels like SEO, email, or influencer partnerships are effective, paid acquisition is likely to succeed. [[concepts/product-market-fit|Product-market fit]] provides the foundational confidence that audiences will respond. Paid channels amplify existing demand rather than creating it from scratch.
