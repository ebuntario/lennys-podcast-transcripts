Mere usage is not enough to drive organizational spread. An individual contributor must love the product to risk their social capital advocating for it internally. This level of [[concepts/user-advocacy|user advocacy]] is necessary because champions are personally driving change and need deep belief to overcome organizational inertia and become effective [[concepts/internal-champions|internal champions]].
