Specialists like SEO or paid acquisition experts should be hired as "surgeons" only after establishing foundational [[concepts/growth-strategy-execution|growth execution]] capabilities within the team. These specialized skills take years to develop and aren't easily taught internally. For first growth hires, prioritize generalists with strong execution and customer knowledge over specialists with narrow expertise.
