Instead of waiting 90 days to measure retention impact from onboarding experiments, use [[concepts/metrics|proxy metrics]] that correlate with long-term success. At Patreon, velocity to reach certain revenue thresholds served as early indicators of creator retention. Combining quantitative proxies with qualitative screening of user behaviors provides confidence in experiment outcomes.
