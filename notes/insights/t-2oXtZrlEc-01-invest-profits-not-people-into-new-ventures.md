When you are a leader in a core product, you must continue to out-invest competitors in that core to avoid disruption. The profits generated from the core should be used to fund new ventures, not by reallocating people away from the core. This [[concepts/core-product-focus|core product focus]] prevents distraction and maintains your competitive moat.
