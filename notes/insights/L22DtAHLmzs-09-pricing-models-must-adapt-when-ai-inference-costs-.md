When an AI product delivers immense, tangible value (like building an app in minutes), users are willing to pay for consumption rather than a simple flat-rate [[concepts/subscription-models|subscription]]. This led to a usage-based pricing model that became an industry standard, moving away from the "all-you-can-eat" Netflix model because it better aligns high inference costs with the high ROI customers receive.
