Imposter phenomenon is widespread, especially in cross-functional roles like product management where you're rarely the expert in the room. While internal work like naming and managing your [[concepts/inner-critic|inner critic]] is valuable, leaders must also recognize their role in systemic issues that contribute to these feelings in others. Creating a psychologically safe environment is a leader's obligation, not just an individual's challenge to overcome.
