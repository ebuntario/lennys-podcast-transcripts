Evals are like quizzes or unit tests for AI models, measuring how well they perform on specific tasks or knowledge domains. Understanding a model's performance on evals (e.g., 60% vs. 99% accuracy) dictates the entire [[concepts/product-development|product design]] you build around it. This skill is crucial for fine-tuning models and creating effective, reliable AI-powered products.
