Leaders must protect large blocks of open time on their calendars. A schedule packed with meetings and one-on-ones is a barrier to working on [[concepts/high-leverage-work|high-leverage problems]] that require deep focus. Intentional [[concepts/time-management|time management]] creates the space needed to dive into details and drive meaningful execution.
