Beyond your core responsibilities (Opportunity Set A), actively seek out unprompted opportunities (Set B) like new projects, skills, or side ventures. These self-directed pursuits lead to unexpected growth and open future doors. Embracing [[concepts/professional-growth|career development]] through Opportunity Set B is how you build a versatile, resilient professional identity.
