Working with other creators allows for [[concepts/cross-promotion|audience sharing]] and exposure to new communities. These partnerships can lead to collaborative content that benefits all parties involved by tapping into each other's fan bases. Facilitating these connections, such as through events, can be a powerful growth lever for creators.
