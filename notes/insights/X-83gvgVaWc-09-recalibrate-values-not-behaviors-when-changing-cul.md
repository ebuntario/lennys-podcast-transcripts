When moving from a strong company culture like Apple to another, you are often hired for the *values* of your previous organization, not the specific *behaviors*. Successful [[concepts/cultural-immigration|cultural immigration]] requires holding onto core values like attention to detail and product excellence, but consciously adapting how those values are expressed within the new culture's norms and communication styles.
