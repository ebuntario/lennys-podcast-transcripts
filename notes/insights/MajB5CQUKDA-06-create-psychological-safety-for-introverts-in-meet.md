Leaders can support [[concepts/introvert-success|introvert success]] by implementing structured participation methods like offline voting or round-robin speaking in meetings. These "bias interrupters" ensure everyone has equal voice regardless of communication style. Creating this [[concepts/psychological-safety|psychological safety]] allows processors time to contribute their valuable insights.
