Miro Connect is a biweekly, in-person demo event where teams showcase work in a social setting. This casual cross-pollination led to an engineer from another team solving a three-month technical challenge in hours after a conversation. Such rituals create [[concepts/serendipitous-innovation|serendipitous collaboration]] moments that formal processes often miss.
